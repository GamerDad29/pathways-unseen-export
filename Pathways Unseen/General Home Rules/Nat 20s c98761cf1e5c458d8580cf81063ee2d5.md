# Nat 20s

### **Natural 20s in Combat and Skill Checks**

**Combat Natural 20s**:

**1. Critical Surge**:

- **Effect**: The character gains an immediate second action in the current combat round.
- **Description**: The character’s critical hit unleashes a surge of adrenaline, enabling them to strike again or perform another action instantly.

**2. Felling Blow**:

- **Effect**: If the target is at 10% or less of its maximum hit points, the attack instantly kills or incapacitates the target.
- **Description**: The character’s critical hit lands with devastating precision, delivering a fatal or incapacitating blow to a weakened opponent.

**3. Battlefield Inspiration**:

- **Effect**: The character’s critical hit inspires an ally, granting them an immediate attack roll against a target within range.
- **Description**: The character’s impressive strike bolsters the morale of their companions, spurring them into immediate action.

**Skill Check Natural 20s**:

**Acrobatics**:

1. **Graceful Agility**:
    - **Effect**: The character executes their maneuver with such grace that they inspire awe in onlookers. This can lead to gaining advantage on social interactions with those who witnessed the feat.
    - **Description**: Whether it's a breathtaking flip or an impossible leap, the character’s agility is mesmerizing, leaving a lasting impression.
2. **Perfect Balance**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s balance is so flawless that they can maintain it under extreme conditions, such as on a moving platform or during combat. They gain advantage on their next Dexterity-based check or saving throw.
    - **Description**: The character’s ability to stay balanced is almost superhuman, allowing them to navigate treacherous terrain or evade attacks effortlessly.

**Animal Handling**:

1. **Instant Bond**:
    - **Effect**: The character forms an immediate and deep bond with the animal, gaining its trust completely. The animal will perform tasks beyond its usual training or capabilities.
    - **Description**: The animal reacts to the character with unwavering loyalty and obedience, willing to go to great lengths to assist them.
2. **Calming Presence**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s presence calms not only the targeted animal but also all nearby creatures. This can prevent a stampede or soothe a group of agitated animals.
    - **Description**: The character’s touch or voice has a magical calming effect, bringing peace to even the most distressed creatures.
