# Attunment Increase - The Process of Soul Expansion

### Accessible Pathways for Adventurers

In the world of "Pathways Unseen," adventurers seeking to expand their attunement capabilities can engage in ancient rites and rituals discovered by priestly practitioners. These practices, known as the Arcane Resonance, enable characters to harmonize with more magical items. These blessings can be acquired through various means, including direct purchases or fulfilling boons for the church.

### The Process of Soul Expansion

**Accessible Pathways:**

- **Rite of the First Expansion**
    - **Requirements (Purchase Option)**: Donate 500 gold worth of rare incense to the church.
    - **Requirements (Boon Option)**: Complete a minor quest for the church, such as aiding in a local dispute or retrieving a sacred artifact.
    - **Skill Check**: Successful Religion check (DC 15).
    - **Effect**: Gain a 4th attunement slot.
    - **Description**: This rite involves deep meditation and prayer, allowing the practitioner's soul to expand and harmonize with an additional magical item.
- **Rite of the Second Expansion**
    - **Requirements (Purchase Option)**: Donate 1000 gold worth of sacred relics to the church.
    - **Requirements (Boon Option)**: Complete a more significant quest for the church, such as defending a temple from invaders or negotiating peace between warring factions.
    - **Skill Check**: Successful Religion check (DC 18).
    - **Effect**: Gain a 5th attunement slot.
    - **Description**: Through prolonged study and sacred rituals, the practitioner's soul opens further, granting the ability to attune to an additional magical item.
- **Rite of the Third Expansion**
    - **Requirements (Purchase Option)**: Donate an ancient tome of knowledge or its equivalent worth to the church.
    - **Requirements (Boon Option)**: Undertake a challenging quest, such as retrieving a lost artifact from a dangerous dungeon or vanquishing a powerful foe threatening the church.
    - **Skill Check**: Successful Religion check (DC 20).
    - **Effect**: Gain a 6th attunement slot.
    - **Description**: This advanced rite involves the absorption of ancient arcane knowledge, allowing the practitioner to harmonize with yet another magical item.
- **Rite of the Ultimate Expansion**
    - **Requirements (Purchase Option)**: Donate 5000 gold worth of celestial essence to the church.
    - **Requirements (Boon Option)**: Complete an epic quest, such as saving a high priest from certain death or restoring a holy site of great importance.
    - **Skill Check**: Successful Religion check (DC 25).
    - **Effect**: Gain a 7th attunement slot.
    - **Description**: The ultimate rite, opening the practitioner's soul to its fullest potential. This powerful ritual grants the ability to attune to a seventh magical item.

### Balancing the Power

### Psychological and Physical Strain

As characters attune to more items, the strain on their mind and body increases, leading to potential consequences starting from the 5th attunement slot.

**4th Attunement Slot:**

- **Effect**: No adverse effects.

**5th Attunement Slot:**

- **Effect**: Minor strain. Occasional headaches and mild fatigue.
- **Mechanic**: On a roll of 1 on a d20 during a long rest, the character suffers a point of exhaustion.

**6th Attunement Slot:**

- **Effect**: Moderate strain. Frequent headaches, fatigue, and occasional hallucinations.
- **Mechanic**: On a roll of 1-2 on a d20 during a long rest, the character suffers a point of exhaustion.

**7th Attunement Slot:**

- **Effect**: Severe strain. Chronic headaches, constant fatigue, vivid hallucinations, and minor personality changes. Risk of losing control over the attuned items.
- **Mechanic**: On a roll of 1-3 on a d20 during a long rest, the character suffers a point of exhaustion. Additionally, once per day, the DM may ask for a Wisdom saving throw (DC 15). On a failure, the character loses control of one of their attuned items for 1d4 hours, during which time the item acts on its own accord.

### Psychological Impact

- **Fear of Loss**: The character develops a fear of losing control over their magical items.
    - **Mechanic**: Wisdom saving throw (DC 12) when faced with the threat of disarmament or theft. On a failure, the character suffers disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks for the next hour.
- **Paranoia**: Increased attunement slots may lead to paranoia about others wanting to steal or misuse their items.
    - **Mechanic**: Charisma saving throw (DC 15) when interacting with strangers. On a failure, the character behaves suspiciously and aggressively, potentially leading to social conflicts.

### Recovery and Healing

- **Therapeutic Rituals**: Characters can participate in rituals to alleviate the strain caused by multiple attunements.
    - **Requirement**: Sacred herbs, calming incense, and a successful Medicine check (DC 15).
    - **Effect**: Reduces the exhaustion risk by one step (e.g., from 1-3 to 1-2 on a d20).
- **Quests for Restoration**: Characters can embark on quests to find rare and powerful artifacts or beings capable of healing the soul.
    - **Reward**: Permanent reduction in the strain effects or removal of the risk entirely.
