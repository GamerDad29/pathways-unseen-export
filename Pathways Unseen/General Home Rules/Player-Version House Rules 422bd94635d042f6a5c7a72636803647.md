# Player-Version House Rules

### 🛡️ **Player Homebrew Rules Summary** 🧙‍♂️

---

Welcome, adventurers! As you embark on your journey in the **"Pathways Unseen"** campaign, here are some unique house rules and systems that will enrich our adventure. These rules are designed to shape your experience and add depth to our story. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with them.

---

### ⚔️ **General Homebrew Rules**

1. **Bonus-Action Potions:**
    
    You can drink a potion as a bonus action. Using a full action to drink a potion maximizes its healing effect.
    
2. **Surprise Attacks:**
    
    Gaining surprise on an enemy grants you advantage on attack rolls against them.
    
3. **Meta-Combat Round:**
    
    Before each round of combat, you’ll have one minute to discuss strategy with your teammates. During combat, communicate what your character can reasonably articulate.
    

---

### 🎲 **Natural 20's in Combat**

1. **Felling Blow:**
    
    If your attack reduces the target to 10% of its maximum HP or less, the target must make a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your ability modifier) or be knocked unconscious.
    
2. **Battlefield Inspiration:**
    
    Scoring a natural 20 has the potential to inspire one of your allies, granting them advantage on their next attack roll.
    

---

### 📜 **Natural 20's in Skill Checks**

Rolling a natural 20 on skill checks triggers something special depending on the ability and situation. Stay tuned for unique and exciting outcomes!

---

### ☠️ **Natural 1's in Combat and Skill Checks**

- **Combat:**
    
    A natural 1 in combat could result in self-inflicted harm, weapon mishap, or providing an enemy with an opportunity.
    
- **Skill Checks:**
    
    A natural 1 in skill checks may lead to catastrophic failures, such as damaging equipment, offending a critical NPC, or causing unintended consequences.
    

---

These homebrew rules will help create memorable moments and add layers of strategy and excitement to our campaign. Let’s embrace the unpredictability of the dice and enjoy the journey ahead!

---

This version highlights key points with emojis and bold text, making the rules easy to navigate and visually appealing. Let me know if there’s anything else you’d like to add or adjust!
