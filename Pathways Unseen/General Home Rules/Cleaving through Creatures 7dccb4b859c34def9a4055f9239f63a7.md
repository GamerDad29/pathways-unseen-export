# Cleaving through Creatures

This homebrew rule allows for dynamic and cinematic combat encounters where melee attackers can continue their onslaught through multiple enemies.

### Cleaving Through Creatures

**Description**: If a melee attack reduces an enemy to 0 hit points, any excess damage can carry over to another enemy within reach.

**Mechanic**:

- **Activation**: When a melee attack reduces an enemy to 0 hit points, the attacker can apply any remaining damage to another enemy within their melee range.
- **Damage Transfer**: The excess damage from the original attack is carried over to the next target, continuing until there is no excess damage left or no more enemies within reach.
- **Limitations**: This can only occur once per turn. The secondary targets must be within the reach of the initial attack (e.g., a character with a 5-foot reach can only cleave into enemies within 5 feet).

### Example

A fighter swings their greatsword, dealing 15 damage to a goblin with 10 HP. The goblin is reduced to 0 HP, and the remaining 5 damage is carried over to another goblin within reach, potentially killing it as well if its HP is low enough.
