# Nat 1s

### **Natural 1s in Combat and Skill Checks**

**Combat Natural 1s**:

**1. Critical Blunder**:

- **Effect**: The character’s attack backfires, dealing damage to themselves or an ally.
- **Description**: The character’s weapon slips or misfires, causing unintended harm.
- **Mechanic**: The character takes damage equal to their weapon's damage dice, or an ally within range takes half the damage.

**2. Disarmed**:

- **Effect**: The character loses grip on their weapon, causing it to fall to the ground or be thrown a short distance away.
- **Description**: The character’s grip fails, and their weapon slips from their hands.
- **Mechanic**: The character’s weapon falls 10 feet away in a random direction, and they must spend an action to retrieve it.

**3. Exposed Position**:

- **Effect**: The character’s failed attack leaves them vulnerable, granting enemies advantage on attacks against them until their next turn.
- **Description**: The character’s misstep exposes them to enemy attacks.
- **Mechanic**: Enemies have advantage on attacks against the character until the start of their next turn.

**Skill Check Natural 1s**:

**Acrobatics**:

1. **Stumble**:
    - **Effect**: The character trips and falls, ending their movement and causing them to be prone.
    - **Description**: The character’s attempt at agility fails, and they lose their balance.
    - **Mechanic**: The character is knocked prone and their movement ends.
2. **Injury**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s misstep results in a minor injury, imposing disadvantage on their next Dexterity-based check.
    - **Description**: The character’s failed acrobatic maneuver leads to a twisted ankle or strained muscle.
    - **Mechanic**: The character has disadvantage on their next Dexterity-based check.

**Animal Handling**:

1. **Frightened Animal**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt to handle the animal results in the animal becoming frightened or aggressive.
    - **Description**: The animal reacts poorly to the character’s approach, becoming agitated.
    - **Mechanic**: The animal either flees or attacks the character.
2. **Lost Trust**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s mishandling causes the animal to lose trust in them, making future interactions more difficult.
    - **Description**: The animal becomes wary and uncooperative.
    - **Mechanic**: The character has disadvantage on future Animal Handling checks with this animal.

**Arcana**:

1. **Backfire**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt to manipulate magical forces results in a backlash, causing minor magical damage.
    - **Description**: The magical energies react unpredictably, causing a small explosion or shock.
    - **Mechanic**: The character takes 1d4 force damage.
2. **False Information**:
    - **Effect**: The character misinterprets the magical knowledge, leading to incorrect conclusions or actions.
    - **Description**: The character’s understanding of the arcane is flawed, resulting in mistakes.
    - **Mechanic**: The character gains incorrect information about the magical topic.

**Athletics**:

1. **Strained Muscle**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s exertion causes a muscle strain, imposing disadvantage on their next Strength-based check.
    - **Description**: The character’s physical effort leads to a painful strain.
    - **Mechanic**: The character has disadvantage on their next Strength-based check.
2. **Fumble**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt to lift or move an object fails, causing it to slip and potentially damage them or an ally.
    - **Description**: The character loses their grip, causing the object to fall.
    - **Mechanic**: The character or an ally takes 1d4 bludgeoning damage from the falling object.

**Deception**:

1. **Caught in the Lie**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s lie is immediately detected, causing distrust and suspicion.
    - **Description**: The character’s deception is obvious, leading to negative consequences.
    - **Mechanic**: The character has disadvantage on social interactions with those who caught the lie.
2. **Unintended Consequences**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s lie backfires, leading to an unintended and problematic outcome.
    - **Description**: The lie creates a situation that the character did not anticipate.
    - **Mechanic**: The DM introduces a complication as a result of the failed deception.

**History**:

1. **False Memory**:
    - **Effect**: The character recalls incorrect historical information, leading to a poor decision or misunderstanding.
    - **Description**: The character’s memory of history is flawed, resulting in mistakes.
    - **Mechanic**: The character acts on incorrect information provided by the DM.
2. **Overlooked Detail**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s focus on irrelevant details causes them to miss crucial information.
    - **Description**: The character’s historical knowledge is distracted by trivial facts.
    - **Mechanic**: The character misses an important detail that could have helped them.

**Insight**:

1. **Misread Intentions**:
    - **Effect**: The character completely misreads someone’s intentions, leading to poor decisions or misunderstandings.
    - **Description**: The character’s intuition fails, causing them to trust the wrong person or doubt an ally.
    - **Mechanic**: The character gains incorrect information about someone’s intentions.
2. **Overconfidence**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s overconfidence in their insight leads them to ignore warning signs or crucial information.
    - **Description**: The character believes they understand the situation perfectly, but they are wrong.
    - **Mechanic**: The character has disadvantage on the next Insight check involving the same person or situation.

**Intimidation**:

1. **Backfire**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt to intimidate backfires, causing the target to become hostile or aggressive.
    - **Description**: The character’s intimidation is met with resistance, escalating the situation.
    - **Mechanic**: The target becomes aggressive or attacks the character.
2. **Lost Credibility**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s failed intimidation causes others to view them as less threatening or authoritative.
    - **Description**: The character’s failure undermines their authority or reputation.
    - **Mechanic**: The character has disadvantage on future Intimidation checks with those who witnessed the failure.

**Investigation**:

1. **Missed Clue**:
    - **Effect**: The character overlooks a crucial piece of evidence or information.
    - **Description**: The character’s investigation fails to uncover important details.
    - **Mechanic**: The character misses a clue that would have advanced the investigation.
2. **False Lead**:
    - **Effect**: The character follows an incorrect lead, wasting time and resources.
    - **Description**: The character’s investigation is misled by incorrect assumptions.
    - **Mechanic**: The character pursues a false lead provided by the DM.

**Medicine**:

1. **Harmful Treatment**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s medical treatment causes more harm than good, worsening the patient’s condition.
    - **Description**: The character’s attempt at healing backfires, causing additional injury.
    - **Mechanic**: The patient takes 1d4 damage instead of being healed.
2. **Misdiagnosis**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s diagnosis is incorrect, leading to ineffective or harmful treatment.
    - **Description**: The character’s understanding of the ailment is flawed, resulting in poor care.
    - **Mechanic**: The character administers a treatment that has no effect or makes the condition worse.

**Nature**:

1. **Misidentification**:
    - **Effect**: The character misidentifies a plant or animal, leading to incorrect actions or dangerous situations.
    - **Description**: The character’s knowledge of nature fails, causing mistakes.
    - **Mechanic**: The character acts on incorrect information about a plant or animal.
2. **Environmental Hazard**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s interaction with the environment triggers a hazard, such as a trap or dangerous animal.
    - **Description**: The character’s actions inadvertently cause a dangerous situation.
    - **Mechanic**: The character triggers a hazard introduced by the DM.

**Perception**:

1. **Blind Spot**:
    - **Effect**: The character fails to notice a crucial detail, allowing danger to go unnoticed.
    - **Description**: The character’s perception is flawed, missing important details.
    - **Mechanic**: The character overlooks a threat or important clue provided by the DM.
2. **False Alarm**:
    - **Effect**: The character perceives a threat that isn’t there, causing unnecessary alarm or wasted effort.
    - **Description**: The character’s perception is misled by false signs of danger.
    - **Mechanic**: The character reacts to a false threat created by the DM.

**Performance**:

1. **Stage Fright**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s performance is marred by nervousness, causing them to falter and fail to impress.
    - **Description**: The character’s confidence fails, leading to a poor performance.
    - **Mechanic**: The character’s performance fails to have the desired effect on the audience.
2. **Heckler’s Distraction**:
    - **Effect**: The character is distracted by a heckler or unexpected interruption, ruining their performance.
    - **Description**: The character’s focus is broken, causing their performance to falter.
    - **Mechanic**: The character’s performance is disrupted by a heckler or interruption created by the DM.

**Persuasion**:

1. **Offensive Approach**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt to persuade offends or alienates the target, causing them to become uncooperative.
    - **Description**: The character’s words have the opposite effect, creating hostility.
    - **Mechanic**: The target becomes uncooperative or hostile.
2. **Misunderstood Intent**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s persuasive attempt is misunderstood, leading to confusion or mistrust.
    - **Description**: The character’s intentions are misinterpreted, causing problems.
    - **Mechanic**: The target misinterprets the character’s words, leading to negative consequences.

**Religion**:

1. **Blasphemous Error**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s religious actions or words are deemed blasphemous, angering nearby believers.
    - **Description**: The character’s mistake causes religious offense, leading to conflict.
    - **Mechanic**: The character faces hostility or punishment from offended believers.
2. **Failed Prayer**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s prayer or ritual fails, resulting in no divine intervention or aid.
    - **Description**: The character’s connection to the divine falters, causing their prayer to go unanswered.
    - **Mechanic**: The character’s prayer or ritual has no effect.

**Sleight of Hand**:

1. **Caught in the Act**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt at sleight of hand is noticed, leading to immediate consequences.
    - **Description**: The character’s actions are seen, causing trouble.
    - **Mechanic**: The character is caught and must face the consequences, such as being accused of theft.
2. **Fumbled Attempt**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt at sleight of hand fails, causing them to drop or mishandle the item.
    - **Description**: The character’s dexterity fails, leading to mistakes.
    - **Mechanic**: The character drops the item or fails to achieve their goal.

**Stealth**:

1. **Noise**:
    - **Effect**: The character makes a noise, alerting nearby enemies to their presence.
    - **Description**: The character’s attempt at stealth is ruined by an unexpected sound.
    - **Mechanic**: Nearby enemies are alerted to the character’s presence.
2. **Tripped**:
    - **Effect**: The character trips and falls, causing noise and losing their stealth advantage.
    - **Description**: The character’s movement fails, causing them to stumble.
    - **Mechanic**: The character is knocked prone and loses their stealth advantage.

**Survival**:

1. **Misguided Path**:
    - **Effect**: The character leads the party in the wrong direction, wasting time and resources.
    - **Description**: The character’s sense of direction fails, leading to mistakes.
    - **Mechanic**: The party is delayed or led into a dangerous area.
2. **Spoiled Supplies**:
    - **Effect**: The character’s attempt to find food or water results in spoiled or poisonous supplies.
    - **Description**: The character’s survival skills fail, leading to harmful consequences.
    - **Mechanic**: The party’s supplies are spoiled or poisoned, causing potential harm.
