# Cleave, Flanking, and Crit Surge

### Cleaving Through Creatures

If you reduce an enemy to 0 HP with a melee attack, any excess damage can carry over to another enemy within reach, allowing you to cleave through multiple foes in a single attack, adding a dynamic and cinematic feel to combat.

**Flanking**: Flanking an enemy gives you advantage on your attack rolls unless the enemy has exceptionally high Perception.

**Crit Surge**: When you score a natural 20 on an attack, you gain an extra action that can be used immediately.
