# The Rusty Anchor

Leader: Captain Finnegan Greybeard (../../NPCs/Captain%20Finnegan%20Greybeard%20960353a74d1e4a74b45b9964f683bf91.md)

**The Rusty Anchor:**

- **Description:** This rough-and-tumble crew, comprised of former sailors and pirates, has carved a lucrative path in smuggling. They specialize in bringing in and out contraband goods, including outlawed magic and rare alchemical substances. Their willingness to take on any job, regardless of the risk, coupled with their boisterous nature, makes them a notorious presence in the city's underworld.
- **Leader:** Captain Finnegan Greybeard, a dwarf rogue with a booming voice and an unwavering sense of loyalty to his crew.
- **Legendary Ability:** Iron Will - Finnegan has an unbreakable willpower, allowing him to resist mind control and other forms of mental manipulation.
