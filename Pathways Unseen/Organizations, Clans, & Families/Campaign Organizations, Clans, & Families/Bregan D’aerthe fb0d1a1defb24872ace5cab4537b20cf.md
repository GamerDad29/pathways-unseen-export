### Bregan D'aerthe: A Millennium of Shadows

### The Rise and Transformation of Bregan D'aerthe

Bregan D'aerthe has evolved from a ragtag band of male Drow outcasts into one of the most formidable clandestine organizations in the Underdark—and beyond. The rise of Bregan D'aerthe is a tale of cunning, adaptability, and a relentless pursuit of power, driven by the enigmatic Jarlaxle Baenre, whose influence has only grown over the centuries.

### The Shadow War and the Underdark's Unification

A thousand years ago, Bregan D'aerthe played a pivotal role in the Shadow War, a conflict that reshaped the very landscape of the Underdark. With the fall of many noble houses and the fracturing of traditional Drow society, Bregan D'aerthe emerged as a stabilizing force, albeit one driven by its own ambitions. Through careful manipulation, alliances, and covert operations, Jarlaxle ensured that his organization not only survived but thrived amidst the chaos.

In the aftermath of the war, Bregan D'aerthe extended its influence far beyond Menzoberranzan, becoming a key player in the unification of the Underdark. By forging alliances with other powerful factions, including deep gnome enclaves and duergar strongholds, Bregan D'aerthe created a network of power that spanned the entire Underdark, facilitating trade, diplomacy, and, of course, subterfuge.

### The Expansion to the Surface

As the Underdark grew more unified and the surface world became increasingly embroiled in its own conflicts, Bregan D'aerthe seized the opportunity to expand its operations above ground. Jarlaxle’s agents infiltrated surface cities, establishing cells that manipulated trade, politics, and even wars to their advantage. The organization’s mastery of subterfuge allowed it to control key smuggling routes, black markets, and mercenary companies, all while maintaining a facade of legitimacy.

### The Draconic Transformation of Jarlaxle

In a twist that no one could have foreseen, Jarlaxle Baenre's power was further amplified through his association with a powerful coven of bronze dragons. What began as a courtship between Jarlaxle and these dragons evolved into something far more significant: Jarlaxle was imbued with the blood of these mighty creatures, granting him extended life, draconic abilities, and the strength of a true dragon. This transformation has kept Jarlaxle youthful and more formidable than ever.

Jarlaxle's offspring, born of his union with the bronze dragons, are half-drow, half-dragon hybrids. These children have inherited both their father's cunning and their mother's draconic power, making them key figures in the future of Bregan D'aerthe. Each possesses unique abilities that blend the shadowy arts of the Drow with the raw elemental power of the dragons, making them fearsome allies—or enemies—in the Underdark's ever-shifting political landscape.

### The Dragon-Blooded Leadership

With Jarlaxle's transformation and the rise of his dragon-blooded offspring, Bregan D'aerthe's leadership has become even more formidable. The organization now operates with a dual focus: maintaining and expanding its power in the Underdark while leveraging its draconic connections to exert influence over the surface world.

Jarlaxle's children, each imbued with different aspects of draconic power, have taken on key roles within the organization. These half-drow, half-dragon leaders command respect and fear, their abilities unmatched by any who would challenge Bregan D'aerthe. They act as enforcers, diplomats, and strategists, ensuring that their father's legacy continues to grow.

### The Eternal Game

For Jarlaxle, life is a game—a game of power, manipulation, and survival. With his newfound draconic abilities and his ever-expanding influence, he plays this game on a scale that few can comprehend. Every alliance, every betrayal, every carefully orchestrated conflict serves a purpose in Jarlaxle's grand design.

Bregan D'aerthe’s operations have become increasingly complex, involving not just the usual subterfuge and mercenary work but also magical research, draconic rituals, and the pursuit of ancient artifacts. Jarlaxle’s long-term goals remain inscrutable, but what is clear is that he is playing a game that spans centuries, with moves and countermoves that reach across both the Underdark and the surface world.

### The Legacy of Bregan D'aerthe

As Bregan D'aerthe moves into its second millennium, it is more powerful than ever. The organization’s reach is vast, its agents are embedded in every corner of the world, and its leadership is unmatched in cunning and strength. Jarlaxle’s legacy is one of adaptability and survival, and under his leadership, Bregan D'aerthe continues to shape the destiny of both the Underdark and the surface world.

For those who dare to challenge Bregan D'aerthe, the consequences are dire. The organization has become a force of nature, a shadow that looms over every political intrigue, every war, and every alliance. In a world where the strong survive and the cunning thrive, Bregan D'aerthe stands as a testament to the enduring power of those who master the game of shadows.

### Bregan D'aerthe: A Millennium of Shadows and Strategy

### Timeline of Bregan D'aerthe’s Evolution

**Year 0-100: The Founding of Bregan D'aerthe**

- **Year 0:** Jarlaxle Baenre, a charismatic and cunning male Drow, founds Bregan D'aerthe in the chaotic aftermath of a noble house’s fall in Menzoberranzan. Initially, the organization is a haven for male Drow outcasts and mercenaries who seek purpose and power in a matriarchal society.
- **Year 50:** The organization expands its operations, moving from small-time mercenary work to more complex jobs involving espionage, sabotage, and manipulation of rival houses. Bregan D'aerthe begins to establish itself as a force to be reckoned with in the political machinations of the Underdark.
- **Year 100:** Jarlaxle solidifies his leadership, using his charisma and strategic brilliance to keep the organization united and focused on growth. The group’s reputation grows as a powerful and reliable, albeit shadowy, organization in Menzoberranzan.

**Year 101-300: The Shadow War and Expansion**

- **Year 120:** Bregan D'aerthe plays a critical role in the Shadow War, a conflict that engulfs the Underdark as rival houses vie for dominance. Jarlaxle’s agents infiltrate noble houses, sowing discord and manipulating events to ensure Bregan D'aerthe’s survival and growth.
- **Year 200:** As the Shadow War reaches its climax, many noble houses fall, leaving a power vacuum in the Underdark. Bregan D'aerthe seizes the opportunity to expand its influence, forming alliances with deep gnome enclaves, duergar strongholds, and other factions to create a network that spans the Underdark.
- **Year 300:** With the Underdark now more unified under a delicate balance of power, Bregan D'aerthe shifts its focus to the surface world. Jarlaxle begins sending agents above ground to infiltrate surface cities, establishing cells that manipulate trade, politics, and warfare for the organization’s benefit.

**Year 301-500: The Silence of the Gods and the Draconic Awakening**

- **Year 350:** The gods of the Underdark, including Lolth, go mysteriously silent, leaving their followers in disarray. Bregan D'aerthe, ever adaptable, uses this chaos to further its own agenda, positioning itself as a stabilizing force in a time of religious uncertainty.
- **Year 400:** Jarlaxle, ever the opportunist, begins courting a coven of bronze dragons, forging a bond that would ultimately change the course of his life and the future of Bregan D'aerthe. Through these dragons, Jarlaxle gains knowledge, power, and eventually, draconic blood.
- **Year 450:** Jarlaxle undergoes a transformation, becoming imbued with the blood of the bronze dragons. This grants him extended life, draconic abilities, and a renewed sense of purpose. His offspring, born of these dragons, are half-drow, half-dragon hybrids who take on key roles within Bregan D'aerthe.

**Year 501-700: Consolidation and Power Plays**

- **Year 500:** With Jarlaxle now possessing draconic power, Bregan D'aerthe’s influence reaches new heights. The organization consolidates its power both in the Underdark and on the surface, controlling key trade routes, black markets, and mercenary groups.
- **Year 600:** Bregan D'aerthe becomes involved in arcane research and the pursuit of ancient artifacts, using its newfound draconic connections to unlock forgotten magic and expand its capabilities. The organization’s operations become increasingly complex, involving not just traditional mercenary work but also magical rituals and draconic studies.
- **Year 650:** The children of Jarlaxle and the bronze dragons, now fully grown and trained, assume leadership roles within Bregan D'aerthe. Each wields a unique blend of drow cunning and draconic power, ensuring the organization’s dominance in both the Underdark and the surface world.

**Year 701-900: The Long Game and the Reawakening**

- **Year 700:** The silence of the gods begins to lift, with whispers of their return spreading through the Underdark. Bregan D'aerthe, having weathered the storm of divine absence, finds itself in a position of unparalleled strength, ready to capitalize on the shifting religious landscape.
- **Year 800:** Jarlaxle’s long game becomes more apparent as he maneuvers Bregan D'aerthe into positions of power across multiple planes of existence. The organization’s influence now extends to the far reaches of the multiverse, with agents embedded in key locations across different realms.
- **Year 850:** The first signs of a new threat emerge in the form of the Shadow Plague, a mysterious force that begins to spread through the Underdark. Bregan D'aerthe, ever pragmatic, adapts its strategies to combat this new menace, using its extensive network to gather intelligence and counter the plague’s spread.

**Year 901-1000: The Modern Era and Jarlaxle’s Legacy**

- **Year 900:** As the millennium draws to a close, Bregan D'aerthe stands as one of the most powerful organizations in existence. Jarlaxle, still youthful and vigorous thanks to his draconic blood, continues to lead with a steady hand, his ambitions now spanning centuries and realms.
- **Year 950:** The Shadow Plague reaches its peak, threatening to engulf the entire Underdark. Bregan D'aerthe’s agents, guided by Jarlaxle’s strategic brilliance, play a crucial role in containing the plague and preventing it from spreading to the surface world.
- **Year 1000:** In the present day, Bregan D'aerthe is more than just a mercenary group—it is a shadow government, an arcane powerhouse, and a draconic dynasty all rolled into one. Jarlaxle’s legacy is one of adaptability, survival, and relentless ambition, with his children poised to carry on his work in the centuries to come.

**Centralized Command:**

- **Jarlaxle Baenre:** The ageless and enigmatic leader of Bregan D'aerthe, now a being of both drow and draconic heritage. His word is law, and his strategic mind ensures that Bregan D'aerthe remains at the forefront of every major event in the Underdark and beyond.

**Regional Cells:**

- **Karnuk - Captain Jevan Alderleaf:** A cunning and charismatic leader who controls Bregan D'aerthe’s operations in Karnuk. Jevan is a master of manipulation and subterfuge, ensuring that the organization’s influence permeates every aspect of the city.

- **Ulfgar - Captain Voryn Shadowcloak:** The formidable and tactical leader of Bregan D'aerthe in Ulfgar. Voryn is a veteran of countless conflicts, his experience and arcane prowess making him a fearsome presence in the Underdark.

**The Dragon-Blooded Children:**

- **Vyrlas Baenre:** The eldest of Jarlaxle’s dragon-blooded offspring, Vyrlas wields the power of the bronze dragons with the cunning of the drow. He is a key strategist within Bregan D'aerthe, responsible for expanding the organization’s influence into new realms.
- **Serylda Baenre:** The only daughter of Jarlaxle and a bronze dragon, Serylda is a master diplomat and manipulator. She uses her draconic charm and drow cunning to forge alliances and navigate the complex political landscape of the Underdark and surface world.
- **Kaelrath Baenre:** The youngest of Jarlaxle’s children, Kaelrath is a formidable warrior who combines the physical prowess of a dragon with the agility and precision of a drow. He serves as an enforcer for Bregan D'aerthe, ensuring that those who cross the organization face swift retribution.
