# Stonecutter's Guild (Multi-city)

General Info: Engineering, Mining, Stonework
Specialization: Engineering, Rare Minerals, Tunneling
Leader: Hulgar Stonecutter (../../NPCs/Hulgar%20Stonecutter%208cb6054dbdd34f43a95ad666df2be893.md), Throgmar Stonehammer (../../NPCs/Throgmar%20Stonehammer%207a88f8dcb78e448da4992e32deb95cec.md)
Primary Location of Operation: Karnuk: A City Forged in Strength (../../Locations/Karnuk%20A%20City%20Forged%20in%20Strength%20c3cece9d17264ac1833848b8a274116e.md), Ulfgar: The Underground Jewel (../../Locations/Ulfgar%20The%20Underground%20Jewel%2069f9ad15c2064e1789ab458fada6bed8.md)

**Stonecutter's Guild:** Headed by the dwarf master craftsman Hulgar Stonecutter, this guild is responsible for constructing and maintaining Karnuk's buildings and fortifications. Their expertise in stone and metal ensures the city's enduring strength.

- **Expertise:** Stonework, mining, and engineering.
- **Role:** This guild forms the backbone of Ulfgar's mining operations. Their skilled laborers and engineers are responsible for:
    - Extracting valuable resources from the depths.
    - Maintaining the intricate network of tunnels and chambers, ensuring the safety and stability of the city's infrastructure.
    - Crafting exquisite stone and metal goods, renowned for their quality and artistry. These handcrafted pieces are highly sought after throughout the region, further bolstering the city's economy.
- **Leader:** **Throgmar Stonehammer:** A dwarf with a salt-and-pepper beard, Throgmar is a skilled stonemason and engineer with decades of experience in mining operations. His sharp mind and strategic thinking have earned him the respect of his peers. He is known for his fair and just leadership, ensuring the guild operates smoothly and efficiently.
