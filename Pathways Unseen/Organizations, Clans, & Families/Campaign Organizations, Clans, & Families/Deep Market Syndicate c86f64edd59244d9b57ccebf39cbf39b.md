## Deep Market Syndicate

### NPC Leader:

**Vexia Nightshade**, a tiefling rogue with a shadowy reputation and a talent for smuggling contraband.

### Background:

The Deep Market Syndicate operates in the shadows, specializing in illicit goods, black market trade, and clandestine transactions. Led by Vexia Nightshade, the Syndicate has a network of spies, smugglers, and informants who operate in both the surface and the Underdark.

### GM Playing Style:

Vexia is a mysterious and elusive figure who prefers to operate behind the scenes. The GM should portray her as cunning and deceptive, with a penchant for secrecy and subterfuge. Vexia is a master of disguise and deception, capable of blending into any environment and manipulating others to further the Syndicate's interests. She trusts few and keeps her true motives hidden, always planning several steps ahead to outmaneuver her rivals.
