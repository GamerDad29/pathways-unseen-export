## Underdark Merchant Alliance

### NPC Leader:

**Zilrax Shadowspire**, a cunning drow merchant with a network of contacts throughout the Underdark.

### Background:

The Underdark Merchant Alliance was formed by Zilrax Shadowspire and other enterprising individuals seeking to establish a monopoly on the trade routes between the Underdark and the surface. They specialize in rare minerals, exotic flora, and magical artifacts harvested from the depths below.

### GM Playing Style:

Zilrax is a charismatic and shrewd negotiator who always seeks to maximize profits for the Alliance. The GM should portray him as confident and calculating, with a silver tongue that can persuade even the most skeptical traders. Zilrax is not afraid to resort to intimidation or subterfuge to maintain the Alliance's dominance in the market.
