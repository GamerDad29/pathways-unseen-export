**Shadow Scale Clan:** Led by the cunning goblin rogue Zara Nightshade, this guild comprises skilled rogues and assassins. Their expertise lies in stealth and agility, making them the go-to choice for security and protection services within the city. They are often hired for covert operations, eliminating threats that endanger Zarakzul's peace and stability.
