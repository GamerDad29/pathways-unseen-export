**Shaping the Future:**

- **Iron Gears:** Led by the ingenious kobold Brik Ironwheel, this engineering guild is responsible for crafting machines and devices that serve diverse purposes, including mining and transportation. Their reputation for exceptional mechanical skills and innovative spirit has led to the development of numerous advanced technologies that are utilized throughout the Underdark.
