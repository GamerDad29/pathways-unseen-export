# **Iron Fist Guild:** Led by the fearsome hobgoblin warrior Grommash Ironfist, this guild boasts skilled mercenaries and sellswords. They are the go-to choice for those seeking bodyguards or soldiers-for-hire, their reputation extending beyond Karnuk's walls.
