# The Twisted Court

Leader: Seraphine Moonwhisper (../../NPCs/Seraphine%20Moonwhisper%20fdfd094a02df4aa89be54ca56fa9c4f0.md)

**The Twisted Court:**

- **Description:** Shrouded in secrecy, this cabal of illusionists and mind manipulators controls the strings of power from the shadows. Through mesmerizing "performances" for the city's elite, they weave subtle suggestions and exert control over the minds of the influential. Membership is highly selective, and their operations unfold within a network of hidden chambers and clandestine meetings.
- **Leader:** Seraphine Moonwhisper, a drow sorcerer with an alluring presence and a mastery of the arcane arts.
- **Legendary Ability:** Mind Control - Seraphine can completely control the minds of her enemies, forcing them to do her bidding without question.
