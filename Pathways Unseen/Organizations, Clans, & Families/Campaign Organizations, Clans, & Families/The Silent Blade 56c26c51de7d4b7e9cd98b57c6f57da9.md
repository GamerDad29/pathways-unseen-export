**The Silent Blade:**

- **Description:** En shrouded in secrecy, these elite assassins operate with unmatched efficiency and lethality. Their services are available to the highest bidder, yet rumors whisper of a code of honor, ensuring they only accept contracts deemed morally justifiable. Their swift execution of assignments leaves them shrouded in an aura of mystery and dread.
- **Leader:** Kaelin Shadowbane, a human assassin known for his lethal precision and ability to vanish into thin air.
- **Legendary Ability:** Invisibility - Kaelin can turn invisible at will, allowing him to move around undetected and strike from the shadows.
