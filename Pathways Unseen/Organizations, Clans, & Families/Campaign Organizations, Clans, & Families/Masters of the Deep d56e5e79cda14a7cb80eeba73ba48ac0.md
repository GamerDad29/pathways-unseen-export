Primary Location of Operation: Zarakzul: A Jewel in the Underdark (../../Locations/Zarakzul%20A%20Jewel%20in%20the%20Underdark%203737ae62b15a4c48b06fe231c0aa7447.md)

**Masters of the Deep:**

- **Deepwater Fishers:** Headed by the experienced kobold Grik Stonefin, this guild specializes in fishing and aquaculture within the city's lifeblood, the underground lake. Their adept fishing techniques ensure a steady supply of fresh fish for Zarakzul's inhabitants. They are also known for their surplus, which they trade with other Underdark settlements, further strengthening the city's economic reach.
