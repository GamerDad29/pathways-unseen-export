# The Velvet Syndicate

Leader: Isadora Nightshade (../../NPCs/Isadora%20Nightshade%2056300a13db1a4a09b13a2ca8ff63351c.md)

**The Velvet Syndicate:**

- **Description:** Sophisticated thieves known for their elegance and non-lethal methods. Operating with a code of honor, they target the city's elite, leaving victims bewildered by the audacity of their stylish heists. Black-tie attire and meticulous planning are hallmarks of this enigmatic group.
- **Leader:** Isadora Nightshade, a half-elf rogue with a mysterious past and a cunning mind.
- **Legendary Ability:** Shadowstep - Isadora can teleport to any shadow within a 100-foot radius, allowing her to quickly move around and escape danger.
