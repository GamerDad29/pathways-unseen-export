**The Iron Circle:**

- **Description:** Former slaves forged in the fires of oppression, this band of fierce fighters navigates the city's subterranean labyrinth. Striking at the heart of Vragathul's slave trade, they protect the vulnerable with unwavering determination. Their expertise in combat and unwavering resolve make them a formidable force for justice.
- **Leader:** Garruk Blackmane, a half-orc fighter renowned for his unwavering courage and immense strength.
- **Legendary Ability:** Indomitable Might - Garruk's strength knows no bounds, allowing him to lift massive objects and overpower his enemies.
