# Deeper than Stone

Leader: Iron Lord (../../NPCs/Iron%20Lord%20f405e8a5d8b946f2ad2a3c37a04c0494.md)

**Brotherhood of the Iron Mine:** Led by the enigmatic "Iron Lord", this guild comprises duergar miners and smiths. They manage the vast iron mines beneath Karnuk, supplying the city and beyond with raw materials, while their skilled smiths forge exceptional weapons and armor.
