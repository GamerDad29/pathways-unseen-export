# The Steel Syndicate

Leader: Kethar Steelheart (../../NPCs/Kethar%20Steelheart%20d38fde5d77f44db5944a64b4901a3b26.md)

- **Leader:** Kethar Steelheart, a stern and calculating bugbear entrepreneur.
- **Specialization:** Metalworking and trade, emphasizing innovation and efficiency.
- **Unique Aspect:** Led by Kethar, this group operates more like a business than a traditional guild. They are known for pioneering new metalworking techniques and aggressively expanding their reach within the Underdark.
