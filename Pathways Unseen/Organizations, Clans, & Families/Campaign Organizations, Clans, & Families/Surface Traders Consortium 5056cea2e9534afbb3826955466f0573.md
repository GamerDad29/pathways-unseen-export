# Surface Traders Consortium

**Surface Traders Consortium**

- **NPC Leader:** Aric Silverthorn, a human merchant prince known for his wealth, influence, and connections in the surface world.
- **Background:** The Surface Traders Consortium was established by Aric Silverthorn and other influential merchants from the surface realms. They specialize in luxury goods, fine craftsmanship, and exotic commodities sourced from the surface world.
- **GM Playing Style:** Aric is a suave and sophisticated businessman who exudes confidence and authority. The GM should portray him as charming and persuasive, with a knack for striking lucrative deals and expanding the Consortium's influence. Aric values loyalty and expects unwavering dedication from his associates, but he can be ruthless when crossed.
