# Emberweave Guild

Leader: Eryndoriel (../../NPCs/Eryndoriel%209e2ac937929c4988935e3cb16747bcda.md)

**Emberweave Guild:** Headed by the elven master weaver Eryndoriel, this guild is renowned for crafting exquisite textiles and garments. Their high-quality fabrics, often dyed with unique techniques, are coveted by the nobility of Vragathul and beyond.
