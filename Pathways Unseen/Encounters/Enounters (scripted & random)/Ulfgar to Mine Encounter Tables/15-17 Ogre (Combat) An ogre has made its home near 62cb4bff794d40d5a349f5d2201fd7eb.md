# 15-17. Ogre (Combat): An ogre has made its home near the road, attacking anyone who comes near. (1 Ogre)

Here’s the Ogre encounter formatted:

---

## 🪓 **Ogre Ambush: The Brute's Lair** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Ogre's DenA massive ogre has claimed a section of the road as its territory, attacking any who dare to venture close.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Narrow Mountain PassThe encounter occurs in a narrow mountain pass where the ogre has set up a rudimentary lair. The towering rocks and boulders limit movement and visibility.*
- **Weather:** *OvercastDark clouds loom overhead, casting deep shadows across the terrain, making the ogre's ambush more effective.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **1 Ogre** (AC 11, HP 59, Greatclub: +6 to hit, 2d8+4 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Ogre: AC 11, HP 59, Brutal Swing: The ogre can make a powerful attack that targets all creatures in a 10-foot line.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat the ogre and clear the roadThe party must confront the ogre, who sees them as intruders in its territory. The adventurers must eliminate the threat to continue their journey.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🪓 **1 =** The ogre throws a large boulder as a ranged attack.
🛡️ **2 =** The ogre is guarding a small treasure hoard in its lair.
🌩️ **3 =** A sudden rockslide makes parts of the terrain difficult to navigate.
🍖 **4 =** The ogre has recently captured prey, and a frightened creature might be discovered in its lair.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *450 XP for defeating the ogre*
- **Loot:** *20 GP, a piece of armor or weapon from a previous victim, and a healing potionThe party finds a small stash of loot in the ogre’s lair, including coins and a useful item.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is particularly strong, consider adding a few goblin minions that the ogre has enslaved to fight alongside it.*
