# 11-13. Giant Spiders (Combat): The party stumbles upon a nest of giant spiders blocking the path. (2 Giant Spiders)

---

## 🕸️ **Webbed Ambush: Giant Spider Nest** 🕷️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Webbed AmbushA perilous encounter with giant spiders lurking in their web-covered nest.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Dark CavernThe party stumbles into a cavern filled with thick webs, the walls glistening with moisture. The air is damp and the floor uneven, littered with bones and debris.*
- **Weather:** *N/AThe cavern is enclosed, with no external weather conditions affecting the encounter.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **2 Giant Spiders** (AC 14, HP 26, Bite: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing damage + poison)
- **Creature Stats:***Giant Spiders: AC 14, HP 26, Bite attack: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing damage + 2d8 poison damage (DC 11 Constitution save for half damage).*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Survive the ambush and clear the pathThe spiders are blocking the only way forward, and the party must eliminate them to continue their journey.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🕷️ **1 =** A third spider descends from above, joining the fray.
🕸️ **2 =** The webs catch fire, creating difficult terrain and dealing 1d4 fire damage per round to anyone within.
⚠️ **3 =** A hidden pit trap is revealed beneath a thin layer of webbing (DC 13 Dexterity save to avoid falling).
💀 **4 =** The remains of a previous victim are found, yielding a small treasure (25 GP and a Potion of Spider Climb).

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *200 XP per spider*
- **Loot:** *25 GP, Potion of Spider Climb*
- *After defeating the spiders, the party finds a small amount of gold and a potion among the remains of a previous victim.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is particularly strong or large, add a third giant spider to the encounter.*
