# 11-13. Wild Boar (Combat): A wild boar charges at the party, defending its territory. (1 Wild Boar)

---

## 🐗 **Wild Boar: The Territorial Charge** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Wild Boar ChargeA large, aggressive wild boar, fiercely protective of its territory, charges at the party with intent to drive them away.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Forest ClearingThe encounter takes place in a dense forest clearing, with tall grass and underbrush obscuring the ground. The boar charges through the foliage, catching the party off guard.*
- **Weather:** *OvercastThe sky is overcast, casting a dim light over the clearing. The air is thick with the smell of earth and wild plants.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **1 Wild Boar** (AC 12, HP 11, Tusk Attack: +3 to hit, 1d6+1 slashing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Wild Boar: AC 12, HP 11, Charge: If the boar moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits with a tusk attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 1d6 slashing damage.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defend against the boar's chargeThe wild boar sees the party as intruders in its territory and charges at them, intent on driving them away. The party must defend themselves or find a way to avoid the boar's wrath.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🌳 **1 =** The boar crashes into a tree, stunning itself for a round.
🩸 **2 =** The boar is more aggressive than usual, gaining a temporary bonus to damage.
🌾 **3 =** The boar’s charge kicks up a cloud of dust and leaves, reducing visibility for a round.
🪶 **4 =** A flock of startled birds takes flight, potentially distracting the party or the boar.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *50 XP for the wild boar*
- **Loot:** *Boar Hide (can be crafted into leather), Tusks (valuable to a merchant for 5 GP each)The boar’s hide and tusks can be taken as trophies or sold for a modest sum.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is larger or more powerful, add another wild boar or increase the boar’s hit points to 20.*
