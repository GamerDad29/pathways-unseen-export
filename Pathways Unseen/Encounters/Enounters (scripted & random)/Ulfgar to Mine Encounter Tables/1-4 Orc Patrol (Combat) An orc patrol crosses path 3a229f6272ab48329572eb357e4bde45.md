# 1-4. Orc Patrol (Combat): An orc patrol crosses paths with the party, mistaking them for enemies. (3 Orcs)

Here’s the Orc Patrol encounter formatted:

---

## 🪓 **Orc Patrol: Unwanted Attention** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Orc PatrolA band of orc warriors, on high alert, mistakes the party for intruders in their territory.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Rocky PassThe encounter takes place in a narrow, rocky pass with steep cliffs on either side. The terrain is uneven, with loose stones underfoot, making sudden movements hazardous.*
- **Weather:** *Overcast with Drizzling RainThe sky is dark and cloudy, with a light drizzle making the ground slippery and the orcs' approach harder to detect.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **3 Orcs** (AC 13, HP 15, Greataxe: +5 to hit, 1d12+3 slashing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Orcs: AC 13, HP 15, Aggressive: As a bonus action, the orc can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defend against the orc attackThe orc patrol, misunderstanding the party’s intentions, charges with weapons drawn. The party must defend themselves or find a way to de-escalate the situation.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🪓 **1 =** A hidden orc archer reveals themselves, firing from a concealed position.
🧱 **2 =** The unstable cliffside begins to crumble, threatening to trap or harm those below.
🛡️ **3 =** One orc carries a banner of a nearby warlord, hinting at a larger force in the area.
🗣️ **4 =** The orcs hesitate, recognizing a symbol or item carried by the party, possibly leading to a tense negotiation.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *100 XP per orc*
- **Loot:** *10 GP, Orcish War Paint (provides +1 to Intimidation checks when applied)The orcs carry minimal loot, but their war paint and weapons might be of interest to the party.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is larger or more powerful, add 2 more orcs or introduce an orc leader with higher stats (AC 15, HP 30, Greataxe: +6 to hit, 1d12+4 slashing damage).*
