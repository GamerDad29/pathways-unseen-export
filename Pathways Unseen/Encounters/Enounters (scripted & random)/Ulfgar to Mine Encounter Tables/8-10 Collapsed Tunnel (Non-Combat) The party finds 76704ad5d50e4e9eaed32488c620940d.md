# 8-10. Collapsed Tunnel (Non-Combat): The party finds the entrance to the mine partially collapsed, requiring effort or magic to clear.

---

## ⛏️ **Collapsed Tunnel (Non-Combat)** 🚧

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Collapsed TunnelThe party finds the entrance to the mine partially collapsed, blocking their way forward.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Mine EntranceThe encounter takes place at the entrance to the mine, where a recent collapse has blocked the main passage. The area is dark and dusty, with loose rocks and debris scattered around.*
- **Weather:** *UndergroundThe air is heavy and still, with the distant sound of dripping water echoing through the tunnels.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **None***This encounter focuses on the environment and the party's ability to navigate the obstacle.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"This passage is blocked... We’ll need to find a way through or around it."Encourage the party to discuss their options and consider different approaches.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Clear the collapsed tunnel or find an alternative routeThe party needs to decide how they will handle the collapsed tunnel, whether by clearing it, using magic, or searching for another way into the mine.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Physical Challenge:** *Use Strength checks to move the larger debris and clear the tunnelCharacters might need to make Strength (Athletics) checks to shift the heavier rocks blocking the tunnel.*
- **Magical Solution:** *Use spells like Mold Earth or Dispel Magic to remove or stabilize the debrisCharacters with appropriate spells could use magic to solve the problem more efficiently.*
- **Exploration Challenge:** *Search the surrounding area for another entrance to the mineA successful Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Perception) check might reveal a hidden or less obvious entrance.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The tunnel shift, creating a new opening or causing more debris to fall
2️⃣ **2 =** The party finds a hidden passage or shortcut while searching for an alternative route
3️⃣ **3 =** The noise of clearing the tunnel attracts a wandering creature or miner's ghost
4️⃣ **4 =** The tunnel collapse reveals a hidden cache of forgotten supplies or equipment

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Rewards:** *Clearing the tunnel grants access to the mine and possibly uncovers hidden items or additional information*
- **Consequences:** *Failing to clear the tunnel might force the party to take a longer or more dangerous route, delaying their progressThe outcome of this encounter could affect the party’s journey and their ability to reach the mine's depths quickly.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party’s abilities.If the party is particularly strong or has the right spells, consider adding environmental hazards or a time constraint to increase the challenge.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Tense and ClaustrophobicThe encounter should convey a sense of urgency and tension, with the party feeling the weight of the earth pressing down on them.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The scent of damp earth and the sound of rocks shifting underfoot*
    - *The dim light filtering through the dust-filled air, making it difficult to see clearlyDetails that enhance the feeling of being trapped or confined in a dangerous environment.*

---

This encounter challenges the party to think creatively and work together to overcome a physical obstacle, with the potential for consequences based on their approach.
