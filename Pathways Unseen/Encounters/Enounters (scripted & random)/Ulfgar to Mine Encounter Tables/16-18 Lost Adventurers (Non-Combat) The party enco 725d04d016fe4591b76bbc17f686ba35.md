# 16-18. Lost Adventurers (Non-Combat): The party encounters a group of lost adventurers who ask for directions or assistance.

---

## 🧭 **Lost Adventurers (Non-Combat)** 🌲

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Lost AdventurersThe party encounters a group of lost adventurers who are disoriented and in need of assistance.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Forest ClearingThe encounter takes place in a small clearing within a dense forest. The trees loom overhead, their branches tangled, creating a canopy that filters the light, giving the area a dim and shadowy appearance.*
- **Weather:** *OvercastThe sky is gray, with clouds threatening rain, adding to the disoriented state of the lost adventurers.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **Captain Haldor** (A grizzled veteran, clearly frustrated and embarrassed about being lost)
    - **Elara** (A young and eager scout, nervous but trying to stay optimistic)
    - **Thorn** (A stoic dwarf warrior, quietly annoyed but focused on finding the way)
    *Each NPC has their own demeanor, which can influence how the party interacts with them.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"I can’t believe we’re lost... Do you know the way to the nearest village?"*
    - *"We’ve been wandering for hours. If you could point us in the right direction, we’d be in your debt."These lines introduce the NPCs' predicament and their need for help.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Provide directions or assistance to the lost adventurersThe party should decide whether to give the adventurers directions, offer to escort them, or perhaps use the situation to their advantage.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Social Challenge:** *Convince the adventurers to trust the party’s guidanceCharacters might need to make Charisma (Persuasion) checks to gain the adventurers' trust, especially if the NPCs are wary or suspicious.*
- **Survival Challenge:** *Determine the correct direction using the party's knowledge of the areaA Wisdom (Survival) check might be required to accurately guide the adventurers back on track.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The adventurers reveal they’re being pursued by a dangerous creature
2️⃣ **2 =** One of the adventurers mentions a hidden treasure they discovered but couldn’t reach
3️⃣ **3 =** The adventurers are not lost at all, but instead are testing the party for a hidden purpose
4️⃣ **4 =** The adventurers offer to share some of their supplies or information in exchange for help

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Rewards:** *The adventurers may offer a small token of appreciation, such as a healing potion or a piece of useful information about the area ahead*
- **Consequences:** *If the party refuses to help or misguides them, the adventurers might spread negative rumors or become hostile in a future encounterHelping the adventurers could lead to future alliances or favors, while neglecting them might have unforeseen repercussions.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the complexity of the situation.Add a dangerous pursuit or increase the adventurers' desperation if the party is experienced or particularly skilled in survival.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Anxious and HopefulThe encounter should mix the tension of being lost with the hope that the party can help, creating a scenario that tests the party's willingness to assist others.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The crunch of leaves underfoot as the adventurers approach, their armor clinking softly*
    - *The smell of wet earth and the sound of distant rustling as animals move through the underbrushInclude details that make the forest feel alive and slightly mysterious.*

---

This encounter offers a chance for roleplaying and problem-solving, allowing the party to engage with NPCs in a way that can have lasting effects on their journey.
