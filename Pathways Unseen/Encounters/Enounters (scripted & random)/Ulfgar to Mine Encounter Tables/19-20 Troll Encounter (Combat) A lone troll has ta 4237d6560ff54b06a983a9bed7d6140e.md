# 19-20. Troll Encounter (Combat): A lone troll has taken up residence near the road and sees the party as its next meal. (1 Troll)

Here’s the Middledark/Underdark Troll Beast encounter formatted:

---

## 🧟 **Middledark Troll Beast: The Hungry Predator** 🪓

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Troll AmbushA fearsome troll, twisted by the darkness of the Underdark, has made its lair near the road. It’s starving and sees the party as an easy meal.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Cavernous RoadsideThe encounter takes place on a narrow road cutting through a large cavern. The walls are jagged, and the ground is uneven, littered with loose stones and small pools of stagnant water.*
- **Weather:** *Damp and HumidThe air is thick with moisture, and a foul stench lingers in the cavern, emanating from the troll’s lair nearby.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **1 Troll** (AC 15, HP 84, Regeneration: Regains 10 HP at the start of its turn unless it has taken acid or fire damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Troll: AC 15, HP 84, Multiattack: The troll makes three attacks—one with its bite and two with its claws. Bite: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage. Claw: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6+4) slashing damage.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat the troll and survive the encounterThe troll is hungry and relentless. The party must bring it down before it tears them apart.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🕳️ **1 =** The troll’s lair is discovered nearby, filled with bones and old treasures.
🔥 **2 =** A small underground fire pit nearby can be used to deal fire damage, preventing the troll’s regeneration.
🪨 **3 =** A section of the cavern wall is weak, and a successful Strength check can bring it down on the troll.
💀 **4 =** The troll is wearing a crude necklace of bones, among which a magical trinket is hidden.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *1800 XP for defeating the troll*
- **Loot:** *50 GP, 1 Potion of Greater Healing, Magical TrinketDefeating the troll rewards the party with its hoarded treasure and possibly a magical item.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is struggling, consider allowing environmental factors (like the fire pit) to play a more prominent role in the battle, or reduce the troll's HP slightly.*
