# 12-14. Goblin Raiders (Combat): A larger group of goblin raiders attempts to overwhelm the party. (5 Goblins)

---

## 🏹 **Goblin Raiders: Overwhelming Numbers** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Goblin RaidA larger group of goblin raiders, emboldened by their numbers, attempts to overwhelm the party with a surprise attack.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Rocky Outcropping in the MiddledarkThe encounter occurs in a rocky area filled with jagged boulders and narrow crevices. The goblins use the terrain to their advantage, darting in and out of cover.*
- **Weather:** *Stagnant AirThe air is heavy and still, adding a sense of foreboding as the goblins close in on the party.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **5 Goblins** (AC 15, HP 7, Shortsword: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Goblin: AC 15, HP 7, Nimble Escape: The goblin can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Survive the goblin ambush and drive them offThe goblins are intent on overwhelming the party with their numbers. The adventurers must hold their ground and repel the attackers.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🎯 **1 =** One goblin is a shaman and casts a spell to disorient the party.
🛡️ **2 =** A hidden goblin sniper provides cover fire from a distance.
🧨 **3 =** The goblins set up a rudimentary trap—a pitfall or snare—that the party must avoid.
💰 **4 =** The goblins are carrying stolen goods, including a small pouch of 10 GP and a crude map leading to their hideout.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *50 XP per goblin*
- **Loot:** *10 GP, Crude Map, 1 Healing PotionThe party finds some stolen goods on the goblins, including a map that could lead to further adventures.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is larger or more experienced, consider adding a goblin boss to lead the raid, making the encounter more challenging.*
