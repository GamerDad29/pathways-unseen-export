# 14-16. Hobgoblin Raiders (Combat): A group of hobgoblin raiders sets up an ambush on the road. (3 Hobgoblins)

---

## 🏹 **Hobgoblin Raiders: The Roadside Ambush** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Hobgoblin AmbushA cunning group of hobgoblin raiders has set up an ambush along the road, waiting to strike at unwary travelers.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *RoadsideThe encounter takes place on a narrow road flanked by dense woods. The hobgoblins have hidden themselves behind rocks and in the underbrush, ready to attack as the party approaches.*
- **Weather:** *OvercastThe sky is overcast, with a slight breeze rustling the leaves. The air is tense, as if nature itself is holding its breath for the impending conflict.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **3 Hobgoblins** (AC 18, HP 11, Longsword: +3 to hit, 1d8+1 slashing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Hobgoblin: AC 18 (chain mail, shield), HP 11, Martial Advantage: Once per turn, the hobgoblin can deal an extra 7 (2d6) damage to a creature it hits with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of the hobgoblin that isn't incapacitated.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Survive the ambush and defeat the hobgoblinsThe hobgoblins are intent on looting the party. The players must either fight off the raiders or find a way to escape the ambush.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🪓 **1 =** One hobgoblin carries a heavy crossbow and starts the encounter from a higher vantage point.
💥 **2 =** A trap is triggered as the party moves forward, dealing 1d6 piercing damage to the leading character.
🕸 **3 =** The hobgoblins have set up a makeshift barricade, limiting the party's movement.
🪤 **4 =** One hobgoblin drops a map leading to a hidden cache of supplies.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *100 XP per hobgoblin*
- **Loot:** *20 GP, 1 Potion of Healing, Map to hidden cacheDefeating the hobgoblins rewards the party with their loot and a potential lead to more treasure.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.Add another hobgoblin if the party is larger than 4 characters or lower the AC to 16 if the party is smaller.*
