# 19-20. Cave Bear (Combat): A cave bear charges at the party, protecting its den. (1 Cave Bear)

---

## 🐻 **Cave Bear Den: Guardian of the Shadows** 🪓

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Cave Bear ChargeA massive cave bear, disturbed from its slumber, emerges from the shadows of its den to protect its territory.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Narrow Cave PassageThe encounter takes place in a tight, rocky passage leading to the cave bear’s den. The ground is uneven, with loose stones and stalagmites that could hinder movement.*
- **Lighting:** *DarknessThe cave is pitch-black, save for any light sources the party brings. The bear’s growls echo ominously through the confined space.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **1 Cave Bear** (AC 12, HP 42, Multiattack: Bite +5 to hit, 1d8+4 damage; Claw +5 to hit, 2d6+4 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Cave Bear: AC 12, HP 42, Darkvision 60 ft. This powerful beast is fiercely territorial and will fight to the death to defend its home.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Survive the bear’s onslaught and either drive it away or defeat it.The party must decide whether to stand their ground and fight or try to escape the enraged creature.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🦴 **1 =** The bear’s cubs, hidden in the den, begin to cry out, distracting the bear and giving the party an opening.
🪨 **2 =** A sudden rockslide partially blocks the passage, creating difficult terrain.
🧳 **3 =** The bear’s den contains a small stash of bones and debris, including a long-forgotten adventurer’s pack.
🦟 **4 =** Swarms of bats are disturbed by the noise, causing chaos and disorientation.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *450 XP for the bear*
- **Loot:** *A well-preserved adventurer’s pack with a Potion of Healing, 10 GP, and a map showing a hidden path through the caves.If the party searches the den after the encounter, they may find these items among the remains and debris.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is smaller or struggling, the bear could be injured or distracted by its cubs, reducing its attack power.*
