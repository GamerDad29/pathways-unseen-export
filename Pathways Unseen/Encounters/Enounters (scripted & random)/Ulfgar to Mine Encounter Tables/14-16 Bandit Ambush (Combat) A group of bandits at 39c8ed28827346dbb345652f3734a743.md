# 14-16. Bandit Ambush (Combat): A group of bandits attempts to rob the party on the road. (4 Bandits)

Here’s the Bandit Ambush encounter formatted:

---

## 💰 **Highway Heist: Bandit Ambush** 🗡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Highway HeistA sudden and ruthless ambush by bandits on a lonely stretch of road.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Wooded PathwayThe party is traveling along a narrow, tree-lined road. The dense woods on either side provide ample cover for the bandits, who lie in wait.*
- **Weather:** *OvercastThe sky is gray, and the air is thick with tension. A light drizzle begins to fall, making the ground slick.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **4 Bandits** (AC 12, HP 11, Scimitar: +3 to hit, 1d6+1 slashing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Bandits: AC 12, HP 11, Scimitar attack: +3 to hit, 1d6+1 slashing damage. Light crossbows (range 80/320): +3 to hit, 1d8+1 piercing damage.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defend against the bandit ambushThe bandits are after the party’s gold and supplies. The party must fend them off or risk losing their belongings.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🏹 **1 =** The bandit leader emerges from hiding, demanding the party surrender.
🌲 **2 =** A nearby tree is felled by the bandits, creating difficult terrain and blocking the path.
🔥 **3 =** One of the bandits carries a torch and tries to set the party’s cart or supplies on fire.
💀 **4 =** A hidden cache of stolen goods is discovered after the battle (contains 50 GP, 2 healing potions).

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *25 XP per bandit*
- **Loot:** *50 GP, 2 healing potions, various mundane items (food, tools, etc.)The party finds a small stash of stolen goods among the bandits’ belongings, including gold and useful items.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is particularly strong or large, add 2 more bandits or include a bandit captain (AC 15, HP 65) to lead the group.*
