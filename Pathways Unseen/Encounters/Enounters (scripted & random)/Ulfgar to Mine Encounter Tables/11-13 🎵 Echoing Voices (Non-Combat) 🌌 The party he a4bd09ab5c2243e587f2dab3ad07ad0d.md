# 11-13.🎵 Echoing Voices (Non-Combat) 🌌 : The party hears eerie, echoing voices coming from deep within the mine, possibly leading them toward something—or away from it.

---

## 🎵 **Echoing Voices (Non-Combat)** 🌌

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Echoing VoicesThe party hears eerie, echoing voices coming from deep within the mine, creating an unsettling atmosphere.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Deep Mine TunnelsThe encounter takes place in the dark, winding tunnels of the mine, where the walls seem to carry whispers and faint voices from unknown sources.*
- **Weather:** *UndergroundThe air is cold and still, with the occasional sound of dripping water echoing through the caverns.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **None***The voices are disembodied, adding to the eerie and mysterious atmosphere of the encounter.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"Did you hear that? It sounds like... voices, but there's no one here..."Encourage the party to speculate about the source of the voices and decide whether to investigate further or avoid them.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Investigate the source of the echoing voicesThe party must decide whether to follow the voices deeper into the mine or steer clear of them, considering the potential dangers or discoveries.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Mental Challenge:** *Resist the unnerving effects of the voices with Wisdom saving throwsThe voices might cause unsettling thoughts or fear, requiring Wisdom saving throws to avoid being distracted or frightened.*
- **Exploration Challenge:** *Follow the voices to discover their sourceThe party could make Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Survival) checks to trace the voices to their origin, potentially uncovering a hidden area or secret.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The voices grow louder, leading to a hidden chamber or forgotten relic
2️⃣ **2 =** The voices suddenly stop, leaving the party in unsettling silence
3️⃣ **3 =** The voices cause a temporary illusion or hallucination, confusing the party
4️⃣ **4 =** The voices turn out to be a warning, alerting the party to a hidden danger ahead

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Rewards:** *Investigating the voices might lead to discovering a hidden passage, lost treasure, or crucial information about the mine’s history*
- **Consequences:** *Ignoring the voices could result in missing important clues, or they might lead the party into a trap or ambushThe outcome of this encounter could significantly impact the party's exploration of the mine and their understanding of its secrets.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the intensity of the voices and their effects based on the party’s level and experience.For a more experienced party, consider adding a supernatural or magical element to the voices, making them more difficult to resist or understand.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Eerie and UnsettlingThe encounter should create a sense of dread and curiosity, with the party feeling like they are being watched or lured into something unknown.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The faint sound of whispers echoing off the stone walls, growing louder or softer seemingly at random*
    - *The cold, damp air that clings to the skin, making every noise seem sharper and more sinisterDetails that enhance the mysterious and eerie atmosphere of the encounter.*

---

This encounter plays on the party’s sense of fear and curiosity, offering the potential for discovery or danger depending on their choices. The echoing voices serve as both a lure and a warning, challenging the party to trust their instincts and navigate the mine's dark depths carefully.
