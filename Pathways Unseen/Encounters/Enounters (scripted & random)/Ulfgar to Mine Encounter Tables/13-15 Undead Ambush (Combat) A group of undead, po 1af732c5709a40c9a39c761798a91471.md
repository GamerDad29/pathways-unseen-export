# 13-15. Undead Ambush (Combat): A group of undead, possibly former miners, attacks the party. (4 Skeletons)

Here’s the Underdark-themed Undead Ambush encounter:

---

## ☠️ **Undead Ambush: Echoes of the Fallen** 💀

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Undead AmbushThe bones of long-dead miners rise from the ground, their hollow eyes burning with unnatural light as they attack the living intruders.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Collapsed Mine ShaftThe encounter occurs in a partially collapsed section of the mine, with loose rocks and debris scattered across the ground. Faint, eerie whispers seem to emanate from the walls, and the air is thick with the scent of decay.*
- **Lighting:** *Dim LightFlickering torches left by the miners cast shadows on the walls, creating an unsettling atmosphere and making it difficult to see the approaching danger.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **4 Skeletons** (AC 13, HP 13, Shortsword: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Skeleton: AC 13, HP 13, Vulnerable to bludgeoning damage. These skeletons are animated by dark magic and driven by a mindless hunger for the living.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat the undead attackersThe party must eliminate the skeletons before they overwhelm them. Alternatively, they could attempt to consecrate the area to lay the spirits to rest.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
☠️ **1 =** One of the skeletons was a miner foreman in life and wields a pickaxe that deals an extra 1d4 damage.
⚒️ **2 =** A trapped spirit lingers in the area, causing fear in a random party member (Wisdom save DC 13).
🕯️ **3 =** A nearby lantern flickers out, plunging part of the area into darkness.
⚰️ **4 =** The bones of an additional skeleton begin to reassemble themselves, adding to the fight if not stopped.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *50 XP per skeleton*
- **Loot:** *A miner’s journal with cryptic notes (potential quest lead), 25 GP in loose coins, and a rusty but usable pickaxe (counts as a simple weapon).If the party searches the remains, they may find these items on the skeletons or hidden in the debris.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is struggling, the skeletons could be brittle from age, taking extra damage from their attacks, or perhaps a surge of divine energy weakens them temporarily.*
