# 17-18. Ancient Machinery (Non-Combat): The party discovers old, broken-down mining machinery that might be repaired or repurposed.

---

## ⚙️ **Ancient Machinery (Non-Combat)** 🛠️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Ancient MachineryThe party discovers old, broken-down mining machinery that might be repaired or repurposed.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Abandoned Mine ChamberThe encounter takes place in a large, dusty chamber within the mine, where rusted gears and broken cogs lie scattered around massive, dormant machinery.*
- **Weather:** *UndergroundThe air is thick with the smell of rust and decay, and the only sounds are the occasional creaks and groans of the old machinery settling into place.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **None***The machinery itself is the focus, presenting an opportunity for problem-solving and creativity.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"This equipment looks ancient, but maybe with some work, we could get it running again..."Encourage the party to investigate the machinery and discuss how it might be repaired or used.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Repair or repurpose the ancient machineryThe party must decide whether to attempt to repair the machinery, potentially using it to aid their exploration or as a defense against potential threats.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Mechanical Challenge:** *Repair the machinery with Intelligence (Investigation) or Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) checksThe party could work together to figure out how the machinery operates and what parts need fixing, potentially using tools or magical assistance.*
- **Creative Use:** *Repurpose the machinery for an unexpected useThe party might find a way to use the machinery creatively, such as setting up a trap or using it to clear a blocked path.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The machinery sputters to life briefly, revealing a hidden compartment with valuable items
2️⃣ **2 =** The machinery malfunctions, causing a minor cave-in or releasing a harmful gas
3️⃣ **3 =** The machinery can be fully repaired, providing a temporary benefit such as increased speed in mining or defense against intruders
4️⃣ **4 =** The machinery is too far gone to repair, but it provides clues to the mine’s history

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Rewards:** *Repairing the machinery might reveal hidden treasures, open new pathways, or provide a temporary benefit to the party's exploration*
- **Consequences:** *Failing to repair the machinery could result in it breaking further or causing an unexpected hazard, like a cave-in or release of toxic fumesThe outcome of this encounter could have lasting effects on the party's exploration and progress within the mine.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the complexity of the machinery and the difficulty of the repair tasks based on the party’s level and skills.For a higher-level party, consider adding a magical or elemental component to the machinery, making it more difficult to repair but also more rewarding if successful.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Intriguing and TenseThe encounter should evoke a sense of curiosity and tension as the party works to uncover the secrets of the ancient machinery and decide how best to use it.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The sound of rusted metal grinding as the machinery is moved or manipulated*
    - *The faint, acrid smell of oil and rust, lingering in the air as the party worksDetails that enhance the mechanical and mysterious nature of the encounter.*

---

This encounter challenges the party to think creatively and use their skills to either repair or repurpose the ancient machinery. The machinery’s potential to aid or hinder their progress adds a layer of tension, encouraging the party to weigh the risks and rewards of their actions carefully.
