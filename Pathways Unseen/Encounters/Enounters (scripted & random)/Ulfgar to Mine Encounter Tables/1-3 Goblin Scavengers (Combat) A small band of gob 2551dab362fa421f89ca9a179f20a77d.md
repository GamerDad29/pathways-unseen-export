# 1-3. Goblin Scavengers (Combat): A small band of goblin scavengers ambush the party, hoping to loot their supplies. (3 Goblins)

---

## 🗡️ **Basic Random Combat Encounter Template** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Goblin AmbushA sudden and vicious ambush by a small band of goblin scavengers.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Forest ClearingA small, open area surrounded by dense trees, with a few scattered boulders providing cover.*
- **Weather:** *ClearThe skies are clear, with a slight breeze rustling the leaves.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **3 Goblins** (AC 15, HP 7, Shortsword: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Goblins: AC 15, HP 7, Shortsword attack: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage, Nimble Escape (can disengage or hide as a bonus action).*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat all goblinsThe goblins have ambushed the party in an attempt to steal their supplies. The party must fend them off.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** 2 more goblins arrive, joining the ambush.
2️⃣ **2 =** A nearby tree falls, creating difficult terrain.
3️⃣ **3 =** Nothing happens, the fight continues as normal.
4️⃣ **4 =** One goblin drops a small pouch containing 5 GP as they flee.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *50 XP per goblin*
- **Loot:** *15 GP total, 1 healing potion found in a pouch dropped by a goblinThe party finds a small amount of gold and a single healing potion after defeating the goblins.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party has more than 4 characters or is particularly strong, add 2 more goblins to the encounter.*
