# 19-20. Wolves (Combat): A pack of wolves stalks the party, attacking when they least expect it. (5 Wolves)

Here’s the Wolves encounter formatted:

---

## 🐺 **Cave Wolf Pack: Silent Stalkers** 🐾

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Cave Wolf PackA pack of subterranean cave wolves silently stalks the party, waiting for the perfect moment to strike.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Underground Cavern PathThe party is traveling through a narrow, rocky cavern with echoing walls. The dim light and jagged stone create the perfect hunting ground for the wolves.*
- **Weather:** *Stale AirThe air is thick and still, carrying the scent of damp earth and the faint musk of the wolves lurking nearby.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **5 Cave Wolves** (AC 13, HP 11, Bite: +4 to hit, 2d4+2 piercing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Cave Wolves: AC 13, HP 11, Keen Hearing and Smell: Advantage on Perception checks that rely on hearing or smell. Pack Tactics: Advantage on attack rolls if an ally is within 5 feet of the target.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Survive the wolf attackThe wolves are hungry and desperate, driven by instinct to attack anything that enters their territory. The party must fend them off or become their next meal.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🐾 **1 =** A larger, alpha wolf joins the fray, coordinating the pack’s attacks.
🦴 **2 =** The wolves drag a fallen party member deeper into the cave, forcing the others to chase after them.
🌑 **3 =** A sudden rockfall creates difficult terrain, limiting movement and making the battle more treacherous.
💉 **4 =** One of the wolves carries a strange disease that can affect those bitten (requires a Constitution saving throw).

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *50 XP per wolf*
- **Loot:** *Wolf pelts (worth 10 GP each), a wolf’s fang (possible alchemical ingredient)The party can skin the wolves for their valuable pelts or find other useful items on the wolves’ territory.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is larger or more powerful, add 2 more wolves or introduce a dire wolf pack leader (AC 14, HP 37) to challenge them.*
