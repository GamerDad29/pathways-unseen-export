# 7-9. Hidden Treasure (Non-Combat): The party discovers a hidden stash of valuable goods, likely left behind by a previous traveler or thief.

Certainly! Here’s the encounter for Hidden Treasure:

---

## 🗝️ **Hidden Treasure (Non-Combat)** 💰

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Hidden TreasureThe party stumbles upon a concealed cache of valuable items, hidden away by a previous traveler or a thief who never returned to claim their loot.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Rocky Outcropping in a Narrow CanyonThe encounter takes place in a secluded, rocky outcropping within a narrow canyon or crevice, providing natural hiding spots for the treasure.*
- **Weather:** *Partially CloudyA dim, overcast sky casts shadows across the canyon, making it easier to overlook the hidden stash.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **None***There are no NPCs directly involved, but the party may speculate on who hid the treasure and why they never returned for it.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"Looks like someone left this here for safekeeping... or maybe they didn’t survive to come back for it."*
    - *"Careful, it could be trapped. Thieves don’t just leave their loot lying around."These lines help set the scene and raise the stakes of investigating the treasure.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Safely retrieve the hidden treasureThe party’s goal is to find and safely retrieve the hidden stash without triggering any potential traps or missing out on additional hidden loot.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Perception Challenge:** *Locate the exact hiding spot of the treasureCharacters may need to make Wisdom (Perception) checks to notice subtle clues, like disturbed earth or out-of-place stones, indicating the treasure’s location.*
- **Trap Disarmament:** *Avoid or disarm potential traps guarding the treasureCharacters might need to make Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) checks or use thieves' tools to safely disarm any traps set by the treasure's previous owner.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The treasure is guarded by a simple trap, such as a pitfall or a poison dart
2️⃣ **2 =** The treasure includes a map or clue leading to a larger hoard elsewhere
3️⃣ **3 =** Another traveler or creature is also searching for the treasure and arrives shortly after the party
4️⃣ **4 =** The treasure includes a cursed item that brings bad luck or a minor curse

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Rewards:** *A stash of gold coins, a few gems, and possibly a minor magical item*
- **Consequences:** *Triggering a trap could result in injury or alerting nearby creaturesSuccessfully retrieving the treasure could provide valuable resources, while failure could lead to injuries or complications.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty of finding or retrieving the treasure.Add more complex traps or hidden compartments if the party is experienced with similar encounters.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Suspenseful and CuriousThe encounter should evoke a sense of discovery and caution, as the party uncovers the hidden treasure while remaining wary of potential dangers.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The sound of a distant breeze echoes through the canyon*
    - *A faint glint of something metallic catches the party's eye, partially buried under rocksInclude details that make the environment feel immersive and heighten the tension of uncovering hidden loot.*

---

This encounter adds an element of treasure hunting to the party’s journey, offering both rewards and risks as they explore the secrets hidden in the Underdark.
