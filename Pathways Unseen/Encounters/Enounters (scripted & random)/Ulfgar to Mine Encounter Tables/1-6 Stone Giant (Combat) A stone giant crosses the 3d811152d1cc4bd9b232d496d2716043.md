# 1-6. Stone Giant (Combat): A stone giant crosses the party's path, angered by their intrusion into its territory. (1 Stone Giant)

Here’s the Underdark-themed Stone Giant encounter:

---

## 🪨 **Stone Giant’s Fury: The Depths’ Guardian** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Stone Giant EncounterA colossal stone giant, one of the ancient protectors of the Underdark, emerges from the shadows of the cavern, angered by the party's intrusion.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Underdark CavernThe encounter takes place deep within the Underdark, in a massive, echoing cavern filled with stalagmites and uneven ground. The cavern walls are covered in phosphorescent fungi that emit a dim, eerie light.*
- **Lighting:** *Dim LightThe bioluminescent fungi provide just enough light to see vague shapes and movements, casting long shadows and making it difficult to see clearly.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **1 Stone Giant** (AC 17, HP 126, Greatclub: +9 to hit, 3d8+6 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Stone Giant: AC 17, HP 126, Rock Throw: The giant can hurl massive boulders at distant targets, dealing 4d10+6 bludgeoning damage. The giant’s skin is covered in ancient runes that glow faintly in the dark.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Survive or pacify the stone giantThe party must either defeat the giant in combat or find a way to appease it and avoid a deadly confrontation. Negotiation might be possible if they can convince the giant of their peaceful intentions.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🪨 **1 =** The giant’s stomp causes a minor earthquake, forcing the party to make Dexterity saving throws to avoid falling debris.
💥 **2 =** The giant recognizes an ancient symbol on one of the party's items and hesitates, lowering its guard momentarily.
🌫️ **3 =** A sudden influx of noxious gas from nearby vents causes the giant to retreat temporarily, giving the party a chance to reposition.
🌑 **4 =** The cavern’s ceiling starts to crumble, creating difficult terrain and forcing the party to move quickly.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *1,800 XP for defeating or outsmarting the stone giant*
- **Loot:** *An ancient stone tablet with runes (scroll of Earthbind), 75 GP worth of rare Underdark minerals, and a Stone Giant’s Amulet (provides advantage on Strength saving throws).If the giant is defeated or appeased, the party may discover these items among the remains of its past conquests or in a hidden alcove.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is struggling, the giant might be open to parley, or an external threat could distract it, giving the party an opportunity to escape or regroup.*
