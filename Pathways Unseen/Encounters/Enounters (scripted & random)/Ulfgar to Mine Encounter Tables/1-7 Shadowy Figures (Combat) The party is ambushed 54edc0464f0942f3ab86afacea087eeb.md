# 1-7. Shadowy Figures (Combat): The party is ambushed by shadowy figures intent on keeping the mines' secrets hidden. (2 Shadows + 3 Bandits)

---

## 🌑 **Shadowy Ambush: Guardians of the Dark Secrets** 🗡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Shadowy FiguresThe party is suddenly ambushed by eerie shadowy figures and their bandit allies, all determined to keep the secrets of the mines hidden.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Dimly Lit Mine ShaftThe encounter takes place in a narrow, dimly lit section of the mine, with crumbling walls and the faint drip of water echoing in the distance.*
- **Lighting:** *Flickering TorchesThe only light comes from a few flickering torches along the walls, casting long, ominous shadows.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **2 Shadows** (AC 12, HP 16, Strength Drain: +4 to hit, 2d6 necrotic damage)
    - **3 Bandits** (AC 12, HP 11, Scimitar: +3 to hit, 1d6+1 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Shadows: AC 12, HP 16, immune to nonmagical attacks not made with silvered weapons.Bandits: AC 12, HP 11, typical thugs armed with scimitars and light crossbows.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat the shadowy figures and bandits or find a way to escape the ambush.The party must either confront these mysterious attackers or retreat and regroup.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🌪️ **1 =** A sudden gust of wind extinguishes the torches, plunging the mine shaft into darkness.
🩸 **2 =** One of the bandits is a deserter and offers to betray the shadows for his life.
🗝️ **3 =** A hidden door is revealed during the fight, leading to a secret chamber.
💀 **4 =** The shadows begin to whisper unsettling secrets, causing a Wisdom saving throw (DC 13) or the party members gain disadvantage on their next attack.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *100 XP per bandit, 200 XP per shadow*
- **Loot:** *25 GP, a small silver key, and a faded map leading deeper into the mines.The party may find these items on the bodies of the fallen or hidden among the debris.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is stronger, add another shadow or increase the number of bandits.*
