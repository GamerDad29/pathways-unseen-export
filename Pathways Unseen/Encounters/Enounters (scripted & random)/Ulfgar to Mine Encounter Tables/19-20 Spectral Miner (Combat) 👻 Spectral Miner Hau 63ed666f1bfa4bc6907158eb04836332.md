# 19-20. Spectral Miner (Combat): 👻 Spectral Miner: Haunting of the Depths 🛡️ (1 Specter)

Here’s the encounter for the Spectral Miner:

---

## 👻 **Spectral Miner: Haunting of the Depths** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Spectral MinerA long-dead miner, now a vengeful specter, haunts the mine, lashing out at intruders who it mistakes for those who wronged it in life.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Abandoned Mine ShaftThe encounter takes place deep within a dark, abandoned mine shaft, where the walls are cold and damp, and the air feels thick with unseen energy.*
- **Lighting:** *Ghostly GlowThe only light comes from the faint, eerie glow of the Spectral Miner, casting ominous shadows on the walls.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **1 Specter** (AC 12, HP 22, Life Drain: +4 to hit, 3d6 necrotic damage, Regain HP equal to half the damage dealt)
- **Creature Stats:***Specter: AC 12, HP 22, can pass through walls and other solid objects, Life Drain ability that reduces the target’s max HP.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat the Spectral Miner or find a way to put its spirit to rest.The party must either fight off the ghostly miner or discover what is needed to quell its rage and free it from its torment.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🪦 **1 =** The specter’s attack causes the walls to crumble, creating difficult terrain.
🧲 **2 =** The miner’s old pickaxe is infused with ghostly energy, adding an extra attack.
⛏️ **3 =** A hidden stash of the miner’s old belongings is found nearby, offering clues to the miner’s past.
🌑 **4 =** The specter’s presence causes all non-magical light sources to dim, reducing visibility further.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *200 XP for defeating the Specter*
- **Loot:** *A spectral pickaxe (a ghostly weapon that can be used to strike ethereal creatures), a journal of the miner’s last days.The party may find these items either on the specter or in a hidden alcove nearby.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is stronger, consider adding a second Specter or increasing the HP of the existing one.*
