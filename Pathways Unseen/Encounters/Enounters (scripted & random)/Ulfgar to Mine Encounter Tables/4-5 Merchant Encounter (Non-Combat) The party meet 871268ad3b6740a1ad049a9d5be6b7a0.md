# 4-5. Merchant Encounter (Non-Combat): The party meets a traveling merchant selling rare and unusual items. The merchant has information about the road ahead.

---

## 🛤️ **Merchant Encounter (Non-Combat)** 🌱

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Wandering MerchantA mysterious merchant crosses paths with the party, offering rare items and valuable information.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Underdark PathwayThe encounter takes place on a narrow, winding path deep within the Underdark, lit by faintly glowing mushrooms.*
- **Weather:** *Stale AirThe air is dry and musty, with an occasional faint draft from unknown origins.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **The Merchant** (Sly, knowledgeable, slightly untrustworthy)*A wandering merchant with a pack full of rare and unusual items, eager to strike a deal with the party.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"Ah, travelers! You look like a discerning bunch. Care to browse my wares?"Provide some sample dialogue or topics to guide the interaction.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Purchase rare items or gain information about the road aheadThe party can negotiate with the merchant to buy items, gather information, or even trade goods.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Social Challenge:** *Haggle with the merchant to lower prices or gain extra informationThe merchant is cunning and enjoys a good haggle, but may let slip valuable information if charmed or coerced.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The merchant offers a rare, magical item for a steep price
2️⃣ **2 =** Another traveler arrives, interested in buying the same item as the party
3️⃣ **3 =** The merchant hints at a hidden danger ahead on the road
4️⃣ **4 =** The merchant tries to sell a cursed item without revealing its true nature

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Information:** *Details about a hidden treasure along the road ahead*
- **Consequences:** *The merchant may become a recurring character, offering future deals or causing trouble depending on the interactionThe party's interaction with the merchant could lead to future encounters, either beneficial or problematic.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the complexity.The merchant might have more valuable items or offer a side quest if the party shows interest.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Mysterious and slightly opportunisticThe merchant's appearance and demeanor should create a sense of intrigue, with an undercurrent of potential danger.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The smell of aged leather and strange herbs wafts from the merchant's pack*
    - *The merchant’s eyes gleam with a mix of greed and curiosity as they size up the partyInclude any details that make the encounter more immersive.*
