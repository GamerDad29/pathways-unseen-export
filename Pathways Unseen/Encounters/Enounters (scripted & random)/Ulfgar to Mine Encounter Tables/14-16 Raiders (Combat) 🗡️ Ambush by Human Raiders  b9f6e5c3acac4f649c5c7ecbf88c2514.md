# 14-16. Raiders (Combat): 🗡️ Ambush by Human Raiders: Guardians of the Mine 🛡️

Here’s the encounter for the human raiders:

---

## 🗡️ **Ambush by Human Raiders: Guardians of the Mine** 🛡️

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Human RaidersA group of determined human scouts ambushes the party, their goal clear: to drive intruders away from the mine at all costs.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Narrow Mine PassageThe encounter takes place in a constricted mine passage, where the walls are close and the ceiling low, adding to the tension of the ambush.*
- **Lighting:** *Dim TorchlightOnly a few torches line the walls, casting eerie shadows and making it difficult to see the attackers until they are close.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **3 Human Scouts** (AC 13, HP 16, Shortsword: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage, Longbow: +4 to hit, 1d8+2 damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Human Scouts: AC 13, HP 16, proficient in ambush tactics, using their longbows to strike from a distance before closing in with shortswords.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Repel the human scouts or find a way to escape their relentless assault.The party must either fight off the attackers or devise a strategy to retreat and avoid further conflict.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🏹 **1 =** One of the scouts carries a poisoned arrow, dealing extra damage.
💀 **2 =** The scouts' leader arrives with reinforcements (1 more scout).
🪓 **3 =** The scouts trigger a hidden trap, causing the ceiling to start collapsing.
🎯 **4 =** A hidden alcove in the passage contains an old, forgotten weapon.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *150 XP per scout*
- **Loot:** *10 GP, a bottle of poison, and a worn map marking the mines' entrances and exits.The party may find these items on the bodies of the fallen or stashed nearby.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is stronger, add another scout or give one of the scouts a higher AC and more HP.*
