# 10-12. Blighted Forest (Non-Combat): The party travels through a section of forest that appears to be dying, with diseased plants and animals. Investigating might reveal the cause.

Certainly! Here’s the encounter for Blighted Forest:

---

## 🌲 **Blighted Forest (Non-Combat)** 🍂

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Blighted ForestThe party travels through a section of forest that appears to be dying, with diseased plants and animals showing signs of a mysterious blight.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Decaying Forest PathThe encounter takes place in a once-lush forest, now withered and dying. The trees are gnarled, their leaves yellowed and falling, while the undergrowth is sparse and sickly.*
- **Weather:** *Damp and OvercastThe air is heavy with moisture, and a thick, low-hanging mist clings to the ground, making the surroundings feel oppressive and eerie.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **None***The party is alone in this desolate part of the forest, but they may come across signs of past inhabitants or other creatures affected by the blight.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"This place feels... wrong. The very air seems sick."*
    - *"Whatever caused this, it’s spreading. We need to find the source before it reaches the village."These lines help convey the seriousness of the situation and the potential danger to nearby areas.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Investigate the cause of the blightThe party should aim to uncover the source of the blight and determine whether it can be stopped or reversed.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Nature Challenge:** *Identify the symptoms of the blight and their originCharacters may need to make Intelligence (Nature) or Wisdom (Survival) checks to recognize signs of the blight and determine its potential source.*
- **Magical Investigation:** *Detect any lingering magic or cursesCharacters might use detect magic or similar spells to sense if the blight has a magical origin, requiring Intelligence (Arcana) checks.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The party finds a dying animal that has strange markings or magical symbols on its body
2️⃣ **2 =** A sudden wind stirs the mist, revealing a hidden path or object
3️⃣ **3 =** A patch of the forest seems to be regenerating, with faint signs of life returning
4️⃣ **4 =** The blight intensifies as the party approaches the suspected source, requiring them to act quickly

---

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Rewards:** *Information on the blight’s cause, potentially a clue to a greater mystery or a useful item that resists the blight’s effects*
- **Consequences:** *Failing to investigate could allow the blight to spread, endangering nearby settlements or even the party themselvesSuccessfully investigating could lead to new quests or plot developments, while ignoring the blight might have long-term negative effects.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the complexity of discovering the blight's cause.Introduce a more powerful magical source or increase the blight's area of effect if the party is high-level or particularly skilled in investigation.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Dark and ForebodingThe encounter should evoke a sense of unease and urgency, as the party navigates through a forest that is unnaturally dying.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *The smell of rot and decay permeates the air, mixing with the damp scent of the forest*
    - *The sound of distant, muffled groans or creaks can be heard, as if the forest itself is in painInclude details that create an atmosphere of dread and the unnatural.*

---

This encounter provides a mix of environmental storytelling and mystery, encouraging the party to engage with the world around them and uncover the dark secrets lurking within the Blighted Forest.
