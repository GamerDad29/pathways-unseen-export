## 🏛️ **Ancient Ruins (Non-Combat)** 🏺

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Forgotten EchoesThe party stumbles upon the crumbling remains of an ancient civilization, long forgotten by time.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Subterranean RuinsThe ruins are nestled deep within a cavern, with towering pillars and shattered stonework hinting at the grandeur that once was.*
- **Weather:** *Stagnant and DustyThe air is dry and still, filled with the scent of ancient dust and the faint echo of footsteps from ages past.*

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **NPCs and Interactions**

- **Key NPCs:**
    - **None directly, but remnants of the past—like inscriptions, statues, and hidden mechanisms—tell stories of those who once lived here.***The party may uncover secrets, decipher ancient scripts, or trigger long-dormant traps.*
- **Dialogue Hooks:**
    - *"These markings... they tell a story, but I can't quite make it out."Provide some sample dialogue as the characters explore and react to their discoveries.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Explore the ruins and uncover their secretsThe party must carefully navigate the ruins, searching for hidden treasures, lost knowledge, or perhaps even a way forward in their journey.*

---

### 🧩 **Challenges and Puzzles** (Optional)

- **Environmental Challenge:** *Avoid Ancient TrapsPlayers may need to make Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks to dodge traps, or Intelligence (History) checks to understand the ruins’ significance.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
1️⃣ **1 =** The ground beneath a character gives way, revealing a hidden chamber
2️⃣ **2 =** The statues suddenly animate, posing a potential threat or puzzle
3️⃣ **3 =** The party discovers a relic, radiating faint magical energy
4️⃣ **4 =** A ghostly figure appears, guiding the party or warning them away

### 💰 **Rewards and Consequences**

- **Information:** *Clues about the ancient civilization or a map leading to another location in the Underdark*
- **Consequences:** *Activating a trap could cause injuries, alert nearby creatures, or collapse part of the ruinsSuccessfully exploring the ruins could reveal valuable treasures or knowledge that aids the party in future encounters.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the complexity.For a more challenging encounter, add more traps or make the ruins structurally unstable, requiring careful navigation.*

---

### 🎨 **Flavor and Theme**

- **Mood:** *Mysterious and AncientThe encounter should evoke a sense of awe and caution as the party explores a place untouched for centuries.*
- **Sensory Details:**
    - *Faint echoes of long-forgotten voices seem to whisper through the halls*
    - *The cold stone beneath their feet feels almost alive, as if the ruins themselves are watchingInclude any details that make the encounter more immersive.*
