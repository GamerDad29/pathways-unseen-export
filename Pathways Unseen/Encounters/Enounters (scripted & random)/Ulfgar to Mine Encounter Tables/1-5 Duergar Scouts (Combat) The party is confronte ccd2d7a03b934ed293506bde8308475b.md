# 1-5. Duergar Scouts (Combat): The party is confronted by a group of Duergar scouts who attack to protect their hidden outpost. (3 Duergar)

---

## 🪓 **Duergar Scouts: The Hidden Outpost** 🔦

---

### 🎲 **Encounter Title**

- **Name:** *Duergar AmbushA group of Duergar scouts, fiercely protective of their hidden outpost, confronts the party with deadly precision.*

---

### 🌍 **Environment**

- **Terrain:** *Narrow Tunnel in the MiddledarkThe encounter takes place in a narrow, winding tunnel with low ceilings and jagged rock walls. The air is stale, and the dim light from the party's torches casts eerie shadows.*
- **Weather:** *Stifling HeatThe oppressive heat of the Middledark adds to the tension, causing sweat to bead on the adventurers' brows.*

---

### 🧌 **Creatures**

- **List of Creatures:**
    - **3 Duergar** (AC 16, HP 26, War Pick: +4 to hit, 1d8+2 piercing damage)
- **Creature Stats:***Duergar: AC 16, HP 26, Multiattack: The Duergar makes two attacks with its war pick. Enlarge (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest): For 1 minute, the Duergar magically increases in size. While enlarged, the Duergar’s weapons deal an extra 1d4 damage.*

---

### 🎯 **Objectives**

- **Primary Objective:** *Defeat the Duergar and secure the tunnelThe scouts are determined to protect their outpost. The party must overcome them to continue their journey.*

---

### 🎲 **Random Elements** (Optional)

*Add some unexpected twists to the encounter!*

- **1d4 Random Event:**
🔮 **1 =** One Duergar uses a potion of invisibility to ambush the party.
💣 **2 =** A section of the tunnel is rigged with explosives. A Duergar tries to trigger it, causing a cave-in.
⚔️ **3 =** The Duergar are carrying a map that reveals a nearby hidden passage.
🧭 **4 =** A nearby rockfall creates difficult terrain, slowing the party’s movement and giving the Duergar an advantage.

---

### 💰 **Rewards**

- **XP:** *700 XP per Duergar*
- **Loot:** *30 GP, 1 Potion of Invisibility, Map of Hidden PassageThe party finds valuable loot and a map that could lead to further adventures.*

---

### ⚖️ **Scaling**

- **Adjustments:***Increase or decrease the difficulty based on the party size and level.If the party is struggling, consider having one Duergar retreat or reducing the Duergar's hit points slightly.*
