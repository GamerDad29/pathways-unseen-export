# Lost Magic (random encounter)

### **The Forgotten Chamber of Shards (Updated with Magical Doorways)**

After their encounter with the Warforged, the party ventures deeper into the Underdark and discovers a hidden, **circular chamber** filled with ancient magic. The air inside is still and peaceful, radiating neutrality and balance, as if the world outside holds no sway here. This is a place from a forgotten time, untouched by conflict.

---

### **Room Description:**

The chamber you step into is striking in its perfection. A perfect circle, its stone walls and floor gleam with a strange, unidentifiable material—smooth, almost polished, and cold to the touch. The air feels charged, thick with latent magical energy. Along the perimeter, **four braziers** burn with an otherworldly light, each casting its own ethereal glow: **red**, **green**, **blue**, and **brown**. The flames flicker unnaturally, their soft hues filling the chamber with a spectrum of colors that seem to pulse in rhythm with the very room itself.

Suspended within the light of these braziers are **six crystalline shards**, floating in midair. Each shard hums with an elemental force that seems almost palpable, radiating heat, chill, vitality, or weight as you approach. Fire, Ice, Nature, and Earth—all represented in these arcane fragments, their energies swirling in tandem with the braziers’ light.

Between the braziers, spaced evenly along the chamber’s edge, are **eight magical doorways**. Each portal glows with an intense **orange-red hue**, the light crackling faintly, like a heartbeat of magical power. The hum of energy from the doorways is faint but constant, a low vibration that tugs subtly at your senses, as if the magic woven into this place is beckoning, inviting you deeper into its mysteries.

The air is thick with anticipation, the energy in the room almost alive, waiting for something—or someone—to unlock its hidden purpose.

---

### **The Shards of Power:**

The **six floating shards** represent elemental forces of a forgotten age:

1. **Two Shards of Fire**: Warm to the touch, radiating an inner heat. Their power feels heavy, almost overwhelming.
2. **Two Shards of Ice**: Cold and weightless, they embody stillness and preservation.
3. **Two Shards of Nature**: Pulsing with life, one heavy like stone, the other light like the wind.
4. **Two Shards of Earth**: Grounded and solid, these shards hum with the power of the land itself, rough and unyielding.

---

### **The Magical Doorways:**

The **eight magical doorways**, glowing with **orange/red magical power**, stand tall and imposing. Each doorway appears as if it could lead somewhere unknown, but instead, they all **lead back to where the party came from**. Whether this is a form of magical safeguard or a designed way to guide travelers out of the chamber remains unclear, but the doorways offer the party a safe passage should they wish to leave.

---

### **Room’s Energy:**

The room feels peaceful and calm, with a strong sense of neutrality. The balance of elements and the presence of these **forgotten shards of power** create an atmosphere of **potential**, while the magical doorways offer the party a sense of security—knowing they can return to the familiar, should they choose to.

---

### **Loot Potential and Significance:**

The party can loot all six shards if they choose. These **elemental shards** will play a key role in their prologue quests, unlocking future powers tied to their elements. The room’s significance is clear—it is a place of ancient power, waiting to bestow its magic upon those who seek it.

The **eight doorways** ensure that the party can leave safely, bringing the knowledge and power they’ve gained with them as they continue their journey.

---

This encounter adds a mysterious, ancient room filled with **elemental shards** and **magical doorways** leading back to familiar territory, setting the stage for deeper adventures and the discovery of ancient power.
