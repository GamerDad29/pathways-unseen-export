# Dancing Lights

## Drow Magic

A half-elf of drow descent can forgo Skill Versatility and instead choose the drow’s **Drow Magic**:

- **Cantrip**: You know the *Dancing Lights* cantrip.
- **3rd Level**: You can cast the *Faerie Fire* spell once with this trait and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.
- **5th Level**: You can cast the *Darkness* spell once with this trait and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.

**Charisma** is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

### Spells

- **Dancing Lights** (Cantrip)
- **Faerie Fire** (1st Level) / *Long Rest*
- **Darkness** (2nd Level) / *Long Rest*
