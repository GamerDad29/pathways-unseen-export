# Session 0 Pre-Notes

## Character Overview

### Character Name: Iblith Gorch

- **Race and Class:** Drow Half-Elf - Shadow Monk
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral

### Background

- **Origins:** Born to one of the few female drow in Bregan D'aerthe, who was considered promiscuous. His mother was previously part of House Xorlarrin, now turned to House Do'Urden.
- **Early Life:** Iblith was kept hidden for several years before being discovered as a half-drow. Before he was to be killed, he was saved by Jarlaxle after seeing his lavender eyes, which reminded Jarlaxle of Drizzt.
- **Monastery:** Jarlaxle had him taken to the starting city and dropped at a monastery (or equivalent) doorstep, where he was raised and trained as a shadow monk.
- **Goal:** Iblith aspires to make a name for himself to join Bregan D'aerthe and prove he is not trash to be discarded.

### Short-Term, Mid-Term, and Long-Term Goals

- **Short-Term Goal:** Build a hand crossbow that uses the drow’s sleep poison darts.
- **Mid-Term Goal:** Execute high-level bounties.
- **Long-Term Goal:** Make a name for himself to join Bregan D'aerthe.

# Character Traits

### Personality Traits

- Guarded, reserved, and cagey.
- Says few words and often uses drow sign language as insults.
- Does not give out his real name unless absolutely necessary.

### Flaws

- Self-loathing leading him to pick fights over small reasons.

### Strengths and Skills

- Trained as a shadow monk, with a focus on stealth and combat.
- Skilled in drow sign language and deception.

## Character Connections

### Relationships with Party Members

- He does not know the party members. However, one of the party members might be a low-level bounty. He plans to give a fake name and use the Hat of Disguise until he is forced to reveal his true half-drow form.

### Affiliations

- Claims affiliation with Bregan D'aerthe, though his connection is only through his birth to a member of the band.

### Personal Stakes

- None initially; he is out for himself, though this could change over time based on character development.

## Role-Playing Preferences

### Preferred Gameplay

- **Most Enjoyed:** Exploration, problem-solving.
- **Least Enjoyed:** Role-playing, combat.

### Character Development

- Open to seeing how the campaign evolves to determine if he remains a loner or becomes more of a team player.

## House Rules and Mechanics

### Homebrew Rules

- Comfortable with the homebrew rules and mechanics introduced in the campaign.

### HP Level Increases

- Prefers rolling dice and rerolling 1s and 2s, but will follow the campaign's rule on this.
