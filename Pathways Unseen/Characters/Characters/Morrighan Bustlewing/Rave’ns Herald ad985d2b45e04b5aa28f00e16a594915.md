# Rave’ns Herald

### **The Rave’ns Herald**

Digging this idea from Gemini:The Raven's Herald:
	•	Background: You lived among a reclusive group of fairies who worshipped a death goddess. When the Queen of the Unseelie Court sought an agent in the mortal world, she appeared to you in a dream, offering power in exchange for spreading the chill of death.
	•	Personality: You embrace the macabre and have a morbid sense of humor. You view death as a natural part of the cycle and see your service as a way to usher in its embrace.

## **Feature: Wanderer**

You have an excellent memory for maps and geography, and you can always recall the general layout of terrain, settlements, and other features around you. In addition, you can find food and fresh water for yourself and up to five other people each day, provided that the land offers berries, small game, water, and so forth.
