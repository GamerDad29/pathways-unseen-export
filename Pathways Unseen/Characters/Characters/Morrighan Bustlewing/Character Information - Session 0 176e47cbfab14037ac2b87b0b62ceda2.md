# Character Information - Session 0

---

## 🦋 **Morrighan Bustlewing - The Rave'n's Herald** 🖤

---

### **🌑 Character Background**

Morrighan Bustlewing is a fairy hailing from the Shadowglade, a hidden enclave within the Feywild devoted to the worship of Morrith, the death goddess. Raised in a community that revered the natural end of life, Morrighan grew to see death not as an end, but as a necessary part of the cycle of existence. Her life changed dramatically when Lady Malfaria, the Queen of the Unseelie Court, appeared to her in a dream, offering her the role of the Rave'n's Herald. Embracing the opportunity with a mix of trepidation and excitement, Morrighan accepted the role, viewing it as an honor and a fulfillment of her destiny.

---

### **🖤 Personality**

Morrighan finds beauty in decay and serenity in the stillness of the grave. Her morbid sense of humor often unnerves those around her, but she remains unfazed, considering their discomfort as a sign of naivety. To Morrighan, death is a natural part of life’s cycle, something to be welcomed rather than feared. She is deeply committed to guiding souls to their final rest and maintaining the delicate balance between life and death.

---

### **🔗 Connections and Allies**

- **Lady Malfaria, Queen of the Unseelie Court:** Morrighan's patron and the shadowy figure guiding her on her mission to spread the influence of death.
- **Morrith, Goddess of Death:** Though rarely communicative, Morrighan feels Morrith’s presence, often receiving visions or omens that guide her actions.
- **Eldrin the Silent:** A fellow worshiper of Morrith, Eldrin taught Morrighan the ways of the death goddess and remains a trusted advisor.

---

### **🎯 Goals and Motivations**

- **Spread the Influence of the Unseelie Court:** Morrighan is driven to advance the power and influence of the Unseelie Court in the mortal world.
- **Maintain the Balance Between Life and Death:** Central to her mission is ensuring that the natural cycle of life and death is respected, opposing those who seek to defy it.
- **Uncover Ancient Secrets:** Morrighan is passionate about uncovering lost knowledge and ancient secrets related to death and the afterlife, seeking to deepen her understanding and power.

---

### **🎭 Roleplaying Hooks**

- **Coping with Reactions to Her Humor:** Morrighan remains unbothered by others’ reactions to her dark humor, sometimes explaining her perspective to those she considers naive but largely remaining unfazed.
- **Embracing the Role of the Rave'n's Herald:** She fully embraces her role, seeing it as the perfect fit for her destiny and the fulfillment of her purpose.
- **Balancing Duties and Personal Beliefs:** Morrighan’s duties to the Unseelie Court align harmoniously with her worship of Morrith, creating a balance between her personal beliefs and her mission.
- **Connections to the Mortal World:** Morrighan has not formed lasting connections in the mortal world, instead using individuals as tools to further her mission, whether manipulating people of high status or utilizing small folk for her purposes.

---

### **📝 Summary**

Morrighan Bustlewing, known as the Rave'n's Herald, is a fairy deeply rooted in the worship of Morrith, the death goddess. Entrusted by Lady Malfaria, Queen of the Unseelie Court, with spreading the influence of death in the mortal world, Morrighan approaches her role with both reverence and enthusiasm. Her macabre sense of humor, commitment to maintaining the balance of life and death, and connections to powerful entities in the Feywild make her a formidable and enigmatic figure. Despite her grim demeanor, Morrighan’s actions are driven by a deep respect for the natural order and the cycles of existence.
