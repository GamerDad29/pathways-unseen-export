# I'M NOT SHORT!!!!

### **Action Type** 1 Reaction

**To Hit** +5

**Attack/Save**DEX 13

**Damage** 4

**Damage Type** Bludgeoning

**Stat** STR

**Range/Area** 5ft. Reach

**Proficient** Yes

As a reaction to being made fun of for being short, you can immediately move up to your movement speed and make a flying headbutt attack, with a -3 attack penalty, against the offending creature.  This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity. If the attack hits, the creature takes 1 point of damage and must make a DEX Saving Throw equal to 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Constitution Bonus.  On a save, the creature is knocked back 5ft.  On a failure, the creature is knocked back 5ft and is knocked prone. After this attack, you are prone.

This reaction must be taken if available. After this reaction is taken, its level increases by 1 until you take a long or short rest, at which it resets to lvl 1. At lvl 2, the attack penalty increases to -5.  If you miss, you fly 5 ft behind the target and are knocked prone. If you hit and they fail the DEX Saving Throw, they are knocked back 10ft and knocked prone.  At lvl 3, the attack penalty is -5 with disadvantage. ## LVL 1: Attack Penalty -3, On target DEX failure, creature is knocked back 5 ft and prone.

## LVL 2: Attack Penalty -5. On miss, move 5 ft past the target. On target DEX failure, creature is knocked back 10 ft and is prone.

## LVL 3: Attack Penalty -5 with Disadvantage. On miss, fly 10 ft behind target and go unconscious. On target DEX failure, creature is knocked back 10 ft, is prone and unconscious.
