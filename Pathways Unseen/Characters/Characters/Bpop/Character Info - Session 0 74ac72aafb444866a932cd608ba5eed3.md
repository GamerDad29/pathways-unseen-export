# Character Info - Session 0

---

## 🛠️ **Bpop - The Kobold Artificer** 🧰

---

### **🔍 Background**

Bpop is a 13-season-old Kobold Artificer with an insatiable curiosity and a passion for invention. Raised in service to a powerful Red dragon, Bpop spent his early years gathering materials and learning from the clan inventor. His life took a dramatic turn when Stone Giants attacked, collapsing the caves he called home. Ingeniously, Bpop earned the trust of the giants, assisting them in maintaining their territories. However, his drive to learn and prove himself led him to leave his past behind and seek out the mysteries of the Underdark's capital city.

---

### **🧠 Personality Traits**

- **Inventive and Curious:** Constantly tinkering, Bpop is driven by a need to create and discover. He eagerly learns from new masters, always looking to improve his craft.
- **Resilient:** Despite his clan’s losses, Bpop has turned his grief into determination, hiding his anger and resentment behind a respectful demeanor.
- **Eager to Prove Worth:** Small in stature but big in ambition, Bpop is determined to be respected in society. Cautious at first, he becomes enthusiastic once trust is established.

---

### **⚙️ Strengths and Skills**

- **Engineering and Tinkering:** A genius with mechanics, Bpop can build, repair, and modify a wide range of devices. His creations are functional, though they may lack the ornamentation of more experienced crafters.
- **Resourcefulness:** Small size and dangerous environments have made Bpop highly resourceful, improvising solutions with limited resources.
- **Steel Defender:** His most prized creation is his Steel Defender, a mechanical guardian that he continuously upgrades and relies on in dangerous situations.

---

### **⚠️ Flaws and Weaknesses**

- **Repressed Anger:** Deeply angry towards both the Red dragon and the Stone Giants, Bpop outwardly idolizes strength and intelligence but secretly resents them. He seeks ways to make powerful groups dependent on his skills.
- **Dislike of Cold:** Having lived in warm tunnels, Bpop strongly dislikes cold environments. He dresses warmly even in cool conditions, which can sometimes be impractical or draw unwanted attention.

---

### **🎯 Goals**

- **Short-Term:** Secure a job where his tinkering skills can shine.
- **Mid-Term:** Explore the world and study under master inventors to enhance his abilities.
- **Long-Term:** Establish himself as a legendary artificer, known for his ingenious creations and contributions to the world of mechanics.

---

### **🔗 Affiliations and Connections**

- **Kobold Clan:** Bpop remains loyal to his former clan, who now assist the Stone Giants by maintaining smaller tunnels. Despite the distance, he feels a strong connection to his people.
- **Stone Giants:** Though conflicted, Bpop respects the giants’ power. He maintains a cautious relationship, aware that they could be both allies and threats.
- **Party Members:** Bpop may have provided functional gear to party members, such as a net for Ibilith Gorch or scale mail for Gnomenclature. These connections could lead to deeper bonds as the party progresses.

---

### **🎭 Role-Playing Preferences**

- **Balanced Gameplay:** Bpop’s player enjoys a mix of combat, exploration, role-playing, and problem-solving, preferring a relaxed and intuitive playstyle where in-game and out-of-game experiences blend seamlessly.
- **Organic Growth:** Bpop’s development is intended to be natural, with level-ups and character growth happening as part of the story rather than being the focus during sessions.

---

### **📝 Session Zero Notes**

During session zero, Bpop's backstory was fleshed out, emphasizing his connections to the Red dragon and Stone Giants, his resourcefulness, and his drive to prove himself. Initial connections with other party members were hinted at through the creation of functional gear, solidifying his role as a resourceful and inventive support character.

---

### **🌟 Overall**

Bpop is a complex and resourceful Kobold Artificer, shaped by his past experiences and driven by a desire to prove himself. His ingenuity and quick thinking make him a valuable ally, while his repressed anger and conflicting feelings towards powerful figures add depth to his character. As the campaign unfolds, Bpop will continue to invent, explore, and navigate the challenges of the Underdark, all while seeking to leave his mark on the world.
