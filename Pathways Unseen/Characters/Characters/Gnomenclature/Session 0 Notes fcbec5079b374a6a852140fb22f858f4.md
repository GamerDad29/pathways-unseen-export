# Session 0 Notes

**GNOMENCLATURE**

Character Background

Give me an overview of your character's background. How did they end up in the capital Underdark city?

- He woke up there in an alley behind the library with a tattoo on his neck and has no idea who brought him there or tattooed him
- He doesn’t know this stuff yet, but:
    - He is part of a secret cult called the Tome, which is a sect that split off The Silent Assembly
    - They tattooed him and dumped him in the capital Underdark city behind the library as that is how they communicate, by writing in a special tome called The Order of Faerûn
    - As the cult is clandestine and thrives on secrecy, the tattoo will fade the more the person talks about the cult, indicating to other members that person has been blabbing
    - The end goal for the cult is to end the war, however, it is also to end it by taking over complete control

What is your character’s backstory? Include details about their family, upbringing, and key life events.

- He was born to a wealthy family but was sent to a boarding school in Candlekeep at an early age so his parents could globetrot and rub elbows with high society.
- While away at school, he learned the value of knowledge as opposed to monetary wealth
- He found a small group of lifelong friends (four halflings and a human wizard) who would often get together to spark intellectual conversation and debate
- He often resented his parents, so after he finished school, instead of returning home, he got a job with the school as a history professor
- He spends most of his free time in the Great Library, trying to ingest everything he can

Provide a one-sentence short-term, mid-term, and long-term goal for your character.

- ST - How the fuck did I get get here and why do I have this tattoo on my neck?
- MT - Find others in this clan and figure out why we were all brought in
- LT - Find the knowledge necessary to help the clan bring about the end of the war

Character Traits

What are your character’s key personality traits? How do they interact with others?

- He is very smart and can be charming, however, he is quite shy and slow to open up to others.
- He likes to solve mysteries and can be very inquisitive, sometimes a little too inquisitive, bordering on intrusive

What are your character’s flaws or weaknesses? How do these affect their behavior?

- While being very smart, he often talks down to others or explains things like he is talking to a child.
