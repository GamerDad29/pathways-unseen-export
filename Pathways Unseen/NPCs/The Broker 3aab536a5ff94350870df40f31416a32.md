# The Broker: The Man with Many Masks

**Alias**: The Broker, The Man with Many Masks

**True Name**: Unknown

**Race**: Unknown (presumed human)

**Class**: Rogue (Arcane Trickster)

**Level**: 20

**Alignment**: Neutral

### Appearance

The Broker is an enigmatic figure who is never seen without one of his many masks. These masks range from intricately designed and ornate to simple and unassuming, each reflecting the personality he adopts at the time. His attire is always tailored and refined, hinting at wealth and sophistication, yet adaptable to blend into various social settings within the black market.

### Personalities and Masks

1. **The Merchant**: A shrewd and calculating trader. This mask is adorned with gold accents and intricate designs, exuding an aura of opulence.
2. **The Scholar**: An erudite and knowledgeable persona. This mask is simple, with subtle engravings of ancient symbols.
3. **The Enforcer**: A ruthless and intimidating figure. The mask for this persona is dark and foreboding, with menacing features.
4. **The Charmer**: A charismatic and persuasive identity. This mask is elegant and stylish, designed to catch the eye.

### Abilities

- **Expert Deception**: Mastery of disguises and personas, making him almost impossible to pin down or deceive.
- **Vast Network**: Connections throughout the Underdark and beyond, allowing him to obtain rare items and information.
- **Arcane Knowledge**: Deep knowledge of arcane rituals and artifacts in his Scholar persona.
- **Combat Skills**: Skilled fighter in his Enforcer persona, adept at both physical combat and intimidation tactics.

### Skills

- **Deception +18**
- **Persuasion +15**
- **Sleight of Hand +17**
- **Stealth +19**
- **Investigation +14**
- **Arcana +12**

### Features and Traits

- **Sneak Attack**: Deals an additional 14d6 damage when attacking with advantage or targeting a creature that is surprised.
- **Spellcasting (Arcane Trickster)**: The Broker can cast spells using his intelligence. Notable spells include Invisibility, Disguise Self, and Charm Person.
- **Evasion**: Takes no damage on a successful Dexterity saving throw, half damage on a failed save.
- **Uncanny Dodge**: Can halve the damage of an attack that he can see.
- **Reliable Talent**: The Broker can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10 when making an ability check.
- **Slippery Mind**: Gain proficiency in Wisdom saving throws.
- **Elusive**: Can't be caught flat-footed or surprised.
- **Stroke of Luck**: Can turn an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw into a critical success once per short rest.

### Equipment

- **+3 Dagger**: Infused with dark magic, allowing it to bypass magical defenses.
- **Ring of Invisibility**: Grants invisibility at will.
- **Cloak of Elvenkind**: Provides advantage on Stealth checks.
- **Bag of Holding**: Contains various masks, trinkets, and magical items.
- **Mask Collection**: Each mask provides different abilities and influences his behavior.

### Role in the Campaign

In the second main quest, "The Masked Deception," the players are tasked with unmasking the true mastermind behind the ambush that framed the drow and incited conflict at the trading crossroads. They discover that The Broker, with his many personas, is intricately involved in the conspiracy, manipulating events from the shadows.

### Key Interactions

1. **Initial Contact**: The players first hear about The Broker through rumors in Vragathul, learning of his reputation as a purveyor of rare items and forbidden knowledge.
2. **Negotiation**: To gain crucial information or items, the players must engage with The Broker in his Merchant persona, navigating a web of deals and trades.
3. **Discovery**: Through a series of encounters, the players uncover the true nature of The Broker’s involvement in the conspiracy, ultimately leading to a confrontation where they must decide how to deal with this multifaceted adversary.

### Connections

- **Shadowed Parliament**: The Broker has ties with this secretive organization, often acting as their intermediary and facilitator.
- **The Black Market**: A prominent figure within Vragathul's black market, The Broker controls much of the trade in rare and illicit items.
- **Various Contacts**: The Broker's network includes informants, spies, and other underworld figures who provide him with information and resources.

### The Masks' Influence

The masks The Broker wears are not just for show; they grant him different abilities and alter his behavior. Each mask's influence reflects the personality it represents, making The Broker a formidable and unpredictable opponent. The players must adapt their strategies depending on which mask The Broker dons during their interactions.

By structuring The Broker like this, we create a rich and complex NPC who serves as both a formidable adversary and a key player in the unfolding campaign.
