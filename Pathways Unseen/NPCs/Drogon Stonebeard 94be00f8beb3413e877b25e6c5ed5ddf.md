# Drogon Stonebeard

Title: Viceroy of Ulfgar
Level: 20
Voice/Style: Stern, authoritative, with a deep and commanding tone.
GM Description and Info: Drogon Stonebeard is the linchpin of Ulfgar's governance, embodying the essence of dwarven resilience and leadership. As the highest-ranking official, he bears the weight of the city's fate on his broad shoulders. His tactical acumen and unyielding dedication to Ulfgar's prosperity and security have earned him the unwavering loyalty of its citizens. Drogon’s governance is characterized by a blend of strict discipline and deep respect for the age-old traditions of the Underdark. His policies are meticulously crafted to ensure the stability and growth of Ulfgar, often involving intricate strategies that balance defense, trade, and diplomacy.

Drogon's background as a former miner and militia leader has equipped him with invaluable insights into the city's workings, both above and below ground. His strategic mind is constantly at work, devising plans to counter potential threats and exploit opportunities for Ulfgar's benefit. His leadership style is hands-on, often seen inspecting the mines or training with the militia, which keeps him grounded and connected with the daily lives of his people. This practical approach, combined with his deep-seated knowledge of the city's resources and geography, makes him an adept problem-solver, capable of swift and decisive action when needed.

Despite his stern exterior, Drogon is not without a softer side. He harbors a profound sense of duty and compassion for his kin, which drives his relentless pursuit of their well-being. His private moments reveal a leader who is deeply introspective, often seeking counsel from the ancient texts and relics that tell the story of Ulfgar’s past. These relics serve as a source of strength and wisdom, guiding his decisions and reinforcing his commitment to the city's legacy. Drogon’s foresight extends beyond immediate concerns, as he is constantly planning for the future, ensuring that Ulfgar remains a bastion of dwarven heritage and a beacon of stability in the tumultuous Underdark.
Player Description: Drogon Stonebeard cuts an imposing figure, his very presence exuding authority and command. Standing tall for a dwarf, his physique is a testament to a life of hard labor and relentless training. His skin, weathered and marked by the scars of countless battles, tells a story of resilience and survival. Drogon's eyes are a piercing blue, sharp and calculating, reflecting a mind that is always several steps ahead. These eyes, often glinting with a cold, steely resolve, can shift from a gaze that seems to pierce through deception to one that reassures and commands loyalty.

His facial features are stern and chiseled, framed by a thick, braided beard that flows like a river of silver, a testament to his age and wisdom. His bald head only adds to his formidable appearance, highlighting the intricate tattoos that mark him as a leader among his people. Drogon’s voice, deep and resonant, carries the weight of authority and the wisdom of years, able to inspire his troops and intimidate his foes with equal measure.

Drogon's armor is a masterpiece, crafted from the finest metals that Ulfgar's legendary forges can produce. It gleams with an almost otherworldly sheen, etched with runes and symbols of protection and power. This armor not only serves as a defense but also as a symbol of his status and the city's craftsmanship. His warhammer, always within reach, is an extension of his will, a tool of both governance and battle.

When Drogon speaks, his words are measured and deliberate, each one chosen to convey his intent with clarity and purpose. He commands the room with his presence, his tone a blend of RA Salvatore’s narrative depth, Matt Mercer’s dynamic character portrayal, and Tolkien’s epic gravitas. To his allies, he is a pillar of strength and wisdom; to his enemies, a formidable and unyielding opponent.

In his interactions with the players, Drogon embodies the spirit of Ulfgar – strong, steadfast, and deeply rooted in tradition. His missions and requests are framed with a sense of urgency and importance, as he entrusts the characters with tasks that could alter the fate of the city. Whether he is recounting the dire situation of his nephew’s lost expedition or the volatile tensions at the trading crossroads, Drogon's presence and words leave an indelible mark, compelling the players to rise to the challenge and prove their mettle.

Org. Explanation: Ulfgar Militia: The primary defense force of Ulfgar, responsible for maintaining security within the city and its immediate surroundings.

Ulfgar Mining Guild: Oversees all mining operations in Ulfgar, ensuring the steady supply of minerals and precious metals.

The Shadowed Axes: A secretive organization that Drogon uses to gather intelligence and conduct covert operations. They are his eyes and ears, operating in the shadows to protect Ulfgar from unseen threats.

The Deepforge Brotherhood: An ancient and secretive group of master forgers and enchanters who create powerful weapons and artifacts for Ulfgar’s elite warriors. Drogon maintains close ties with this organization to ensure the city's champions are well-equipped.
Addl. Info: Drogon's leadership has seen Ulfgar through numerous challenges, including internal strife, external threats, and economic upheaval. His policies focus on strengthening defenses, fostering trade relationships, and promoting innovation in the city's forges and workshops. Drogon’s involvement in secret organizations allows him to maintain a tight grip on the city's security and ensures he is always one step ahead of potential threats.
Allegiance: Karnuk, Phoenix's Respite (Form. Neverwinter), Vragathul
Notable Relationship NPCs: Master Blacksmith of Karnuk, Scholars of Phoenix's Respite, Viceroy Xarath of Vragathul
Religion: The Silent Vigil
Relgion Cont.: Adapting to the Silence:

• The Silent Vigil: With the gods having fallen silent, Drogon, like many dwarves, has had to adapt his faith. The Silent Vigil is a practice that has emerged among dwarven communities, where followers continue to honor Moradin's teachings through their actions and traditions, even in the absence of direct divine guidance. This practice involves maintaining the forges, continuing to craft with excellence, and upholding the values of honor, duty, and perseverance.

• Reverence for Ancestors: In addition to honoring Moradin, Drogon places a strong emphasis on the veneration of ancestors. This practice involves commemorating the deeds and wisdom of past generations, drawing strength from their legacy. Ancestor worship provides a source of spiritual support and continuity, reinforcing the communal bonds within Ulfgar.

Current Religious Practices:

• Rituals and Ceremonies: Despite the silence of the gods, rituals and ceremonies dedicated to Moradin and the ancestors are still regularly performed in Ulfgar. These include the Blessing of the Forge, where craftsmen dedicate their work to Moradin, and the Honoring of the Ancestors, where stories of great deeds and sacrifices are recounted during communal gatherings.

• Symbols and Relics: Drogon ensures that the symbols of Moradin, such as the hammer and anvil, are prominently displayed within Ulfgar. Relics and artifacts from the time when the gods were active are preserved and revered, serving as reminders of the divine legacy and the principles that guide the community.
Personal Beliefs:

• Faith in Action: For Drogon, faith is expressed through action. By leading Ulfgar with integrity, fostering craftsmanship, and protecting his people, he believes he is fulfilling Moradin's will and honoring his ancestors. This pragmatic approach to faith helps him stay grounded and focused on the tangible aspects of leadership and community building.

• Hope for the Future: While the gods' silence is a source of sorrow, Drogon holds onto hope that one day they may speak again. Until then, he remains steadfast in his duties, drawing strength from the enduring spirit of his people and the timeless teachings of Moradin.

Summary
Drogon Stonebeard's religious practices and beliefs are deeply rooted in the traditional worship of Moradin, adapted to a world where the gods have fallen silent. Through the Silent Vigil and reverence for his ancestors, Drogon continues to uphold the values of his faith, expressing his devotion through his actions as a leader and craftsman. This pragmatic and resilient approach to religion helps him guide Ulfgar with strength and integrity, honoring the divine legacy in a time of uncertainty.

## Allegiance’s

### Cities with Deep Allegiance

1. **Vragathul**
    - **Reason for Allegiance**: Vragathul is a massive underground metropolis that serves as a central trading hub within the Underdark. Given Ulfgar's economic prosperity driven by mining and crafting, it is likely that there are strong trade relations between Ulfgar and Vragathul. Drogan would prioritize maintaining and strengthening this relationship to ensure a steady flow of goods and resources, which is crucial for both cities' economies.
2. **Karnuk**
    - **Reason for Allegiance**: Karnuk, known for its skilled blacksmiths and exceptional craftsmanship, shares similar values and industries with Ulfgar. The two cities likely have a strong alliance based on mutual respect for craftsmanship and trade. Drogan would see Karnuk as a vital ally in maintaining the quality and reputation of Ulfgar's own production.
3. **Phoenix's Respite (Formerly Neverwinter)**
    - **Reason for Allegiance**: Phoenix's Respite focuses on trade of resources crucial for the Underdark's defense efforts and has risen anew, embodying hope and perseverance. Drogan would value an alliance with this city to bolster Ulfgar's defenses and to gain access to valuable resources. Additionally, the city's focus on uncovering ancient magic aligns with Ulfgar's interest in innovation and arcane advancements.

### Key Individuals with Deep Allegiance

1. **Viceroy Xarath of Vragathul**
    - **Reason for Allegiance**: As a key figure in the High Council of Vragathul, Viceroy Xarath would be an essential ally for Drogan. The two leaders likely collaborate on matters of trade, defense, and political strategy, strengthening the ties between their cities and ensuring mutual prosperity and security.
2. **Master Blacksmiths of Karnuk**
    - **Reason for Allegiance**: The master craftsmen and blacksmiths of Karnuk would be valuable allies for Drogan, providing expertise and collaboration in forging high-quality weapons and armor. This relationship would enhance Ulfgar's reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and ensure a steady supply of superior goods.
3. **Scholars and Mages of Phoenix's Respite**
    - **Reason for Allegiance**: The scholars and mages specializing in ancient magic and enchanted defenses in Phoenix's Respite would be crucial allies for Drogan. Their knowledge and expertise would help Ulfgar innovate and strengthen its own magical defenses and arcane capabilities.
