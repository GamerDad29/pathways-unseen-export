# The Slave Rescue

## Quest Overvieww

## Introduction

- **Setting**: A brutal slave market deep within Vragathul's underground.
- **Hook**: Garruk Blackmane, the leader of the Iron Circle, asks the players to assist in rescuing a group of slaves. While he leads the assault, the players must create a diversion and free the captives.

### Key Locations

- **The Slave Market**:
    - Scene: The market is a grim place, filled with the sounds of chains and the cries of the enslaved. Guards patrol the area, watching for any sign of trouble.
    - Hidden threat: The market is guarded by slavers (AC 14, HP 40) and trained beasts (AC 15, HP 45), with a fortified command post at the center.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: The market is heavily guarded, with reinforced checkpoints and aggressive guards.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: A group of slavers spots the players and attempts to capture them.
3-4: A hidden trap door leads to an underground cell filled with additional captives.
5-6: A patrolling beast becomes aware of the players and begins tracking them.
7-8: The players find a secret stash of weapons that can be used against the slavers.
9-10: The players overhear a conversation revealing the slavers' leader's location.

### Clues & Progression

- The players discover a ledger detailing the movement of slaves and information on the slavers' operations, providing them with leverage against the market's overseers.
