## Quest Overview

# **Smuggler's Run**

### Introduction

- **Setting**: The treacherous smuggling routes leading into Vragathul.
- **Hook**: Captain Finnegan Greybeard, leader of the Rusty Anchor, hires the players to smuggle rare alchemical substances into Vragathul. The players must avoid detection and outmaneuver patrols.

### Key Locations

- **The Smuggling Route**:
    - Scene: The route is a maze of tunnels and hidden paths, with checkpoints and patrols that must be avoided.
    - Hidden threat: The route is patrolled by guards (AC 15, HP 40) and traps (DC 16 to detect/disarm) set to catch smugglers.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: The smuggling route is fraught with dangers, including patrols, traps, and natural hazards.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: A patrol spots the players and gives chase.
3-4: The players find a hidden cache of supplies left by previous smugglers.
5-6: The players encounter a natural hazard, such as a cave-in or flood.
7-8: The players find a secret passage that bypasses a heavily guarded checkpoint.
9-10: The players overhear a conversation revealing the location of an upcoming patrol.

### Clues & Progression

- The players discover a map of the smuggling route, revealing hidden paths and shortcuts. They also find information on upcoming patrols and checkpoints, allowing them to plan their route more effectively.
