# Mystery of the Mind

## Introduction

- **Setting**: The opulent residences of Vragathul's wealthy elite.
- **Hook**: Seraphine Moonwhisper, leader of the Twisted Court, suspects a group of wealthy patrons are resisting her mind control. She tasks the players with investigating how they are protecting themselves and neutralizing the threat.

### Key Locations

- **The Noble Residences**:
    - Scene: These grand homes are filled with luxury and protected by both physical and magical means. The residents are powerful and paranoid.
    - Hidden threat: The residences are guarded by enchanted wards (DC 18 to detect/disarm), and mind-controlled servants (AC 14, HP 30) who will fight to protect their masters.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: The residences are heavily fortified with both magical and physical defenses.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: A mind-controlled servant becomes suspicious and reports the players to their master.
3-4: A magical trap is triggered, alerting the residence's owner.
5-6: The players find a hidden chamber filled with arcane artifacts.
7-8: The players overhear a conversation that reveals the source of the residents' resistance.
9-10: The players uncover a hidden passage leading to the master of the house's study.

### Clues & Progression

- The players find evidence of an artifact or ritual being used to protect the residents from mind control. They also discover documents hinting at the existence of a secret cabal resisting the Twisted Court's influence.
