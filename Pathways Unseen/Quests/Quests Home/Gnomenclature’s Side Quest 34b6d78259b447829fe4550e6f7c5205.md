# Prologue Quest - Gnomenclature

**Prologue Quest**: "The Echoes of Scribing"

**Objective**: Enhance Gnomenclature's clerical abilities by uncovering ancient texts and scrolls that detail lost divine knowledge and powerful rituals.

**Ongoing Quest**: Gnomenclature must seek out forgotten libraries, hidden archives, and ancient texts to gain new spells, rituals, and divine insights that can aid the party in their adventures.

## Quest Overview

**Title**: The Echoes of Scribing
**Campaign Significance**: This prologue quest is essential for Gnomenclature's development, providing him with new spells, powerful rituals, and connections with divine scholars. It integrates with the larger campaign by introducing key NPCs and locations that will be important later.

## Quest Details

### 1. Investigate the Ancient Battlefield

### Introduction

- **Setting**: An ancient battlefield in the depths of the Underdark, filled with remnants of a long-forgotten war.
- **Hook**: Gnomenclature and his party discover an ancient archive buried beneath the ruins.

### Key Locations

- **Battlefield Ruins**:
    - Scene: Scattered debris, broken weapons, and lingering spirits of the fallen.
    - Hidden threat: Spectral guardians and animated scrolls.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: Generally safe but haunted by undead at the edges.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Wandering Skeleton
3-4: Lost Wight
5-6: Undead Warhorse
7-8: Ghostly Warrior
9-10: Treasure Hunter (NPC seeking relics)

### Clues & Progression

- Discover clues pointing towards the Hidden Sanctuary, leading to the next stage.

### 2. Navigate the Hidden Sanctuary

### Introduction

- **Setting**: A hidden, bustling sanctuary in the city of Vragathul.
- **Situation**: Gnomenclature must navigate the city's underground to find a hidden sanctuary of knowledge.

### Key NPCs

- **The Keeper**: A mysterious figure with valuable divine knowledge.

### Objectives

- Gain access to the Hidden Sanctuary.
- Negotiate for divine relics and hidden texts.
- Uncover hidden threats or double-crosses.

### Challenges

- Persuasion and Intimidation checks to gather information.
- Combat with cultists or rival seekers.

### 3. Explore the Sacred Library

### Setting

- **Description**: An ancient ruin or forgotten cavern with dusty shelves and hidden knowledge.
- **Situation**: Uncover the instructions for powerful divine rituals and spells amidst guardians and traps.

### Objectives

- Find and decipher the divine texts.
- Avoid or defeat magical guardians.

### Methods

1. Skill checks to navigate traps.
2. Combat with constructs or magical wards.
3. Research to uncover hidden secrets.

### 4. Access the Elemental Shrine

### Setting

- **Description**: The Elemental Shrine in the city of Karnuk, known for its divine and elemental power.
- **Situation**: Gain access to the shrine to enhance Gnomenclature’s divine abilities.

### Objectives

- Gain mentorship from the shrine keeper.
- Complete the enhancement ritual.

### Challenges

- Skill challenges in divine magic and ritual practices.
- Bonding with the mentor.

### 5. Encounter the Giant Phylactery

### Introduction

- **Setting**: Deep within the Underdark, you stumble upon a hidden cave where the heart-gems of ancient giants are stored.
- **Hook**: Discover the phylactery and the potential to imbue Gnomenclature's shield with powerful elemental or magical properties.

### Key Locations

- **Phylactery Chamber**:
    - Scene: A grand, ancient hall filled with glowing gems of various colors.
    - Hidden threat: Elemental guardians or traps protecting the gems.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: Relatively safe, but with powerful guardians at the edges.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Fire Elemental
3-4: Earth Elemental
5-6: Water Elemental
7-8: Air Elemental
9-10: Ancient Guardian (Construct)

### Clues & Progression

- Choose a giant's gem to imbue into Gnomenclature's shield, each offering unique abilities.

### Heart-Gems of the Giants

1. **Fire Giant Heart-Gem (Inferno Core)**
    - **Description**: Glows with an inner flame, warm to the touch.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to unleash a burst of radiant fire.
    - **Ability**: **Inferno Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can release a burst of radiant fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC 13) or take 3d6 radiant fire damage, or half as much on a successful save. The shield also allows Gnomenclature to channel this energy to gain temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt.
2. **Frost Giant Heart-Gem (Glacier Core)**
    - **Description**: Icy blue, cold to the touch, emitting a frosty mist.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to freeze and protect.
    - **Ability**: **Glacial Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can create a protective barrier around an ally. The barrier grants temporary hit points equal to twice Gnomenclature's level and resistance to all damage types for 1 minute.
3. **Storm Giant Heart-Gem (Tempest Core)**
    - **Description**: Crackles with electrical energy, buzzing faintly.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to call down celestial lightning.
    - **Ability**: **Tempest Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can call down a bolt of celestial lightning to strike a target within 30 feet. The target must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC 13) or take 3d8 lightning damage, or half as much on a successful save. The shield also allows Gnomenclature to blind the target for 1 round.
4. **Stone Giant Heart-Gem (Earth Core)**
    - **Description**: Heavy and solid, with a deep earthy hue.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to create a seismic shockwave.
    - **Ability**: **Tremor Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can create a seismic shockwave in a 10-foot radius. Each creature in that area must make a Strength saving throw (DC 13) or be knocked prone and take 3d6 bludgeoning damage. The shield also allows Gnomenclature to create difficult terrain in the area for 1 minute.
5. **Cloud Giant Heart-Gem (Nimbus Core)**
    - **Description**: Light and airy, shifting colors like the sky.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to create a cloud of divine mist.
    - **Ability**: **Nimbus Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can create a 20-foot radius cloud of divine mist that provides heavy obscurement for 1 minute. The shield also allows Gnomenclature to teleport up to 30 feet as a bonus action while within the mist.
6. **Hill Giant Heart-Gem (Boulder Core)**
    - **Description**: Rough and rugged, brown with a rocky texture.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to increase its weight and force.
    - **Ability**: **Boulder Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can create a powerful shockwave that deals an additional 2d8 bludgeoning damage and pushes the target 10 feet away. The shield also grants Gnomenclature temporary hit points equal to twice his level.
7. **Rune Giant Heart-Gem (Mystic Core)**
    - **Description**: Etched with ancient runes, glowing faintly.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to cast minor divine spells.
    - **Ability**: **Runic Shield**: The shield can cast Shield or Magic Missile once per long rest, using Gnomenclature’s spellcasting ability. The shield also grants advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
8. **Titan Heart-Gem (Colossus Core)**
    - **Description**: Larger and more potent, radiating immense power.
    - **Effect**: Grants the shield the ability to temporarily enlarge.
    - **Ability**: **Colossus Shield**: Once per long rest, the shield can grow to large size for 1 minute. While enlarged, the shield grants advantage on Strength checks and saving throws, its melee weapon attacks deal an extra 1d4 damage, and it has advantage on checks to grapple or shove a creature.
