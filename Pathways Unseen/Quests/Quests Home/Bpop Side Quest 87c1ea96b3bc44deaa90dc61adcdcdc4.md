# Prologue Quest - Bpop

**Prologue Quest**: "The Master Tinker's Secret"

**Objective**: Enhance Bpop's tinkering abilities and discover new blueprints and schematics to improve his inventions and constructs.

**Ongoing Quest**: Bpop must seek out rare components, ancient schematics, and master tinkerers to gain new knowledge and improve his creations, including his Steel Defender.

## Quest Overview

**Title**: The Master Tinker's Secret

## Quest Details

### 1. Investigate the Ancient Battlefield

### Introduction

- **Setting**: An ancient battlefield in the depths of the Underdark, filled with remnants of a long-forgotten war.
- **Hook**: Bpop and his party discover ancient schematics and rare components amidst the debris.

### Key Locations

- **Battlefield Ruins**:
    - Scene: Scattered debris, broken weapons, and lingering spirits of the fallen.
    - Hidden threat: Spectral guardians and animated armors.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: Generally safe but haunted by undead at the edges.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Wandering Skeleton
3-4: Lost Wight
5-6: Undead Warhorse
7-8: Ghostly Warrior
9-10: Treasure Hunter (NPC seeking relics)

### Clues & Progression

### 2. Navigate the Black Market

### Introduction

- **Setting**: A hidden, bustling market in the city of Vragathul.
- **Situation**: Bpop and his party must negotiate with "The Broker" for rare components and hidden schematics.

### Key NPCs

- **The Broker**: A mysterious figure with valuable information.

### Objectives

- Gain access to the Black Market.
- Negotiate for components and hidden schematics.
- Uncover hidden threats or double-crosses.

### Challenges

- Persuasion and Intimidation checks to gather information.
- Combat with hired thugs or rival buyers.

### 3. Explore the Forbidden Library

### Setting

- **Description**: An ancient ruin or forgotten cavern with dusty shelves and hidden knowledge.
- **Situation**: Uncover the instructions for powerful constructs and gadgets amidst guardians and traps.

### Objectives

- Find and decipher the crafting instructions.
- Avoid or defeat magical guardians.

### Methods

1. Skill checks to navigate traps.
2. Combat with constructs or magical wards.
3. Research to uncover hidden secrets.

### 4. Access the Elemental Forge

### Objectives

- Complete the enhancement ritual.

### Challenges

- Skill challenges in crafting and arcane lore.
- Bonding with the mentor.

### 5. Encounter the Giant Phylactery

### Introduction

- **Setting**: Deep within the Underdark, you stumble upon a hidden chamber where the heart-gems of ancient giants are stored.
- **Hook**: Discover the phylactery and the potential to imbue Bpop's constructs with powerful elemental or magical properties.

### Key Locations

- **Phylactery Chamber**:
    - Scene: A grand, ancient hall filled with glowing gems of various colors.
    - Hidden threat: Elemental guardians or traps protecting the gems.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: Relatively safe, but with powerful guardians at the edges.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Fire Elemental
3-4: Earth Elemental
5-6: Water Elemental
7-8: Air Elemental
9-10: Ancient Guardian (Construct)

### Clues & Progression

- Choose a giant's gem to imbue into Bpop's construct, each offering unique abilities.

### Heart-Gems of the Giants

1. **Fire Giant Heart-Gem (Inferno Core)**
    - **Description**: Glows with an inner fire, hot to the touch.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to unleash a burst of flame.
    - **Ability**: **Inferno Burst**: Once per long rest, the construct can release a burst of fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC 13) or take 3d6 fire damage, or half as much on a successful save. The construct also gains resistance to fire damage.
2. **Frost Giant Heart-Gem (Glacier Core)**
    - **Description**: Icy blue, cold and emits a frosty mist.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to freeze enemies.
    - **Ability**: **Glacial Strike**: Once per long rest, the construct can make a melee attack that deals an additional 2d6 cold damage and forces the target to make a Constitution saving throw (DC 13) or be frozen in place (paralyzed) until the end of its next turn. The construct also gains resistance to cold damage.
3. **Storm Giant Heart-Gem (Tempest Core)**
    - **Description**: Crackles with electrical energy, buzzing faintly.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to call down lightning.
    - **Ability**: **Lightning Strike**: Once per long rest, the construct can call down a lightning bolt to strike a target within 30 feet. The target must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC 13) or take 3d8 lightning damage, or half as much on a successful save. The construct also gains resistance to lightning damage.
4. **Stone Giant Heart-Gem (Earth Core)**
    - **Description**: Heavy and solid, with a deep earthy hue.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to create tremors.
    - **Ability**: **Tremor Slam**: Once per long rest, the construct can slam the ground, creating a tremor in a 10-foot radius. Each creature in that area must make a Strength saving throw (DC 13) or be knocked prone. The construct also gains advantage on checks to avoid being moved or knocked prone.
5. **Cloud Giant Heart-Gem (Nimbus Core)**
    - **Description**: Light and airy, shifting colors like the sky.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to create a cloud of mist.
    - **Ability**: **Mist Shroud**: Once per long rest, the construct can create a 20-foot radius cloud of mist that provides heavy obscurement for 1 minute. The construct can see through this mist as if it were clear. The construct also gains the ability to teleport up to 30 feet as a bonus action while within the mist.
6. **Hill Giant Heart-Gem (Boulder Core)**
    - **Description**: Rough and rugged, brown with a rocky texture.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to increase its weight.
    - **Ability**: **Boulder Smash**: Once per long rest, the construct can make a melee attack that deals an additional 2d8 bludgeoning damage and pushes the target 10 feet away. The construct also gains temporary hit points equal to its level when it uses this ability.
7. **Rune Giant Heart-Gem (Mystic Core)**
    - **Description**: Etched with ancient runes, glowing faintly.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to cast minor spells.
    - **Ability**: **Runic Magic**: The construct can cast Shield or Magic Missile once per long rest, using Bpop’s spellcasting ability. The construct also gains advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
8. **Titan Heart-Gem (Colossus Core)**
    - **Description**: Larger and more potent, radiating immense power.
    - **Effect**: Grants the construct the ability to temporarily enlarge.
    - **Ability**: **Colossus Form**: Once per long rest, the construct can grow to large size for 1 minute. While enlarged, the construct gains advantage on Strength checks and saving throws, its melee weapon attacks deal an extra 1d4 damage, and it has advantage on checks to grapple or shove a creature.
