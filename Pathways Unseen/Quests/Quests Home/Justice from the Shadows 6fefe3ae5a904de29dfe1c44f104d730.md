# Justice from the Shadows

## Quest Overvieww

# Introduction

- **Setting**: The corrupt official's mansion, a symbol of wealth and power within Vragathul.
- **Hook**: Kaelin Shadowbane, leader of the Silent Blade, tasks the players with eliminating a corrupt official who has been oppressing the people. The players must use stealth and cunning to succeed.

### Key Locations

- **The Official's Mansion**:
    - Scene: The mansion is lavish, filled with opulent decorations and guarded by loyal soldiers. The official himself is paranoid and well-protected.
    - Hidden threat: The mansion is guarded by elite soldiers (AC 16, HP 50) and hidden traps (DC 17 to detect/disarm), with the official employing personal bodyguards (AC 17, HP 60).

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: The mansion is heavily guarded, with elite soldiers and numerous traps.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: A patrol discovers the players and raises the alarm.
3-4: The players find a hidden armory filled with weapons and armor.
5-6: The players discover a secret passage leading directly to the official's quarters.
7-8: The players find a hidden stash of incriminating documents.
9-10: The players overhear a conversation revealing the official's weaknesses.

### Clues & Progression

- The players uncover documents revealing the official's corrupt dealings and alliances. They also find evidence that can be used to blackmail or expose the official's crimes.
