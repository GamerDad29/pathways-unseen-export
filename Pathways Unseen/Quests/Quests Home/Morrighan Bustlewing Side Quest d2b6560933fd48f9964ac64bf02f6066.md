## Prologue Quest - Morrighan Bustlewing

Prologue Quest: "The Arcane Ascension"
Objective: Enhance Morrighan's arcane abilities and deepen her connection to her patron by uncovering powerful eldritch secrets and artifacts.

Ongoing Quest: Morrighan must seek out rare tomes, hidden rituals, and ancient artifacts to gain new powers and spells, improving her bond with her patron.

## Quest Overview

**Title**: The Arcane Ascension
**Campaign Significance**: This prologue quest is crucial for Morrighan's character development, providing her with unique spells, powerful artifacts, and deepening her connection with her patron. It also ties into the larger campaign by introducing significant NPCs and locations.

## Quest Details

### 1. Investigate the Ancient Battlefield

### Introduction

- **Setting**: An ancient battlefield in the depths of the Underdark, filled with remnants of a long-forgotten war.
- **Hook**: Morrighan and her party discover enchanted metal shards and mystical gemstones amidst the debris.

### Key Locations

- **Battlefield Ruins**:
    - Scene: Scattered debris, broken weapons, and lingering spirits of the fallen.
    - Hidden threat: Spectral guardians and animated armors.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: Generally safe but haunted by undead at the edges.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Wandering Skeleton
3-4: Lost Wight
5-6: Undead Warhorse
7-8: Ghostly Warrior
9-10: Treasure Hunter (NPC seeking relics)

### Clues & Progression

- Discover clues pointing towards the Forbidden Library, leading to the next stage.

### 2. Navigate the Black Market

### Introduction

- **Setting**: A hidden, bustling market in the city of Vragathul.
- **Situation**: Morrighan and her party must negotiate with "The Broker" for rare components and hidden rituals.

### Key NPCs

- **The Broker**: A mysterious figure with valuable information.

### Objectives

- Gain access to the Black Market.
- Negotiate for components and hidden rituals.
- Uncover hidden threats or double-crosses.

### Challenges

- Persuasion and Intimidation checks to gather information.
- Combat with hired thugs or rival buyers.

### 3. Explore the Forbidden Library

### Setting

- **Description**: An ancient ruin or forgotten cavern with dusty shelves and hidden knowledge.
- **Situation**: Uncover the instructions for powerful eldritch spells and rituals amidst guardians and traps.

### Objectives

- Find and decipher the crafting instructions.
- Avoid or defeat magical guardians.

### Methods

1. Skill checks to navigate traps.
2. Combat with constructs or magical wards.
3. Research to uncover hidden secrets.

### 4. Access the Elemental Forge

### Setting

- **Description**: The workshop of a reclusive half-drow artificer.
- **Situation**: Learn the final steps in crafting from a skilled mentor.

### Objectives

- Gain mentorship from the artificer.
- Complete the arcane enhancement ritual.

### Challenges

- Skill challenges in crafting and arcane lore.
- Bonding with the mentor.

### 5. Encounter the Giant Phylactery

### Introduction

- **Setting**: Deep within the Underdark, you stumble upon a hidden chamber where the heart-gems of ancient giants are stored.
- **Hook**: Discover the phylactery and the potential to imbue Morrighan's arcane focus with powerful elemental or magical properties.

### Key Locations

- **Phylactery Chamber**:
    - Scene: A grand, ancient hall filled with glowing gems of various colors.
    - Hidden threat: Elemental guardians or traps protecting the gems.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: Relatively safe, but with powerful guardians at the edges.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Fire Elemental
3-4: Earth Elemental
5-6: Water Elemental
7-8: Air Elemental
9-10: Ancient Guardian (Construct)

### Clues & Progression

- Choose a giant's gem to imbue into Morrighan's arcane focus, each offering unique abilities.

### Heart-Gems of the Giants

1. **Fire Giant Heart-Gem (Inferno Core)**
    - **Description**: Glows with an inner fire, hot to the touch.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to unleash a burst of flame, dealing extra fire damage on hit and creating a fiery explosion once per day.
2. **Frost Giant Heart-Gem (Glacier Core)**
    - **Description**: Icy blue, cold and emits a frosty mist.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to freeze enemies on a critical hit, dealing extra cold damage and reducing the target's speed.
3. **Storm Giant Heart-Gem (Tempest Core)**
    - **Description**: Crackles with electrical energy, buzzing faintly.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to call down lightning, dealing additional lightning damage and stunning the target once per day.
4. **Stone Giant Heart-Gem (Earth Core)**
    - **Description**: Heavy and solid, with a deep earthy hue.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to create tremors, knocking back enemies and reducing their movement speed.
5. **Cloud Giant Heart-Gem (Nimbus Core)**
    - **Description**: Light and airy, shifting colors like the sky.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to create a cloud of mist, providing concealment and allowing Morrighan to teleport short distances.
6. **Hill Giant Heart-Gem (Boulder Core)**
    - **Description**: Rough and rugged, brown with a rocky texture.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to increase its weight, dealing massive bludgeoning damage and pushing enemies back.
7. **Rune Giant Heart-Gem (Mystic Core)**
    - **Description**: Etched with ancient runes, glowing faintly.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to cast minor spells, such as Shield or Magic Missile, once per day.
8. **Titan Heart-Gem (Colossus Core)**
    - **Description**: Larger and more potent, radiating immense power.
    - **Power**: Grants the focus the ability to temporarily enlarge Morrighan, increasing her spellcasting range and potency.

## Main Antagonists

1. **Nera the Shadowblade** (Rogue Assassin)
    - Special: Expert in stealth and ambush tactics, uses poisoned blades.
2. **Vorrik the Enchanter** (Arcane Trickster)
    - Special: Uses illusion magic to deceive and trap opponents.
3. **Rendrak the Brute** (Barbarian)
    - Special: Uses brute strength and relentless attacks, difficult to take down.
4. **Sylra the Whisperer** (Warlock)
    - Special: Manipulates shadows and dark magic, causing fear and confusion.
5. **Torgan the Tracker** (Ranger)
    - Special: Expert in tracking and hunting, uses traps and ranged attacks effectively.

## Aftermath & Resolutions

- **Enhanced Arcane Focus**: Successfully enhance Morrighan's arcane focus with a chosen heart-gem.
- **Allies and Contacts**: Gain new allies like the Arcane Artificer and "The Broker."

## Rewards & Consequences

- **Experience**: 1,800 XP to reach level 4.
- **Enhanced Arcane Focus**: Choose from Fire, Frost, Storm, Stone, Cloud, Hill, Rune, or Titan heart-gems.
- **Allies and Contacts**: Gain new allies like the Arcane Artificer and "The Broker."

## Fey Court Contact

### Introduction

During Morrighan's quest, she encounters a member of the Fey Court who offers guidance and aid. This contact serves as a bridge between Morrighan and her patron, providing her with unique insights and tasks that further her connection to the Fey Court.

### Key NPC

**Lirien Moonwhisper** (Fey Court Envoy)

- **Race**: Eladrin (Fey Elf)
- **Class**: Druid (Circle of Dreams)
- **Appearance**: Lirien has an ethereal presence, with hair that shimmers like moonlight and eyes that glimmer with the colors of the feywild.
- **Role**: Lirien provides Morrighan with tasks and guidance, helping her deepen her connection to her patron and the Fey Court.
- **Personality**: Lirien is wise and enigmatic, speaking in riddles and metaphors. They have a deep understanding of the Feywild and its magic.

### Tasks from Lirien

1. **Gather Moonlit Dew**: Morrighan must collect dew from a

rare fey plant that only blooms under the light of a full moon in the Underdark.
2. **Rescue a Fey Creature**: A mischievous sprite has been captured by Underdark slavers. Morrighan must free the sprite and return it to the Feywild.
3. **Restore a Fey Shrine**: An ancient fey shrine in the Underdark has been desecrated. Morrighan must cleanse and restore it, re-establishing a connection to the Feywild.

### Rewards from Lirien

1. **Blessing of the Fey**: Grants Morrighan an additional spell slot or a unique fey-themed spell.
2. **Feywild Trinket**: A magical item that enhances Morrighan's abilities or provides a unique boon.
3. **Fey Knowledge**: Insight into the Feywild and its mysteries, helping Morrighan understand her patron and the arcane secrets she seeks.

By integrating Lirien Moonwhisper and the Fey Court tasks into Morrighan's prologue quest, the storyline becomes richer and more intertwined with her warlock background, providing a deeper connection to her patron and the Feywild.
