# Quest Overview

**Title**: The Many Masks of Deceit

**Campaign Significance**: This quest delves deeper into the conspiracy, uncovering layers of deception and leading the characters to a mysterious NPC known as the Masked Man, who manipulates events from the shadows. It also touches on the Shadow Plague and introduces multiverse elements.

## Associated Quests

1. Lost Miners & Diplomatic Relations
2. Paths to Peace
3. The Hidden Web
4. Prologue Quests for Player Characters

## Quest Details

### 1. Discovering the Frame

### Introduction

- **Setting**: Ulfgar, within Drogan Stonebeard's chamber.
- **Hook**: Viceroy Drogan reveals that new evidence suggests the hit squad was hired to frame a specific group and escalate tensions. He also mentions strange sightings of creatures not native to the Underdark, hinting at multiverse incursions.

### Key Locations

- **The Black Market Tavern**:
    - Scene: A dimly lit tavern known for illegal dealings and covert meetings.
    - Hidden threat: Spies and informants lurking to protect their secrets. A Kender (from Krynn) can be found here, lost and curious, providing clues about the multiverse breach.

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: The tavern is filled with shady characters, making it dangerous if provoked.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: Pickpocket attempt by a mischievous Kender.
3-4: Bar brawl involving surface and Underdark factions.
5-6: Shady dealer offering rare items from other planes.
7-8: Informant with useful intel about the Shadow Plague.
9-10: Undercover guards from a rival city.

### Clues & Progression

- Obtain information leading to a mysterious contact known as the Masked Man, who is believed to have orchestrated the framing. The Kender’s presence and tales hint at larger multiverse troubles.
- **Meeting the Blacksmith**: In the tavern, the characters meet a skilled blacksmith who mentions being able to forge unique weapons. He offers to help with their prologue weapons if they can bring him specific rare materials found in the mines or Zarakzul.

### 2. The Masked Man's Game

### Introduction

- **Setting**: A secluded alley in Ulfgar.
- **Situation**: The characters meet the Masked Man, who wears different masks representing different personalities. Each personality offers a clue leading to the next stage. One personality might mention strange shadows or whispers in the dark, referencing the Shadow Plague.

### Key NPCs

- **The Masked Man**: A mysterious figure with multiple personalities, each one knowledgeable about different aspects of the conspiracy. One personality is aware of the multiverse breaches.

### Objectives

- Gather information from the Masked Man’s different personalities.
- Track down leads provided by each personality.

### Challenges

- Interacting with the unpredictable personalities of the Masked Man.
- Deciphering which clues are genuine and which are red herrings.

### 3. Tracking Down Information

### Setting

- Various locations in Ulfgar and the surrounding Underdark, including a newly discovered tunnel where the Shadow Plague’s influence is strong.

### Objectives

- **Main objective 1**: Investigate a secret meeting place in the Underdark.
- **Main objective 2**: Shake down a known informant in Zarakzul.
- **Main objective 3**: Infiltrate a hidden base of operations used by the hit squad, encountering a creature from another verse.

### Methods

1. Bribery and persuasion to gain access to restricted areas.
2. Stealth and subterfuge to gather intelligence unnoticed.
3. Direct confrontation to extract information from reluctant sources.
4. Encounter and deal with a displaced creature from the multiverse, hinting at larger disturbances.
5. **Meeting the Blacksmith Again**: In Zarakzul, the characters find the blacksmith’s contact who provides the rare materials needed for forging the prologue weapons.

### 4. Unmasking the Truth

### Investigation Elements

- **Clue or Evidence 1**: Documents found in the hit squad’s base revealing the Masked Man’s involvement and references to the Shadow Plague.
- **Clue or Evidence 2**: Testimony from a captured informant linking the Masked Man to the syndicate.
- **Clue or Evidence 3**: A hidden lair containing masks and records of the Masked Man’s activities, along with strange artifacts from other planes.

### Key Suspects/Antagonists

- **Suspect 1**: The Masked Man (various personalities)
- **Suspect 2**: A low-level syndicate member who facilitated the hiring of the hit squad.

### Challenges

- Identifying the real Masked Man among the multiple personalities.
- Overcoming the Masked Man’s final attempt to escape or mislead the characters.

## Main Antagonists

1. **The Masked Man** (Mastermind Rogue/Illusionist)
    - Special: Can switch between personalities, each with unique abilities and information. One personality is aware of the Shadow Plague and multiverse breaches.
2. **Syndicate Enforcer** (Enforcer Rogue)
    - Special: Skilled in intimidation and combat, loyal to the syndicate.
3. **Hit Squad Coordinator** (Spy Rogue)
    - Special: Expert in covert operations and evasion.
4. **Undercover Informant** (Charlatan Rogue)
    - Special: Deceptive and manipulative, provides false leads to misdirect the players.

## Aftermath & Resolutions

- Option 1: Expose the Masked Man and dismantle his network, gaining valuable allies and resources.
- Option 2: The Masked Man escapes, leaving behind clues for future confrontations, maintaining tension and intrigue.
- Option 3: The characters form an uneasy alliance with one of the Masked Man’s personalities to further their own goals.

## Rewards & Consequences

- **Rewards**: Unique magical items, valuable intelligence, increased political influence, and hints about the multiverse.
- **Consequences**: Potential retaliation from the syndicate, ongoing mistrust from the surface factions, and increased encounters with multiverse creatures.

## Additional Notes

- The Masked Man’s personalities should be distinct and memorable, providing both comic relief and serious challenges.
- Use the Masked Man to introduce new plot threads and potential future quests, keeping the players engaged and curious.
- Balance combat and investigation to maintain a dynamic and engaging quest.
- Integrate elements of the Shadow Plague and multiverse breaches to foreshadow larger events in the campaign.
- Prologue quests for the player characters can be interwoven into this quest, providing personal stakes and motivations. For example, clues about their backgrounds or personal enemies might surface during the investigation.
- **Meeting the Blacksmith**: Ensure the characters have multiple interactions with the blacksmith, tying their quest for prologue weapons to their larger mission. This not only provides tangible rewards but also deepens their connection to the setting and its inhabitants.
