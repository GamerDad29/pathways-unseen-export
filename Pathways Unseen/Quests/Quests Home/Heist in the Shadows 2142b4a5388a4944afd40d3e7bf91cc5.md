# Heist in the Shadows

## Quest Overvieww

### **Heist in the Shadows**

### Introduction

- **Setting**: A lavish estate on the outskirts of Vragathul, fortified with magical and physical defenses.
- **Hook**: Isadora Nightshade, the cunning leader of the Velvet Syndicate, contacts the players with a mission to retrieve a rare artifact hidden within a well-guarded estate. She offers them riches and rewards in return for their success.

### Key Locations

- **The Velvet Estate**:
    - Scene: The estate is a grand and opulent mansion, with lush gardens, guarded corridors, and hidden treasures. Magical wards and vigilant guards protect the valuable items inside.
    - Hidden threat: The estate is guarded by enchanted sentinels (AC 17, HP 65, magic-resistant) and elite guards (AC 16, HP 50), along with a magical trap system (DC 18 to detect/disarm).

### Exploration

- **Area Safety**: The estate is highly secure, with multiple layers of magical defenses and vigilant guards.
- **Random Encounters** (d10):
1-2: A patrolling guard becomes suspicious and begins investigating the players' presence.
3-4: The players stumble upon a hidden magical trap that must be disarmed.
5-6: A secret passage is discovered, leading to a more secure area of the estate.
7-8: The players find a hidden cache of valuables, including jewels worth 500 gp.
9-10: A coded message reveals the location of the artifact within the estate.

### Clues & Progression

- The players uncover detailed maps of the estate, revealing hidden passages and the location of the artifact. They also find documents hinting at the artifact's true power.
