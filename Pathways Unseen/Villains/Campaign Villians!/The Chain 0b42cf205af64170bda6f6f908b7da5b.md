# The Chain

![The Chain.png](The%20Chain/The_Chain.png)

The Chain is a gaunt, ancient elf who has dominated the world for over a millennium. This formidable figure ignited the 1000-year war, driven by an unyielding hatred for the Underdark and a vision to cleanse Toril of what he deems its greatest scourge. His title, "The Chain," comes from his vow: "I will chain myself to this throne until the denizens of the Underdark are no more and the scourge of Toril is vanquished." True to his word, he has remained physically chained to his throne for over a thousand years, an unbroken symbol of his relentless pursuit.

From his throne in what was once Silverymoon, now the largest and most influential city in Faerûn, The Chain rules with a cold and unrelenting hand. Though he is not unnaturally youthful, his body shows the wear of centuries, gaunt and aged, yet sustained by sheer willpower and the dark forces that seem to drive him. His presence is a haunting reminder of the lengths to which one can go when consumed by a singular purpose.

The Chain's influence is vast, extending over what has become the central hub of power in Faerûn, despite it not being officially recognized as the capital. His crusade against the Underdark has shaped the very fabric of the world, leading to dramatic shifts and a deeply divided land. He remains a constant, looming threat to the denizens of the Underdark, his undying resolve fueling a campaign that shows no signs of abating. His power, both feared and reviled, has made him one of the most infamous figures in history.
