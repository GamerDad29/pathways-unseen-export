# **Trading Post and Tavern - "Shadowed Flask"**

The **Shadowed Flask** is a hive of chaotic energy and activity, a bustling crossroads for all the varied denizens of the Underdark. Constructed with dark, jagged stone and reinforced with leather and chitin, the tavern’s rugged exterior belies the lively, multifaceted atmosphere within. It's more than just a place to rest—it's a hub of commerce, intrigue, and socialization for travelers of every imaginable origin.

Inside, the tavern is a sprawling, two-story establishment filled with mismatched tables and chairs. The walls are covered with tapestries depicting ancient battles, maps of forgotten tunnels, and trophies from countless hunts. Strange, glowing fungi hang from the rafters in woven baskets, casting eerie, multicolored shadows across the crowd. It's hard to find an empty seat here; the place is filled to the brim with a diverse assortment of patrons: a hobgoblin mercenary counting her coin at the bar, a hulking ogre laughing loudly in a corner with a tankard that's almost comically small in his massive hands, and—yes—is that a troll wearing glasses, squinting over a worn, ancient tome?

A dragonborn clad in a mix of chainmail and leather negotiates with a deep gnome over a stack of glowing crystals, while a tabaxi bard strums a delicate melody on a lute, their long tail twitching rhythmically to the beat. In one corner, a group of heavily armored tortles sits together, each bearing a distinctive insignia—a clenched fist over a crossed polearm, the symbol of the **Iron Shell Mercenaries**. They are well-known throughout the Underdark for their fighting prowess and discipline, their polearms resting against their shells, ready at a moment's notice. Their eyes scan the room with a vigilant intensity, making it clear they're not just here for the ale.

Near the bar, **Rizilra**, the sharp-eyed drow owner, oversees it all with a knowing smile. Her fingers drum lightly on the counter as she nods to her bartenders, keeping a close eye on the many deals and whispers exchanged around her. At the back, in a small alcove, sits **Sarnathel "Sarn" Dustwren**, the gnome scribe, diligently recording the arrivals and departures of caravans and travelers, his quill dancing across the pages of his ledger. He pauses only to accept a “donation” or offer snippets of information for a price.

A long queue of caravans, carts, and wagons stretches from the courtyard outside, forming an eclectic line of traders, beasts, and mercenaries. The courtyard serves as a staging area for the caravans. Here, large reptilian pack animals share space with creatures like giant spiders and even a few subterranean drakes. Guards of all races—hobgoblins, orogs, dragonborn, and even a few surface adventurers—keep watch, negotiating loudly or sharing tall tales of their recent journeys through the Underdark's dark and treacherous paths.

## **The Shadowed Flask - Layout and Atmosphere**

**Layout:**
The Shadowed Flask is a dimly lit, low-ceilinged tavern carved into the rough stone of Murkhold Crossing. The main room is spacious but cluttered, with sturdy wooden tables and benches scattered haphazardly, each showing the wear of countless travelers. The bar, made of polished dark stone, stretches along one wall, lined with an array of mismatched bottles filled with strange, glowing liquids. Behind the bar, shelves carved into the stone hold an eclectic mix of items—glassware, odd trinkets, and small statues, all illuminated by the soft, flickering light of enchanted candles.

A narrow staircase at the back of the room leads to a mezzanine level where more private seating and a few small, cozy rooms can be found, often rented out to travelers needing a place to rest. The walls of the tavern are decorated with a variety of trophies from Rizilra’s past adventures—drow weapons, strange crystals, and faded maps of ancient Underdark routes.

**Atmosphere:**
The Shadowed Flask is filled with the thick, earthy scent of mushrooms and damp stone, mixed with the pungent aroma of strong, spiced ales and the occasional whiff of exotic pipe smoke. The air is cool and heavy, carrying the sound of low conversations, the clinking of glasses, and the distant hum of Underdark creatures outside. The lighting is dim, with most of the illumination provided by faintly glowing fungi in sconces and the occasional magical lantern, casting long shadows that dance across the walls.

**Decor:**
The tavern is decorated with a mix of dark, worn fabrics and rough-hewn wooden furnishings, creating a rustic yet comfortable environment. The tables are adorned with simple, chipped mugs and plates, while the walls feature tapestries depicting various Underdark landscapes—twisting caverns, underground rivers, and strange, alien flora. Rizilra has also placed several small, dark green potted plants on the bar and shelves, their leaves glowing faintly, adding to the eerie, otherworldly ambiance.

**Notable NPCs in the Shadowed Flask**

</aside>

1. **Rizilra - Drow Tavern Owner**
    - **Description**: Sharp-eyed and calculating, Rizilra runs the Shadowed Flask with keen insight into the goings-on of Murkhold Crossing. She has a vast network of contacts and a reputation for knowing more than she lets on.
    - **Rumor**: “I heard Rizilra’s been getting a lot of visitors from the surface lately. Something about a new trade route or a hidden cache of surface goods.”
2. **Sarnathel "Sarn" Dustwren - Gnome Scribe**
    - **Description**: Meticulous and detail-oriented, Sarn is in charge of documenting caravan movements and transactions. He offers information for the right price and takes 'donations' to ensure safe passage.
    - **Rumor**: “Sarn mentioned something odd about the last caravan from the east—missing supplies and a guard who vanished in the night.”
3. **Thurl Ironmantle - Duergar Smith**
    - **Description**: A grizzled Duergar blacksmith with a face as rugged as the weapons he forges. Known for his skill in crafting and enchanting weapons, Thurl has a sharp tongue and an even sharper blade. His workshop is filled with the clanging of hammers and the smell of molten metal.
    - **Rumor**: “Thurl’s heard whispers that the Master Blacksmith of Karnuk, a legendary figure even among the Duergar, has gone missing. Seems like some folks are quietly searching for a replacement… or a successor.”
4. **Drusk - Kuo-toa Healer**
    - **Description**: Mysterious and reclusive, Drusk runs the Healer’s Hut, offering strange remedies and insights into the flora and fauna of the Underdark. They speak in cryptic phrases and seem to know more than they reveal.
    - **Rumor**: “Drusk has been gathering some rare herbs lately… says they’re for a ritual to cleanse a ‘shadow sickness.’ Makes you wonder what that’s all about.”

---

These NPCs offer minor but intriguing hints about the activities and mysteries in and around Murkhold Crossing, providing the party with potential leads to explore.
