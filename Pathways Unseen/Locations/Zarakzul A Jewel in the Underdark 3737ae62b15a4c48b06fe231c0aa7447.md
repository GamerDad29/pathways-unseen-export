DM Description: —-

Zarakzul is a vibrant and industrious city, built around a vast underground lake that sustains its inhabitants. The kobolds and goblins who call this city home are skilled fishermen and craftsmen, their lives deeply intertwined with the natural bounty of the lake. The city's peaceful relations with its neighbors, including Vragathul and Karnuk, contribute to its overall security and prosperity.

However, Zarakzul is not without its challenges. The threat of raids from duergar and orc groups, as well as a growing undead menace, keeps the inhabitants vigilant and prepared for danger. Notable figures such as Graxor the Fishmaster, Jalla Bonecarver, and Sarlac the Watchful play key roles in maintaining the city's stability and prosperity.

——

Nestled northwest of Vragathul, Zarakzul emerges as a vibrant city within the depths of the Underdark. While relatively small in size, it boasts a unique population of kobolds and goblins who thrive on the city's natural bounty.

A City Built by the Lake
Zarakzul flourishes around a vast underground lake, its lifeblood. This freshwater source not only sustains the inhabitants but also fuels a thriving fishing industry. Many of the city's structures hug the cavern walls, their design reflecting a deep connection to this vital resource.

Masters of the Depths
The kobolds and goblins of Zarakzul are adept fishermen, adeptly navigating the depths of their lake. Their expertise translates into a flourishing trade in fish and other aquatic resources, ensuring a steady flow of goods and prosperity. Beyond the lake, their skills extend to hunting and trapping, venturing into the surrounding caverns to supplement their diet with game.

A Legacy of Craft
Despite its modest size, Zarakzul fosters a community of skilled artisans and craftsmen. Tucked within the city are numerous workshops where the rhythmic clang of hammers and the meticulous work of skilled hands create practical tools, weapons, and other necessities. These artisans possess a particular talent for working with bone and shell, crafting intricate and beautiful objects that stand as testaments to their ingenuity.

A Network of Cooperation
Zarakzul fosters peaceful relations with its neighbors, including the bustling metropolis of Vragathul, the stoic city of Karnuk, and nearby settlements of kobolds and goblins. This network of alliances contributes to the city's overall sense of security and allows for mutually beneficial trade and cultural exchange.
Facing the Darkness

While generally peaceful, Zarakzul, like any Underdark city, is not without its dangers. The inhabitants remain vigilant, wary of the lurking creatures that call the caverns home. This constant awareness is especially crucial due to:

• Raiding Threats: Zarakzul faces a significant threat from frequent raids by nearby groups of duergar and orcs. These brutal raiders seek to steal the city's resources and enslave its inhabitants. While the kobolds and goblins have successfully repelled these attacks so far, they must maintain a strong defense and constant vigilance to protect their home.

• Undead Menace: Additionally, there are disturbing reports of a growing number of undead creatures stirring in the surrounding tunnels. Left unchecked, this growing menace could pose a significant threat to the city's safety and security.

A City of Bounty
Despite the dangers, Zarakzul is renowned for two unique treasures:

• Skilled Fishermen and Freshwater Pearls: The city's inhabitants have developed exceptional fishing techniques, allowing them to harvest the lake's bounty and produce some of the most flavorful fish dishes in the Underdark.

• Freshwater Pearls: The lake is home to a unique species of freshwater mussels that produce valuable pearls. The kobolds and goblins of Zarakzul harvest and trade these pearls with other settlements in the Underdark, adding another layer to their economy and cultural significance.

Zarakzul stands as a testament to the resourcefulness and resilience of its inhabitants. This small city, nestled within the depths, offers a glimpse into a unique and thriving community, where the bounty of the Underdark, the skill of its people, and the ever-present challenges weave a tapestry of life, industry, and unwavering determination.

## 1. **The Phantom Pearl Diver**

**Description**: Recently, the city's renowned pearl divers have been disappearing without a trace, along with their precious hauls of freshwater pearls. Graxor the Fishmaster, deeply concerned, believes that a ghostly entity or a powerful underwater predator is behind these disappearances. Adventurers must investigate the lake, uncover the truth behind the vanishings, and recover the stolen pearls.

**Key NPCs**:

- Graxor the Fishmaster
- Families of the missing pearl divers

**Challenges**:

- Navigate the treacherous depths of the underground lake.
- Discover clues leading to an ancient, submerged temple inhabited by a malevolent water spirit.
- Defeat the water spirit and rescue any surviving pearl divers.

**Rewards**:

- Payment in gold and valuable pearls.
- A unique magical item blessed by the recovered pearl divers.

## 2. **The Bonecarver's Oath**

**Objective**: Aid Jalla Bonecarver in crafting an enchanted artifact by retrieving rare and dangerous materials.

**Description**: Jalla Bonecarver has been commissioned to create a powerful artifact to protect Zarakzul from an impending threat. To craft this masterpiece, she requires rare materials from a hidden, magical forest guarded by ancient, sentient trees. Adventurers must brave the forest, earn the trust of the guardians, and retrieve the necessary materials without causing harm to the forest's delicate ecosystem.

**Key NPCs**:

- Jalla Bonecarver
- Guardians of the enchanted forest

**Challenges**:

- Navigate the forest's magical illusions and traps.
- Earn the trust of the forest guardians through diplomacy and problem-solving.
- Safely harvest the required materials without disturbing the forest's balance.

**Rewards**:

- The crafted artifact, imbued with protective enchantments.
- A token of friendship from the forest guardians, granting safe passage through their realm.

## 3. **Echoes of the Deep**

**Objective**: Investigate the source of strange, hypnotic echoes emanating from a newly discovered cavern.

**Description**: Mysterious echoes have been heard resonating through the tunnels near Zarakzul, causing disorientation and hallucinations among the city's inhabitants. Sarlac the Watchful believes these echoes are linked to a powerful, ancient entity awakening from a long slumber. Adventurers must explore the cavern, uncover the source of the echoes, and prevent the entity from fully awakening.

**Key NPCs**:

- Sarlac the Watchful
- Scholars of Zarakzul

**Challenges**:

- Resist the echoes' disorienting effects while navigating the labyrinthine cavern.
- Decipher ancient runes and symbols to uncover the entity's history and weaknesses.
- Confront and contain the entity before it regains its full power.

**Rewards**:

- Payment in gold and rare artifacts.
- An enchanted item that protects against mental manipulation.

## 4. **The Festival of Lights**

**Objective**: Assist in preparing and protecting the city during the annual Festival of Lights.

**Description**: Zarakzul's Festival of Lights is a time of celebration and cultural exchange, drawing visitors from neighboring cities. This year, however, there are rumors of an imminent attack by a rival faction seeking to disrupt the festivities. Adventurers must help prepare for the festival, ensure the safety of the attendees, and thwart any attempts to sabotage the event.

**Key NPCs**:

- Festival organizers and local artisans
- Visitors from neighboring cities

**Challenges**:

- Aid in the setup and decoration of the festival, showcasing Zarakzul's unique culture.
- Uncover and neutralize potential threats from the rival faction.
- Ensure the safety of key figures attending the festival, including influential diplomats and traders.

**Rewards**:

- Special recognition and honorary titles within Zarakzul.
- Unique festival-themed magical items.

**Objective**: Unravel the mystery of a secretive cult that has taken root in Zarakzul.

**Description**: Whispers of a shadowy cult practicing dark rituals have reached the ears of Zarakzul's leaders. This cult, known as the Whispering Shadows, seeks to harness forbidden magic to gain control over the city. Adventurers must infiltrate the cult, uncover their plans, and put an end to their nefarious activities.

**Key NPCs**:

- Sarlac the Watchful
- Former cult members seeking redemption

**Challenges**:

- Infiltrate the cult by posing as new recruits or through stealth.
- Gather intelligence on the cult's rituals and hierarchy.
- Disrupt a major ritual and confront the cult's leader to dismantle the organization.

**Rewards**:

- Payment in gold and valuable resources.
- A relic recovered from the cult, offering unique magical properties.

## 5. **The Whispering Shadows**

The citizens of Zarakzul have been experiencing eerie whispers echoing through the caverns at night. These whispers have caused unrest and fear, as they seem to originate from the ancient ruins located deep beneath the city. The council has decided to hire a group of adventurers to investigate the source of these whispers and put an end to the disturbances.

**Objectives:**

1. **Investigate the Source:** Track down the origin of the whispers within the ancient ruins.
2. **Uncover the Mystery:** Discover who or what is causing the whispers.
3. **Neutralize the Threat:** Deal with the source of the whispers, whether it be a malevolent entity, ancient curse, or powerful artifact.

**Key Locations:**

1. **The Whispering Cavern:** The entrance to the ancient ruins where the whispers are most audible.
2. **The Chamber of Echoes:** A large cavern where sound is amplified, possibly housing clues or traps.
3. **The Forgotten Shrine:** An ancient shrine dedicated to a long-forgotten deity, possibly the source of the whispers.

**NPCs:**

1. **Elder Zara Nightshade:** Leader of the Shadow Scale Clan, who offers insight into the ruins’ history.
2. **Grik Stonefin:** A seasoned fisher who first reported the whispers and provides a map of the ruins.
3. **Brik Ironwheel:** Head of the Iron Gears, who offers mechanical assistance and traps to aid the adventurers.

**Potential Challenges:**

1. **Haunting Spirits:** Ghostly apparitions that guard the ancient ruins.
2. **Ancient Traps:** Mechanical traps left by the original builders of the ruins.
3. **Cultists of the Whispering Shadows:** A hidden cult that worships the source of the whispers and seeks to protect it.

**Rewards:**

1. **Ancient Relics:** Magical items or artifacts found within the ruins.
2. **Gold and Gems:** Treasures hidden away by the ancient inhabitants.
3. **Guild Favors:** Increased reputation and favor with the various guilds in Zarakzul.

## Allegiances of Zarakzul

### **1. Vragathul: The Underground Metropolis**

**Description**: Vragathul is a major hub of industry and commerce within the Underdark. Its diverse population includes dark elves, goblinoids, beast-kind, and surface races. This city is a vital trading partner for Zarakzul, given its extensive trade networks and economic power.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Trade and Commerce**: Zarakzul's fishermen and artisans trade their goods with Vragathul, benefiting from the metropolis's vast market reach.
- **Shared Threats**: Both cities face threats from surface incursions and other hostile forces, fostering a sense of mutual defense and cooperation.

### **2. Karnuk: A City Forged in Strength**

**Description**: Karnuk is a fortified city known for its exceptional blacksmiths and artisans. Its strategic location and military strength make it a formidable ally.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Economic Cooperation**: Karnuk's blacksmiths supply Zarakzul with high-quality weapons and armor, essential for the city's defense against raiders and other threats.
- **Mutual Defense**: The cities share intelligence and resources to repel common enemies, such as duergar and orc raiders.

### **3. Ulfgar: The Underground Jewel**

**Description**: Ulfgar is a city with a rich mining heritage, known for its abundance of precious metals and gems. It is led by Viceroy Drogan Stonebeard, a staunch advocate for peace and cooperation.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Trade and Resources**: Zarakzul trades its aquatic resources and crafted goods for Ulfgar's mined wealth, creating a mutually beneficial economic relationship.
- **Shared Ideals**: Both cities emphasize tolerance and cooperation among different races, working together to promote peace and stability in the Underdark.

### **4. The Silent Assembly**

**Description**: A faction of Underdark scholars and mages who seek to understand the silence of the gods. They are known for their secretive nature and vast knowledge of ancient magics and forgotten lore.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Knowledge Exchange**: Zarakzul's leaders, like Grixiz, value the Assembly's ancient knowledge, often seeking their counsel on magical and strategic matters.
- **Cultural Preservation**: The Assembly aids in preserving the cultural heritage and history of Zarakzul, reinforcing its identity and resilience.

### **5. The Deep Lords**

**Description**: A coalition of powerful Underdark houses banded together for mutual protection and dominance. They are influential in the political sphere and control many of the Underdark's resources.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Political Alliances**: Grixiz works with the Deep Lords to maintain Zarakzul's standing in the broader political landscape of the Underdark.
- **Resource Allocation**: The Deep Lords' control over resources ensures Zarakzul receives necessary materials for its crafts and defenses.

### **6. Guardians of the Shadows**

**Description**: Shadow Scale Clan, led by Zara Nightshade, comprises skilled rogues and assassins specializing in stealth and agility. They provide security and protection services within Zarakzul.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Security**: The Shadow Scale Clan ensures the safety of Zarakzul's inhabitants and resources, protecting against internal and external threats.
- **Covert Operations**: Their expertise in covert operations helps maintain Zarakzul's stability and influence.

### **7. Deepwater Fishers**

**Description**: Headed by Grik Stonefin, this guild specializes in fishing and aquaculture within Zarakzul's underground lake. They ensure a steady supply of fresh fish for the city.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Economic Strength**: The Fishers' surplus is traded with other Underdark settlements, bolstering Zarakzul's economic reach.
- **Cultural Identity**: The Fishers' role in sustaining Zarakzul's population reinforces the city's cultural identity and self-sufficiency.

### **8. Iron Gears**

**Description**: Led by Brik Ironwheel, this engineering guild is responsible for crafting machines and devices serving diverse purposes, including mining and transportation.

**Key Points of Allegiance**:

- **Innovation**: The Iron Gears' technological advancements help Zarakzul maintain its infrastructure and improve its defenses.
- **Economic Development**: Their innovative spirit and mechanical skills contribute to Zarakzul's prosperity and industrial growth.

### Summary

Zarakzul's allegiances are deeply rooted in economic cooperation, mutual defense, and shared cultural values. The city's strategic partnerships with Vragathul, Karnuk, Ulfgar, and various influential factions within the Underdark ensure its stability and prosperity. Under the leadership of Viceroy Grixiz, Zarakzul navigates the complexities of the Underdark, fostering alliances that strengthen its position and safeguard its future.

## Key Points of Interest

### The Underground Lake

- **Significance**: The lake is the city's lifeblood, providing water, food, and a central hub for trade and daily life.
- **Potential Encounters**: The lake might hide ancient secrets, underwater caves, or even dangerous aquatic creatures. It’s an ideal setting for quests involving exploration, resource gathering, or monster hunting.

### The Artisans' Workshops

- **Significance**: These workshops produce practical tools, weapons, and unique bone and shell crafts.
- **Potential Encounters**: Players could be hired to protect shipments, uncover sabotage attempts by rival cities, or seek rare materials to craft powerful items.

### The Fishing Guild

- **Significance**: Headed by Grik Stonefin, this guild ensures a steady food supply and economic trade.
- **Potential Encounters**: Quests might involve protecting fishing expeditions from predators, resolving disputes with other guilds, or discovering new fishing techniques or areas.

### The Pearl Caves

- **Significance**: Hidden within the caverns surrounding the lake, these caves are home to the unique species of freshwater mussels that produce valuable pearls.
- **Potential Encounters**: Players might be tasked with harvesting pearls while avoiding aggressive cave-dwelling creatures or rival pearl hunters. Additionally, there could be a mystery involving a cursed pearl or an ancient legend tied to the caves.

### The Gloomroot Forest

- **Significance**: A dense, bioluminescent fungal forest located on the outskirts of Zarakzul, providing a rich source of rare herbs and fungi used in medicine and alchemy.
- **Potential Encounters**: The forest is inhabited by dangerous creatures and cunning predators. Quests might include gathering rare ingredients for a crucial potion, investigating strange happenings in the forest, or dealing with a rogue alchemist hiding among the Gloomroot.

### The Crystal Gardens

- **Significance**: An area within the city where natural crystal formations grow, some with magical properties. These crystals are used in crafting and enchanting.
- **Potential Encounters**: Protecting the gardens from crystal poachers, discovering new types of crystals, or dealing with an outbreak of wild magic affecting the area. The players might also be involved in a dispute between enchanters over the best crystals.

### The Ancient Library

- **Significance**: A repository of knowledge containing ancient tomes and scrolls, some predating the founding of Zarakzul. It's a vital resource for scholars and magic users.
- **Potential Encounters**: Protecting the library from thieves, recovering stolen or lost tomes, or uncovering forbidden knowledge hidden in its depths. There could also be a storyline involving a ghostly librarian or a sentient book.

### The Forgotten Temple

- **Significance**: An ancient, partially collapsed temple located in a secluded cavern. It holds historical and religious significance, with ties to forgotten gods and old magic.
- **Potential Encounters**: Players might explore the temple to uncover its secrets, face ancient guardians, or deal with cultists trying to revive old, dangerous rituals. The temple could also be a place to find powerful relics or ancient prophecies.

### The Trade Bazaar

- **Significance**: The bustling heart of Zarakzul's commerce, where traders from various Underdark settlements gather to exchange goods.
- **Potential Encounters**: Players could engage in trade negotiations, protect the market from thieves, or uncover and thwart a smuggling ring. The bazaar might also be the center of a political intrigue involving various merchant guilds.
