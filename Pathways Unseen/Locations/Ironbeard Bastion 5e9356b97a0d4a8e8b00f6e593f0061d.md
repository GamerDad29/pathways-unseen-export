# Ironbeard Bastion: The Surface Stronghold

## Campaign Significance:

Ironbeard Bastion is a crucial stronghold located on the surface, serving as a vital resource center and a bastion of resistance against the Underdark. Nestled within iron-rich mountains, this fortress is a hub of activity, mining, and trade. Its strategic position and skilled workforce make it a cornerstone of surface defenses against the threats from below.

## Quests Associated With It:

1. **The Iron Road**: Escort a crucial shipment of iron ore through perilous mountain passes to a nearby city. Along the way, the players must fend off bandits, negotiate with rival miners, and ensure the safe delivery of the resources.
2. **Veins of Power**: Investigate a newly discovered vein of a rare, magical ore deep within the mountains. The players must secure the site, fend off rival claims, and navigate the dangers lurking in the depths to bring back the valuable material.
3. **Mountain Defenders**: Ironbeard Bastion is facing increasing attacks from creatures emerging from the Underdark. The players are tasked with fortifying the defenses, training the militia, and leading a counter-attack to secure the stronghold.
4. **Missing Miners**: Miners from Ulfgar have disappeared in the mountains surrounding Ironbeard Bastion. The players must uncover the fate of these miners, dealing with both natural and supernatural threats.
5. **The Heart of the Mountain**: A powerful artifact is rumored to be hidden deep within the mountains. The players must venture into the depths to retrieve it, facing ancient guardians and treacherous terrain.

## Quests Starting Here:

1. **The Prospector’s Plight**: Assist a group of dwarven prospectors in exploring a newly discovered cave system rumored to be rich in resources. The players must map the area, identify valuable deposits, and protect the prospectors from any threats.
2. **Forge of Legends**: Aid the master blacksmiths of Ironbeard Bastion in crafting a legendary weapon. The players must gather rare materials, protect the smiths during the forging process, and ensure the weapon’s completion.
3. **A Miner’s Appeal**: Investigate a series of mysterious cave-ins that have been disrupting mining operations. The players must determine whether these incidents are natural disasters or acts of sabotage and find a way to restore the mine’s safety.

## DM Description:

Ironbeard Bastion stands as a testament to dwarven ingenuity and perseverance. Nestled within a range of iron-rich mountains, this fortified stronghold is both a bustling industrial hub and a secure fortress. Massive iron gates, flanked by vigilant sentinels, guard the entrances. Inside, the stronghold is a maze of tunnels and caverns, echoing with the sounds of industry.

The city’s architecture is rugged and functional, with towering forges, expansive warehouses, and well-organized mining operations. The central plaza features a grand statue of Moradin, the dwarven god of creation, symbolizing the city’s dedication to craftsmanship and resourcefulness. The air is thick with the scent of iron and coal, and the constant clanging of hammers against anvils fills the atmosphere.

## Player Description:

As you approach Ironbeard Bastion, the air grows cooler, the shadows longer, and the landscape more rugged. The stronghold’s iron gates stand imposingly against the backdrop of the mountains, guarded by vigilant dwarves who scrutinize all who pass.

Inside, the stronghold is a hive of activity. Dwarves move with purpose through the well-lit tunnels, hauling carts filled with precious ores, tending to the forges, and trading in the bustling markets. The architecture is solid and practical, with every stone and beam meticulously placed for both function and defense.

In the heart of the city, a grand statue of Moradin, the Soul Forger, presides over the central plaza, his hammer raised in eternal blessing over the industrious city. The sounds of industry—hammers clanging, forges roaring, and miners working—create a symphony of productivity and purpose.

## Notable NPCs:

1. **Thorin Ironbeard**:
    - **Title**: Warden of the Forge
    - **Description**: A stout, burly dwarf with a beard as black as coal and eyes that gleam with determination. Thorin oversees all mining and forging operations in Ironbeard Bastion, ensuring the quality and efficiency of the city’s output.
    - **Role**: Provides quests related to mining, resource management, and defense. Players might interact with him to gain insights into the stronghold’s operations and challenges.
2. **Brynna Steelgrip**:
    - **Title**: Master Blacksmith
    - **Description**: A muscular dwarf with arms like tree trunks, covered in soot and scars from years at the forge. Brynna is renowned for her unparalleled skill in metalwork and crafting enchanted items.
    - **Role**: Offers crafting services, commissions for legendary items, and quests related to gathering rare materials. Players might seek her expertise to forge powerful weapons and armor.
3. **Eldrin Oreseer**:
    - **Title**: Chief Prospector
    - **Description**: An elderly dwarf with a sharp eye and a keen sense for valuable ores. Eldrin has spent decades mapping the mountains and discovering new veins of precious metals.
    - **Role**: Provides quests related to exploration, resource discovery, and securing mining rights. Players might consult him for guidance on finding rare materials and uncovering hidden treasures.

## Organizations:

1. **The Miners' Guild**:
    - **Description**: This guild is composed of the best prospectors and miners in Ironbeard Bastion. They are responsible for extracting valuable ores and ensuring the stronghold’s continuous supply of resources.
    - **Prominent Members**: Eldrin Oreseer (Chief Prospector), several experienced miners and geologists.
    - **Role in the City**: Central to the city’s economy and resource management, they also collaborate with other strongholds for trade and resource sharing.
2. **The Forgemasters**:
    - **Description**: An elite group of blacksmiths and metalworkers who craft the finest weapons, armor, and tools in the region. They are known for their precision and innovative techniques.
    - **Prominent Members**: Brynna Steelgrip (Master Blacksmith), several skilled blacksmiths and enchanters.
    - **Role in the City**: Producing high-quality goods for trade and defense, they also take on special commissions and collaborate with other cities on major projects.
3. **The Iron Guard**:
    - **Description**: The city’s elite military force, tasked with defending Ironbeard Bastion from external threats and maintaining order within. They are highly trained and fiercely loyal.
    - **Prominent Members**: Captain Thrain Stonehelm, several veteran soldiers and strategists.
    - **Role in the City**: Ensuring the security of the stronghold, repelling invaders, and safeguarding key locations.

## Allegiances:

Ironbeard Bastion maintains a staunch stance against the Underdark, focusing on protecting the surface from perceived threats below:

- **Hostile Stance Towards the Underdark**: Ironbeard Bastion sees the Underdark as a constant threat and maintains a state of readiness against incursions. Its resources are dedicated to fortifying defenses and training the Iron Guard for potential conflicts.
- **Alliances with Surface Cities**: Ironbeard Bastion has strong alliances with other surface cities like Phoenix's Respite (Formerly Neverwinter) and Luminary Haven (Formerly Silverymoon), collaborating on trade, defense, and mutual support against the Underdark.
- **Isolationist Policies**: While willing to trade with trusted partners, Ironbeard Bastion is cautious about outsiders and maintains strict security measures to protect its resources and people.

## Additional Info for the GM:

- **Economic Significance**: Ironbeard Bastion’s wealth of resources and skilled labor make it a vital economic center. Control over this stronghold can shift the balance of power on the surface.
- **Strategic Location**: The stronghold’s position within iron-rich mountains makes it a prime target for rival factions. Players might need to defend it or reclaim it from hostile forces.
- **Cultural Hub**: Ironbeard Bastion hosts numerous festivals celebrating dwarven craftsmanship and mining. These events can serve as backdrops for quests or social encounters.

In the heart of the stronghold, a grand forge roars with life, its flames dancing in shades of orange and gold. Dwarves move with purpose, their hammers ringing out in a rhythmic symphony of industry. The air is filled with the scent of iron and coal, mingling with the hearty aroma of dwarven ale and cuisine.

Above the forge, a towering citadel rises, its windows glowing with a warm, inviting

light. This is Ironbeard Bastion, a symbol of dwarven strength and resilience, standing firm against any threat that may come its way.
