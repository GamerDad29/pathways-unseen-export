# Black Market Syndicate Quest

### Leads to Begin the Quest Against the **Deep Market Syndicate**

(Designed to leverage Ibilith's connection to **Bregan D’aerthe** while offering flexibility for other party members to engage.)

---

### **Primary Lead: Suspicious Merchant Activities**

The **Grand Market Square** offers the first hints of Syndicate influence. While most merchants trade openly, there’s a noticeable tension among certain vendors. Rumors circulate of **disappearing goods**, violent extortions, and **black-market stalls that vanish overnight.**

### **How the Lead Unfolds:**

1. **Eavesdropping (Perception DC 12):** The party overhears a surface merchant muttering about being approached for protection money by a Syndicate enforcer. The merchant is afraid to speak openly.
2. **Direct Questions (Charisma DC 14):** Asking merchants about Syndicate influence reveals nervous deflections. Some hint at a "shadow market" that only opens to those who know the right passphrase.
3. **Ibilith’s Connection:**
    - Ibilith can discreetly contact **Bregan D’aerthe** for insight. The drow operatives, while uninterested in direct involvement, suggest the **Deep Market Syndicate’s operations** threaten UTTP’s stability—and their own interests.
    - They provide Ibilith with a lead: **a black-market auction happening soon**, where rare and dangerous items will be sold. Bregan D’aerthe suggests attending to gather intelligence or disrupt the event.

---

### **Secondary Lead: Violence in the Market**

Reports of **violent incidents** tied to the Syndicate emerge as the party investigates. A trader was **murdered** in a dispute over cursed goods, and the body is still at the scene, surrounded by guards.

### **How the Lead Unfolds:**

1. **The Crime Scene:**
The party stumbles upon a group of **UTTP guards** (led by a subordinate of Gorunn Stonebreaker) investigating a merchant’s murder. The body lies slumped near a stall selling cursed artifacts, and witnesses whisper about "black-market thugs" who fled after the attack.
    - **Clues:** A **dagger with Syndicate markings** was left behind (Investigation DC 13). It points to their involvement.
    - The guards lack the authority to pursue Syndicate operatives openly and begrudgingly ask the party for help if they seem competent.
2. **Lyra’s Insight:**
If the party mentions the cursed artifacts to **Lyra Shadowheart**, she reveals her growing concern about these items. Her magical examination (if requested) confirms traces of **necromantic corruption**, hinting at deeper dangers.

---

### **Tertiary Lead: Missing Persons**

Several lower-tier merchants and porters have **vanished** recently. Rumors suggest they were taken by the Syndicate for failing to pay debts or for speaking out.

### **How the Lead Unfolds:**

1. **Search for Survivors:**
    
    A frantic kobold approaches the party, begging for help finding their missing sibling, a porter who worked the lower levels of UTTP.
    
    - **Clue:** A torn piece of clothing near a shadowy alcove leads to an **abandoned storeroom**. Inside, a prisoner (or body) offers critical information:
        - The Syndicate is holding captives in **hidden chambers** beneath the market.
        - They use **levitation platforms** after hours to transport their victims.
2. **Gorunn Stonebreaker’s Interest:**
    
    If the party involves **Gorunn**, he offers limited aid but warns that directly confronting the Syndicate without proof could spark violence.
    

---

### **Leads Specific to Ibilith’s Role**

1. **Bregan D’aerthe’s Objective:**
    
    The operatives share that the **Deep Market Syndicate** is attempting to secure powerful **arcane relics** that threaten not just UTTP’s economy but the **delicate power balance in the Underdark**. Bregan D’aerthe suspects the Syndicate has ties to **surface agents**, destabilizing both realms.
    
    - **Specific Instruction:** "Blend into the shadow market. Observe. Strike only if the Syndicate’s interests conflict with ours."
2. **Exclusive Access to the Auction:**
    
    Bregan D’aerthe provides Ibilith with a **coded invitation** to a Syndicate auction. The passphrase: **"The deeper the dark, the sharper the blade."**
    
    - The auction takes place in a hidden **cavern below UTTP**, accessed through a trapdoor in an abandoned stall.

---

### **Core Leads Summary**

1. **Market Whispers:** Gather intelligence on black-market stalls through perception, intimidation, or leveraging contacts like Bregan D’aerthe.
2. **Violent Incident:** Investigate a murder tied to cursed artifacts, uncovering Syndicate markings and cursed goods.
3. **Missing Persons:** Follow the trail of abducted individuals to locate hidden Syndicate chambers below the market.
4. **Bregan D’aerthe’s Tip:** Use Ibilith’s connection to infiltrate a Syndicate auction, gathering key intelligence on their operations.

---

### **The Auction as a Catalyst**

The auction provides a pivotal moment to expose Syndicate operations and gather evidence for the UTTP Council.

- **Items for Sale:** The Syndicate will auction rare but dangerous goods, such as:
    - A **cursed sword** emitting whispers.
    - An **obsidian orb** pulsing with necromantic energy.
    - **Shadow ink** rumored to allow its user to rewrite contracts with dark powers.
- **Tension at the Auction:**
    - Rival buyers may spark conflict, giving the party a chance to exploit the chaos.
    - Syndicate enforcers will be on high alert, testing the party’s stealth, deception, or combat abilities.

---

### **Galvanizing the Council**

The party’s efforts directly affect the UTTP Council’s ability to unite:

1. **Elandra Brightwood (Diplomacy):** Success in exposing the Syndicate strengthens Elandra’s trust in the party, allowing her to rally merchants to stand against corruption.
2. **Gorunn Stonebreaker (Security):** A decisive strike against the Syndicate earns Gorunn’s respect and galvanizes his forces to crack down on criminal elements.
3. **Lyra Shadowheart (Arcane):** Investigating the cursed items and their arcane corruption helps Lyra uncover early signs of the **Shadow Plague**, increasing her commitment to eradicating the Syndicate.

---

This setup provides multiple entry points into the Syndicate’s operations, leverages Ibilith’s unique connections, and ties directly into UTTP’s growing tensions, creating a compelling and layered questline.
