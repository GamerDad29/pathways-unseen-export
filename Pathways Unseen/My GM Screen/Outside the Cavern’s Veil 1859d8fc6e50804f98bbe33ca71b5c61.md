# Outside the Cavern’s Veil

### **Ambush Outside the Cavern’s Veil – Restated with Tactics, Taunts, Loot, and Knockout Information**

This ambush is designed to challenge the party, emphasizing the Syndicate’s coordination and brutality while sowing doubt about the Syndicate's deeper plans.

---

### **Syndicate Combatants**

### **1. Syndicate Wizard**

- **Tactics**:
    - The Wizard stays at the rear, using spells to disrupt the party's strategy.
    - Focuses on disabling or isolating the most dangerous threats with spells like *Hold Person*.
    - Uses *Misty Step* to reposition if cornered and keeps out of melee range.
    - Targets low-AC or squishy party members (Cleric, Warlock) with damage spells to disrupt support and utility.
- **Taunts**:
    - “You thought the Syndicate would let you walk free? Foolish.”
    - “Do you even know who you’re dealing with? Your deaths will be quick... if you beg.”
    - “I’ve killed better adventurers than you. This won’t take long.”
- **Knockout Information**:
    - If captured, the Wizard sneers and spits insults, but under duress reveals the Syndicate is heavily involved in magical experiments tied to the Old Market.
    - **Key Quote**: *“You think you’re clever? This operation is just one root in the Syndicate’s web. Cut it, and two more will grow.”*

---

### **2. Syndicate Acid Viper**

- **Tactics**:
    - The Acid Viper targets front-line combatants (Barbarian, Monk) to tie them down, using its venom and acid attacks to weaken their defenses.
    - Moves to block escape routes or protect the Wizard.
    - If injured, it becomes more aggressive, focusing on whoever dealt the most damage to it.
- **Behavior**:
    - The Acid Viper does not taunt but hisses and sprays acid when threatened. It exudes a menacing presence, reinforcing its role as a tool of Syndicate brutality.
- **Knockout Information**:
    - The Viper is an expendable weapon for the Syndicate. Investigating its body reveals it was bred and trained for combat by the Syndicate, hinting at their access to resources like alchemy and beast taming.

---

### **3. Syndicate Crossbowmen (x2)**

- **Tactics**:
    - Positioned on rooftops for high-ground advantage, the Crossbowmen focus on ranged suppression, targeting spellcasters and ranged attackers.
    - Use *Pinning Shots* to restrain mobile party members, such as the Monk or Artificer.
    - If cornered, they attempt to disengage and reposition to maintain their advantage.
- **Taunts**:
    - “We’ve been watching you. Did you really think you could sneak around our city?”
    - “Stay still—makes it easier to put a bolt in your head!”
    - “Oh, don’t worry. We’ll take good care of your corpses when this is over.”
- **Knockout Information**:
    - A captured Crossbowman will be more willing to talk than the Wizard, especially under intimidation. They reveal Syndicate patrol routes and possible informants in the Old Market.
    - **Key Quote**: *“You think you’ve won? You’re just another name on the Syndicate’s list. Rynvek won’t let this slide.”*

---

### **4. Syndicate Captain**

- **Tactics**:
    - Commands the ambush, focusing on frontline engagement. Targets the Barbarian or Monk to keep them occupied.
    - Uses taunts to provoke and distract the party, aiming to draw attention while the Crossbowmen and Wizard do damage.
    - If reduced to low health, fights defensively to buy time for allies or retreats if the ambush fails.
- **Taunts**:
    - “You think you can waltz into our market and walk away? Not a chance.”
    - “I’ve faced tougher fighters in my sleep. Show me what you’ve got!”
    - “You don’t even know what you’re meddling with. The Syndicate runs this place!”
- **Knockout Information**:
    - The Captain is the most tight-lipped but offers cryptic warnings if coerced.
    - **Key Quote**: *“You think taking me out will stop the Syndicate? Rynvek’s got plans you can’t even comprehend.”*
    - May reveal that Rynvek operates out of a hidden chamber beneath the Old Market’s central plaza.

### **Roll Table for Capturing Combatants**

If the party captures and interrogates a Syndicate member, roll **1d8** to determine how much information they provide:

1. **Refusal to Speak**: The combatant mocks the party and refuses to talk.
    
    *“Kill me if you want. You’ll learn nothing from me.”*
    
2. **Cryptic Warning**: Offers a vague, ominous statement.
    
    *“You think you’ve won? You don’t know how deep the Syndicate runs.”*
    
3. **Basic Info**: Reveals basic Syndicate patrol routes in the Old Market.
    
    *“Fine, fine! There’s a patrol near the Rusted Gate—take it up with them!”*
    
4. **Name of an Informant**: Mentions a Syndicate informant in the Old Market.
    
    *“Talk to Krass. He works for us, but he’s a drunk. He’ll slip up if you press him.”*
    
5. **Clue to a Safehouse**: Gives the location of a minor Syndicate safehouse.
    
    *“There’s a stashhouse near the Broken Arch. You didn’t hear it from me.”*
    
6. **Key Syndicate Name**: Reveals Rynvek as a key leader in the Syndicate.
    
    *“Rynvek’s the one pulling the strings. Good luck getting to him, though.”*
    
7. **Upcoming Plans**: Discloses an upcoming Syndicate operation.
    
    *“They’ve got a meeting tomorrow night near the Shattered Fountain. That’s all I know!”*
    
8. **Critical Intel**: Shares the location of the Syndicate’s central stronghold in the Old Market.
    
    *“Alright, alright! The Syndicate’s base is beneath the central plaza. There’s a hidden passage in the plaza’s south wall.”*
