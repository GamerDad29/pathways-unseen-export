**Brekka Stonegut**

*The only person in the Blackfire Alchemists who joined specifically because she wanted to stop hurting people — and the irony of that has not escaped her.*

### **Appearance**

Brekka is a broad-shouldered dwarf woman in her middle years, her auburn beard shot through with grey at the plaits, her face carrying the particular topography of someone who has walked through fire — literally, twice — and come out mostly whole. She wears no armor on principle, favoring a heavy canvas work coat with reinforced elbows that makes her look more like a warehouse foreman than a soldier, which is entirely the point. Her hands are large enough to palm a human's skull, and she keeps them very deliberately still, very deliberately open, whenever she's talking to someone she's trying not to frighten.

### **Personality**

- **Trait:** Brekka has an almost aggressive commitment to straightforwardness — she states prices, consequences, and intentions plainly, and finds deception physically exhausting in the way seasickness is exhausting.
- **Bond:** She sends a third of everything she earns back to the families of soldiers she commanded in a border campaign that went catastrophically wrong. She has never told the families where the money comes from.
- **Flaw:** She cannot walk away from a fight she believes is unjust, even when walking away is tactically correct and she knows it — a fact Zogrin exploits with guilt-laden regularity.

### **Secret**

Brekka has recently encountered a Spellfire Initiate — a young dwarven girl in Karnuk's lower quarter — and has been quietly sheltering her from interests she suspects include the Blackfire Alchemists themselves. Zogrin collects people with unusual magical properties the way other goblins collect coins. Brekka doesn't know what he wants Spellfire for, but she knows what a cage looks like from the inside.

**Roleplaying Hook:** The party is hired by Brekka directly — unusually, she's gone around the guild's official channels — to locate a shipment of Alchemist's Supplies that went missing before it reached the guild. She pays in advance, which is unusual. She mentions, too casually, that Zogrin doesn't know she's hired them. The question of *why* unfolds slowly: the shipment contains a formula she's trying to intercept before it's synthesized.

### **Suggested CR**

6

👤 *Stats for use in combat (if needed):*

</aside>

> Brekka uses the **Veteran** stat block (MM p. 350) as a base with the following modifications: Replace her longsword with a **Reinforced Maul** (2d6+4 bludgeoning). Add **Alchemical Resistance** — Brekka has advantage on saving throws against poison and resistance to poison damage from years of occupational exposure. Once per short rest she may use **Suppression Canister** as an action: she hurls a Blackfire-manufactured gas canister at a point within 30 ft. All creatures in a 10-ft. radius must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution save or be **Incapacitated** until the end of their next turn (creatures immune to poison automatically succeed). She has **Indomitable** (1/day) as a fighter.
