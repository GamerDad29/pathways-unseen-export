**Britta Duskmantle "Two-Floors" Sorn**

*The woman who built a legitimate business on top of an illegitimate one, and has spent forty years making sure no one can tell the difference.*

### **Appearance**

Britta is a compact, slate-grey deep gnome woman with the posture of someone who has been holding a secret upright for four decades — spine rigid, chin perpetually raised, eyes that move across a room the way a chess master reads a board. Her hair is the white of cave coral, worn in a severe knot pierced by two bone pins carved from something no one has been brave enough to ask about. She wears an apron so spotlessly clean it reads as a threat.

### **Personality**

- **Trait:** Hospitality is a weapon, and Britta wields it with surgical precision — her warmth is real, her memory for names is perfect, and her tolerance for disorder is absolute zero.
- **Bond:** The Mycelium Veil was built over three decades of brutal, careful labor. She will let kingdoms burn before she lets the tavern burn.
- **Flaw:** She has renegotiated her moral ledger so many times she genuinely cannot identify where the justifications end and the truth begins.

### **Secret**

The basement doesn't just *connect* to the Hagshold — Britta is the Hagshold's appointed surface-agent, warden, and tithe-collector. She skims twelve percent off every deal brokered below and has done so since the three hags who own the space granted her the building's deed in exchange. She tells herself she is neutral. The hags find this *adorable.*

**Roleplaying Hook:** Britta introduces herself to the party the moment they walk in — personally, which should feel like an honor and reads as surveillance. She offers the best room at a modest rate, remembers every drink order, and at some point during their first night, a note appears under the door of whichever party member looks most desperate. It reads: *"Whatever you came to the Underdark to find — I know where it is. Come to the bar alone. —B."*

### **Suggested CR**

5 (Use **Spy** stat block, *MM* p. 349, add *Gnome Cunning* and *Stone Camouflage*)
