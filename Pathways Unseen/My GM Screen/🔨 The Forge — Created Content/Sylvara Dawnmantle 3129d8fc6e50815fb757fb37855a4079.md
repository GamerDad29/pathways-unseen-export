**Sylvara Dawnmantle**

*The woman who swore an oath to protect the living world — and is slowly realizing the god-king she once revered may be its greatest wound.*

### **Appearance**

Sylvara stands tall and weathered, her bark-brown skin etched with old silver scars that catch the light like cracked bark after a frost. Her pale gold eyes carry the particular stillness of someone who has seen centuries pass and found them wanting. She wears battered half-plate the color of autumn leaves, its surface carved with intertwining vines, one pauldron deliberately defaced — the emblem of The Chain's holy order scratched away but never fully gone.

### **Personality**

*Trait:* She speaks slowly and deliberately, weighing each word as though language itself is sacred — and breakable.

*Bond:* She once pledged her sword and her soul to The Chain's crusade against the Underdark. Every life she took in that war is a name she still carries.

*Flaw:* She cannot admit she was wrong until the proof is so overwhelming it shatters her — and even then, her pride makes her frame it as evolution rather than failure.

### **Secret**

Sylvara was among the first paladins to receive The Chain's divine investiture — she is one of perhaps a dozen living souls through whom his power still flows. She has begun to feel that connection *pulsing* again after years of silence, like a chain pulled taut in the dark. She doesn't yet know if it is a summons — or a warning.

**Roleplaying Hook:** The party encounters Sylvara standing alone at the edge of a battlefield ruin, pressing her palm flat against the scorched earth as if listening. She does not reach for her weapon. She simply looks up and says, quietly: *"You've felt it too. Haven't you. Something waking."* She is not asking.
