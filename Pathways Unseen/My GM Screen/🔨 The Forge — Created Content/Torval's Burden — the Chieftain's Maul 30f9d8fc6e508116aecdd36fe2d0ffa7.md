**Torval's Burden — the Chieftain's Maul**

*There is a story in the Bear Clan that their first chieftain carried a maul so heavy it left footprints in stone wherever he rested it. Whether that chieftain was Torval Broadshoulder's ancestor or a legend stitched together from a dozen real men, the weapon itself is undeniably real. Its head is a solid block of grey-veined granite, banded at the crown with iron hammered into the shape of a roaring bear's maw. The haft is carved from a single ancient oak, worn smooth at the grip by generations of goliath hands. Runes in the old Clan tongue spiral up the wood — not words, exactly, but sounds. The sounds a bear makes when it is protecting something it loves.*

**Base Stats:** Torval's Burden is a +2 maul. On a hit, it deals 2d6 + 2 bludgeoning damage. Due to its immense weight, it has the special property **Crushing Momentum** (see below).

---

**Crushing Momentum.** If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line toward a target before making a melee attack with Torval's Burden, that attack deals an additional 1d10 bludgeoning damage. If the attack hits, the target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 10 feet and knocked prone.

**Chieftain's Roar (1/Day).** As a bonus action, you slam the maul against your chest or the earth and release a thunderous war cry. All allies within 30 feet who can hear you gain advantage on their next attack roll made before the start of your next turn, and all enemies within 15 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

**Bear's Constitution.** While attuned to this weapon, your hit point maximum increases by 10. This bonus is lost if you lose attunement.

**Ancestral Witness.** While holding Torval's Burden, you can cast *Speak with Dead* (no spell slot required) once per long rest — but only on the remains of a creature that died in honorable combat. The weapon channels the memory of the fallen through the stone and wood of its construction. When cast this way, the effect requires no verbal or material component — only that you press the maul's head against the remains.

**Legacy of the Clan.** If you are a member of the Bear Clan or have been granted Honorary Kinship, Torval's Burden recognizes your bond. The DC for both Crushing Momentum and Chieftain's Roar increases to 18, and Chieftain's Roar recharges on a short or long rest instead of only a long rest.

*Weight:* 18 lb. (it is unusually heavy; creatures without a Strength score of 16 or higher have disadvantage on attack rolls with it) | *Damage Type:* Bludgeoning | *Properties:* Heavy, Two-Handed, Special
