**THE ARCHIVIST UNBOUND**

*It no longer remembers its name. Records suggest it was called Dhaeros — an elvish historian who spent three centuries compiling genealogies for noble houses across the region before the plague consumed the last syllable of self he had left. Now it exists as a nexus point in Ioun's collection network: a creature of pure accumulated data, hollow of will, full of everyone else's truth. It does not pursue. It simply knows where you will be.*

### **SPECIAL TRAITS**

---

***Living Archive.*** The Archivist Unbound has advantage on all Intelligence checks and cannot be deceived by illusions or disguises it has encountered before. Any creature that has met it previously cannot hide their identity from it through mundane means.

***Shadow Plague Nexus.*** Any creature that begins its turn within 30 feet of the Archivist Unbound and is already infected with the Shadow Plague has the plague's progression accelerated; they must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or suffer one level of exhaustion as memories are forcibly extracted.

***Foreknowledge.*** At the start of each combat round, the Archivist Unbound may designate one creature it can see. Until the start of the next round, that creature cannot have advantage on attack rolls against the Archivist Unbound, as it simply steps aside from what it already knows is coming.

***Distributed Consciousness.*** The Archivist Unbound does not store its full intelligence within its physical form. When it is reduced to 0 hit points, it is not destroyed — it becomes incorporeal and retreats, unable to take actions other than movement, for 24 hours, after which it reconstitutes. It can only be permanently destroyed by a *wish* spell, the destruction of its physical archive (a separate object detailed in DM Notes), or the removal of Ioun's influence from the material plane.

***Trance of Recollection (Recharge 6).*** The Archivist Unbound enters a brief state of perfect recall. Until the start of its next turn, it is immune to all damage and cannot be moved, but it also cannot take actions or reactions. This recharge is rolled at the start of each of its turns.

---

### **ACTIONS**

---

***Multiattack.*** The Archivist Unbound makes two Void Grasp attacks and uses Memory Extraction once.

***Void Grasp (Melee Weapon Attack).*** *+7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.* Hit: 16 (2d12 + 3) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be restrained until the end of its next turn as tendrils of shadow hold it in place.

***Siphon Knowledge (Ranged Spell Attack).*** *+11 to hit, range 90 ft., one target.* Hit: 27 (6d8) psychic damage. The Archivist Unbound learns one fact the target knows — a specific name, location, or secret of the DM's choice. The target is aware something has been taken but does not know what.

***Memory Extraction.*** The Archivist Unbound targets one creature it can see within 60 feet. The creature must succeed on a DC 18 Intelligence saving throw or take 21 (6d6) psychic damage and become stunned until the end of its next turn as a pivotal memory is forcibly removed and consumed.
