**Marta "Clinker" Voss**

*She lost three fingers to a lava dragonling at seventeen and spent the next decade figuring out how to make that never happen to anyone else — unless she wanted it to.*

### **Appearance**

Marta is built like a siege engine given a bad haircut — broad through the shoulders, short through the legs, and apparently immune to the concept of intimidation. Her right hand is missing its ring, middle, and index fingers, replaced by articulated iron prosthetics she had fitted specifically to hold a dragonling's muzzle harness. She keeps her black hair close-cropped and her Syndicate brand — a coiled serpent bisected by a pickaxe — visible on her neck, because she wants people to know exactly who they're dealing with. Burn scars mottle her forearms like maps of forgotten roads.

### **Personality**

*Trait.* Marta communicates almost entirely in dark humor and blunt operational assessments. She has no patience for ceremony, titles, or what she calls "villain speeches."

*Bond.* Her crew of six junior handlers are fiercely loyal to her, and she to them — she has buried two already and considers that number a personal failure.

*Flaw.* She is incapable of admitting she is in over her head until the situation is genuinely catastrophic, and sometimes not even then.

### **Secret**

Marta knows exactly what happened to Borin — she was there. She didn't order it, but she didn't stop it either, and the memory sits in her chest like a swallowed coal. She's been drinking harder since.

**Roleplaying Hook:** The party runs into Marta in the middle of a controlled dragonling transfer that is rapidly becoming uncontrolled — a crate has cracked, a Feral Lava Dragonling is loose in a crowded market corridor, and she is barking orders at panicking handlers while somehow remaining the calmest person in the street. She may draft the party on the spot, with zero pleasantries: *"You. Yes, you. Can you run fast? Great. Chase it left, I'll cut it off. We'll talk money after."*

### **Suggested CR**

6

### **DM NOTES**

---

*Marta is the Syndicate's troubleshooter — she handles acquisitions that go sideways, transports that get complicated, and dragonlings that have become liabilities. She's aware of Vexia Nightshade's broader agenda but operates with enough autonomy that she doesn't always know the full picture. Her guilt over Borin's fate makes her a potential source of crucial information if the party can find the right lever — she won't be charmed or intimidated easily, but genuine moral pressure or evidence that the party already knows most of the truth might crack her open. Her iron prosthetic fingers can be used mechanically: she has advantage on checks to handle, restrain, or muzzle dragonlings of Small size or smaller.*
