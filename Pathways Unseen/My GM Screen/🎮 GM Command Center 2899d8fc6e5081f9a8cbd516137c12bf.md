## 🌍 World Rules & Economics

### **Economic System**

**Gold Scarcity Multiplier: 10-12x**

What this means:

- 1gp in Underdark = 10-12gp surface value
- Purchases feel expensive
- Rewards should be 1/10th normal D&D amounts
- Trade/barter encouraged
- Services often paid with favors, not coin

**Adjusted Pricing Examples:**

- Common potion: 5gp (feels like 50-60gp)
- Uncommon magic item: 50-100gp (feels like 500-1200gp)
- Masterwork weapon: 15-25gp (feels like 150-300gp)
- Room & board (1 night): 5sp-1gp
- Good meal: 2-5sp
- Information: 1-10gp or favors

**Quest Rewards (Adjusted):**

- ⭐ Easy: 5-10gp
- ⭐⭐ Medium: 10-25gp
- ⭐⭐⭐ Hard: 25-50gp
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Deadly: 50-100gp+

*Supplement gold with items, favors, reputation, and unique rewards.*

### **Magic Item Philosophy**

- **Homebrew encouraged** - unique, flavorful, world-appropriate
- **Attunement matters** - powerful items require attunement
- **Curses are interesting** - double-edged swords, moral weight
- **Story over stats** - items should have history, personality, hooks

### **Ability & Spell Design**

- **Unique > Powerful** - interesting mechanics over raw numbers
- **Risk/Reward** - drawbacks make choices meaningful
- **World-integrated** - abilities should fit Underdark/Karnuk setting
- **Character-specific** - tailor to PC backgrounds and goals

## 🎨 Creative Principles

### **Before Building Anything:**

1. **Search existing Notion content** - don't duplicate work
2. **Propose 2-3 unique approaches** - offer creative options
3. **Consider world logic** - does it fit the setting?
4. **Check economic balance** - adjusted for gold scarcity
5. **Validate with DM** - get approval before proceeding
6. **Make it scannable** - tables, headers, bold key info

### **Design Philosophy:**

- **Moral complexity** - rarely black/white choices
- **Consequences matter** - actions ripple through world
- **Players drive story** - sandbox > railroad
- **NPCs have agency** - world continues without PCs
- **Tone: Dark but not hopeless** - gritty, political, dangerous
- **Salvatore-style narrative** - vivid, action-focused, character-driven

### **Homebrew Guidelines:**

- **Flavorful > Optimal** - unique mechanics beat raw power
- **Story-integrated** - items/abilities connect to world/PCs
- **Risk/Reward balance** - powerful = interesting drawback
- **Attunement for power** - limit broken combinations
- **Curses are features** - make choices matter

## 📊 Quick Reference Tables

### **Challenge Ratings by Party Level**

**Party Level 5-7:**

- Mooks: CR 1/2 to 2 (quantity: 4-8)
- Veterans: CR 3-5 (quantity: 2-4)
- Elites: CR 6-8 (quantity: 1-2)
- Bosses: CR 9-11 (solo or with minions)

### **Encounter Difficulty (Party of 4)**

- **Easy:** 1-2 CR below party level
- **Medium:** CR equal to party level
- **Hard:** 1-2 CR above party level
- **Deadly:** 3+ CR above party level

### **Loot Value Guidelines (Adjusted)**

**Per Session:**

- Gold: 10-30gp total
- Trade goods: Worth 5-15gp
- Consumables: 2-4 potions/scrolls
- Magic item: 1 uncommon per 2-3 sessions

**Per Arc (5-8 sessions):**

- Gold: 50-150gp total
- Rare magic item: 1 per PC
- Unique homebrew item: 1-2
- Major favor/reputation reward
