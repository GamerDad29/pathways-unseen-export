# Bpops Dream

*The scene shifts, your vision clouded by a strange, misty darkness. Shapes begin to form—trees twisted and warped, their branches reaching like skeletal hands. You see a figure standing among the shadows, its form tall and commanding, cloaked in ancient, glistening chains that stretch outwards, anchoring into the very fabric of reality. You feel a chill creep over your skin as the chains rattle, echoing through the void.*

*Then, the figure speaks, a deep voice that resonates through your very bones:*

*"Beware... The Chain stirs."*

*The figure turns, and though its face is shadowed, you sense its gaze piercing through you. The chains rattle louder, the sound rising to a cacophony that fills your mind, each link clinking like a bell tolling doom. The ground shifts beneath your feet, and you fall, tumbling into the abyss…*

*You wake with a start, your heart pounding, your hand brushing against your arm—and that’s when you feel it. A new tattoo, fresh and raw, lines burning with a faint, cold light. As your eyes adjust, you see intricate patterns forming down the palm of your hand…an elven throne shackled in chains*
