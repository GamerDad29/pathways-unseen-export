# Introducting Morrighan

---

### **🗺️ Map Description: The Shadowed Grove**

---

**As the party winds their way through the labyrinthine tunnels of the Underdark, the path begins to narrow, the air growing colder with each step. The faint, distant sounds of dripping water echo like a distant heartbeat, growing softer until they vanish entirely. Ahead, the tunnel opens into a vast cavern, but what awaits them is far from the lively vibrancy one might expect in a place touched by the Feywild.**

**The grove that lies before them is a haunting sight—what might have once been a thriving, magical forest of towering mushrooms has now succumbed to a pervasive decay. The massive fungal stalks stretch high into the cavern’s ceiling, their caps drooping under the weight of their own rot. Once vibrant, luminescent colors are now muted, faded to ashen grays and sickly greens. The bioluminescent fungi that cling to the stone walls cast a ghostly, greenish glow across the grove, their light flickering as if struggling against an unseen force.**

**The ground beneath the party’s feet is soft, almost spongy, covered in a thick layer of decaying plant matter. The air is thick with the pungent odor of decay—a mix of damp earth and the unmistakable stench of rot, clinging to the back of the throat. The silence is unsettling, broken only by the occasional creak or groan from the towering mushrooms as if the grove itself were alive and in pain.**

**Here and there, the remnants of what might have once been small Fey creatures lay scattered—tiny bones half-buried in the detritus, overgrown with dark moss. The sense of loss and abandonment is palpable, as if the very essence of the grove has been drained away, leaving only a husk of what once was.**

**In the center of the grove, where the light is weakest and the shadows are longest, a particularly large mushroom stands, its cap split and oozing a thick, black sap that drips slowly onto the ground below, forming a pool that reflects the dim light like a mirror of ink. It is here that the party feels the temperature drop even further, a chill that seeps into their bones as the shadows seem to shift and coalesce, bringing with them the unmistakable presence of something—or someone—watching them.**

---

### **🌒 The Encounter**

**As the party steps into the grove, they notice the shadows seem to shift unnaturally. The temperature drops noticeably, and the already sparse light seems to dim further. The towering mushrooms, their caps drooping and stems blackened with decay, cast long, distorted shadows. Suddenly, one of the shadows begins to move—unnaturally stretching and coalescing into the shape of a small figure. Morrighan Bustlewing emerges from the darkness, her dark wings folded around her like a cloak, blending seamlessly with the gloomy surroundings. Her presence is both commanding and unsettling, perfectly at home in this macabre setting.**

**Dialogue:**

Morrighan’s voice carries a quiet strength as she addresses the party, her tone calm and assured, yet tinged with an eerie resonance.

*"So, you are the ones Drogon spoke of. He has sent me to guide you through these forsaken depths. This grove... it was once vibrant, full of life, but now it echoes with the finality of death—a fitting place for our meeting. Together, we shall ensure that death does not claim what it should not—or perhaps, that it does."*

She gazes at each party member with her intense, gleaming eyes, assessing them before continuing.

*"The Feywild has many secrets, and I bring with me the knowledge of death’s delicate balance. I trust you understand the gravity of this mission. There is more at stake here than the lives of mere miners."*

---

### **🔮 Outcome**

- **Joining the Party:** Morrighan explains that her Feywild contact, someone Drogon trusts implicitly, has sent her to assist in their quest. She is here to help them navigate the darker, more dangerous paths of the Underdark, and to use her abilities to ensure that their mission succeeds. Her connection to the Feywild and her understanding of death will be crucial, especially when dealing with the unknown threats that lie ahead.
- **Insight and Guidance:** Morrighan provides the party with insights into the dangers they may face, particularly the supernatural or death-related hazards that could await them. She warns them of places where the veil between life and death is thin, and where spirits might linger.
- **Long-Term Impact:** Morrighan’s introduction sets the tone for a deeper, more mystical aspect of the campaign. Her presence hints at the involvement of powerful Feywild entities and foreshadows encounters with otherworldly forces that the party may not fully understand but must navigate with caution.

---

### **📜 Drogon's Message**

Before the party departed, Drogon left them with a cryptic message:

*"In the darkness where life and death entwine, you will find an ally who walks between worlds. Trust her guidance, for she carries the wisdom of those who see beyond the mortal veil."*
