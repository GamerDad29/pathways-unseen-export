## 🎭 **Brindle Nacklewitt III** – Player Description

A small figure emerges from behind a towering mushroom stalk, no taller than a halfling but with an energy that fills the cavern. His **bright eyes glitter with boundless curiosity**, darting from person to person as if each of you is the most fascinating thing he’s ever seen — which, at this exact moment, might be true.

His **sandy-blonde hair sticks up in a haphazard topknot**, more accident than style, with stray bits of moss and crystal dust clinging to it. A **patched vest of mismatched colors** hangs over a travel-worn tunic, and at his side swings a satchel that bulges with trinkets, mushrooms, shiny stones, and the occasional glint of something that might actually belong to one of you.

He grins wide, a grin that says he’s already decided you’re friends, whether you agree or not. His **voice is rapid, playful, and impossible to ignore**, words tumbling out faster than you can keep up with.

And when he introduces himself, he does so with exaggerated flair:

> “Brindle Nacklewitt the Third, at your service! Healer of owies, finder of shinies, and — oh! — borrower of things you didn’t even know you’d lost. Don’t worry, I always give them back… eventually.”
> 

Around him, there’s an air of **strange luck and improbable survival**. Despite being alone in a cavern filled with plague, shadows, and horrors, he seems completely unafraid — as though danger simply hasn’t figured out how to catch him yet.

# 🎲 Brindle Nacklewitt III Roll Tables

## 🌱 Out of Combat (d12)

Roll whenever Brindle rummages in his satchel, tries to "help," or gets curious.

1. **Pocket Pastry** – Produces a half-stale pastry from Krynn. Restores 1d4 HP if eaten, but tastes like moldy honey.
2. **Borrowed Trinket** – Produces something from a party member's pocket. "Oh, was this yours? I was just keeping it safe!"
3. **Glowcap Shroom** – A glowing mushroom. Provides bright light in 10 ft. and dim in 20 ft. for 1 hour.
4. **Dusty Compass** – Spins wildly. Points toward the nearest crystal vein.
5. **Strange Key** – A brass key that opens… *something.* Not anything here, though.
6. **Helpful Herb** – Smells awful, but acts as antitoxin when brewed into tea.
7. **Bag of Pebbles** – Mundane stones… except one hums faintly with ley energy.
8. **Sling Stone with Face** – Stone painted like a smiley face. If thrown, casts *Thaumaturgy* (sound effect).
9. **Spider Fang Necklace** – Harmless, but wearing it grants +1 to Intimidation for 1 hour.
10. **Kender Tales** – He tells a long, winding story that accidentally reveals lore about the Shadow Plague (or something he shouldn't know).
11. **Moth in a Jar** – Escapes, shedding faintly glowing dust. Restores 1 spell slot (1st level) to a caster.
12. **Oops!** – Drops something fragile (crystal shard, vial, egg sac). DM decides if it breaks or reveals something important.

## ⚔️ In Combat (d12)

Roll when Brindle takes his "Trickster Actions" or when you want chaos mid-fight.

1. **Oops, I Fixed It!** – Heals an ally for 1d8+2 HP.
2. **Slip & Sling** – Accidentally shoots sling stone at an enemy: 1d6 damage.
3. **Distracting Shout** – Taunts an enemy; that enemy makes a DC 13 Wis save or attacks with disadvantage until next turn.
4. **Pocket Smoke** – Throws a smoke pellet: creates 10-ft lightly obscured area for 1 round.
5. **Spider Silk Spool** – Tosses sticky silk: enemy must succeed DC 12 Dex save or be restrained until end of next turn.
6. **Tripwire Trick** – Sets a quick tripline (5 ft space). First enemy to move through must succeed DC 12 Dex save or fall prone.
7. **Satchel Surprise** – Pulls random item (d6): mushroom bomb, sling stone, rope, tiny vial of ichor, squeaky toy, glowing shard.
8. **Lucky Dodge** – Gains advantage on his next saving throw.
9. **Distracting Spark** – Crystal fragment glows in his hand. Enemy in 15 ft must save DC 13 Con or be blinded until end of turn.
10. **Healing Poultice** – Uses an herbal concoction: stabilizes dying ally automatically.
11. **Unnerving Giggle** – Giggles at the worst moment. One enemy within 10 ft makes DC 12 Wis save or is frightened until end of turn.
12. **Catastrophic Success** – Pulls a trinket that accidentally mimics a cantrip (*Sacred Flame*, *Minor Illusion*, or *Prestidigitation*, DM choice).

6. **Chime of Restful Sleep** – When rung, all allies within 30 ft. gain the benefits of a short rest in 15 minutes. 1/day.
7. **Coin of Unlikely Fortune** – Flip before a roll. On heads, gain advantage. On tails, regain 1d4 temporary hit points.
8. **Jar of Fey Honey** – One spoonful heals 1d8 HP and grants advantage on the next saving throw vs. charm or fear. 5 uses.
9. **Crystalline Latch-Key** – Insert into any mundane lock. The lock opens… and closes again 1 minute later with an arcane seal (DC 18 to break).
10. **Potion of Glittering Steps** – Drinking this potion allows you to cast *Misty Step* 1d4 times over the next hour. Leaves a trail of faint sparkles.
11. **Feather of the Moon-Owl** – When held aloft, grants the *Faerie Fire* spell (DC 14) once per day.
12. **Band of Shared Burdens** – A silver ring that lets the wearer transfer up to 10 damage per round to themselves from an ally within 10 ft.
13. **Satchel of Odd Remedies** – At dawn, roll a d4. Satchel contains that many "poultices" which act as *Healing Word* (1d4+2).
14. **Mask of Spore Sight** – A crude fungal mask. Grants advantage on Perception checks in dim light/darkness and lets you see invisible creatures outlined in spores for 1 minute (1/day).
15. **Cup of Eternal Broth** – Produces hot soup once per day that heals 2d6 HP to those who drink it and removes 1 level of exhaustion.
16. **Stone of Echoing Courage** – Once per day, can cast *Heroism* (2 allies). The stone glows faintly with golden veins.
17. **Webbed Grapple Hook** – Made from hardened Mycolurk silk. Can reach twice as far as a normal rope (100 ft). Once per day, casts *Web* when thrown.
18. **Charm of Feystep** – A delicate pendant. Once per long rest, the wearer can teleport 30 ft. and becomes invisible until the start of their next turn.
19. **Everbloom Mushroom** – Glows faintly pink. When eaten, heals 1d6 HP and grants resistance to necrotic damage for 10 minutes. 3 uses before it wilts.
20. **Kender's Lucky Button** – A shiny button Brindle insists he found in a dragon's hoard. Once per day, reroll any failed d20 roll.
