# Scene: The Stone-Root Hall of Ulfgar

*Beneath a mountain. Beneath silence. Where secrets are kept in stone and flame.*

---

The circle hums once, then fades. Your boots touch Ulfgar’s stone once more.

But the air here is not the same.

Gone is the simmering peace of UTTP or the chaos of surface trade. This hall is a war-wound stitched in granite. Heavy banners drape from walls carved with names of the fallen. Long-forgotten runes pulse dimly on the pillars—reminders of oaths made in darker ages.

At the center of it all, standing beneath a burning anvil suspended by blacksteel chains, waits **Viceroy Drogan Stonebeard**.

His armor is etched with slag and soot. His beard—iron-threaded and fire-beaded—has grown since last you saw him, but the eyes remain the same. One glints like golden ore. The other is a dead gem—still, polished, unblinking.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "Well now. Look what the Deep spat back."
> 

He doesn’t approach—he waits. Dwarves do not rush family.

When you speak, you see it: the tremor in his jaw when Veylan’s name is uttered. The stillness when “Warforged” and “teleportation glyphs” are described. And finally, when “Seraphine,” “Shadow Plague,” and “Deep Syndicate” are laid bare—he closes his one good eye.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "Damn it all. They were always testing the wards. Always… sniffing for where the stone thins."
> 

He moves to a wide slab of obsidian near the war table. With a gloved hand, he wipes dust from a carved channel—revealing an old, red-inked mark.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "Karnuk. That's where the rail lines cross like veins. Where metal sings to mages, and secrets hide in the slag. It's where we sent our outcasts. Our firebloods. Our dead who wouldn’t lie still."
> 

He looks back to you now. The anger is gone. What remains is resolve.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "They’re building something there. Beneath the forges. Beneath the syndicate fronts. And now the Warforged bleed shadow, and changelings trade names like coin."
> 

He places a closed fist on the map. When he opens it, you see a **black iron badge**, scorched on the edges—its rune partially melted.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "This was pulled from a dead dwarf’s spine. Found in Karnuk’s refuse tunnels. Still glowing. Still humming."
> 

He offers it forward.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "You say you’re ready for the next step. This is it. Karnuk won't greet you with coin and council. They’ll try to bury you before the gate closes."
> 

He straightens, full height now, voice rising with fire in his throat.

> Drogan:
> 
> 
> "You don’t just walk into Karnuk. You descend. You burn. You survive. And if you come out again… we’ll *all* know what breathes beneath the fire."
>
