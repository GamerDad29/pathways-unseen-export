## **Bregan D’aerthe Membership – What It Means for Iblith**

### **I. Core Identity Within the Syndicate**

**Role:**

Iblith is now a *Bounty Agent* in **the Outer Circle** of Bregan D’aerthe—freelance, deadly, and selectively trusted. He is **not yet Inner Circle**, but he’s *on their radar*. His deeds are watched. His results are whispered about.

**Path Status:**

Yes, Iblith remains a **bounty hunter**—but now **his contracts come from deeper sources**. No longer working street-level; he’s working from Bregan dossiers, often tied to **political sabotage**, **rogue assets**, or **intelligence collection**.

---

### **II. Immediate Mechanical and Narrative Perks**

**1. The Sigil of Shadow Trust (Item):**

A silver coin etched with Bregan’s symbol. Functions as:

- **Proof of Rank**: Grants access to restricted areas where Bregan agents operate.
- **Whispered Network**: Once per long rest, Iblith can speak a secret phrase and cast *Sending* to any known Bregan agent.
- **Shadow Favor**: Once per week, Iblith may call in **a single favor** from a Bregan cell in any major city (spy help, transport, misinformation, etc.).

**2. Gold & Gear Access:**

He gains access to **Bregan-exclusive black market gear**, poisons, and disguise kits. Prices are 20% lower than surface rates and occasionally he’s gifted **experimental items** for field testing.

**3. Bounty Priority:**

He’s assigned **Tier II bounties**—targets with broader implications (traitors, changeling infiltrators, rogue artificers). He receives **first right of refusal** on such bounties in his zone.

**4. Optional Training Path:**

If you want to explore progression, Iblith may undergo **Shadowstep Training**, unlocking advanced stealth and teleportation techniques. (Narrative trial required—usually includes working directly under **Thalvorn**, Bregan’s elite operative.)

---

### **III. Expectations & Dangers**

**Loyalty is Expected, Not Assumed:**

Jarlaxle doesn’t demand obedience—but he *demands results*. Iblith’s progress will be tracked by operatives. If he fails missions or betrays syndicate secrets, he’ll be hunted.

**The Inner Circle Watches:**

Missions may be “tests” in disguise. Occasionally, he’ll be shadowed by another Bregan agent (known or not) to assess loyalty and skill.

**No Public Face:**

He can’t openly flaunt Bregan membership. If asked, he denies. If compromised, he’s expected to vanish or eliminate witnesses. It's not a guild. It's a **phantom shadow**.

### **IV. Opportunities from the Inside**

**1. Bregan D’aerthe Contracts Table (Coming Soon):**

A rollable table of bounties, ranging from spy extraction to changeling assassinations, corruption hits, or even *turning* enemy agents.

**2. Cross-City Influence:**

**3. Special Missions:**

When the Shadow Plague thread unravels, Jarlaxle may ask Iblith to do what no one else can: **silence the source**. That could mean traveling across planes, poisoning a planar well, or assassinating a “friendly” NPC who’s gone too far.
