# 🧾 Frank’s Rewards & The Broker’s Contact

---

# 🎭 Narrative Setup

When the party returns, Frank greets them with nervous excitement — pacing, hands wringing, but his grin is genuine. He showers them with thanks, offers food and drink, and produces a series of **“special selections”** from the deeper vaults of his Phylactery.

Before handing anything over, Frank lowers his voice:

> “I wasn’t the one who made this possible, you know. My benefactor — a very clever sort — is interested in your futures. You’ll find him in Karnuk, if you’re wise enough to look for him. Folks call him The Broker. Dangerous? Certainly. But he pays well for results.”
> 

This ties neatly into your Broker NPC — the one pulling strings behind Frank, already watching the party with interest.

# 🎁 Reward Options (6 each, tailored by archetype)

---

### ⚔️ **For Cannonball (Barbarian)**

1. **Muggy’s Twin** – A smaller mimic-mug, non-sentient, that always keeps drinks cold or hot. (Flavorful gag gift.)

### 2. **The Bear’s Fang**

A crude fang dangling from a bronze loop, carved with Bear Clan runes.

- **Effect:** When Cannonball roars or uses Intimidation, he gains advantage if the target can see the earring. If he crits on an Intimidation roll, the target must immediately attack him on their next turn if able.

## ⚔️ Cannonball (Halfling Barbarian – Path of the Giant)

1. **Embersteel Mesh Wraps**
    - Blackened Forge Dwarf steel hammered into flexible bands that coil around his forearms.
    - While wielding a greataxe, add **+1 damage**.
    - Once per day, when raging, his reach extends an extra 5 ft. for one attack as the wraps flare with molten light.
2. **Totem of the Roaring Giant**
    - A carved bone idol lashed with iron.
    - Once per short rest, unleash a roar that forces creatures of your choice within 30 ft. to make a Wis save (DC 14).
    - On a fail, they must target him until the start of his next turn. On success, they have disadvantage on attacks that don’t include him.
3. **Belt of the Mountain Root**
    - Thick leather girdle etched with dwarven knotwork.
    - Once per long rest, while raging, he can **pull a creature within 20 ft. up to 10 ft. closer** as a bonus action (Strength save DC 14).
4. **Stonebrew Cask**
    - A dwarven mini-keg strapped to his hip.
    - Once per long rest, drink to gain temp HP equal to his Con modifier + level.
    - When the effect ends, he suffers disadvantage on Wisdom saves until his next short rest.
5. **Bear-Claw Fetish**
    - Primitive bone charm tied with bear hair.
    - Once per rage, he may reroll a failed Strength save.
6. **Grumblegut, the Mimic Strap** *(new sentient companion)*
    - A living leather strap that binds to his weapon haft. It grumbles, mutters, and sometimes bites at enemies when he rages.
    - Currently harmless, but grows more animated with each kill while attached.
    - Flavor gag (Bear Clan “collects odd pets”), but over time it may evolve:
        - Stage I: Just grumbles and shifts.
        - Stage II: Can *lash out* for 1 damage when Cannonball crits.
        - Stage III: Develops a crude voice/personality, calling for blood.

## 🐀 Iblith (Drow Half-Elf Monk – Way of Shadow, Bregan D’aerthe)

Let’s give him the **crossbow loader gadget** you described — sleek, silent, and flexible.

1. **Poisoner’s Kit, Parliament-Made** – +2 to poison crafting rolls.
2. **Cloak of Obscured Sight** – Once per long rest, hood grants invisibility for 1 round.
3. **Silent Bolt Reservoir** – A sleek, mechanized wrist-loader crafted in the Bregan D’aerthe style. Holds 3 specialty bolts at a time, each **1/day use**:
    - **Shadow Bolt** – Deals +1d6 necrotic.
    - **Snaring Bolt** – On hit, target must succeed on a Str save DC 13 or be restrained until end of their next turn.
    - **Venom Bolt** – On hit, target must succeed on a Con save DC 13 or take 2d6 poison.
        
        Reloads at dawn.
        
4. **Ledger of Faces** – Criminal sketches; advantage on Deception/Disguise for 24 hours.
5. **Spider Emblem Token** – Break to summon harmless illusory drider distraction (keep if you want as flavor).
6. **Contact’s Missive (Bregan D’aerthe)** – A sealed note from Jarlaxle’s operative (that V-name drow from UTTP). Gives him access to Bregan’s shadow-ritual to unlock +1 attunement slot.

## 🐦 Morrighan (Fey Warlock, pivoting into Sorcerer)

We’ll lean her kit into **sorcerer foreshadowing + fey arcana**, removing wand/candle duplicates.

1. **Pendant of Hollow Flame** – Once per long rest, grants resistance to fire for 1 minute.
2. **Ring of Shadowsong** – When casting spells, faint whispers impose disadvantage on Insight checks against her.
3. **Glittering Ember Veil** – A translucent scarf that shimmers with ember-motes. Once per long rest, she may reroll a failed Charisma-based save or spell attack.
4. **Fey-Touched Tearstone** – A crystal vial holding a single iridescent tear. When crushed, it restores one expended 2nd-level spell slot. (1/week)
5. **Sorcerous Embershard** – A blackened crystal shard that hums with unstable arcane energy. Once per long rest, reroll a failed Charisma save or spell attack. Resonates as her sorcerer bloodline awakens.
6. **Ashen Grimoire Leaf** – A single burned page from a fey grimoire. Once per long rest, when casting a spell, she may add +1d4 to the spell’s damage or healing.

# 🧭 GM Notes

- **The Broker** is the unifying thread: every PC gets either a **clue, note, or token** tying them directly to him in Karnuk.
- **Flexibility:** Each character has 6 options, allowing you to select what best fits their arc or roll randomly.
- **Flavorful Tie-In:** Rewards blend useful combat boosts with quirky, story-rich items (tying into Seraphine, Muggy, Shadow Plague, and Fey Grove).
