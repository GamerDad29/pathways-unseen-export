### 🎭 **Frank’s Request: “A Filament Most Fabulous”**

Frank glides up with that unmistakable sparkle in his eye, swirling a martini that somehow glows neon pink.

> “Darlings, you simply must help me. I’m working on my next masterpiece—a self-wrapping corset of sentient silk—but I’m woefully short on the only thread strong and suggestive enough for the job: Mycolurk webbing.
> 
> 
> “You’ll know it when you see it—**it glistens like moonlight on oiled desire and smells faintly of unspoken promises**. And yes, the spiders are a tad territorial, but oh, the things I could craft with such material!”
> 
> He twirls in place, sparkles bursting behind him. “Fetch me a few solid strands, and I’ll open my *real* inventory. The **behind-the-curtain stock**. Discretion and decadence guaranteed.”
> 

---

### 🧶 **In-World Reason**

- **Mycolurk silk** is *incredibly strong*, naturally reactive to temperature shifts, and conducts enchantment better than silver thread.
- Frank’s experiments into **glamour-threaded illusionwear** require this rare filament.
- Only this silk can **withstand the enchantments** used in his line of “combat-ready couture.”

### **🕷️ Mycolurk – Underdark Fungal-Spider Hybrid**

**Creature Type:** Aberration (or Monstrosity, GM’s discretion)

**CR Estimate:** 2–4, depending on maturity

**Environment:** Spore-laced, bioluminescent caverns; nests suspended by silk across chasms or over fungal groves

---

### **Traits and Behavior**

- **Silk Production:** Mycolurks produce a filament known as **Mycolurk Silk**—iridescent, scent-shifting, and enchantment-conductive. It reacts to light and heat, making it highly prized for illusionwork and reactive armor.
- **Territorial:** Mycolurks aggressively defend their nests and silk reserves. They are drawn to movement and warmth but retreat if their silk is scorched or disrupted.
- **Hive-Like Instincts:** Though not intelligent, they function in clusters and show rudimentary pack strategy, such as herding prey or protecting egg sacs.
- **Spore Camouflage:** Their bodies secrete harmless yet disorienting spore clouds when threatened—imposing disadvantage on ranged attacks or Insight checks.
- **Venom:** Their bite may induce **hallucinogenic visions** or **mild paralysis** (Con save DC 13, GM may use effects similar to *Tasha’s Hideous Laughter* or *Slow*).

---

### **Known Uses of Mycolurk Silk**

- Conducts enchantment more efficiently than silver or mithril thread.
- Can be woven into **glamour-reactive garments**, such as mood-shifting cloaks or illusion-bound fabrics.
- Reacts to temperature changes, making it ideal for **adaptive magical gear**.
- Incompatible with necrotic energy—disintegrates near undeath, making it useful for detecting necrotic auras.
