# Road to Uttp

## **The Journey to UTTP: A 7-Day Expedition**

Each day of travel through the upper-underdark offers a unique blend of wonder and danger, with the changing landscape shaping the adventurers' experiences. Below is a fully immersive description for each day’s journey and the corresponding campsite.

---

### **Day 1: The Descent Begins**

**Travel Description:**

The air grows cooler as the party descends deeper into the Underdark. The tunnel’s walls are jagged, marked by deep grooves where ancient tools once mined precious ore. In some places, faint glimmers of mica catch the torchlight, casting fleeting rainbows on the stone. A low hum resonates through the tunnels, almost like a heartbeat, broken occasionally by the faint skittering of unseen creatures. The path narrows at intervals, forcing single-file movement, while the oppressive quiet presses down like a shroud.

**Campsite: The Narrow Nook**

The group discovers a small alcove nestled against the rock face. A patch of glowing moss provides faint green light, throwing long, flickering shadows across the uneven floor. The alcove is dry and shielded from the winds that whisper faintly through the tunnels, but its cramped quarters leave little room for comfort.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“As you settle into the alcove, the faint glow of the moss lends an otherworldly feel to the space, its light pulsing softly like a sleeping heartbeat. The rock walls are cool to the touch, etched with grooves and scars that whisper of tools long since abandoned. The air is still, heavy with the scent of earth and moss, broken only by the occasional distant drip of water. It feels safe—for now—but the shadows cling close, as though unwilling to relinquish their secrets.”

---

### **Day 2: The Forgotten Crossroads**

**Travel Description:**

The tunnel widens into a sprawling cavern where paths diverge in multiple directions, each disappearing into darkness. Ancient stone markers, worn smooth by time, stand sentinel at the crossroads. Faint etchings in an extinct dialect of Dwarvish hint at this place’s former significance. A soft blue glow emanates from a subterranean river far below, its light dancing across the cavern walls like ghostly flames. The air here is damp, carrying the faint metallic tang of minerals and the sweet scent of bioluminescent algae.

**Campsite: The Forgotten Crossroads Camp**

A flat outcrop overlooking the glowing river provides a serene spot to rest. The remnants of an old campfire lie at its center, the blackened stones arranged in a careful circle. An ancient stone bench, its edges smoothed by countless years, sits nearby, offering a semblance of comfort amidst the rugged terrain.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“As you approach the outcrop, the faint blue glow of the river far below casts strange shadows across the cavern walls. The campfire stones, blackened and cracked, whisper of travelers who came before you, their stories lost to time. The stone bench offers an unexpected touch of civilization amidst the wilderness, its surface cool and smooth beneath your fingers. Above, the cavern ceiling stretches high, dotted with faintly glowing patches of algae, their light mingling with the river’s eerie luminescence. The air is damp, carrying a chill that seeps into your bones, yet there’s a strange tranquility here, as though the cavern itself is holding its breath.”

---

### **Day 3: The Fire Veins**

**Travel Description:**

The temperature rises sharply as the tunnel slopes downward, the walls growing darker, streaked with veins of molten rock that glow a deep, menacing red. The air grows thick and heavy, carrying the acrid scent of sulfur. The occasional hiss of steam escaping from cracks in the stone adds to the oppressive atmosphere, and faint tremors ripple beneath your feet. Shadows seem to flicker and twist in the dim red light, giving the illusion of movement where none exists.

**Campsite: The Fire Veins Rest Stop**

A natural alcove carved into the rock provides some relief from the heat. Its walls are lined with smooth shards of obsidian, which reflect the flickering glow of the molten veins. The ground is warm to the touch, forcing careful placement of bedrolls to avoid discomfort.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“The alcove offers a brief respite from the oppressive heat of the fire veins, though the air remains thick and stifling. Obsidian shards glisten like black glass, their edges catching the red glow of the molten rock outside. The faint hiss of escaping steam fills the air, a constant reminder of the volatile environment. Placing your bedrolls requires care, as the ground radiates warmth, threatening to singe exposed skin. The flickering light creates shifting shadows on the walls, turning the space into a kaleidoscope of red and black.”

### **Day 3: Orc Band Encounter at the Fire Veins**

In the oppressive heat of molten veins, the party encounters **Krashak Ironscar** and his band. Tasked with guarding Borin’s trade routes, the orcs are unaware of Borin’s fate. The fiery backdrop of the veins sets the stage for a tense encounter.

- **Krashak Ironscar (Orc War Chief, Level 5):**
    
    *“We are the iron that guards the flame. Speak your truths, or face my wrath.”*
    
- **Outcomes:**
    - Diplomacy: Persuasion (DC 15) convinces the orcs to aid the party.
    - Combat: Failure results in a close-quarters battle against the orc band.
    

**To the Esteemed Drogon Stonebeard,**

We, the Clan of Broken Tusks, under Chief Grulfang Iron-Eye, swear fealty to you and your cause. As agreed, we shall guard the trade routes near Uttp and the Ironvein Mine, keeping them safe from all trespassers. In exchange, you will provide us with supplies: steel for weapons, grains for our people, and ale to warm our hearts.

We know well the rising tensions with the surface-dwellers. Let them feel our might if they seek to disrupt our pact. Your enemies shall be our enemies, and the blood of any who challenge you shall soak the ground.

May this pact bind us in strength.

**Signed,**
Chief Grulfang Iron-Eye

Broken Tusks Clan

### **Abilities**

**Orcish Fury (3/Day)**

When Grulfang is reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, he can drop to 1 hit point instead.

**Relentless Endurance (1/Day)**

If Grulfang fails a saving throw or would be knocked unconscious, he can choose to automatically succeed instead.

**Indomitable (3/Day)**

Grulfang can reroll a failed saving throw.

**Great Weapon Master**

Grulfang can take a -5 penalty to attack rolls with a heavy weapon to deal +10 damage on a hit.

**Savage Attacker**

Once per turn, Grulfang rerolls a weapon damage die and uses the higher result.

**Reckless Attack**

At the start of his turn, Grulfang can attack recklessly, gaining advantage on all melee attack rolls, but all attack rolls against him have advantage until his next turn.

**Brutal Critical (3 Dice)**

Grulfang rolls three additional weapon damage dice when he scores a critical hit.

**Danger Sense**

Grulfang has advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects he can see.

**Frenzy (Berserker Rage)**

When raging, Grulfang can make a bonus action melee attack on each of his turns.

**Fast Movement**

Grulfang’s movement increases by 10 feet while not wearing heavy armor.

**Unstoppable Rage**

Grulfang cannot be charmed, frightened, or incapacitated while raging.

**Retaliation**

When a creature within 5 feet of Grulfang hits him with an attack, he can use his reaction to make a melee weapon attack.

---

### **Actions**

**Multiattack**

Grulfang makes three attacks with his double-bladed axe.

**Double-Bladed Axe (Bloodspite)**

*Melee Weapon Attack:* +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.

*Hit:* 22 (2d10 + 7) slashing damage, or 32 (2d10 + 7 + 10) if using Great Weapon Master.

- **Cleave**: On a hit, Grulfang can attack another target within 5 feet of the original target.

**Furious Leap (Recharge 5-6)**

Grulfang leaps up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space within range. Creatures within 10 feet of the point where he lands must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) bludgeoning damage on a failed save or half as much on a success.

**Berserker's Roar (Recharge 6)**

Grulfang lets out a blood-curdling roar. All creatures within 30 feet must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. Creatures frightened in this way can repeat the saving throw at the end of their turns.

---

### **Reactions**

**Counterstrike**

If a creature misses Grulfang with a melee attack, he can use his reaction to immediately make a melee weapon attack against that creature.

**Deflecting Sweep**

When a creature within 5 feet of Grulfang targets an ally with an attack, Grulfang can use his reaction to impose disadvantage on that attack by striking their weapon aside with his axe.

---

### **Legendary Actions**

Grulfang can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature’s turn.

**Mighty Strike**

Grulfang makes one melee weapon attack.

**Tactical Advance**

Grulfang moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Rage Unleashed (Costs 2 Actions)**

Grulfang enters a rage if he is not already raging.

### **Day 4: The Glowing Glade**

**Travel Description:**

The oppressive heat gives way to a sudden, surreal beauty as the tunnel opens into a cavern filled with towering fungal trees. Their caps glow softly in hues of blue and green, casting a tranquil light over the moss-covered floor. The air here is fresh, tinged with a faint sweetness, and the sounds of the Underdark fade into an almost reverent silence. Small streams wind through the glade, their waters sparkling with faint bioluminescence.

**Campsite: The Glowing Glade**

Beneath the largest fungal tree, a natural hollow forms a shelter. Its floor is cushioned by thick, soft moss, and its canopy provides a sense of protection. The atmosphere feels almost enchanted, a rare haven in the harshness of the Underdark.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“The glade is a vision of serenity, its glowing fungal trees casting gentle light over the moss-covered ground. Beneath the largest tree, a natural hollow offers a perfect shelter, its floor soft and inviting. The air carries a faint sweetness, the kind that lingers at the edge of memory, evoking distant, forgotten comforts. Streams wind lazily through the glade, their bioluminescent waters adding a faint shimmer to the already ethereal scene. For the first time in days, the darkness feels distant, replaced by an almost sacred tranquility.”

---

### **Day 5: The Chasm Ledge**

**Travel Description:**

The path narrows again as the cavern walls rise sharply on either side, guiding the party to a ledge overlooking a vast chasm. The faint glow of lava far below illuminates the jagged rock formations, casting eerie, shifting shadows. The air grows colder, tinged with the faint metallic tang of stone and a lingering dampness that clings to your skin. Loose gravel crunches underfoot, echoing ominously into the abyss.

**Campsite: The Chasm Ledge**

The ledge offers just enough space to set up camp, though its proximity to the drop adds a sense of unease. The chill air is a stark contrast to the heat of the fire veins, and the distant hum of the trading post is a faint, comforting reminder of civilization.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“The ledge is narrow but stable, its rocky surface cold beneath your hands. Far below, the faint glow of molten rock casts an ominous light, illuminating jagged formations that seem to reach toward you like grasping hands. The air is damp, clinging to your skin with an almost ghostly touch. Though the space is quiet, the occasional echo of a falling stone reminds you of the vastness of the chasm beyond. In the distance, the faint hum of activity promises the trading post is near, lending a sliver of comfort.”

---

### **Day 6: The Crystalline Chamber**

**Travel Description:**

The cavern opens into a breathtaking crystalline chamber, its walls and ceiling studded with shards of quartz that refract the light into a kaleidoscope of colors. The air hums faintly, the vibrations resonating through your bones. A faint mist rises from small pools scattered across the floor, their surfaces shimmering with an iridescent sheen. The atmosphere is charged with magic, tingling against your skin.

**Campsite: The Crystalline Chamber**

A natural rock formation provides a barrier from the open chamber, while the scattered crystals offer soft, refracted light. The pools of water are cool and refreshing, though their magical hum hints at a deeper mystery.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“The crystalline chamber feels like a cathedral of light, its shimmering walls casting rainbows that dance across the floor. The air is alive with a faint hum, a vibration that seems to echo within your very being. Pools of water scatter the floor, their surfaces rippling with iridescent light. Though the chamber is beautiful, its magic is palpable, creating a sense of both wonder and unease.”

---

### **Day 7: The Approach to UTTP**

**Travel Description:**

The final leg of the journey is marked by an eerie stillness. The cavern widens into a vast, open space where the faint glow of UTTP can be seen in the distance. The path is smooth but worn, the result of countless years of travel. The air grows warmer, carrying the faint scent of stone and smoke, mingled with the unmistakable smell of civilization.

**Campsite: The Outskirts of UTTP**

A flat expanse of cavern floor offers a rare luxury of space. The distant glow of UTTP is visible, casting a faint light that illuminates the area. A nearby spring provides fresh water, a rare and welcome luxury.

**Description to Read to Players:**

“The cavern opens wide, revealing the faint glow of UTTP far in the distance. The smooth stone beneath your feet feels almost unnatural, worn down by centuries of travel. The air is warm and dry, carrying the faint scent of stone and smoke. The campsite is spacious, the nearby spring offering the rare comfort of fresh, cool water. As you settle in, the promise of civilization ahead feels almost tangible, a light at the end of the endless dark.”
