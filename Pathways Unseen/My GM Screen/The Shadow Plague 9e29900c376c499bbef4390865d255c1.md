# The Shadow Plague

### **Restated Information on the Shadow Plague:**

**Ioun’s Shadow Plague** serves as a tool for comprehensive data collection, where those infected become unwitting conduits for Ioun to gather detailed information on their thoughts, actions, emotions, and experiences.

### **Symptoms:**

1. Paranoia and the feeling of being watched.
2. Darkened veins under the skin.
3. Severe insomnia and intense nightmares.
4. Memory gaps and forgetfulness.
5. Emotional detachment and numbness.
6. Whispers influencing thoughts.
7. Physical weakness and fatigue.
8. A faint, shadowy aura visible to those attuned to magic.

### **Methods of Spread:**

1. **Arcane Infection:** Through exposure to tainted spells, rituals, or artifacts.
2. **Corrupted Knowledge:** By reading forbidden texts or accessing certain knowledge.
3. **Psionic Transmission:** Via intense emotions or shared memories among psionically sensitive individuals.

These aspects are essential for understanding the role of the Shadow Plague within the **Pathways Unseen** campaign.
