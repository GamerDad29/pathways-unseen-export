**Bearhide Cloak**

- **Description**: A thick, fur-lined cloak made from the pelt of a massive bear, its fur a mix of black and silver. The hood is fashioned from the bear’s head, its gleaming teeth and fierce expression adding an intimidating flair. The inside is lined with stitched runes from the clan’s spiritual tradition.
- **Effect**:
    - Grants **resistance to cold damage**.
    - Once per long rest, Cannonball can pull the cloak tightly around himself to gain **temporary hit points equal to his level + Constitution modifier** as the spirit of the bear protects him.
- **Narrative Flavor**:
    - *“She said this was from a bear you helped bring down on your first hunt. She wanted it to protect you—said you’ve got the strength of a bear in you, but it doesn’t hurt to carry a little more.”*
