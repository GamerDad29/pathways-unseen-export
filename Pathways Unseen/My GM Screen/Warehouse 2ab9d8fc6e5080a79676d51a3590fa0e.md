## SPECIAL DISCOVERY (Automatic if they loot all three)

**Serial Plate Correlation**: All three warforged have tattooed serial numbers etched into their metal ribs:

- Tick: `SC-23:17-MARK`
- Tock: `SC-23:17-HUNT`
- Knifey-Stabby: `SC-23:17-FANG`

SC = Shadow Construct. 23:17 = Deployment timestamp (matching the chronometer). These weren't just hired muscle—they were *manufactured for this specific job*. Someone with serious resources built three warforged assassins, corrupted them with shadow-tech, synchronized their deployment, and sent them with a single objective:

**Kill the drow. Make it public. Send a message.**

The question is: message to whom?

# **THE SECRET COMPLEX BEHIND THE DOOR**

Lord Crownjaw’s “warehouse” is only the mouth of a much deeper beast.

Behind the sliding stone panel lies a **five-chamber criminal stronghold**:

---

# **SMALL ROOM 1 — The Intake Room**

The moment the door opens, dust shifts like it’s afraid of you.

**What’s inside:**

• A ledger table with chains bolted to the legs

• Several crates of contraband:

• Stolen Iron Order uniforms

• Fake Syndicate insignias

• A box of blank identity slates

• A chalkboard with a map of Karnuk smudged by frantic hands

• A bucket of old blood, half-dried

**Hook:**

A ripped page from a ledger:

“— deliver the bodies directly to C.J. No witnesses. The drow must not escape.”

---

# **SMALL ROOM 2 — The Tinkering Nook**

This room hums with half-functional machinery.

**What’s inside:**

• Disassembled Warforged parts (arms, neck couplers, facial plates)

• A cracked Shard-Battery — leaking faint purple light

• Tools meant for *precision vivisection*

• Blueprints for:

• Knife-class upgrades

• Tick/Tock ocular arrays

• A NEW design labeled only “The Maw”

**Hook:**

The machinery is recent. Someone is actively modifying or *building* warforged.

---

# **SMALL ROOM 3 — The Crowning Room**

This is Lord Crownjaw’s trophy den.

**What’s inside:**

• Severed metal masks from failed warforged

• A wall of skulls from various humanoids

• A velvet case containing:

• A gilded jawbone (his sigil)

• A message scroll:

“Crownjaw — do not fail us. The Parliament is watching.”

**Hook:**

There’s a hidden switch in the jawbone case that opens the *big room*.

---

# **LARGE ROOM — The Syndicate Strategy Floor**

Wide, tall, low-lit — this is where Crownjaw runs his empire.

**Features:**

• A massive round table carved with Karnuk’s map

• Tokens marking Syndicate, Iron Order, Deep Market, and Parliament positions

• A hanging cage above the table with a dead spy (fresh last week)

• Message tubes along the wall for coded dispatch

• Two more hidden exits

• A faint smell of embalming resin and ozone

**Key Discovery:**

A full parchment with the Shadowed Parliament’s seal burned into the corner:

“Begin the uprising. The drow weapon is only the spark.”

---

# **THE GATHERING AREA — The Lord’s Hall**

This is where Crownjaw meets clients, issues threats, and breaks kneecaps.

**What’s inside:**

• A long crescent couch of stitched hides

• A bar cart with a crystalline decanter labeled “Do Not Drink”

• A raised dais where Crownjaw sits

• A hidden balcony where two assassins usually watch

• A large mural depicting Crownjaw dragging a Syndicate lieutenant by the throat

**Major Lead:**

Under the dais is a thick journal filled with:

• Names of buyers

• Meeting schedules

• Parliament ciphers

• THREE circled entries:

• “The Drow.”

• “The Shadow Plague.”

• “The Maw — test subject pending.”
