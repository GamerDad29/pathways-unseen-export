# 🌙 **Yklwa of the Phantom Bloom**

### **Base Properties**

- You may use your **Charisma modifier** instead of Strength or Dexterity for attack and damage rolls with this weapon.
- The yklwa is considered a **spellcasting focus** for your Warlock and Sorcerer spells.

---

### **Bloom of Hexes**

- When you hit a creature with the yklwa, you may immediately apply the effects of **Hex, Hexblade’s Curse, or Unsettling Whispers** (if active) without requiring a separate bonus action.
- *This represents the weapon itself “carrying” your magic into the strike.*

---

### **Immovable Strike (Control Feature)**

- When you hit with a thrown attack, you may activate the **Immovable Rod** enchantment embedded within the weapon (no action required).
- The struck target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** (DC = 8 + Prof + Charisma) or become **Restrained** as the weapon holds them mid-motion, pinning them in space.
- **Flying creatures** that fail fall.
- **Huge or larger creatures** have advantage on the save.
- Once you use this feature, you cannot do so again until after a **short or long rest**.

---

### **Evolution Hooks (for future sessions)**

- **Sorcerous Metamagic Tie-In:** Once she multiclasses, the yklwa could store 1–2 sorcery points, usable only for Metamagic (flavor: the weapon *blooms* with spectral power as she bends magic through it).
- **Battlefield Bloom:** Later upgrades could expand the Immovable Strike into an **area lockdown**, like a 10–15 ft. “phantom bloom” of spectral roots restraining multiple enemies.
- **Planar Bloom:** Eventually, the yklwa could tether into Fey or Shadow planes — freezing enemies halfway “between places” instead of just pinning them in space.
