## How to Use These Tables

**1. Random Roll:** d20 on appropriate district/time table

**2. Directed Choice:** Pick encounter that fits current situation

**3. Foreshadowing:** Use peaceful encounters to hint at violent ones coming

**4. Escalation:** Start peaceful → tense → violent if party doesn't de-escalate

**5. Frequency:** 1 encounter per 30 minutes of travel, more if loitering

**6. Consequences:** Encounters should have lasting effects:

- Enemies made
- Allies gained
- Information learned
- Family reputation affected

**7. Layer with Rumors:** Combine encounters with Rumor Mill for maximum immersion
