# Bread and Beer

---

### **Bakery**

Upon entering, the party finds themselves in a large, welcoming space filled with the scent of freshly baked bread and the lingering warmth of ovens recently extinguished. The room is meticulously organized with rows of tables on the left side, each covered with bowls of flour, rolling pins, and trays of dough left in various stages of preparation.

- **Tables and Bread Shelves:** To the immediate left of the door, shelves are filled with loaves of dark, crusty dwarven bread. The tables further down are cluttered with rolling pins, mixing bowls, and baking tools, giving the impression that whoever was here left in a hurry.
- **Ovens on the Right Wall:** Along the right side are several stone ovens, still warm to the touch, their embers faintly glowing. There’s an assortment of bread molds, proving baskets, and stoneware trays stacked neatly nearby.
- **Baked Goods:** Some loaves appear half-baked, with a few fully prepared breads sitting atop the tables. A small scattering of flour and grains trails along the floor, leading toward the large keg at the back, hinting at regular movement.

*Atmosphere:* The room has a comforting, homey feel, but the emptiness gives it an eerie edge. The contrast between the comforting smell of bread and the silence suggests that something interrupted this place abruptly.

*Perception/Investigation Check (DC 12):* Players who examine the area will notice signs that the bakery was actively used until recently, as indicated by the warmth of the ovens and fresh dough on the tables.

### **Brewery**

Entering the adjacent room, the party is met with a stronger, heady smell of fermenting alcohol. The room is filled with rows of massive kegs, and the faint bubbling of ale in various stages of fermentation can be heard. The space is darker and more cavernous, lined with massive barrels on every wall and several smaller kegs scattered on the floor.

- **Fermentation Vats:** Along the back wall are large vats filled with bubbling ale in various stages of fermentation. The air is thick with a yeasty, sour smell, and the stone floor is damp from spilled liquids.
- **Tapped Kegs by the Door:** Near the entrance, three kegs are tapped and marked with color-coded symbols:
    - **Pink Barrel - Sweetstone Delight:** A weak, sweet ale with a light fruity aftertaste. A dwarven favorite for unwinding after a day of mining.
    - **Blue Barrel - Deep Ember Stout:** A dark, bitter ale with a high alcohol content, known for its strong hoppy flavor and roasty notes.
    - **Green Barrel - Old Forge Reserve:** An ale aged in oak barrels, bearing a smoky, whiskey-like finish.
- **The Gutbuster Keg (Secret Door):** At the far end of the brewery stands an enormous keg labeled **“Gutbuster.”** A successful **Perception (DC 15)** or **Investigation (DC 12)** check reveals that this keg sounds hollow. Upon lifting the keg and revealing the hidden latch beneath, a secret alcove with a concealed stone door is found.

*Atmosphere:* The brewery has a deep, grounded feel, with the smell of fermenting ale and wood filling the air. A faint chill permeates the room, and the silence contrasts with the room’s former life as a hub of dwarven merrymaking.

---

### **Fey Treasury Room**

Stepping through the hidden door, the party finds themselves in a space that feels entirely unlike the rest of the mine. Here, Morrighan’s connection to the Fey Realm pulses with unexpected strength, and she can feel the presence of her familiar as if they were nearby. Summoning magic flows freely here, as if the room itself welcomes such practices.

The room is filled with carefully cultivated nature magic, a stark contrast to the rest of the Iron Hallow Mine. The polished dark marble floors are laced with delicate veins of copper, and the ceiling glows faintly with a soft, ethereal light.

- **Central Path of Grass and Dirt:** Down the center of the room runs a strip of grass and dirt, thriving under the gentle glow. The grassy path feels enchanted, with a faint warmth and vibrancy that hints at Fey magic.
- **Fey Trees:** Lining the path are several small, silver-leaved trees, their bark shimmering as though imbued with stardust. Their branches sway gently, as if reacting to the presence of the party, particularly Morrighan, who feels a sense of welcome and recognition here.
- **Open Chests and Empty Sacks:** Scattered along the sides of the room are open chests and bags that have been emptied of their contents. Jewelry boxes and coin sacks lie open, with a few faint traces of precious stones and metals indicating their previous use. The only chest that remains closed is a massive, ornate one in the far corner.
- **The Ornate Chest (Trapped):** This grand chest, decorated with intricate carvings, appears sealed. Sparks of electricity crackle across its lock and hinges, indicating that a powerful trap is in place. The trap, once activated, can only be disabled with a **Dexterity (Thieves’ Tools) Check (DC 14)** or **Arcana Check (DC 15)** to carefully dispel the remaining magic.

---

### **Disarming the Trapped Chest**

To disarm the chest, players can attempt a **Dexterity (Thieves’ Tools) Check (DC 14)** to disconnect the trap’s components or an **Arcana Check (DC 15)** to dispel the remaining magical charge. Once disarmed, the chest opens to reveal the treasures within.

### **D8 Loot Table – The Fey Treasury Chest**

1. **Glade’s Whisper Ring**
    - A silver ring imbued with Fey magic. Grants the wearer advantage on Nature checks while in forested or magical areas.
2. **Potion of Fey Sight**
    - A shimmering green potion that, when consumed, grants the drinker **Darkvision** up to 60 feet for 1 hour.
3. **Elven Sash of Lightness**
    - A gossamer belt that grants the wearer a featherlight step. The wearer can ignore difficult terrain in forested or rocky areas once per day.
4. **Scroll of Sylvan Song**
    - This scroll allows the user to cast **Speak with Animals** once, providing a brief connection to natural creatures.
5. **Evershade Leaf Amulet**
    - An amulet that grants the wearer resistance to necrotic damage for 1 minute, usable once per day.
6. **Potion of Vigor**
    - A robust, earthy-tasting brew that grants the drinker 5 temporary hit points and resistance to poison damage for 1 hour.
7. **Minor Fey Wand**
    - A slender wand crafted from Fey tree wood, allowing the user to cast **Druidcraft** at will.
8. **Glimmerdust Satchel**
    - A small leather pouch filled with magical dust. When thrown, it creates a blinding effect, forcing creatures within 5 feet to make a DC 12 Constitution save or be blinded until the end of their next turn.
