# Drow Hand Signal Lesson

Here’s a streamlined **training mechanic** for teaching **Drow Hand-Sign (Silent Tongue)** from Iblith to the rest of the party, broken into learnable *“words”* or *“phrases,”* each with an associated training time and roll.

---

## 🖐️ **Drow Hand-Sign Instruction System**

### **Teaching Model**

Each “word” is a **simple gesture cluster** representing:

- A concept (“Danger” / “Ally”)
- A direction (“North” / “Split”)
- A tactic (“Flank” / “Withdraw”)
- A warning (“Magic Ahead” / “Watch Sky”)

Training time depends on party member aptitude, with Iblith acting as the sole fluent teacher.

---

### 🧠 Notes:

- **Advantage**: If Iblith spends additional downtime with one PC, grant advantage on the check.
- **Failure**: PC can retry the same word after a long rest.
- **Cap**: Limit to 3–5 words per downtime session (e.g. evening camp).

## **Drow Hand-Sign (Silent Tongue) Learning System**

### **Words Already Mastered:**

- **Foe** *(Enemy/Hostile)*
- **Danger** *(Immediate threat)*
- Magic (Warning)

### **Expanded Learning Progression**

### **Tier 1: Survival Basics (DC 12)**

*Essential for staying alive in the Underdark*

- **Stop** *(Halt/freeze)*
- **Quiet** *(Reduce noise)*
- **Safe** *(All clear)*
- **Retreat** *(Fall back)*
- **Down** *(Get low/hide)*

### **Tier 2: Movement & Direction (DC 13)**

*Tactical positioning*

- **Forward** *(Advance)*
- **Split** *(Divide group)*
- **Circle** *(Surround/flank)*
- **High** *(Elevated position)*
- **Water** *(Liquid/stream ahead)*

### **Tier 3: Threats & Warnings (DC 14)**

*Specific Underdark dangers*

- **Magic** *(Arcane presence)*
- **Eyes** *(Being watched)*
- **Poison** *(Toxic hazard)*
- **Many** *(Large numbers)*
- **Big** *(Large creature/threat)*

### **Tier 4: Complex Tactics (DC 15)**

*Advanced coordination*

- **Wait** *(Hold position)*
- **Now** *(Execute/go)*
- **Help** *(Assist/support)*
- **Follow** *(Trail behind)*
- **Guard** *(Protect/watch)*

### **Learning Mechanics:**

- **Training Time**: 30 minutes per word during camp
- **Check**: INT or WIS (player's choice) vs DC
- **Teacher**: Only Iblith can teach (he's the only one fluent)
- **Retry**: Failed words can be re-attempted after long rest
- **Practice**: Once learned, party members need to use signs in actual situations to retain fluency

### **Special Combinations:**

Once players know multiple words, they can combine them:

- **"Danger + Magic"** = *Magical threat*
- **"Many + Foe"** = *Large enemy force*
- **"Stop + Eyes"** = *Freeze, we're being watched*

### **Fluency Tracker:**

- **0-5 words**: Basic survival communication
- **6-10 words**: Tactical coordination possible
- **11-15 words**: Advanced silent operations
- **16+ words**: Near-native fluency
