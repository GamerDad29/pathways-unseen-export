### **Revised Scene: Lyra and the Bregan D’aerthe Connection**

As Iblith wraps up his dealings in UTTP, **Lyra Shadowheart** intercepts him. Her calm and enigmatic demeanor betrays no emotion, but her golden eyes carry a glimmer of mischief, hinting at knowledge she has yet to share.

**Lyra’s Dialogue**:

- *“Iblith, before you go stirring up more trouble in the Old Market, a… friend of yours wishes to speak. You’ll find them waiting at the tea house just beyond the southern arch. Best not to keep them waiting. You know how Jarlaxle feels about patience.”*

Lyra’s cryptic words direct Iblith to the meeting with Drelvyn Faerzhyn, where the exchange takes place.

### **Drelvyn’s Hand-Off Scene**

---

After arriving at the tea house, Iblith finds **Drelvyn Faerzhyn** seated in a quiet corner, his usual smug smile greeting him. The severed hand is exchanged with little ceremony, as Drelvyn examines it with a mix of fascination and morbid delight.

**Drelvyn’s Dialogue**:

- *“Ah, exquisite work, my dear Iblith. Jarlaxle will be pleased, as always. Of course, you’ll find he prefers to reward his favorites with something… lasting.”*
- He slides the chosen reward across the table, offering a final word of advice:
    - *“Consider this a reminder of the Captain’s appreciation. But don’t get too comfortable. You know how Jarlaxle feels about complacency.”*

With that, Drelvyn departs, leaving Iblith to contemplate his next move—unaware that **Kala the Whispir** watches from the shadows, ready to make her play.

### **Kala the Whispir’s Introduction**

**Who She Is**:

Kala the Whispir is Iblith’s current mark, a slippery and resourceful thief operating in UTTP. She has sharp instincts, a knack for vanishing when things go south, and a habit of toying with her hunters. Her nickname, "Whispir," comes from her ability to slip through the cracks of even the tightest nets.

- **Appearance**: A wiry woman of indeterminate age, Kala blends into her surroundings effortlessly. Her patched, dark clothing and quiet demeanor allow her to pass as just another street rat in the Old Market. Her sharp eyes miss nothing, and her hands are always poised for mischief.

**Motivations**:

- Kala is aware that Iblith is after her and has been tracking him since he entered UTTP. She isn’t looking for a fight—she prefers outsmarting her enemies. After spotting him meeting with Drelvyn, Kala decides to steal Iblith’s payment, buying her time and humiliating him in the process.

### **Kala’s Attempt to Pickpocket Iblith**

**Scene**:

As Iblith leaves the tea house with his bounty payment secured, Kala watches from the shadows, her sharp eyes locked on the velvet pouch at his belt. She moves like a shadow, weaving through the bustling crowd, waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

---

**Kala’s Tactics**:

1. **Approach**:
    - Kala takes advantage of a busy market street to blend in. She uses passing crowds, street vendors, and distractions (a juggler dropping flaming torches) to mask her movements.
2. **Diversion**:
    - Kala may intentionally bump into someone else near Iblith to create chaos. A spilled basket of mushrooms or a shouting merchant provides cover for her to get close.
3. **The Pickpocket Attempt**:
    - Kala reaches for the pouch of gold, her movements deft and precise. She’s not aiming for a fight—if caught, she’ll flee immediately.

---

### **If Iblith Notices (Perception Success)**:

1. **Confrontation**:
    - Kala immediately tries to talk her way out of trouble.
        - *“Ah, so sharp-eyed! It was just a test, friend, a test. Wanted to see if you’re as good as they say. No hard feelings, eh?”*
    - If pressed, she’ll attempt to bolt, using the crowded street as cover.
2. **Combat (Optional)**:
    - Kala is more likely to flee than fight, but if cornered, she’ll lash out with a dagger, aiming for non-lethal strikes. She’ll also throw a small bag of **blinding powder** to make her escape easier.
3. **Captured**:
    - If Iblith captures Kala, she’s surprisingly calm. She smirks and shrugs, saying:
        - *“Well, I suppose I’ll be adding my hand to your collection, won’t I? Just make it quick, will you?”*
    - Kala might offer to work for Iblith instead, proposing her skills as an infiltrator or spy in exchange for her life.

---

### **If Kala Escapes**:

- She disappears into the Old Market, leaving Iblith with only frustration and the realization that she’s just as clever as her reputation suggests.
- Later, she could leave a mocking message for Iblith, written on a scrap of paper and pinned to a wall:
    - *“Catch me if you can, spider’s pet.”*

### **Clues and Progression**

1. **If Captured**:
    - Kala reveals limited intel on the Syndicate, claiming she has no allegiance to them but knows they’ve been expanding their reach in the Old Market.
        - *“The Syndicate doesn’t like me, either. You might say we have… mutual enemies. Maybe we can help each other?”*
2. **If She Escapes**:
    - Kala remains a recurring figure, popping up at inconvenient times to taunt Iblith or sabotage his efforts. She could later become an ally if the party sees value in her skills.
