### **Warforged Volatile Core (Exploding Heart)**

*Exotic crafting component, unstable*

What remains of the Warforged’s chest is a cracked adamantine housing wrapped around a glowing arcane reactor. The core no longer beats. It **ticks**. Heat bleeds through the seams in uneven pulses, and every few seconds a low harmonic hum vibrates through metal nearby, like a forge remembering how to burn.

The core is inert for now. It will not explode on its own. But it was designed to fail catastrophically, and it remembers that purpose.

---

### **Raw Properties**

- Weight: 8 lb

• Value: 500–750 gp to the right buyer

• Highly illegal in most cities

• Cannot be safely used without specialized tools or training

If subjected to fire, lightning, or a critical hit while unsecured, the core becomes **Unstable**.

---

### **Unstable Reaction**

When the core becomes unstable, roll a d6 at the end of the round.

On a 1, it detonates.

Detonation:

• Radius: 10 ft

• Damage: **3d10 thunder**

• Dexterity save DC 14 for half damage

Once stabilized, this check is no longer required.

---

### **Artificer Integration Paths (Future Use Hooks)**

An artificer who studies the core for at least 8 hours and succeeds on an **Intelligence (Arcana or Tinker’s Tools) check DC 14** learns one viable application. Further study may unlock more.

Possible eventual uses include:

• Powering a heavy construct, siege device, or automaton

• Converting it into a limited-use breaching charge

• Using it as a fuel source for experimental weapons

• Stabilizing it into a permanent power core (rare crafting path)

Each use requires **stabilization**, rare materials, and risk. Failure never means “nothing happens.”

---

### **Narrative Truth**

This core is proof that the Warforged were not built to live forever.

They were built to **end violently**, on someone else’s terms.

Anyone who recognizes what this is will react with fear, greed, or both.

## The Kender’s Sense of the Plague

- Unique gift — cannot be replicated easily.
- Smells like rot mixed with burnt fungus and sour metal.
- Only the Kender can sense it innately; others may attempt experiments (Arcana/Medicine/Nature DC 16) but risk exposure.
- If a PC fails by 5+, they risk a *Minor Plague Affliction* (headache, hallucinations, faint whispers).

## Bpop’s Infected Crystal

- Medium-sized crystal (fist-sized).
- T-minus 30 days until next infection stage.
- Every 7 days the crystal grows darker and pulses faintly.
- At 30 days → progression: **Spreads taint to nearby organic matter** (plants, food, water, wounds).
- Kender can track the crystal’s corruption via scent; others may notice subtle magical hums or whispers.
