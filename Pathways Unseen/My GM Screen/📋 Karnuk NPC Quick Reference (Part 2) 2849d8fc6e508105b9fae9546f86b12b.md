## Wand-Thief Quest NPCs (Continued)

### Silra Inkvein (Ghoststep holder)

**Level 8 Arcane Trickster Rogue**

**AC:** 18 (studded leather + DEX)

**HP:** 65 (8d8 + 16)

**Speed:** 30 ft.

**Initiative:** +4

**Stats:** STR 10 (+0), DEX 18 (+4), CON 14 (+2), INT 16 (+3), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 14 (+2)

**Saves:** DEX +7, INT +6

**Skills:** Acrobatics +7, Deception +5, Investigation +6, Perception +4, Sleight of Hand +10, Stealth +10

**Signature Weapon:** Poisoned Dagger (+7 to hit, 1d4+4 piercing + poison) + **Ghoststep wand** (stolen)

**Key Abilities:**

- **Sneak Attack** - 4d6
- **Cunning Action**
- **Evasion**
- **Uncanny Dodge**
- **Ghoststep Wand** (stolen, 7 charges) - *Misty Step* (1 charge), *Invisibility* (2 charges), *Etherealness* (3 charges, 1 minute)
- **Spellcasting** (DC 14, +6 to hit)
    - Cantrips: *Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Prestidigitation*
    - 1st (4 slots): *Disguise Self, Silent Image*
    - 2nd (3 slots): *Invisibility, Shadow Blade*

**Combat Style:**

- Uses Ghoststep wand liberally (knows its value)
- Hit-and-run with *Invisibility*
- *Etherealness* to escape through walls
- Sneak Attack from hiding

**Personality:** Cunning • Greedy • Paranoid

**Voice:** Scarlett Johansson (Lucy) - cold calculation

**Wants:** Keep wand, fence other stolen goods, disappear from Karnuk

**Escape Plan:** *Etherealness* through walls, already has exit route planned

**Tactics:**

- Pre-combat: *Invisibility* from wand
- Round 1: Sneak Attack from stealth
- Uses wand's *Misty Step* every turn to reposition
- Cunning Action to Hide
- *Etherealness* at 30 HP, phases through wall, gone

**Wand Location:** Always on her person (hidden in boot, Perception DC 18)

**Challenge:** She KNOWS the wand is valuable. Won't give it up easily.
