# Thurl Convo

![Thurl.png](Thurl%20Convo/Thurl.png)

---

### **Thurl Ironmantle’s Dialogue Outline:**

**Opening Statement:**

- **Thurl:** "Ah, Borin Stonebeard, a fine dwarf if there ever was one. Came through here on his regular journeys—always trading ore, recouping supplies, and gathering any scrap of information he could find about the routes. Never saw a hint of weariness in him; in fact, the lad seemed more eager with every visit, as if he was on the brink of something big."

**Player Questions and Thurl’s Responses: - a 16 or above gets them the key to Borins Stash**

1. **Player asks about Borin’s work or mission:**
    - **Thurl:** “Borin was focused, driven even. His mission wasn’t just about digging deeper; he was mapping new routes to improve trade, to open new channels between the Underdark and the surface. Every time he came back, he spoke of finding new veins, new paths. Seemed convinced it was his calling.”
2. **Player inquires about unusual activity or people around Borin:**
    - **Thurl:** “Oddly enough, there were a few unusual types sniffing around, asking about him. Mostly merchants, some surface folk too. Seemed interested in where he was headed next, but Borin wasn’t the type to gossip. He kept things close to his chest.”
3. **Player questions any rumors about Borin’s death or disappearance:**
    - **Thurl:** “There are whispers, of course. Some say he was ambushed by creatures of the Underdark, others reckon he stumbled into something he shouldn’t have. But I can’t see why anyone would harm a dwarf just trying to find a way through these cursed tunnels. He wasn’t after treasure, just a better way to get from here to there.”
4. **Player probes for details on the route or Borin’s plans:**
    - **Thurl:** “He had big ideas, grand ones. Talked about creating safer routes, avoiding the more dangerous paths, maybe even finding a direct route to the surface that wouldn’t take days. I think he wanted to make life a little less miserable for those traveling these parts.”
5. **Player investigates further about the surface involvement:**
    - **Thurl:** “Surface folk? Can’t imagine they’d care much about a dwarf digging tunnels. But, now that you mention it, there was a lot of interest in Borin’s work from outsiders. Didn’t make much sense to me at the time. Maybe someone knew more than he did…”

**Closing Statement:**

- **Thurl:** “I don’t know why someone would want Borin dead, but if you find out, I’d like to know. He was a good dwarf, and whatever he was onto, he didn’t deserve to vanish like that.”

---

This dialogue provides a foundation for the players to explore further while maintaining the mystery surrounding Borin's death and potential surface involvement. It ties into his regular activities and his dedication to creating better routes.
