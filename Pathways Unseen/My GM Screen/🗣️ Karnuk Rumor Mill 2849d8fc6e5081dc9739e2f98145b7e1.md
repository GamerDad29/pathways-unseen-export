## How to Use This

**At Taverns/Shops:** Roll or pick 2-3 rumors, share naturally through NPC conversations

**Party Asks Around:** Let them roll Investigation/Persuasion, give 1-3 rumors per success

**Overheard:** When party's just walking around, drop 1 rumor as color

**Targeted Intel:** If party's investigating specific family, give relevant rumors from that section

**Verification:** Rumors can be verified through Investigation checks or asking multiple NPCs (corroboration)
