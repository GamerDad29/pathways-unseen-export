# Session 3 Prep

---

### **Session 3 Preparation Adjustments:**

1. **Introduce "The Chain" in Conversation:**
    - **Rizalia** or **Thurl** can mention "The Chain" in passing, perhaps when discussing recent disturbances or power shifts in the Underdark.
    - Example dialogue: "Word is, 'The Chain' has been restless. The usual conflicts are flaring up again, and that always means trouble for us here."
    - Keep it vague and ominous, hinting at a larger, looming threat without giving away too much.
2. **Hint at the Sighting of a Kender:**
    - While at the Shadowed Flask or speaking with another NPC like **Sarnathel Dustwren**, someone might whisper about a peculiar sighting.
    - Subtle mention: “There’s been talk… a little figure, nimble as a shadow, seen poking around the outskirts. Not a child, they say… something stranger, not sure...”
    - Ensure it remains a mere rumor, leaving the players curious but without concrete information.
3. **Use Rumors and Suspense:**
    - Integrate these hints in casual conversation or through side remarks. Players should feel that these are minor, passing details within the larger narrative, making them wonder whether to investigate further or let it pass.

By adding these elements, you'll build intrigue around "The Chain" and introduce the Kender subtly, enhancing the mystery and depth of your campaign.
