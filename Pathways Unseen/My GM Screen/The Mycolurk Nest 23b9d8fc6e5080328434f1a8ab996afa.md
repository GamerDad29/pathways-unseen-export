## 🌫️ **Scene Description – The Mycolurk Nest**

> The path narrows as the stone grows slick beneath your boots—no longer raw earth, but something spongier, veined with threads of luminous white. Mushrooms of impossible shapes coil upward from the walls, their caps glistening wet, pulsing faintly as if breathing.
> 
> 
> The air is close here. Warm. Damp. Each breath tastes of mold and memory.
> 
> Around a bend, the tunnel expands just slightly—just enough for **a web-strewn hollow** to yawn open, its ceiling lost in shadow. Sticky strands run from stalactite to wall to floor, many snapped, many slick with moss. Bones glimmer faintly beneath them.
> 
> **Then you feel it.**
> 
> Not sound. Not sight. But a vibration—**a tremble across the ground**, so slight it might’ve been imagined.
> 
> The mushrooms to your left **tilt unnaturally**, and from somewhere above, there’s the *crackling shift* of something descending on too many legs.
>

## 🎁 Loot Items

### 🕸️ **Wand of Webs**

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

A twisted length of pale chitin and silver thread, humming faintly with dormant magic.

- Has **3 charges**, regains 1d3 daily at dawn.
- **Cast *Web*** (DC 13) using 1 charge.
- If cast inside fungal terrain, web range expands by 5 feet and becomes semi-translucent green.

---

### 🍄 **Spore-Encrusted Ring**

*Wondrous item, uncommon (does not require attunement)*

Forged from tarnished copper and bone, overgrown with dormant mycelium.

- Grants **advantage on saving throws against poison**.
- Each long rest, **roll 1d4**:
    - On a **4**, a harmless mushroom sprouts on the user’s back or shoulder and remains for 24 hours. Glows faintly. May attract attention from spore-sensitive creatures.

## 🌟 Frank’s Quest — “A Filament Most Fabulous”

Source: [Franks Quest, Notion]

- **Request:** Frankimus the Full-Staffed wants **Mycolurk silk**, the rarest filament in the Underdark. He describes it as glistening *“like moonlight on oiled desire”* and faintly scented of *“unspoken promises.”*
- **Reason:**
    - In-world, Mycolurk silk is *incredibly strong*, temperature-reactive, and conducts enchantment better than silver.
    - It’s critical for Frank’s **glamour-threaded illusionwear** experiments.
    - It’s the only filament that can withstand the enchantments of his “combat-ready couture.”
- **Reward Options (player’s choice):**
    1. **Custom Enchanted Item** – tailored to the PC’s personality (mood-reactive cloak, illusion-gloves, etc.).
    2. **Access to Frank’s Private Chest** – rare, weirder items (d6 table available).
    3. **Favor Token of Frankimus** – a sealed kiss on a card, redeemable once to:
        - Teleport to Frank’s Phylactery (even mid-combat),
        - Force a Charisma reroll,
        - Cause one enemy to be disarmed by fabulousness (disadvantage on next turn).

## 🕷️ The Mycolurk Nest

Source: [The Mycolurk Nest, Notion]

- **Scene:** A spongy, fungal tunnel veined with luminous threads. The chamber opens into a **web-strewn hollow** with snapped strands and faintly glimmering bones. Vibrations and tilting mushrooms herald the Mycolurks’ approach.
- **Creatures:** Aberration/monstrosity hybrids (CR 2–4).
    - Produce **Mycolurk silk** (iridescent, enchantment-conductive).
    - Spore camouflage clouds (disadvantage on ranged/Insight checks).
    - Venom can cause hallucinations or mild paralysis.
    - Hive-like pack instincts.
- **Loot Already Placed Here:**
    - **Wand of Webs** (3 charges, Web spell, fungal terrain enhancement).
    - **Spore-Encrusted Ring** (advantage vs poison; quirky mushroom growth effect).

## 🔗 How This Ties Into Your Current Loot/Harvest Work

- The **Queen Spider’s silk** can be re-flavored as **Mycolurk silk** — the very quest item Frank is seeking.
- This lets the party’s **harvest checks** double as progress toward Frank’s quest. For example:
    - On a **success (DC 15+):** PCs recover spools of **usable Mycolurk silk**, which Frank will reward.
    - On a **failure:** the silk tears, becomes tainted, or releases spores (forcing Con saves).
- The **treasure items** (Wand of Webs, Spore-Encrusted Ring) found in the nest **directly reinforce Frank’s theme** and give an extra reason for the players to engage with the fungal terrain.

- The spider’s **Silken Spools** are explicitly **Mycolurk Silk**.
- Bringing those back completes Frank’s flamboyant side quest and unlocks his *real* stock (or one of the other reward paths).
