### **Phase 1: The Weapon’s Foundation**

**Narrative Setup**:

The forge blazes like a fragment of the sun, casting long, flickering shadows on the stone walls of Grovan’s workshop. The Immovable Rod, now glowing molten orange, hovers above the anvil, suspended by threads of Morrighan’s magic. With each rhythmic strike of Grovan’s hammer, the rod bends and twists, shedding sparks that spiral like tiny fireflies. Morrighan stands at the heart of it all, her hands glowing faintly as spectral vines coil and uncoil around her arms, feeding raw power into the creation.

Grovan shouts, his voice booming over the forge:

*“The soul of the weapon is set here, lass. Don’t hold back now, or you’ll regret it for all eternity!”*
