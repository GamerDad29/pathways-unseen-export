# Forge Dwarves

![image.png](Forge%20Dwarves/image.png)

The **Forge Dwarves** are a fabled and reclusive lineage of dwarves, known to most only in stories and whispered legends. Long ago, when the conflicts of the Underdark grew fierce and bloody, the Forge Dwarves took a different path than their kin. While other dwarven clans prepared for battle, the Forge Dwarves turned inward, abandoning the skirmishes and forging a legacy of craft rather than conquest. Their sole commitment for centuries was to arm and equip other dwarves, providing tools and weaponry of unmatched quality and resilience. They became known as the silent architects of dwarven strength, supporting dwarvenkind without taking sides in their struggles.

The true location of the Forge Dwarves’ homeland remains shrouded in mystery. Some say their hidden forges lie deep within the molten caverns at the heart of the Underdark, where even magma is humbled by their command. Others believe they have secluded themselves in an ancient fortress, protected by unbreakable metal doors and guarded by constructs of their own making. What all tales agree upon is that their craft is unrivaled, a symphony of fire, metal, and magic that yields the finest armor, weapons, and tools ever known in the Underdark.

Over generations, the constant exposure to intense heat, fumes, and the enchantments of the forge has altered their very nature. Forge Dwarves are unmistakable in appearance: their skin, as dark and gleaming as polished onyx, seems almost metallic. Their eyes burn with an inner fire, a bright, molten hue that hints at the power of the forges they serve. They are no longer mere craftsmen—they are embodiments of the forge itself, born and molded to create. Their bodies, honed and tempered, have adapted to the furnace, and they move with the calm, measured strength of those who live for purpose alone.

Recently, rumors have stirred in the Underdark that the Forge Dwarves are expanding their reach. Once exclusive to dwarven needs, their arms and armor have begun appearing in the hands of other denizens of the Underdark—mercenaries, traders, and even the occasional surface-dweller. The intricate insignia of the Forge Dwarves, once a mark of dwarven allegiance, is now becoming a sought-after symbol of power and status. Those bearing their creations are often met with wary respect, for a weapon from the Forge Dwarves is a tool of legends. Their name has become a byword for quality, danger, and mystery.

What drove the Forge Dwarves to extend their influence beyond the dwarven realm remains unknown. Some speculate they are compelled by new forces or debts owed to unknown patrons, while others fear they may be preparing for an impending conflict. But as their creations spread through the Underdark, so too does a question among its denizens: are the Forge Dwarves mere smiths, or is there a deeper purpose, a greater fire, fueling their works?
