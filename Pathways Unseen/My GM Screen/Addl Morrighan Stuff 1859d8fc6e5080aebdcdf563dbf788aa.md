### **Glimershade Bark**

**Description:**

Glimershade Bark is harvested from the ancient **Duskmere Trees** of the Feywild, known for their twilight glow and magical properties. The bark is iridescent, shifting colors like a fading sunset, and hums faintly when touched. It is prized for its ability to absorb and amplify magical energy, making it invaluable to enchanters, armorers, and alchemists.

### **Five Options for Using Glimershade Bark**

1. **Reinforced Arcane Armor**
    - **Effect:** The bark can be used to create lightweight yet highly durable armor, granting the wearer resistance to **force damage** and a +1 bonus to AC.
    - **Special Feature:** Once per short rest, the armor can emit a blinding burst of twilight energy, forcing all enemies within 10 feet to make a DC 14 Constitution save or be blinded for 1 round.
    - **Narrative Flavor:** The bark’s faint shimmer becomes a radiant glow when activated, wrapping the wearer in protective twilight.
2. **Shadowwoven Cloak**
    - **Effect:** Glimershade Bark can be woven into a cloak that grants **advantage on Stealth checks** in dim light or darkness.
    - **Special Feature:** As a bonus action, the wearer can vanish into shadow for 1 round, becoming invisible until the start of their next turn. Usable once per long rest.
    - **Narrative Flavor:** The cloak’s surface ripples like water under moonlight, blending seamlessly into surrounding shadows.
3. **Warden’s Shield**
    - **Effect:** The bark reinforces a shield, allowing it to **absorb magical energy**. The shield gains 3 charges, which can be used to negate spell damage (1 charge per spell level). The charges replenish at dawn.
    - **Special Feature:** When fully charged, the shield emits a faint aura that grants allies within 10 feet advantage on saving throws against spells.
    - **Narrative Flavor:** The shield’s surface displays swirling colors of the Feywild, calming those who stand near it.
4. **Arcane Infusion for Weapons**
    - **Effect:** A weapon infused with Glimershade Bark deals an additional **1d6 radiant damage** in dim light or darkness.
    - **Special Feature:** Once per long rest, the weapon can release a radiant pulse, dealing **2d6 radiant damage** to all enemies within 15 feet.
    - **Narrative Flavor:** The weapon hums faintly, and its edges glow like the horizon at dusk whenever it strikes.
5. **Feybound Amulet**
    - **Effect:** Crafted into an amulet, the bark grants the wearer the ability to cast *Faerie Fire* (DC 13) once per long rest and advantage on saving throws against being charmed.
    - **Special Feature:** When worn by a fey-touched creature, the amulet allows the wearer to communicate telepathically with any fey creature within 30 feet.
    - **Narrative Flavor:** The amulet’s surface shifts in color, reflecting the emotions of the wearer and faintly whispering fey words.

### **The Crafting in Karnuk**

The ancient city of **Karnuk**, renowned for its skilled artisans and deep ties to the arcane, is the ideal place to forge a weapon infused with **Glimershade Bark**. The city’s **Verdant Forge**, located in a hollowed-out crystalline tree in the city’s heart, is said to be the only forge in the realm capable of melding Feywild materials with mortal craftsmanship.

**Crafting Process:**

Morrighan must work with **Feysmith Valtheris Nightbloom**, a half-elf artisan whose ancestry ties to both the Feywild and Karnuk’s traditions. The crafting process is not merely physical but also spiritual, requiring Morrighan to pour a fragment of her willpower and connection to the Feywild into the creation.

**Valtheris’s Role:**

- Valtheris serves as both a guide and smith, aiding Morrighan in channeling the magic of the Glimershade Bark.
- *Dialogue:* *"The Verdant Forge does not merely shape metal and bark—it shapes destiny. This weapon will be more than a tool; it will be a piece of you, bound to the Feywild and your patron both. Tread carefully, for what you create may yet define you."*

**Potential Challenges:**

1. **Feywild Rituals:** Morrighan must perform a ritual at the **Twilight Altar** within the forge, harmonizing her will with the bark’s natural magic. Failure could lead to instability in the weapon.
2. **Patron Interference:** The undead patron might resist Morrighan’s attempt to balance Fey and necrotic energies, leading to moments of doubt or magical backlash.
3. **Unsealy Involvement:** Sylvara Duskpetal may arrive unexpectedly, offering “a final boon” in exchange for another favor, further complicating Morrighan’s allegiance.

**Final Result:**

The weapon will emerge as a masterpiece of **Feywild artistry**, infused with Morrighan’s essence and tailored to her unique blend of fey and undead influences. Let me know if you’d like specific rolls or further development of this crafting sequence!
