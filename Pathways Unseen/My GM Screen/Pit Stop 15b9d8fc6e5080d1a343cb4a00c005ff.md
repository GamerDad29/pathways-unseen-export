# Pit Stop

### **Surfie Strike Force Encounter**

The Surfie Strike Force consists of two warriors, one wizard, and two rangers. They are well-equipped and battle-hardened, making this a challenging encounter for the party. The group is organized and employs coordinated tactics to maximize their advantage in the harsh environment of the Underdark.

---

### **Surfie Warrior (Level 4)**

**Medium Humanoid (Human), Lawful Neutral**

**Armor Class:** 17 (Chainmail and Shield)

**Hit Points:** 45 (6d8 + 12)

**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR:** 16 (+3)

**DEX:** 12 (+1)

**CON:** 14 (+2)

**INT:** 10 (+0)

**WIS:** 12 (+1)

**CHA:** 11 (+0)

**Saving Throws:** STR +5, CON +4

**Skills:** Athletics +5, Perception +3

**Senses:** Passive Perception 13

**Languages:** Common

---

### **Actions**

**Multiattack.** The warrior makes two melee attacks with their longsword.

- **Longsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.*Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage, or 9 (1d10 + 3) if used with two hands.

---

### **Reactions**

**Shield Wall (Recharge 5-6):** If the warrior is adjacent to another ally with a shield, they can impose disadvantage on one ranged attack targeting either of them.

**3rd Level Spells (1 Slot):** *Fireball*

---

### **Tactics**

The wizard prioritizes crowd control and high-damage spells, staying behind the warriors for protection. They use *Misty Step* to reposition and *Fireball* to target clustered enemies.

### **Surfie Ranger (Level 4)**

**Medium Humanoid (Half-Elf), Neutral Good**

**Armor Class:** 14 (Leather Armor)

**Hit Points:** 40 (6d10 + 6)

**Speed:** 35 ft.

**STR:** 12 (+1)

**DEX:** 16 (+3)

**CON:** 13 (+1)

**INT:** 11 (+0)

**WIS:** 14 (+2)

**CHA:** 10 (+0)

**Saving Throws:** DEX +5, WIS +4

**Skills:** Stealth +5, Survival +4, Perception +4

**Senses:** Passive Perception 14

**Languages:** Common, Elvish

---

### **Actions**

**Multiattack.** The ranger makes two ranged attacks with their longbow or two melee attacks with their shortsword.

- **Longbow.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target.*Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.
- **Shortsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.*Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.

**Hunter’s Mark (1/Day).** Marks one target for bonus damage. Adds 1d6 to each attack against the marked creature.

---

### **Tactics**

The rangers use *Hunter’s Mark* to prioritize high-threat targets. They remain mobile, utilizing cover and terrain to avoid melee combat.

---

### Encounter Setup

- **Terrain:** A narrow cavern with natural stone pillars and loose rubble. The environment provides half and three-quarters cover for ranged attackers.
- **Tactics:**
    - **Warriors** create a defensive line to protect the wizard and rangers.
    - **Wizard** targets groups with AoE spells and uses *Misty Step* to evade danger.
    - **Rangers** flank the party, using the terrain for maximum advantage.

### Loot

- **Wizard’s Pouch:** Contains spell components and a **Scroll of Scorching Ray.**
- **Ranger Quiver:** Holds 10 **enchanted arrows (+1 to attack and damage rolls).**
- **Warrior’s Satchel:** Includes **a small polished shield amulet** (grants +1 AC when worn).
- **General Loot:** 15 gp, 40 sp, and an ornate map detailing surface access points to the Underdark.

## **Writ of Cleansing**

**By Decree of the Eternal Sovereign**

**The Chain**

**Keeper of the Purity of Toril**

---

**To All Loyal Subjects and Noble Houses of the Surface Lands:**

Let it be known that the filth and corruption of the Underdark must no longer fester beneath the soil of our sacred lands. By my command, I grant authority and recompense to any who take up the sacred duty of cleansing the world of these abominations.

For every intelligent creature of the Underdark slain, the bearer of this writ shall be entitled to claim the following bounties:

- **Drow (Dark Elf):** 40 gold pieces
- **Duergar (Dark Dwarf):** 30 gold pieces
- **Svirfneblin (Deep Gnome):** 20 gold pieces
- **Illithid (Mind Flayer):** 100 gold pieces
- **Kuo-Toa:** 15 gold pieces
- **Beholders (or their kin):** 200 gold pieces
- **Other Sentient Aberrations:** As deemed appropriate by the Magistrate.

The hunting and extermination of these creatures is deemed an act of divine righteousness, a sacred endeavor to purify our world from the shadows that threaten its light.

---

**Terms and Conditions:**

1. **Proof of Deed:** A head, intact and identifiable, must be presented to the nearest magistrate or appointed collector to receive payment.
2. **Sanctioned Targets Only:** Any unauthorized attacks on surface-dwelling creatures or peaceful emissaries of the Underdark will result in immediate forfeiture of this writ and punishment under the laws of the realm.
3. **Noncompliance:** Any harboring, aiding, or conspiring with the listed targets will be treated as high treason, punishable by death.

---

**Signed in Eternal Vigilance,**

**The Chain**

**Sovereign Ruler and Keeper of Order**

### **Surfie Strike Force: Interrogation Answers**

If the party captures and interrogates a member of the Surfie Strike Force, their responses will vary depending on the approach. Below are potential answers to **Intimidation** and **Persuasion** checks.

---

### **Intimidation Responses**

**DC 10-14:**

- *"You think you’re scary? I’ve faced worse in the Chain’s service. But fine, fine—I’ll tell you this much: there’s gold in killing your kind, and we’re not the only ones taking the bounty."*

**DC 15-19:**

- *"Alright, alright! We’re here under orders, alright? The writ’s from the Chain himself. Purge the Underdark, wipe out the filth, and get paid for each ugly head we bring back. That’s all I know!"*

**DC 20+:**

- *"Don’t kill me! Please! Look, I’ll spill it all. We were told to focus on the major routes—the Chain doesn’t want the Underdark feeding their rebellion. The Shadowed Parliament whispered to our leaders; they want chaos down here. The bounty? It’s just a tool to get the job done."*

---

### **Persuasion Responses**

**DC 10-14:**

- *"Look, I don’t want trouble, but orders are orders. You’d do the same if it were your home being invaded. We’re just doing what we’re told."*

**DC 15-19:**

- *"The Chain’s convinced the Underdark’s plotting against the surface. There are whispers of alliances—Drow, Duergar, and even worse things. We’re here to stop it before it spreads."*

**DC 20+:**

- *"Alright, I’ll talk. We’ve been told the Shadowed Parliament is pushing the Chain, feeding him half-truths and fear about the Underdark. They’re using him to incite war while they pull the strings. The Parliament’s the real enemy—not you, not me. But we’re pawns, and pawns don’t survive this game."*

---

### **Key Details They Might Reveal**

1. **The Writ’s True Purpose:** The bounties serve to mobilize common warriors under the guise of protecting the surface, while sowing fear and distrust between realms.
2. **Shadowed Parliament’s Role:** The Shadowed Parliament manipulates the Chain, exaggerating the threat from the Underdark to escalate conflict.
3. **Targets of Interest:** The Surfies are instructed to focus on trade routes and settlements—any place critical to the Underdark’s stability.
4. **Future Threats:** Other strike forces are moving deeper into the Underdark, targeting settlements like **UTTP** and Drow enclaves.

---

### **Final Plea (Optional for Both Approaches)**

- *"Let me go, and I’ll tell my commander we found nothing here. You’ve got bigger problems than us—you don’t stop the Parliament, and this war will consume everything, surface and Underdark alike."*

This could lead to a moral dilemma, forcing the party to decide whether to release their captive or eliminate them to prevent further harm.
