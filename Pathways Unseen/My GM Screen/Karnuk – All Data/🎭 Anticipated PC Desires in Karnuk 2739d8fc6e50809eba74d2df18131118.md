# 🎭 Anticipated PC Desires in Karnuk

---

---

### 🐀 **Iblith (Drow, Bregan D’aerthe)**

**What He’ll Ask / Do:**

- Seek direct contact with Bregan D’aerthe in the city.
- Commission or craft his custom hand crossbow.

**Prepared Options:**

- **Contact:** *Jevan Alderleaf* (Bregan’s captain in Karnuk) or *Veyran Veyth* (his handler from UTTP). Either can act as his fixer.
- **Crossbow Crafting:**
    - *Steel Syndicate Labs:* Experimental mechanisms, but risky — Syndicate may try to “own” him.
    - *Azrael (Forge Drow):* Can design something elegant, but will demand a secret or favor in return.
    - *Thorgar Ironhammer:* Old-school craftsman route — solid, reliable, traditional.
- **Flavor Hook:** Rumors of “Silent Mechanisms” — prototype repeating hand crossbows hidden in Syndicate vaults.

---

### 🐉 **Bpop (Artificer, Kobold Tinkerer)**

**What He’ll Ask / Do:**

- Gawk at mechs, tech, foundries, and anything new to tinker with.
- Likely want to dabble in forbidden or experimental designs.

**Prepared Options:**

- **Steel Syndicate (Kethar Steelheart):** Offers to let him inside mech forges — in exchange for loyalty.
- **The Iron Lord (Iron Mine):** Provides rare ores and alchemical reagents for “dangerous experiments.”
- **Dockside Options:** Access to smuggled surface parts (guns, clockwork).
- **Toys & Options:**
    - Clockwork pets (spy birds, ratlings).
    - Prototype grenades (alchemical, smoke, thunder).
    - Embersteel scraps (dangerous to handle, volatile when powered).
- **Flavor Hook:** He overhears whispers of a *“walking forge”* — a mech converted into a mobile workshop.

---

### ⚔️ **Cannonball (Barbarian, Bear Clan)**

**What He’ll Ask / Do:**

- Find taverns, places to drink, brag, tell Bear Clan tales.
- Wants martial recognition (weapons, gear, glory).
- Simple brain — likely misses subtleties, but goes straight for bravado.

**Prepared Options:**

- **Taverns:** The *Broken Anvil* (dockside guild bar), the *Ember Keg* (Iron Hammer haunt). Both rowdy, perfect for Bear Clan boasting.
- **Guild Recognition:** Grommash Stonefist (Deepstone Miners) offers him a “trial fight” in the pits — winner gets a custom weapon commission.
- **Unique Item Options:**
    - *Bear Clan Totem of Rage* (advantage on Intimidation while raging).
    - *Warstrap Mimic (Grumblegut)* evolving further here.
    - *Embersteel Mesh Wraps* could be upgraded to pull foes closer (martial magnetism).
- **Flavor Hook:** Karnuk whispers about an *“Arena of Ash”* — a gladiator pit where Bear Clan strength could win him fame or infamy.

---

### 🐦 **Morrighan (Fey Warlock → Sorcerer)**

**What She’ll Ask / Do:**

- Likely repulsed by machinery, industry, and smoke.
- Will search for any trace of magic, Fey presence, or arcane secrets buried under steel.

**Prepared Options:**

- **Brotherhood of the Forge (Azrael):** Sees her Fey spark as a tool for ornamental enchantments.
- **Hidden Pockets:** There are whispers of *ley-lines buried under Karnuk*, suppressed by machinery. Morrighan could sense them.
- **Magic vs. Machine Conflict:** She could uncover Fey-tainted ore (like corrupted embersteel), or a Fey prisoner being drained for power.
- **Item/Quest Hooks:**
    - *Blade of Sundering Light* (cutting through steel/machine with radiant flame).
    - *Pendant of Hollow Flame* upgrade — now able to detect nearby magical nodes in the industrial smog.
    - Quest from Azrael: recover a relic of the Fey used as a forge-focus.
- **Flavor Hook:** She sees the mechs not as marvels, but as **perversions** — echoes of how the industrial consumes the natural. This creates moral tension for her.

# 🧭 DM Flexibility Tip

You can toss the ball to the players with:

- “Who’s the first person you’d know in a place like this?”
- “Which family’s banners feel most familiar to you, and why?”

Then drop one of these prepped NPCs/options into their answer, letting them feel like co-authors.
