# 🏙️ Karnuk — The Iron Syndicate City

---

### **Atmosphere**

- Think *Chicago / New York in the 1940’s*: smoky taverns, guild banners like neon signs, enforcers in armored uniforms patrolling with brutal efficiency.
- The streets echo with the hammering of forges, but behind the clang of steel is the hush of whispers — deals made in alleys, coin exchanged in dark warehouses, rival guild members eyeing each other across the tavern floor.
- The **docks** are the beating heart — barges, ferries, and skiffs all laden with weapons, armor, raw ore, and illicit cargo. Dockworkers are just as likely to be informants or smugglers as they are laborers.

---

### **The Five “Families” (Guild Powers)**

Reflavored from your notes, they become mafia families:

1. **Iron Hammer Clan (The Old Guard)**
    - Dwarf-run, led by Thorgar Ironhammer.
    - Reputation: *Old money, traditional, honorable but stubborn.*
    - “The Family that built this town.” Respected but fading compared to the new blood.
2. **Deepstone Miners (The Muscle)**
    - Hobgoblins under Grommash Stonefist.
    - Reputation: *Roughnecks, enforcers, and union muscle.*
    - Run the docks and control labor. You don’t move a crate in Karnuk without their say-so.
3. **Brotherhood of the Forge (The Artists)**
    - Drow smiths led by Azrael.
    - Reputation: *Refined, aloof, brilliant artisans.*
    - Handle the high-end market, rare commissions, ornamentals. A bit like a family of jewel thieves.
4. **Brotherhood of the Iron Mine (The Deep Operators)**
    - Duergar led by the mysterious “Iron Lord.”
    - Reputation: *Secretive, cold, industrial-scale mining.*
    - Think “the family that owns the refineries.” No glamour, but they bankroll the city.
5. **Steel Syndicate (The New Blood)**
    - Bugbears under Kethar Steelheart.
    - Reputation: *Aggressive, ambitious, cutthroat.*
    - The rising power — modernizing, bringing in new tech, rumored ties to the mechs.

---

### **Political Structure**

- **Vorgoth the Ironclad (Viceroy):** The “Mayor / Boss of Bosses,” keeps the peace by balancing guild rivalries. But everyone knows if he didn’t have the mechs, he’d already be in a shallow grave.
- **Garruk Bloodaxe (Shadowed Parliament):** Secretly pulling strings like a rival mafia don with foreign backing.

# 👥 PC-Specific Contacts

Here’s what each character could hook into once inside Karnuk:

---

### ⚔️ **Cannonball (Barbarian)**

- **The Deepstone Miners (Muscle):** Cannonball’s Bear Clan reputation + brute force fits with dockworkers and hobgoblin bruisers. They’d see him as a natural enforcer or bodyguard-for-hire.
- **Thorgar Ironhammer (Iron Hammer Clan):** Respects warriors; could forge him custom Bear Clan-inspired armor/weapon.
- **Flavor Hook:** He’s seen as “muscle that doesn’t ask questions,” a role the guilds love.

---

### 🐉 **Bpop (Artificer)**

- **Steel Syndicate:** Perfect match — experimental tech, weaponization of embersteel, mech development.
- **Brotherhood of the Forge:** Could recognize his ingenuity and give him access to rare crafting secrets.
- **Flavor Hook:** Bpop might be shown the “underbelly” of mech construction — and offered the temptation to build one himself.

---

### 🐀 **Iblith (Monk / Bregan D’aerthe)**

- **Jevan Alderleaf (Bregan’s Man in Karnuk):** His *direct* Bregan D’aerthe contact — gives him leverage over guild politics.
- **Azrael (Brotherhood of the Forge):** Drow solidarity; could rope Iblith into sabotage or espionage against rival guilds.
- **Flavor Hook:** He’s the only one with a straight path into the underworld elite, the shadow-broker network.

---

### 🐦 **Morrighan (Warlock/Soon-to-be Sorcerer)**

- **Brotherhood of the Forge (The Artists):** Her Fey magic + ornamental smithing = natural partnership. Azrael may see her as a patron’s emissary.
- **Steel Syndicate (New Blood):** Her ember magic could tie her to experimental weaponry or magical advancements.
- **Flavor Hook:** She’s mysterious, Fey-marked — Karnuk’s guild leaders would see her as either a dangerous outsider or a weapon to be harnessed.
