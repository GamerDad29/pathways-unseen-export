# LOCATIONS

---

| **Tavern** | **Main NPC** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **The Coal Draught** | **Branna “Ashlung” Dorrim** | Dwarven matron with ruined lungs from decades in the forges. Coughs black, jokes louder. Knows every dockhand by name, calls PCs “sweetheart” and “idiot” in the same breath. |
| **Grinder’s Rest** | **Torvik “Knuckleblood” Harl** | Hobgoblin miner with scarred, bandaged fists. Runs the Rest like a pit boss — no credit, no excuses. Secretly pays guild dues for orphans of fallen miners. |
| **The Broken Cask** | **Della Raincup** | Wiry halfling who worships the leaking roof as “luck water.” Bounces through puddles mid-laugh, half prophet, half hustler. Dice seem to favor those who toast her blessings. |
| **Red Lantern Inn** | **Veyros “Red-Eye” Caldran** | Duergar barkeep with a glowing crimson-glass eye. Keeps strict neutrality; whispers cut sharper than blades. Anyone spilling blood vanishes into the alley — never seen again. |
| **The Rat’s Tail** | **Nix “Longtail” Verrin** | Ratfolk barkeep with fur patches and too-sharp teeth. His “staff” of rats bring him secrets. Rumors are steeply priced but eerily accurate — unless the rats lied first. |
| **Anvil & Ember** | **Korga Firebelch** | Bugbear blacksmith scarred by forge-flame, laugh like a warhorn. Serves drinks that blister throats, respects those who endure. Claims to have forged half the weapons in Karnuk. |

# 🗡️ Weaponsmiths of Karnuk

| **Weaponsmith** | **Main NPC** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Edge & Ember** | **Maela Emberborn** | Fiery-haired half-elf, forges glowing embersteel blades. Claims each one is “hungry” and chooses its wielder. |
| **The Split Hilt** | **Hargun Splithand** | Gruff dwarf with mismatched prosthetic fingers; crafts weapons from salvaged parts. Each blade bears scars like its maker. |
| **Rivet & Bolt** | **Tibbin Geargrin** | Eccentric gnome gunsmith, laughs too loud when something misfires. Swears, “If it doesn’t explode, it’s not invention!” |
| **Slagfang Smithy** | **Urka Slagfang** | Orc smith with jagged teeth and scarred arms. Specializes in cruel, serrated weapons. Rumor: she sings to her steel as it cools. |
| **Old Iron’s Stand** | **Old Iron** | Veteran dwarf missing both hands. Commands apprentices with a glare. His name is legend, his patience thin. |
| **The Blood Anvil** | **Serrith Veyne** | Pale drow who quenches every blade in blood. Her shop smells of copper and rust. She insists her weapons “wake thirsty.” |
