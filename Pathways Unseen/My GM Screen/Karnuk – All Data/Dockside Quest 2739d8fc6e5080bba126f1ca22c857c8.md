# Dockside Quest

---

# ⚓ Why the Party Goes to the Docks (Karnuk)

## 🗝️ Core Premise

The **docks are the beating heart of Karnuk….**guild turf wars, smuggling, and black-market drops. The party doesn’t need an outside summons; the job comes from **inside the city**, through one of the guild families, fixers, or underworld brokers.

---

## 🎯 Quest-Givers (Pick to Fit Party Flow)

### 1. **Jevan Alderleaf (Bregan D’aerthe Captain)**

- **Why He Sends Them:** A discreet pickup is scheduled at Dock 7 — a satchel left beneath Crane Three.
- **What He Tells Iblith:** “Easy work. A test. Go fetch what’s ours and don’t make noise.”
- **What’s Actually There:** The ambush. The satchel is bait, containing a fragment of Iron Order scrap and a scorched playing card marked “Veyran.”
- **Why It Matters:** Bregan keeps Iblith’s intro mission small, but it plugs directly into the Shadowed Parliament/Warforged arc.

---

### 2. **Dockside Lieutenant (Deepstone Miners)**

- **Why They Send Them:** Need muscle to move a “contraband crate” past Syndicate eyes. Cannonball is a natural hire.
- **What They Promise:** “Shift the box, take your cut, no questions.”
- **What’s Actually There:** The crate is empty — the bruisers turned it into a lure. When the PCs arrive, they’re “collecting their cut” in fists and cudgels.
- **Why It Matters:** Ties Cannonball’s Bear Clan flavor into Karnuk’s bruiser culture and sets up the ambush as personal betrayal.

---

### 3. **Azrael the Artisan (Brotherhood of the Forge)**

- **Why He Sends Them:** A page from his grimoire was stolen, last seen traded at the docks. He pays well for retrieval.
- **What They Expect:** A simple fetch for a scrap of parchment.
- **What’s Actually There:** The parchment is tucked in a pouch carried by the bruisers — they never intended to hand it over.
- **Why It Matters:** Morrighan’s Fey/arcane ties get spotlighted, and the Forge Brotherhood thread enters play early.

---

### 4. **Kethar Steelheart (Steel Syndicate Boss)**

- **Why He Sends Them:** Tests loyalty. Orders Bpop to “observe” Syndicate cargo coming in at Dock 7.
- **What He Says:** “Stand there. Watch. Report back.”
- **What’s Actually There:** A Syndicate rival’s bruisers are waiting; Bpop walks straight into a guild turf setup.
- **Why It Matters:** Makes Bpop’s mech/Steel Syndicate tie visceral and dangerous from the start.

---

## 🔑 What They’ll Always Find (Regardless of Hook)

- A **bounty notice** freshly posted: reward for the PCs, signed with Steel Syndicate / Kethar’s crest (or forged to look that way).
- A **physical clue** tied to the larger arc:
    - Mask shard (Iron Order)
    - Shadow-tainted vial (Parliament)
    - Scorched playing card marked “Veyran”
- **Dockside whispers:** Workers vanish, then reappear afterward muttering, “They’re marked. Whoever they are, they’ll bring more trouble to Karnuk.”

---

## 🧭 Why This Works

- **Internal Source:** Every version originates in Karnuk’s guild underbelly, not Ulfgar or UTTP.
- **Flexible NPC Hooks:** You can choose Bregan (Iblith), Deepstone (Cannonball), Forge (Morrighan), or Syndicate (Bpop).
- **Immediate Stakes:** They’re targeted the moment they bite — ambush + bounty + clue into the larger Warforged/Shadow Parliament threads.
