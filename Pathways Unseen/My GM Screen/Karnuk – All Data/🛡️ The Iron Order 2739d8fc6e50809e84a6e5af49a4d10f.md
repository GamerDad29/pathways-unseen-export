### **Player-Facing Description**

> The streets of Karnuk shudder into silence. From the smoke and shadow emerge four armored figures, boots striking in perfect rhythm, not a sound out of place. Each is different in frame — one a towering juggernaut, another lean and lithe, a third cloaked in shadow, a fourth haloed by faint arcane sparks. Yet all share the same mask: a smooth iron visage, identical and expressionless, runes glowing faintly where eyes should be.
> 
> 
> Conversation dies. Doors slam shut. No one dares look directly at them.
> 
> For centuries, the Iron Order has patrolled Karnuk — unbribable, unshakable, unyielding. Whispers say they are the **descendants of the city’s founding families**, sworn to bear the mask until death, their bloodlines bound to Karnuk itself. Others say the masks *are* their faces now.
> 
> When they pass, even the guild enforcers step aside. For the Iron Order serves no family, no guild, no coin. They serve only **the city**.
> 

---

### **Lore Notes (DM Side)**

- **Masks:** Identical for every member; faceless iron with faint rune glows. Player cannot tell races until they fight.
- **Ancestral Duty:** Bound bloodlines from Karnuk’s founders. The myth that they cannot be bribed is *true*.
- **Presence:** Streets clear when they appear. They are the **living law** of Karnuk.
- **Organization:** They patrol in units of four — called **Phalanxes**. Each Phalanx is a level-15 “adventuring party” with variant roles.

---

### **What They’re Called**

Each 4-person squad is known as a **Phalanx** of the Iron Order.

- On the street: “A Phalanx is coming.”
- In whispers: “Four Masks walk tonight.”

---

### **Example Phalanx Markup**

**Phalanx of the Ember Sigil** *(Sample Composition)*

- **Iron Captain (Hobgoblin Warlord)** — Polearm master, aura of command.
- **Iron Juggernaut (Duergar Enforcer)** — Enlarges, crushes with a hammer that channels *Thunderwave*.
- **Iron Shade (Drow Shadowcaster)** — Wields *Darkness, Hold Person, Greater Invisibility*.
- **Iron Pyre (Tiefling Pyromancer)** — Throws *Fireball, Wall of Fire*, hands crackle with flame.

**Squad Traits:**

- Always act with perfect discipline.
- All wear the same faceless mask.
- Will not retreat unless ordered by Vorgoth himself.

---

### **Atmosphere in Play**

- **Arrival:** “The air grows heavy, like the street itself is holding its breath. Then — four visages of iron, in step, in silence.”
- **Reaction:** Guild thugs slink back, citizens scatter.
- **Tone:** The Iron Order are not just guards. They are a living reminder that Karnuk is **not a free city — it is a cage kept locked by four Masks at a time.**
