# The Wand-Thief of Karnuk

Quest hook featuring Keeper Thalnys Shardbound.

Three stolen wands must be recovered from guild-affiliated buyers in Karnuk:

- Serpent's Fang at Deepstone Docks
- Widow's Kiss at Brotherhood of the Forge
- Ghoststep at Black Ink House

Reward: 500gp + choice of one wand + Thalnys's favor

Thief: Vendra Quickfingers (hired by Garruk Bloodaxe/Shadowed Parliament)
