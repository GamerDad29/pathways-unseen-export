# 🏙️ Karnuk: The Iron Syndicate City

---

### **Atmosphere**

- Think *Chicago / New York in the 1940’s*: smoky taverns, guild banners like neon signs, enforcers in armored uniforms patrolling with brutal efficiency.
- The streets echo with the hammering of forges, but behind the clang of steel is the hush of whispers — deals made in alleys, coin exchanged in dark warehouses, rival guild members eyeing each other across the tavern floor.
- The **docks** are the beating heart — barges, ferries, and skiffs all laden with weapons, armor, raw ore, and illicit cargo. Dockworkers are just as likely to be informants or smugglers as they are laborers.

---

### **The Five “Families” (Guild Powers)**

1. **Iron Hammer Clan (The Old Guard)** — Dwarves led by Thorgar Ironhammer. Traditional, honorable, but fading. “The Family that built this town.”
2. **Deepstone Miners (The Muscle)** — Hobgoblins under Grommash Stonefist. Roughnecks and dock enforcers. Run the labor, run the docks.
3. **Brotherhood of the Forge (The Artists)** — Drow smiths led by Azrael. Aloof but brilliant; handle rare, ornamental work.
4. **Brotherhood of the Iron Mine (The Deep Operators)** — Duergar led by the Iron Lord. Industrial-scale mining. Cold, secretive, bankroll the city.
5. **Steel Syndicate (The New Blood)** — Bugbears under Kethar Steelheart. Aggressive, ambitious, tied to Karnuk’s mechs.

---

### **Political Structure**

- **Vorgoth the Ironclad (Viceroy):** The “Mayor / Boss of Bosses.” Keeps peace by balancing rival families. Everyone knows without the mechs, he’d already be dead.
- **Garruk Bloodaxe (Shadowed Parliament):** A rival don with foreign backing, quietly manipulating guild politics.

# 👥 PC-Specific Contacts

### ⚔️ Cannonball (Barbarian)

- **Deepstone Miners (Muscle):** Natural enforcer; fits their dockside bruiser culture.
- **Thorgar Ironhammer (Old Guard):** Respects Bear Clan warriors.
- **Flavor Hook:** Seen as muscle that doesn’t ask questions.

### 🐉 Bpop (Artificer)

- **Steel Syndicate (New Blood):** Perfect match — mech experimentation.
- **Brotherhood of the Forge (Artists):** Might respect his tinkering ingenuity.
- **Flavor Hook:** Tempted by the underbelly of mech construction.

### 🐀 Iblith (Monk / Bregan D’aerthe)

- **Jevan Alderleaf (Bregan’s Man in Karnuk):** His golden ticket.
- **Azrael (Forge Drow):** Could draw him into sabotage.
- **Flavor Hook:** Direct line into the city’s shadow-broker elite.

### 🐦 Morrighan (Warlock → Sorcerer)

- **Brotherhood of the Forge:** Fey-magic curiosity.
- **Steel Syndicate:** Ember-magic could tie into their weapons.
- **Flavor Hook:** Karnuk sees her as a dangerous weapon or prize.
