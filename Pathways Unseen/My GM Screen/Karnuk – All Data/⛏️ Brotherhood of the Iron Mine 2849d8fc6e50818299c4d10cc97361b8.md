# ⛏️ Brotherhood of the Iron Mine

## The Deep Operators

**Race:** Duergar

**Leader:** "The Iron Lord" (identity unknown)

**Reputation:** Secretive, cold, industrial-scale mining

**Motto:** "The family that owns the refineries"

---

## Leadership

### Don "The Iron Lord" (600+ years?)

**Appearance:** Never seen in person. Communicates through enchanted iron masks worn by intermediaries. Rumored to be duergar, mind flayer, or brain in jar.

**Personality:** Cold, calculating, inhuman. Speaks in economic terms. Views people as resources to extract and expend.

**Voice:** Modulated through masks—emotionless, echoing, terrifying

**Motivation:** Control all raw materials. Corner embersteel market. Own every family's debt.

**Weakness:** Unknown. May not exist as single entity—could be council using figurehead.

### Underboss: Kharzon Deepdelve (290 years)

**Role:** Iron Lord's Voice, day-to-day operations

**Appearance:** Duergar in black iron armor, face always hidden behind ornate mask

**Personality:** Speaks in Iron Lord's voice (enchanted mask). Unknown if has independent thoughts.

**Status:** Possibly just puppet

### Capo: The Countess (200 years)

**Role:** Mine supervision, worker discipline

**Appearance:** Scarred duergar woman, blind in one eye (mining accident)

**Personality:** Brutal efficiency. Treats workers as expendable. "Quotas are quotas."

**Method:** Collapses tunnels on underperformers. "Accidents happen."

### The Silent (Enforcer Corps)

12 duergar in identical armor:

- Never speak
- Appear from shadows
- Rumored same person in multiple places (teleportation? clones?)

## Territory

### Primary Holdings

- **The Deep Mines** - Vast network far below Karnuk (embersteel/ore extraction)
- **The Refineries** - Processing facilities
- **The Vaults** - Most fortified location in Karnuk (raw material storage)
- **The Counting House** - All financial records (supposedly)

### Secondary Influence

- Raw material markets (control supply = control economy)
- Banking and loans (more capital than anyone)
- Real estate (own half of Karnuk through foreclosures)
- Mining equipment manufacturing

### Hidden Territory

- Tunnels connecting to other Underdark cities (rumored)
- Secret slave pens ("volunteers" supplemented with purchased labor)
- Embersteel refining secrets (known only to them)

## Operations

### Legitimate Income

- Mining operations (supply 60% of Karnuk's raw materials)
- Refining services
- Material sales
- Banking and lending

### Gray Market

- Price-fixing (artificial scarcity)
- Debt slavery (workers become "indentured")
- Monopolistic practices (buying out competitors)

### Criminal

- Slavery (kidnapping surface-dwellers, Underdark outcasts)
- Embersteel smuggling to Karnuk's enemies
- Mine collapse insurance fraud
- Assassination via "accidents"

## Family Culture

### Code of Conduct

- Efficiency above all. Waste is death.
- Silence is strength. Loose lips are liabilities.
- Profit justifies any action.
- Iron Lord's word is law. Question nothing.

### Rituals

- **The Descent** - New members spend 30 days in deepest mine, alone, minimal rations
- **"The Weighing"** - Annual review of productivity. Bottom 10% disappear.
- **Blood Oath** - Drink liquid embersteel (kills most; survivors gain fire resistance)

### Signs of Respect

- Speaking in someone's presence (silence is default)
- Sharing ration tokens (valuable underground)
- **"Iron Nod"** - Slight head tilt acknowledging competence
