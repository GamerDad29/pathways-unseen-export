# ⚒️ Iron Hammer Clan

## The Old Guard

**Race:** Dwarves

**Leader:** Don Thorgar Ironhammer

**Reputation:** Old money, traditional, honorable but stubborn

**Motto:** "The Family that built this town"

---

## Leadership

### Don Thorgar Ironhammer (Patriarch, 487 years)

**Appearance:** White-bearded dwarf with forge-scarred arms. Missing three fingers on left hand (Thousand Year War). Wears simple leather apron over mithral chain. His hammer "Oathkeeper" hangs at his belt—never drawn since war ended.

**Personality:** Gruff, honorable to a fault, deeply traditional. Believes in contracts written in blood. Despises "new ways" and shortcuts. Calls Steel Syndicate "children playing with fire."

**Voice:** Robert De Niro in The Irishman—quiet authority, disappointed rather than angry.

**Motivation:** Preserve dwarven codes. Ensure family legacy outlives him. Prevent Karnuk from becoming "just another pit of greed."

**Weakness:** Stubborn to point of self-destruction. Refuses to adapt even when losing territory.

### Underboss: Borin "The Ledger" Ironhammer (112 years)

**Role:** Day-to-day operations, accounts, contract enforcement

**Personality:** Calculating, pragmatic, quietly resentful of grandfather's rigidity

**Secret:** Secretly meeting with Steel Syndicate. Considering coup.

### Capo: Merra Ashforge (340 years)

**Role:** Production oversight, quality control, artisan contracts

**Personality:** Stern perfectionist. Every piece bears her inspection mark.

**Loyalty:** Absolute to Thorgar and old ways.

### Enforcer: Kragath "Hammerfall" (290 years)

**Role:** Debt collection, contract enforcement

**Personality:** Polite, apologetic, brutally efficient. "Nothing personal, just business."

**Method:** Breaks kneecaps with precise hammer strikes. Never kills unless ordered.

## Territory

### Primary Holdings

- **The Anvil Quarter** - Traditional forge district, upper-middle terraces
- **Founder's Plaza** - Historic market square where first guild charter was signed
- **The Old Mint** - Converted coinsmith halls, high-security storage
- **Ironheart Tavern** - Unofficial headquarters, neutral ground for all families

### Secondary Influence

- Traditional weapon markets
- Apprenticeship guilds
- Veterans' lodges
- Historical monuments

### Lost Territory (past 50 years)

- Lower docks (to Deepstone Miners)
- Experimental foundries (to Steel Syndicate)
- Three major warehouses (seized by Vorgoth)

---

## Operations

### Legitimate Income

- Master-craft weapons and armor (highest quality)
- Apprenticeship programs
- Historical artifact preservation
- Contract arbitration services

### Gray Market

- Weapon "refurbishment" (removing serial marks)
- Forge access rental
- "Insurance" against Syndicate harassment

### Criminal

- Loan-sharking (fair terms, written contracts)
- Smuggling historical artifacts to surface
- Blackmail using old ledgers (centuries of secrets)

---

## Family Culture

### Code of Conduct

- Your word is your bond. Break it, you die.
- Quality over quantity. Always.
- Blood debts paid in full—no exceptions.
- Respect old ways or leave family.

### Rituals

- New members swear oaths over Thorgar's anvil, hand on hot iron
- Monthly "Remembrance Forge" crafting weapons for fallen
- Annual pilgrimage to pre-war battlefields

### Signs of Respect

- Remove gloves when shaking hands (show scarred palms)
- Present gift-weapons pommel-first
- Never sit in patriarch's chair, even when empty
