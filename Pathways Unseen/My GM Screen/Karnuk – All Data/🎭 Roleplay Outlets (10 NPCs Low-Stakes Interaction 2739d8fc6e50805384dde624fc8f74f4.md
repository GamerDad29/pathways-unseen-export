# 🎭 Roleplay Outlets (10 NPCs / Low-Stakes Interactions)

1. **Dockhand Lira** — jaded human woman, complains about guild dues, trades rumors for cheap ale.
2. **Grim Tav** — duergar barkeep, knows who fought who the night before, calls everyone “kid.”
3. **Runner Kip** — goblin courier, full of gossip but terrified of Syndicate eyes.
4. **Marn the Sweep** — elderly dwarf sweeping dockside alleys, hears everything, ignored by everyone.
5. **Zesha Quickfingers** — tiefling card-dealer in the tavern corner, always “knows a guy.”
6. **Orlith the Boatwright** — dragonborn carpenter fixing barges; talks politics if you lend a hand.
7. **Jenda Mosscloak** — half-elf herbalist selling poultices, worried about the Shadow Plague.
8. **Broll the Ratcatcher** — scarred halfling with a dozen cages; his “rats” overhear whispers.
9. **Kragni Flintjaw** — retired mercenary drinking away his pension, eager to tell old war stories.
10. **“Whistler” Vek** — mute kobold beggar, whistles tunes that line up with current dockside drama.

# ⚖️ Moral Dilemmas (10 No-Win Choices)

1. Rescue a beaten Deep Gnome slave, but blow your cover to Syndicate enforcers.
2. Accept pay to guard a crate, then discover it contains Warforged parts.
3. Save dockworkers trapped in a fire, or catch the arsonist before he slips away.
4. A guild lieutenant offers shelter — but only if you rough up a rival’s apprentice.
5. A starving family begs for coin; giving it up leaves you short for bribes later.
6. The Iron Order demands identification; comply and risk exposure, or refuse and trigger violence.
7. Find a sick child with Shadow Plague — mercy kill or risk contagion spreading.
8. A gang of orphans tries to rob you; punish them harshly or let them go and risk repeat attacks.
9. A rival guild bribes you to ignore evidence that would help Bregan D’aerthe.
10. A dock brawl breaks out — intervene to stop bloodshed, or let it run its course and avoid guild attention.

# 🔗 Evidence Trails (8 Interlocking Clues)

1. **Bounty Poster** — still damp ink, Kethar’s crest (real or forged).
2. **Iron Order Mask Shard** — runic scrap, reeks of forge-smoke.
3. **Shadow-Tainted Vial** — faint chill, same as the Kender “smell plague.”
4. **Scorched Playing Card** — Queen of Spades, marked “Veyran.”
5. **Broken Parley Token** — ivory coin snapped in half; sign of a voided pact.
6. **Gauntlet Cuff Note** — paper scrap stuffed into a soot-black glove: “No mercy, no witnesses.”
7. **Grimoire Fragment** — half-burned parchment with Azrael’s sigil.
8. **Spent Brass Cap** — single casing from a firearm, proof of new weapons tech moving through the docks.
