# 🏛️ The Five Families - Overview

The five criminal families that control Karnuk's economy and politics.

## Overview

Karnuk is controlled by five major guild families operating like 1940s mafia organizations:

1. **Iron Hammer Clan** - The Old Guard (Dwarves)
2. **Deepstone Miners** - The Muscle (Hobgoblins)
3. **Brotherhood of the Forge** - The Artists (Drow)
4. **Brotherhood of the Iron Mine** - The Deep Operators (Duergar)
5. **Steel Syndicate** - The New Blood (Bugbears)

## Power Dynamics

### Current Balance (Strongest → Weakest)

1. Brotherhood of the Iron Mine (control resources)
2. Deepstone Miners (control transportation)
3. Steel Syndicate (rising fast)
4. Brotherhood of the Forge (smaller but deadly)
5. Iron Hammer Clan (fading glory)

### The Viceroy's Control

**Vorgoth the Ironclad** maintains power by:

- Playing families against each other
- Controlling the war-mechs
- Strategic contracts favoring weaker families
- Using the Iron Order as neutral enforcers

**Shadow Threat:** Garruk Bloodaxe (Shadowed Parliament) undermines stability by feeding resources to Steel Syndicate.

## Active Conflicts

**Iron Hammer vs. Steel Syndicate**

- Syndicate buying Iron Hammer properties
- Borin (Thorgar's grandson) considering defection
- Thorgar refusing to modernize

**Deepstone vs. Steel Syndicate**

- Warehouse 7 disputed territory
- Syndicate mechs threatening dock workers
- Sabotage operations planned

**Brotherhood of Forge vs. Everyone**

- Assassinating rival artisans
- Industrial espionage
- Maintaining dominance through fear

**Iron Mine vs. No One**

- Quietly buying debts
- Waiting for others to weaken
- Playing all sides
