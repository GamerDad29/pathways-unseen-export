# 🏪 Specialty Shops - Overview

36 unique specialty shops across Karnuk's five family territories, each reflecting the character and operations of their controlling faction.

---

## Economic Note

**Gold is 10x more valuable in this economy.** A 5gp purchase here equals a 50gp purchase in standard D&D economies. Prices reflect Karnuk's industrial efficiency and the relative scarcity of adventuring wealth.

## Using These Shops

Each shop has:

- **A unique proprietor** with personality and connections
- **Services with pricing** adjusted for 10x gold value
- **A story hook** connecting to family conflicts and campaign threads

Shops can serve as:

- Quest locations
- Information sources
- Suppliers for specific needs
- Sites of family conflicts
- NPC introduction points
