# 🎯 The Wand-Thief of Karnuk

## Keeper Thalnys Shardbound's Missing Arsenal

A quest for the party upon arriving in Karnuk.

---

## Opening: The Construct Messenger

A mechanical spider approaches the party at The Coal Draught (or wherever they're staying). Obsidian glass body, eight crystalline legs, glowing violet runes.

It speaks in Thalnys's voice:

> "You are the outsiders who entered Karnuk under the Broker's seal. You carry Frank's mark. Good. I require competent thieves, and competence is rare in this smoke-choked pit."
> 

Delivers a sealed letter, then vanishes.

---

## The Letter

**To the Nameless Competents,**

Three of my wands were stolen during transport to Karnuk six days past. The caravan guards were bribed. The wands are bonded to my shield's resonance network.

They call to me from three locations:

- **Serpent's Fang** — Deepstone Docks, Warehouse 7 (held by Krev Ironjaw)
- **Widow's Kiss** — Brotherhood of the Forge (held by Zethrin Ashveil)
- **Ghoststep** — Black Ink House (held by Silra Inkvein)

I cannot travel to Karnuk myself. I require agents.

Retrieve my wands. All three. Intact.

**Compensation:**

- 500 gold pieces
- Your choice of one recovered wand to keep
- My favor

I chose you because Frank spoke well of you, and Frank does not lie to me.

**Keeper Thalnys Shardbound**

---

## Meeting Thalnys

The spider leads them to the lower docks. A sending-image of Thalnys appears — floating on her disc, shield beside her.

> "Good. You didn't run. That suggests either bravery or desperation. Both are useful."
> 

**She explains:**

- The theft was six days ago on the Old Ember Road
- The thief had insider knowledge
- The buyers are guild-affiliated (this will cause problems)
- The thief is still in Karnuk — she can feel them through resonance

> "Find my wands, and you'll find the thief. I want them alive."
> 

---

## WAND ONE: Serpent's Fang

**Location:** Warehouse 7, Deepstone Docks

**Holder:** Krev Ironjaw (hobgoblin enforcer, Deepstone Miners)

**Scene:** Krev is using the wand to intimidate a late debtor.

**Retrieval Options:**

- **Stealth:** Roof vents (Acrobatics 14, Stealth 15)
- **Social:** Persuasion 16 or Deception 14 to claim it's cursed
- **Force:** Fight Krev (Hobgoblin Warlord CR 6), backup in 3 rounds
- **Leverage:** Buy his debt from Marrek Goldjaw (150gp)

**Complication:** Krev used it — only 2 charges left (normally 4).

---

## WAND TWO: Widow's Kiss

**Location:** Brotherhood of the Forge, Upper Terraces

**Holder:** Zethrin Ashveil (young drow artificer, Azrael's apprentice)

**Scene:** Zethrin is studying it obsessively in his workshop.

**Retrieval Options:**

- **Appeal to Pride:** Claim Azrael sent you (Persuasion 14)
- **Theft:** Sleight of Hand 15 during distraction
- **Trade:** Offer drow/arcane knowledge (Persuasion 14, advantage if Morrighan/Iblith)
- **Azrael:** Appeal directly via Iblith's connections

**Complication:** Damaged silver filigree — max charges reduced from 7 to 5.

**Special:** If Morrighan present, Zethrin becomes fascinated by her fey magic.

---

## WAND THREE: Ghoststep

**Location:** Black Ink House, Deep Market

**Holder:** Silra Inkvein (drow moneylender)

**Scene:** She uses it to teleport into debtors' homes at midnight.

**Retrieval Options:**

- **Become Debtors:** Take loan, negotiate wand as payment (requires job first)
- **Expose:** She violated guild neutrality (Investigation 15, Intimidation 16)
- **Bregan Connection:** Iblith invokes Bregan D'aerthe ties (Persuasion 14)
- **Heist:** Disarm wards (Arcana 16, Thieves' Tools 17), shadow mastiff
- **Buy Back:** 600gp (haggle to 450gp with Persuasion 14)

**Complication:** Only 1 charge left, crystal cracked. Major repair needed.

**Special:** Silra recognizes Morrighan's Unseelie marks, offers info on Seelie spy in Karnuk.

---

## The Thief Revealed

After recovering 2+ wands, Thalnys sends:

> "The thief is Vendra Quickfingers, halfling fence at Shadowcloak tent, Dockside Market. She still carries my tool harness. Bring her alive."
> 

**The Confrontation:**

Vendra is terrified, surrenders immediately:

> "I didn't know they were bonded! My kid's sick — healers charge more than I make in a year!"
> 

Hands over tool harness (enchanted thieves' tools — DC 17 becomes DC 14).

**The Choice:**

1. **Deliver to Thalnys** — She interrogates, learns Garruk Bloodaxe (Shadowed Parliament) hired Vendra. Vendra disappears. Full payment.
2. **Let Her Go** — Warn her to flee. Thalnys livid, reduces reward 200gp. Vendra becomes future ally.
3. **Negotiate** — Persuasion 18 to spare Vendra as informant. Recurring NPC.

---

## Resolution & Rewards

**Thalnys's Reactions:**

- **Serpent's Fang:** "Typical. Hobgoblins treat magic like a club."
- **Widow's Kiss:** (long silence) "Three days to repair. Was the apprentice still breathing?"
- **Ghoststep:** (dangerously quiet) "Nearly destroyed. This was my escape, my survival."

Then: "But you retrieved them. Most would have run. You didn't."

**Payment:**

- 500gp (300gp if freed Vendra, 400gp if negotiated)
- Choice of one wand:
    - **Serpent's Fang** (Magic Missile, 7 charges, 1d6+1/dawn)
    - **Widow's Kiss** (Scorching Ray, 5 max, 1d4+1/dawn)
    - **Ghoststep** (Misty Step, 7 after rebuild, 1d6+1/dawn)

**Bonus (if exceptional):**

> "Consider my workshop a safe haven. I owe you a favor. One. Don't waste it."
> 
- Thalnys as permanent ally
- Workshop safe house
- Future Shadow Plague hooks
- Custom wand priority

---

## Consequences

**Guild Fallout:**

- Deepstone Miners remember if you fought Krev
- Brotherhood colder if you crossed Azrael
- Black Ink marks you if you threatened Silra

**Shadowed Parliament:**

If Vendra revealed Bloodaxe:

> "They're testing my defenses. If they're watching me, they're watching anyone I hire."
> 

Party now on Parliament radar.

**Vendra (if spared):**

- 10% black market discount
- Underworld intel
- Emergency safe house

---

## Final Scene

As party leaves, Thalnys calls out:

> "One more thing."
> 

Tosses them **Nightveil's Whisper** (Shield spell, 1 charge, non-recharging).

> "If you find yourselves in a fight you can't win... use this. Once. Then return it. Insurance."
> 

> "Try not to die stupidly. I dislike wasting investments."
> 

Faintest smile.

For the first time since losing her legs, Thalnys feels hope.
