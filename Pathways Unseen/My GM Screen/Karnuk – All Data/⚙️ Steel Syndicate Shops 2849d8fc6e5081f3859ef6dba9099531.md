# ⚙️ Steel Syndicate Shops

## The Foundries / Innovation District

Cutting-edge establishments pushing technological boundaries.

---

## 1. Gearworks Emporium

**Proprietor:** Tikka Sparkfuse (kobold artificer, 23 years, genius)

**Specialty:** Artificer components and experimental gadgets

**Services:**

- Artificer component kits (5-20gp)
- Experimental gadgets (2gp 5sp-50gp, 30% malfunction chance)
- Custom contraption commissions (10gp+, 1-2 weeks)
- Repairs for broken magical items (7gp 5sp, 50% success rate)

**Hook:** Veysha Geargrind's protégé. Tests experimental weapons on "volunteers." Not all survive.

---

## 2. The Iron Augment

**Proprietor:** Vex Ironlimb (bugbear with prosthetic arm, 36 years)

**Specialty:** Prosthetic limbs and body augmentation

**Services:**

- Basic prosthetic limbs (50gp, functions normally)
- Enhanced prosthetics (150gp, +1 to Str or Dex)
- Integrated weapons (prosthetic with blade/crossbow, 100gp)
- Body augmentation consultations (free, creepy)

**Hook:** Works for Kethar Steelheart. Reports "upgrade potential" for forced mech pilot recruitment.

---

## 3. The Volatile Flask

**Proprietor:** Krazzit Boombreath (goblin, 41 years, burn-scarred)

**Specialty:** Experimental alchemy and explosives

**Services:**

- Alchemist's fire (2gp 5sp per flask)
- Experimental explosives (5gp, damage 2d6 to 6d6, DM rolls secretly)
- Enhanced acid vials (5gp, 3d6 acid damage)
- "Krazzit's Special" (10gp, mystery effect)

**Hook:** Developing explosives for Syndicate assault on Iron Hammer warehouses.

---

## 4. The Chrome Salvage Yard

**Proprietor:** Vorgash "Scraps" (half-orc, 44 years)

**Specialty:** Mech parts and war machine components

**Services:**

- Mech armor plating (decorative, 10gp per plate)
- Construct parts (arms, legs, cores, 20-200gp)
- Heavy machinery components (2gp 5sp-50gp)
- "Restricted" components (military-grade, 100gp+, illegal)

**Hook:** Occasionally "finds" parts from Vorgoth's war-mechs. How without execution is mystery.

---

## 5. The Electron Forge

**Proprietor:** Veysha Geargrind herself

**Specialty:** Custom weapon modifications

**Services:**

- Weapon modifications (+1 enhancement, additional damage type, 75gp)
- Experimental ammunition (lightning/acid rounds, 5gp per 10)
- Weapon analysis and reverse-engineering (20gp)
- "Geargrind Signature" (personal mods by Veysha, 200gp, appointment only)

**Hook:** Getting appointment means you're in with Syndicate elite. Kethar knows who you are.

---

## 6. The Neon Piston

**Proprietor:** Rhythm (warforged bartender/merchant)

**Specialty:** Energy drinks and performance enhancers

**Services:**

- Energy drinks (5sp, advantage on Con saves 1 hour)
- Focus serum (1gp, advantage on Int checks 1 hour)
- Stamina boost (1gp 5sp, reduce exhaustion 1 level for 4 hours, crash after)
- "Pilot's Edge" (2gp 5sp, +2 initiative next combat, addictive)

**Hook:** Dealing experimental combat drugs. Workers getting addicted and dying. Nobody cares.
