# ⚒️ Iron Hammer Shops

## The Anvil Quarter / Founder's Plaza

Traditional establishments honoring old dwarven craftsmanship and codes.

---

## 1. The Ancestor's Forge

**Proprietor:** Grimna Truehammer (342-year-old dwarf matron)

**Specialty:** Historical weapon restoration and authentication

**Services:**

- Weapon restoration (10-50gp depending on damage)
- Historical appraisal (3gp)
- Lineage verification for noble families (5gp)
- "Ancestor bonding" ritual (15gp + materials)

**Hook:** Grimna refuses to restore any weapon made after the Thousand Year War. "Modern smiths have no soul."

---

## 2. Oathkeeper's Rune Shop

**Proprietor:** Thaldrin Stonescript (210-year-old dwarf runecaster)

**Specialty:** Traditional runic engraving and contract magic

**Services:**

- Weapon rune engraving (+1 enhancement, 50gp, 3 days)
- Contract rune-binding (10gp)
- Protective runes for homes/shops (20gp)
- Curse removal from broken oaths (30gp)

**Hook:** Steel Syndicate pressuring him to learn "modern shortcut" runes. He's insulted but tempted.

---

## 3. The Silver Grindstone

**Proprietor:** Borak & Morak Keenedge (twin dwarves, 156 years old)

**Specialty:** Blade sharpening to perfection

**Services:**

- Standard sharpening (5sp, same day)
- Master sharpening (+1 damage for 3 strikes, 2gp 5sp, 1 day)
- "Keenedge Special" (+2 damage for 1 strike, 5gp)
- Edge repair after battle (1gp 5sp)

**Hook:** Twins argue constantly but work in perfect unison.

---

## 4. Forgemaster's Provisions

**Proprietor:** Helga Emberbrew (401 years)

**Specialty:** Supplies for smiths and apprentices

**Services:**

- Smith's tool kits (5gp basic, 20gp master)
- Specialty coals (5sp-5gp per sack)
- Leather aprons (fire-resistant, 7gp 5sp)
- Apprentice starter packages (3gp down, credit available)

**Hook:** Disrespect her, every forge closes to you.

---

## 5. The Veteran's Vault

**Proprietor:** Sergeant Durnak "One-Eye" Ironfist (267 years)

**Specialty:** Military surplus

**Services:**

- Used armor (2gp 5sp-20gp)
- Battle-worn weapons (5-30gp)
- Military rations (1gp per week)
- War maps (5gp)

**Hook:** Cannonball's scars earn 25% off.

---

## 6. Coalfire Scriptorium

**Proprietor:** Master Telden Ironquill (198 years)

**Specialty:** Legal contracts

**Services:**

- Contract drafting (5gp simple, 20gp complex)
- Contract review (2gp 5sp)
- Guild arbitration (50gp)
- Historical research (10gp)

**Hook:** Breaking contracts triggers magical mark.
