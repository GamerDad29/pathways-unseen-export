# ⚓ Deepstone Miners

## The Muscle

**Race:** Hobgoblins

**Leader:** Don Grommash Stonefist

**Reputation:** Roughnecks, enforcers, union muscle

**Motto:** "You don't move a crate in Karnuk without our say-so"

---

## Leadership

### Don Grommash Stonefist (Boss, 52 years)

**Appearance:** Massive hobgoblin with iron-gray skin and jaw like an anvil. Both fists wrapped in spiked steel gauntlets he never removes (rumored fused to bone). Wears dock-worker overalls over armor. Right ear missing—bitten off in tavern fight.

**Personality:** Direct, loud, surprisingly fair. Rules through fear and respect equally. "I don't lie, I don't cheat, but I WILL break your arms if you cross me." Fiercely loyal to crew.

**Voice:** James Gandolfini's Tony Soprano—working-class accent, sudden rage explosions.

**Motivation:** Protect workers. Control Karnuk's lifeblood (shipping). Eventually overthrow Vorgoth, install "workers' council."

**Weakness:** Nephew Krev is an idiot, but Grommash refuses to see it.

### Underboss: "Mother" Vrekka Stonefist (48 years)

**Role:** Logistics, dock scheduling, bribery network

**Personality:** Cold, calculating, terrifyingly competent. The real brains.

**Appearance:** Scarred hobgoblin woman in oil-stained leathers, always smoking pipe

**Method:** "We don't threaten. We just... stop loading your shipments. Permanently."

### Capo: Throk "The Hook" (35 years)

**Role:** Dockside operations, smuggling coordination

**Personality:** Jovial giant who laughs while cracking skulls

**Weapon:** Longshoreman's cargo hook

### The Breakers (Enforcer Squad)

Six hobgoblin thugs, dock brawl veterans:

- Skarn
- Drokka
- Vesh
- Morgul
- Thrax
- Rekka

Specialize in "workplace accidents." Tattoo tally marks for problems solved.

## Territory

### Primary Holdings

- **Deepstone Docks** - Entire lower waterfront (23 piers, 40+ warehouses)
- **Warehouse District** - Massive cliff-face storage
- **The Kettle** - Dockside slum where workers live
- **Grinder's Rest Tavern** - Headquarters (entry by permission only)

### Secondary Influence

- All cargo transport
- Most manual labor in Karnuk
- Underground fighting pits
- Black market stolen goods

### Contested Territory

- **Warehouse 7** - Disputed with Steel Syndicate
- **The Spillway** - Drainage tunnels contested with Iron Mine

---

## Operations

### Legitimate Income

- Cargo loading/unloading (control ALL dock labor)
- Warehouse rental
- Transport services
- Shipment "security"

### Gray Market

- Protection rackets
- Goods "inspection fees" (10% skim)
- Expedited processing (bribes)

### Criminal

- Smuggling (weapons, contraband, people)
- Cargo theft and resale
- Labor extortion
- Underground fighting rings (enslaved combatants)

---

## Family Culture

### Code of Conduct

- Crew before self. Always.
- Earn your position—nepotism gets you killed.
- Snitches fed to magma flows.
- Fair day's pay for fair day's work (genuinely believe this).

### Rituals

- **"The Haul"** - New members carry full cargo crate alone up 100 dock stairs
- **Weekly Counting** - All profits divided publicly (transparency = loyalty)
- **Breaker Tattoos** - Tally marks for each problem solved

### Signs of Respect

- Share dock-worker rations (brotherhood)
- Call someone "loader" instead of name (highest honor)
- **Stonefist Salute** - Fist to heart, then forehead
