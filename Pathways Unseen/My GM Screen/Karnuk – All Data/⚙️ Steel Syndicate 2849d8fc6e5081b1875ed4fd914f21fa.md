# ⚙️ Steel Syndicate

## The New Blood

**Race:** Bugbears

**Leader:** Don Kethar Steelheart

**Reputation:** Aggressive, ambitious, cutthroat

**Motto:** "The future belongs to those who build it"

---

## Leadership

### Don Kethar Steelheart (Boss, 34 years)

**Appearance:** Young bugbear with ambition burning in yellow eyes. Missing right tusk (smashed in coup killing previous boss). Modern armor—plates riveted with industrial precision, visible gears. Left arm is mechanical prosthetic (built by his artificers).

**Personality:** Ruthless, innovative, impatient. Despises tradition. "The old families are corpses that don't know they're dead." Obsessed with war-mechs and modernization.

**Voice:** Young Michael Corleone—quiet, intense, dangerous

**Motivation:** Overthrow old guard. Control Karnuk's future through technology. Build mech army loyal only to him.

**Weakness:** Overconfident. Makes enemies faster than allies.

### Underboss: Veysha "Geargrind" (41 years)

**Role:** Mech development, weapons research, industrial espionage

**Appearance:** Bugbear female covered in burn scars, missing fingers replaced with tools

**Personality:** Mad scientist energy. Giggles when things explode.

**Loyalty:** To innovation, not Kethar. Will betray if offered better resources.

### Capo: Thrak "Ironjaw" (28 years)

**Role:** Muscle, intimidation, hostile takeovers

**Appearance:** Scarred bugbear with metal jaw (lost original to acid)

**Personality:** Loves violence. Loyal because Kethar allows creative brutality.

**Method:** Experimental weapons—flamethrowers, lightning gauntlets, explosive crossbow bolts

### Shadow Advisor (Unknown)

**Identity:** Shadowed Parliament plant (unknown to Kethar)

**Role:** Feeding information and resources to destabilize other families

**Goal:** Weaken all families so Parliament can seize control

## Territory

### Primary Holdings

- **The Foundries** - Modern facilities with assembly lines
- **Mechworks Plaza** - War-mech construction and testing
- **The Spark** - Neon-lit tavern/headquarters (music-pumping, unlike any other family HQ)
- **Innovation District** - Experimental workshops, artificer labs

### Secondary Influence

- Modern weapon markets
- Tech development
- Younger generation workers (sick of old family strictness)
- Underground racing (modified mechs)

### Expanding Territory

- Aggressive buyouts of Iron Hammer properties
- Sabotaging Deepstone dock operations
- Recruiting Brotherhood of Forge artisans

---

## Operations

### Legitimate Income

- Modern weapons manufacturing
- Mech construction and rental
- Industrial automation equipment
- Security contracts (providing mech guards)

### Gray Market

- Forced buyouts of competitors
- Industrial accidents targeting rivals
- Selling experimental weapons without safety testing
- Poaching skilled workers from other families

### Criminal

- Arms dealing to Karnuk's enemies
- Sabotage and terrorism (blamed on "malfunctions")
- Blackmail using surveillance tech
- Drug manufacturing (using chemical refineries)

---

## Family Culture

### Code of Conduct

- Innovate or die. Tradition is weakness.
- Results matter, methods don't.
- Loyalty to Syndicate, not individuals.
- Future belongs to those who build it.

### Rituals

- **"The Gauntlet"** - Run through mech testing ground during live-fire exercises
- **Weekly Innovation Competitions** - Best design gets bonus pay
- **"The Proving"** - Annual mech battles where promotions are earned

### Signs of Respect

- Share blueprints (ultimate trust)
- Offer to "upgrade" someone's gear
- **"Steelheart Fist"** - Mechanical fist-bump that sparks with electricity
