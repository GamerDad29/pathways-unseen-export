# ⛏️ Brotherhood of the Iron Mine Shops

## The Deep Mines / The Refineries

Industrial suppliers and the financial heart of the Iron Lord's empire.

---

## 1. The Ore Vault

**Proprietor:** Karzen Deepvein (duergar, 312 years)

**Specialty:** Raw ore and ingots

**Services:**

- Iron ingots (1gp per 10 lbs)
- Steel ingots (2gp 5sp per 10 lbs)
- Embersteel ingots (10gp per 10 lbs, Brotherhood controlled)
- Mithral (50gp per lb, rare)

**Hook:** Keeps records of every sale. Iron Lord knows who buys what. Large purchases trigger "interest."

---

## 2. The Deep Pick

**Proprietor:** Grotha Irongrip (duergar, 267 years, missing three fingers)

**Specialty:** Mining equipment and safety gear

**Services:**

- Mining picks (never dull, 7gp 5sp)
- Safety helmets with light stones (2gp 5sp)
- Breathing filters for toxic gas (5gp, 10 uses)
- Structural support beams (1gp 5sp each)

**Hook:** Sells to independent miners. Iron Lord hates this. Walking dangerous line.

---

## 3. The Refinery's Edge

**Proprietor:** Molok Flameheart (magman, elemental being)

**Specialty:** Metal purification and alloys

**Services:**

- Ore purification (5gp per batch)
- Custom alloy creation (10-50gp)
- Chemical reagents for smithing (1-10gp)
- Fire-resistant containers (2gp 5sp)

**Hook:** Bound to Iron Lord by ancient contract. Desperately wants freedom.

---

## 4. The Stone Sage

**Proprietor:** Urloth "The Mapper" (duergar, 389 years)

**Specialty:** Mine maps and geological surveys

**Services:**

- Mine tunnel maps (5-20gp)
- Geological surveys (identifies ore veins, 10gp)
- Safety inspections (7gp 5sp)
- "Forgotten" vein locations (50gp, highly illegal)

**Hook:** Playing dangerous game. If Iron Lord discovers independent maps, he's dead.

---

## 5. The Crystal Hollow

**Proprietor:** Shirrik (deep gnome, 142 years)

**Specialty:** Raw gemstones and minerals

**Services:**

- Gemstone purchases (pays 75% of value, cash immediately)
- Gemstone sales (1-100gp)
- Crystal specimens (2gp 5sp-50gp)
- Appraisal services (1gp)

**Hook:** Not owned by Iron Lord. Pays adventurers to "find" gems in "abandoned" mines (steal from Brotherhood).

---

## 6. The Iron Ledger

**Proprietor:** No visible owner (clerks rotate)

**Specialty:** Banking, loans, debt collection

**Services:**

- Loans (10% interest per month, collateral required)
- Debt payment
- Account inquiries
- Foreclosure warnings

**Hook:** Trap. Interest compounds until you're enslaved in mines. Hundreds "volunteered" this way.
