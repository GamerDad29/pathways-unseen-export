# 🕷️ Brotherhood of the Forge Shops

## Cathedral of Steel / Silkweb District

Luxury establishments catering to the wealthy and dangerous.

---

## 1. The Gilded Fang

**Proprietor:** Velrith Nightgrace (drow enchantress, 412 years)

**Specialty:** Luxury weapon enchantments

**Services:**

- Cosmetic enhancements (gold inlay, gems, 10-50gp)
- Status enchantments (weapon glows, 20gp)
- Functional enchantments (+1 weapons 50gp, +2 weapons 200gp)
- "Signature pieces" (100gp+)

**Hook:** Azrael's niece. Reports on every client. Brotherhood knows your wealth.

---

## 2. The Venom Gallery

**Proprietor:** Xilrath "The Pale" (albino drow, 290 years)

**Specialty:** Poisons and antidotes

**Services:**

- Basic poisons (sleep, paralysis, 5-15gp)
- Exotic toxins (memory loss, truth serum, 20-50gp)
- Antidotes (10gp, must specify poison)
- Alchemical components (1-10gp)

**Hook:** The Spider's supplier. Ask too many questions, get a visit.

---

## 3. Nightsilk Atelier

**Proprietor:** Syndra Nightsteel (Azrael's daughter)

**Specialty:** Enchanted clothing and hidden armor

**Services:**

- Enchanted formal wear (glamour effects, 20gp)
- Armored clothing (AC 12-15 as robes, 50-150gp)
- Disguise outfits (advantage on Deception, 30gp)
- Custom tailoring (5gp+)

**Hook:** Every outfit has hidden listening rune. Brotherhood hears everything.

---

## 4. The Crystal Crucible

**Proprietor:** Master Thavron (drow, 601 years, blind)

**Specialty:** Gemstone cutting and crystal focusing

**Services:**

- Gemstone cutting (5-50gp)
- Spell focus creation (20gp)
- Arcane lenses (see invisible/illusions, 30gp)
- Crystal repair (10gp)

**Hook:** Can "read" aura through crystals. Knows party's magic within minutes.

---

## 5. The Obsidian Quill

**Proprietor:** Ereleth Inkweaver (drow, 189 years)

**Specialty:** Forgeries and disappeared records

**Services:**

- Masterwork documents (10gp)
- Forgeries (guild papers, 20-50gp)
- Record alteration (erase criminal history, 100gp)
- Secret correspondence (5gp)

**Hook:** Brotherhood intelligence. Knows every secret because she's written half.

---

## 6. The Mourning Blade

**Proprietor:** Zhavir Sorrowsong (drow priest, 445 years)

**Specialty:** Funeral weapons and memorial smithing

**Services:**

- Memorial weapons (names of fallen, 20gp)
- Funeral masks (5gp)
- "Last blade" consecration (30gp)
- Soul-binding ritual (50gp, controversial)

**Hook:** Knows who's planning assassinations via "guilt offerings." Won't tell, but might hint.
