# 🎯 The Wand-Thief of Karnuk

## Keeper Thalnys Shardbound's Missing Arsenal

A quest hook for the party arriving in Karnuk, featuring the drow artificer Thalnys Shardbound.

---

## Opening Scene: The Message Arrives

The party is settling into Karnuk. The air tastes of copper and coal dust. Anvils ring in the distance like church bells marking time in hell.

Then a messenger arrives. Not a guild runner. Not a dockhand. A **construct**.

It's small, barely two feet tall, shaped like a mechanical spider with eight crystalline legs that click-clack across the tavern floor. Its body is obsidian glass, glowing faintly with violet runic script.

It stops. Eight gem-eyes swivel to focus on them. Then it speaks in a voice like wind through hollow bone—feminine, precise, irritated:

> *"You are the outsiders who entered Karnuk under the Broker's seal. You are the ones who carry Frank's mark. Good. I require competent thieves, and competence is rare in this smoke-choked pit."*
> 

The construct's abdomen splits open, extruding a rolled piece of parchment sealed with black wax. The seal bears the image of a shield crossed with twelve wands.

> *"Read it. Memorize it. Burn it. If you accept, follow my emissary to the lower docks. If you refuse, know that I will remember your cowardice."*
> 

Then the construct scuttles away into the shadows.

---

## The Letter

**To the Nameless Competents,**

Three of my wands were stolen during transport to Karnuk six days past. The shipment was intercepted between UTTP and the Triadic Gates by parties unknown. The caravan guards were bribed, not killed—a mercy I now regret.

The wands are bonded to my shield's resonance network. I can feel them, faintly, like phantom limbs. They call to me from three locations:

- **Serpent's Fang** (bone wand, copper inlay) — *Deepstone Docks, Warehouse 7*
- **Widow's Kiss** (obsidian glass, silver filigree) — *The Forge District, Brotherhood of the Forge*
- **Ghoststep** (translucent phasing crystal) — *The Deep Market, Black Ink House*

Each wand was sold to a different buyer. Each buyer believes they purchased legitimate goods. None will surrender easily.

I cannot travel to Karnuk myself—my floating disc drains charges over such distances, and I will not crawl like a beggar through your streets. I require agents.

Retrieve my wands. All three. Intact. Uncharged is acceptable; destroyed is unforgivable.

**Your compensation:**

- 500 gold pieces (shared)
- Your choice of one recovered wand to keep permanently
- My favor, which in the Underdark is worth more than coin

Refuse, and I simply hire others. But know this: I chose *you* because Frank spoke well of you, and Frank does not lie to me.

Do not disappoint me.

**Keeper Thalnys Shardbound**

*Wand-Wall Arcanum, Guardian of the Middle Ways*

## Meeting Thalnys

The mechanical spider leads the party to the **lower docks**—a stretch of basalt piers jutting over molten magma flows.

In a forgotten maintenance alcove, they find **Thalnys's remote outpost**: a circle of hovering rune-stones projecting a shimmering sending-image of the drow artificer herself. She floats on her disc, shield resting beside her, crimson eyes sharp as scalpels.

> *"Good. You didn't run. That suggests either bravery or desperation. Both are useful."*
> 

### The Theft

- Six days ago, her caravan was ambushed on the Old Ember Road
- Guards were bribed (she's "dealt with" them)
- Shipment contained twelve wands; only three were valuable enough to fence
- The thief knew exactly what to steal = insider knowledge

### The Buyers

1. **Serpent's Fang** — **Krev Ironjaw** (Deepstone Miners enforcer)
2. **Widow's Kiss** — **Zethrin Ashveil** (drow artificer, Azrael's apprentice)
3. **Ghoststep** — **Silra Inkvein** (Black Ink House moneylender)

### The Complication

> *"The thief is still in Karnuk. I can feel them through the resonance network. Find my wands, and you'll find the thief. When you do... I want them alive. They have questions to answer."*
> 

> *"One more thing. The buyers are guild-affiliated. Retrieving my property will put you at odds with the Deepstone Miners, the Brotherhood of the Forge, and the Black Ink moneylenders. Tread carefully. Karnuk's families do not forgive easily."*
>

## WAND ONE: Serpent's Fang

**Location:** Warehouse 7, Deepstone Docks

**Holder:** Krev Ironjaw (hobgoblin enforcer)

### The Scene

Warehouse 7 is controlled by Deepstone Miners. Krev is inside using the wand to intimidate a late debtor—five spectral missiles hovering around the bone wand's tip.

> *"You're three weeks late, Fennick. That's three fingers. Unless you've got the coin?"*
> 

### Retrieval Options

**Stealth & Theft**

- Roof ventilation grates (Acrobatics DC 14, Stealth DC 15)
- Wand on Krev's belt when not in use
- If caught: 6-8 hobgoblin thugs swarm

**Social Maneuvering**

- Claim to be collectors/guild reps
- Persuasion DC 16 or Deception DC 14
- Success: sells for 200gp or trades for info
- Failure: alerts crew

**Brute Force**

- Krev fights (Hobgoblin Warlord CR 6, wand attacks)
- Backup in 3 rounds (2d4 thugs)
- Grommash Stonefist remembers this

**Leverage**

- Investigation DC 13: Krev owes Marrek Goldjaw 150gp
- Buy debt, use as leverage
- Or offer to clear debt for wand

### Complication

Krev used it today—**2 charges left** instead of 4. Thalnys will notice.

---

## WAND TWO: Widow's Kiss

**Location:** Brotherhood of the Forge, Upper Terraces

**Holder:** Zethrin Ashveil (young drow artificer)

### The Scene

Zethrin works in the apprentice wing—brilliant, insecure, desperate to prove himself to master Azrael. He bought the wand thinking it was a rare surface artifact and is studying it obsessively.

The wand sits on his workbench, surrounded by notes and tools.

### Retrieval Options

**Appeal to Pride**

- Persuasion DC 14 or Insight DC 12
- Claim Azrael sent them to "evaluate progress"
- He'll show off work, opening theft/trade window
- Deception DC 16 for guild officials

**The Theft**

- Limited security (Azrael trusts apprentices)
- Sleight of Hand DC 15
- If caught, he handles it himself (avoids embarrassment)

**Trade for Information**

- Offer drow cultural knowledge or arcane secrets
- Interested in surface techniques, Fey enchantment
- Persuasion DC 14
- Morrighan's fey connection or Iblith's heritage = advantage

**Azrael's Intervention**

- Appeal to Azrael directly (via Iblith)
- He'll order surrender... but expects a favor

### Complication

Zethrin used it three times. Damaged silver filigree reduces max charges from 7 to 5. Repair needed. Thalnys will be unhappy.

### Special Encounter

If Morrighan present, Zethrin becomes fascinated by her ember-touched magic and fey resonance. Asks increasingly personal questions about her patrons and transformation.

---

## WAND THREE: Ghoststep

**Location:** Black Ink House, Deep Market

**Holder:** Silra Inkvein (drow moneylender)

### The Scene

Narrow building between foundries. Walls lined with bone ledgers—skulls carved with debts.

Silra sits behind obsidian desk, arms covered in living tattoos. Beautiful like a knife—sharp, cold, perfect. Uses Ghoststep to teleport into debtors' homes at midnight.

Wand rests in velvet case on desk, visible but clearly *hers*.

### Retrieval Options

**Become Debtors**

- She offers loans, tattoos contract on skin
- Go into debt, negotiate wand as payment
- First complete a job (collect from Deepstone-protected debtor)
- Creates moral dilemmas

**Expose Her Client**

- Investigation DC 15: she violated guild neutrality
- Used Ghoststep on Grommash's nephew (400gp debt)
- Threaten exposure unless she surrenders
- Intimidation DC 16 or Persuasion DC 17

**Iblith's Bregan Connection**

- Invoke Bregan D'aerthe ties
- She owes them smuggling favors
- Persuasion DC 14 with backing
- She'll remember this leverage

**The Heist**

- Warded (Glyph of Warding, Alarm spell)
- Arcana DC 16 detect, Thieves' Tools DC 17 disarm
- Shadow mastiff (CR 2) in back
- She's CR 5 spellcaster (Warlock)

**Buy It Back**

- 600gp asking (paid 300gp)
- Haggling DC 14 lowers to 450gp
- Thalnys reimburses but notes "lazy" choice

### Complication

Extensive use—**1 charge left**. Phasing crystal has hairline cracks. Significant repair needed. Thalnys will be furious.

### Special Encounter

Silra recognizes Morrighan's Unseelie markings. Offers "professional courtesy"—50% discount or free info about fey-touched in Karnuk. Could reveal **Seelie Court spy** operating in city.

## The Thief Revealed

After recovering two wands, Thalnys sends:

> *"The resonance trace has clarified. The thief is Vendra Quickfingers, a halfling at the Shadowcloak tent in Dockside Market. She sold my wands. She still carries my tool harness. Bring her to me. Alive."*
> 

### Finding Vendra

Cloth stall by day (enchanted fabrics). Fences stolen goods at night.

### The Confrontation

She's expecting them and terrified.

> *"I didn't know they were bonded! The guards said normal shipment! I just—my kid's sick, and healers charge more than I make in a year!"*
> 

Surrenders immediately. Hands over tool harness (enchanted thieves' tools—DC 17 becomes DC 14).

### The Choice

**Deliver to Thalnys**

- Interrogation reveals Garruk Bloodaxe hired her (Shadowed Parliament)
- Vendra "disappears"
- Full payment

**Let Her Go**

- Warn her to flee
- Thalnys livid, reduces reward 200gp
- Vendra's daughter survives
- Future underworld ally

**Negotiate**

- Persuasion DC 18 to spare Vendra
- She works as informant
- Recurring NPC

## Resolution & Rewards

### Thalnys's Reactions

**Serpent's Fang** (2/4 charges):

> *"Typical. Hobgoblins treat magic like a club. At least the bone is intact."*
> 

**Widow's Kiss** (damaged):

> *(long silence)* *"The silver work will take three days to repair. Three. Days. Tell me, was the drow apprentice still breathing when you left him?"*
> 

**Ghoststep** (1 charge, cracked):

> *(dangerously quiet)* *"This... this is nearly destroyed. This was my escape. My survival. And now it's broken."*
> 

Deep breath.

> *"But you retrieved them. That matters. Most would have run. You didn't. So."*
> 

### Payment

- **500gp** (300gp if let Vendra go, 400gp if negotiated)
- **Choice of one wand:**
    - **Serpent's Fang** (Magic Missile, 7 charges, recharges 1d6+1/dawn)
    - **Widow's Kiss** (Scorching Ray, 5 max after repair, recharges 1d4+1/dawn)
    - **Ghoststep** (Misty Step, 7 after rebuild in 2 weeks, recharges 1d6+1/dawn)

### Bonus Reward

If exceptional (all three wands minimal damage, Vendra alive, Bloodaxe intel):

> *"You've proven competent. Rare. Consider my workshop a safe haven. And if you need custom wand-work... I owe you a favor. One. Don't waste it."*
> 

Grants:

- Thalnys as permanent ally
- Workshop safe house between UTTP-Karnuk
- Future Shadow Plague research hooks
- Custom wand commissions (priority orders)

## Consequences

### Guild Fallout

- **Deepstone Miners:** Fighting Krev = Grommash remembers
- **Brotherhood of Forge:** Humiliating Zethrin/crossing Azrael = cold relations
- **Black Ink House:** Threatening/robbing Silra = marked as high-interest

### Shadowed Parliament

If Vendra revealed Bloodaxe:

> *"Bloodaxe is Shadowed Parliament. Testing my defenses. They're interested in my work. Problematic. Watch yourselves. If they're watching me, they're watching anyone I hire."*
> 

Party now on Parliament radar.

### Vendra's Gratitude

If spared, becomes recurring fence:

- 10% discount black market
- Karnuk underworld intel
- Emergency safe house

---

## DM Notes

### Tone

Noir investigation + high-stakes theft. Party are fixers, not heroes. Navigate guild politics.

### Pacing

- Session 1: Contract, investigate, plan
- Session 2: Execute retrievals
- Session 3: Confront Vendra, return, consequences

### Flexibility

Wands recoverable in any order. Difficulty escalates:

- Serpent's Fang (easiest)
- Widow's Kiss (moderate)
- Ghoststep (hardest)

### Player Agency

Reward creativity. Cannonball smashes, Iblith leverages Bregan, Morrighan charms, Bpop out-tinkers.

### Themes

- Competence vs Compassion
- Guild Politics
- Cost of Magic (wands = Thalnys's self)

## Final Scene

As party leaves:

> *"One more thing."*
> 

Thalnys draws **Nightveil's Whisper** (her first wand).

> *"If you find yourselves in a fight you can't win... use this. Once. Then return it. Consider it insurance."*
> 

Tosses darkwood wand (Shield spell, 1 charge, non-recharging).

> *"Try not to die stupidly. I dislike wasting investments."*
> 

Faintest hint of warmth. Suggestion of smile.

Mechanical spider scuttles to her shoulder.

For the first time since losing her legs, Keeper Thalnys Shardbound feels:

*Hope.*
