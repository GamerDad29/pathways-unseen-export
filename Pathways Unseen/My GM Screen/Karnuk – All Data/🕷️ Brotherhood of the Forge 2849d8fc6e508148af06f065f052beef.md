# 🕷️ Brotherhood of the Forge

## The Artists

**Race:** Drow

**Leader:** Don Azrael the Artisan

**Reputation:** Refined, aloof, brilliant artisans

**Motto:** "A family of jewel thieves"

---

## Leadership

### Don Azrael the Artisan (Master Smith, 487 years)

**Appearance:** Tall, elegant drow with silver-white hair in intricate braids. Acid scars across left cheek (failed assassination). Flowing silk robes over mithral chain. Fingers heavy with enchanted rings. Carries jeweled smithing hammer—ceremonial but deadly.

**Personality:** Refined, soft-spoken, utterly ruthless. Speaks in metaphors about art and perfection. Views violence as "crude" but necessary. Appreciates beauty in all forms—including beautiful deaths.

**Voice:** Mads Mikkelsen's Hannibal Lecter—cultured, precise, menacing.

**Motivation:** Elevate smithcraft to high art. Eliminate "lesser" artisans who sully the craft. Acquire rare materials by any means.

**Weakness:** Obsessive perfectionism. Will delay critical operations to perfect single detail.

### Underboss: Syndra Nightsteel (203 years)

**Role:** Contract acquisition, client relations, assassination coordination

**Personality:** Charming, seductive, merciless. Father's favorite.

**Method:** Poisons disguised as gifts—ornate daggers with toxin-coated hilts, jewelry releasing gas

### Capo: Zethrin Ashveil (80 years)

**Role:** Research, experimental techniques, unwitting scapegoat

**Personality:** Brilliant but insecure. Desperate to prove himself.

**Status:** Doesn't know he's being used as deniable asset

**Current:** Holds Widow's Kiss wand (stolen from Thalnys)

### Master Assassin: "The Spider"

**Role:** Wetwork, elimination, industrial espionage

**Identity:** Unknown—never seen, never speaks

**Signature:** Victims wrapped in wire, displayed as "sculptures"

## Family Culture

### Code of Conduct

- Perfection or death. No middle ground.
- Beauty justifies any act. Ugliness unforgivable.
- Loyalty to craft first, family second, self never.
- Knowledge is power—guard jealously.

### Rituals

- **Initiation** - Craft masterwork in 24 hours. Failure = exile or death.
- **"The Melting"** - Failed works melted, molten metal poured over artisan's hands (branding as inadequate)
- **Annual Culling** - Weakest apprentice killed, tools distributed to survivors

### Signs of Respect

- Present work for critique (ultimate trust)
- Use someone's personal hammer (sacred honor)
- Drow sign language complimenting technique (verbal praise is cheap)
