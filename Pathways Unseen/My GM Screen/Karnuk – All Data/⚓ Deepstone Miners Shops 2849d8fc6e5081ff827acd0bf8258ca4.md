# ⚓ Deepstone Miners Shops

## The Docks / The Kettle

Working-class establishments serving dock workers, smugglers, and laborers.

---

## 1. The Iron Anchor

**Proprietor:** Captain Vross Saltstone (hobgoblin, 41 years)

**Specialty:** Ship supplies and cargo equipment

**Services:**

- Rope and chain (1sp per 50ft, reinforced 5sp)
- Cargo nets and hooks (1-5gp)
- Barge maintenance supplies (varies)
- "Discreet" cargo coverings (2gp)

**Hook:** Vross is a Deepstone informant. Everything reaches Mother Vrekka within the hour.

---

## 2. Slagbin's Salvage

**Proprietor:** Slagbin (goblin, age unknown, possibly immortal)

**Specialty:** Salvaged goods and hidden treasures

**Services:**

- Random salvage (5sp-5gp)
- Scrap metal by weight (1sp per 10 lbs)
- "Finder's service" (5gp + item cost)
- Disposal of "evidence" (10gp)

**Hook:** Found a partial war-mech arm in magma. Won't sell it. Says it "talks to him at night."

---

## 3. The Rigger's Knot

**Proprietor:** Old Kessa (half-orc, 67 years, missing left hand)

**Specialty:** Knot-tying and climbing gear

**Services:**

- Climbing gear (harness 1gp, pitons 1sp each)
- Custom rope-work (5sp-2gp 5sp)
- Knot-tying lessons (5sp, advantage on Athletics)
- "Kessa's Guarantee" ropes (double price, magically reinforced)

**Hook:** Knows every smuggling route based on rope wear patterns.

---

## 4. The Longshoreman's Rest

**Proprietor:** Brak Hammerfist (bugbear, 29 years)

**Specialty:** Worker's gear and protective equipment

**Services:**

- Worker's gloves (5sp)
- Steel-toed boots (1gp 5sp)
- Tool belts with enchanted pockets (5gp)
- Hard hats (2gp, +1 AC vs falling objects)

**Hook:** Secretly organizing dock workers to demand better pay from Grommash.

---

## 5. The Drowned Crate

**Proprietor:** "Fingers" Molvek (ratfolk fence)

**Specialty:** Black market goods

**Services:**

- Fencing stolen goods (50% of value)
- Purchasing "lost" cargo (varies)
- Forged documentation (5-20gp)
- Smuggling arrangements (10gp+)

**Hook:** Has list of every item stolen in docks for past year. Thalnys's wands may be listed.

---

## 6. The Heavy Haul

**Proprietor:** Gorma Stoneback (dwarf, 178 years)

**Specialty:** Pack animals and transport

**Services:**

- Mule rental (5sp per day)
- Giant lizard rental (2gp per day)
- Cart rental (1gp per day)
- Transport services (5gp+)

**Hook:** Knows every route in/out of Karnuk. Helped smugglers, merchants, refugees. Never talks.
