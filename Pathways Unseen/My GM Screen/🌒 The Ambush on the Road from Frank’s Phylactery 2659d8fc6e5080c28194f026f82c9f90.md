# 🌒 The Ambush on the Road from Frank’s Phylactery

# 🎭 Player-Facing Scene Setup (Read-Aloud)

---

> The cavern road stretches out ahead, slick with condensation, its walls glistening faintly in your torchlight. The air is close, heavy with the sour musk of lichen and the faint rustle of unseen insects. For a while, the only sound has been your own footsteps and the occasional drip of water falling into unseen pools.
> 
> 
> Then it changes.
> 
> The silence deepens. No more drips, no more chittering insects — just the hollow echo of your own breathing. From the shadows of a jagged outcropping above, a boot scuffs stone. A whisper of leather. And then, shapes emerge: lean figures, weapons glinting faintly, eyes hungry. One of them grins, showing teeth filed to points. Another skitters down the wall with ratlike quickness, his nose twitching at the air.
> 
> They’ve been waiting for you.
>

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Bandit Personalities & Descriptions

### 1. **Captain Veyth “The Smile”**

- **Appearance:** A wiry human with sunken cheeks, a scar down one side of his mouth forcing a permanent sneer. His armor is mismatched, stitched together from scavenged pieces.
- **Voice:** Smooth, almost salesman-like. Words drawl with forced civility.
- **Sample Lines:**
    - *“Drop your packs, friends. We’ll be sure to put your coin to better use.”*
    - *“Oh, don’t bleed on the stones — stains are hard to scrub out.”*
    - (If wounded): *“Heh… sharper than you look. But blood’s blood, and I’ve seen enough to know it spills the same.”*

---

### 2. **Kael the Ratman**

- **Appearance:** A twisted humanoid with patchy fur, elongated incisors, and twitching whiskers. Wears a hooded cloak that reeks of damp straw. His movements are jerky, twitchy, and quick.
- **Voice:** High-pitched, nasal, words spilling out in staccato bursts.
- **Sample Lines:**
    - *“Shiny, shiny! Smell it, I do — coin, trinkets, tasty bits in your bags!”*
    - *“Cut ‘em! Cut ‘em quick before they squeal too loud!”*
    - (If bloodied): *“No! Not me, not me! Rats always escape!”*

---

### 3. **Lyris the Needle**

- **Appearance:** Half-drow woman, lean and angular. Pale eyes glow faintly in the dark, and her hair is pulled into tight braids. Wields slim daggers coated in something oily.
- **Voice:** Quiet, calm, deliberate. Never raises her voice.
- **Sample Lines:**
    - *“One step closer and I’ll bleed you clean.”*
    - *“I don’t miss twice.”*
    - (If defeated): *“Should’ve stayed in the dark… should’ve stayed hidden.”*

---

### 4. **Brokk Stonehand**

- **Appearance:** Stocky duergar with one arm encased in a crude iron gauntlet bolted to his flesh — more weapon than limb. His beard is ash-gray, and his eyes are flat and cold.
- **Voice:** Gravelly, low, each word ground out like stone on stone.
- **Sample Lines:**
    - *“Talk’s over. Pay in blood.”*
    - *“I’ve killed better folk than you, softskins.”*
    - (If injured): *“A scratch. You’ll need more than that to break stone.”*

---

## 🎁 Loot

These aren’t professional raiders — they’re scavengers who’ve been shadowing Frank’s shop. Their haul is modest, but with some flavor:

- **Captain Veyth’s Ledger:** A small leather notebook with crude tally marks of “jobs.” Final page notes: *“Watch the gnome. He smells of coin and secrets.”* (Foreshadowing Frank’s notoriety.)
- **Kael the Ratman’s Trinket Pouch:** 1d4 shiny buttons, a half-gnawed bone, and a strange black coin that feels cold to the touch (tie to Shadowed Parliament if desired).
- **Lyris’s Daggers:** A pair of slim drow-forged daggers coated in crude paralytic poison (DC 12 Con save or poisoned for 1 round).
- **Brokk’s Iron Gauntlet:** Too heavy for most, but an artificer could repurpose it. Current use: counts as a simple melee weapon (1d6 bludgeoning).
- **Shared Purse:** 22 gp, 15 sp, and a cracked amethyst worth 40 gp.

## 🎭 Encounter Flavor Beats

- The ambush isn’t about **killing** the party outright — it’s intimidation, quick profit, and survival instinct.
- Perfect for Brindle to shine:
    - **Pocket of Surprises**: smokescreen or odd trinket distracts Kael.
    - **Accidental Aid**: trips into Brokk, shoving him off-balance for a reroll save.
    - **Fearless Taunt**: mocks Veyth’s scar until the captain focuses on him instead of a squishier PC.
