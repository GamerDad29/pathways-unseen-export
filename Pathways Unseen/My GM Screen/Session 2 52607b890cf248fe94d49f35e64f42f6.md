# Session 2

[Player Version Recap](Session%202/Player%20Version%20Recap%207e1127191c51469e95936a02efcea3a0.md)

> After awakening from the night of sleep, after having an interaction with the Mysterious Stranger, **Ibilith** and **Cannonball** awake to find that Gnomenclature and Bpop are gone - without a trace. After investigation, there’s not even a shred of evidence showing that they are not here.
> 

> On the road to the Mushroom Grove, **Ibilith** and **Cannonball** come accross a very nice wagon pulled by Rothe that has a wagon wheel snapped - they are escorted by 4 Tortle Guards that belonged to the Iron Shell Mercenaries - Ibilith circled the wagon and investigated it.
> 
> 
> **Ibilith** and **Cannonball** learn that the two Hobgoblins are **Lord** and **Lady Crownjaw** of Vragathul, headed to Karnuk  
> 
> **Cannonball** offers to help and proceeds to lift the wagon - barely able to lift - Ibilith attempts to negotiate cost for their help - as **Cannonballs** strength gives away, **Ibilith** decides to help and VERY deftly slips the wheel on. As a reward, **Lady Crownjaw** listens to Cannonball tell them all about Bear Clan and then gives Cannonball blue saphire earrning 
> 
> **Lord Crownjaw** offers **Ibilith** 10 gold, he takes half 5 and other half if you ever need my services - offering him services in the name of of **Silinrule.** 
> 

> Character make it to the Mushroom Grove and learn meet **Morrigahn**. She says, “*So, you are the ones Drogon spoke of. He has sent me to guide you through these forsaken depths. This grove... it was once vibrant, full of life, but now it echoes with the finality of death—a fitting place for our meeting. Together, we shall ensure that death does not claim what it should not—or perhaps, that it does."*
> 
> 
> She gazes at each party member with her intense, gleaming eyes, assessing them before continuing.
> 
> *"The Feywild has many secrets, and I bring with me the knowledge of death’s delicate balance. I trust you understand the gravity of this mission. There is more at stake here than the lives of mere miners.”*
> 
> **Morrighan** joins the party and conveys they need to go through the crossroads to reach the miners - that’s the extent of her knowledge. 
> 
> Before the party leaves, **Ibilith** recognizes that this grove was vibrant with life when he was last here. He spends a good deal of time to look through the whole area and investigate his surroundings. In his searching the party sees him meticulously search through the area, at one point even diving to the bottom of a pool of water to discover…something. Morrighan shares some super tasty mushrooms with Cannonball as well.  
> 
> They eventually leave and head towards Murkhold Crossing
> 

> The party reaches Murkhold Crossing just as the enormous doors are going to shut - the guard slightly recognizes Ibilith and they have a shared interaction. When the party is let, in they see this intersting and odd Trading Post of Murkhold Crossing, *“A small trading outpost deep within the Underdark at the intersection of five underground tunnels, serving as a marketplace and refuge. Built from sturdy, dark stone and reinforced with scavenged metal, it has a practical appearance.”*
> 

> The party hads to the Shadowed Flask, run by Rizalia a Drow of large reputation. Gorgeous, even by Drow standards, she deftly runs this enormous Tavern/Trading-post area. This is where all of the traders and caravaners stay; it’s enormous, three stories tall and houses hundreds if neccesary. As the party walks into the bar, they are greeted by quite the site…
> 
> 
> “*"Stepping into the Shadowed Flask, you're immediately struck by the sheer volume of sound and the overwhelming diversity of its patrons. Every corner seems to tell a different story—a hobgoblin and a drow exchange heated words over a deal gone awry; a troll, wearing spectacles far too small for his broad face, mutters to himself over a stack of crumbling parchment. At the bar, multiple dragonborn in half-plate armor nurses a drink, while beside them, a tabaxi plays a soft, enchanting tune on a well-worn lute, their emerald eyes scanning the room with a mixture of curiosity and amusement.*
> 
> *"To their left, a group of heavily armored tortles—broad and imposing—sit with their backs straight and their hands never far from the hafts of their weapons, their shells painted with the unmistakable insignia of the Iron Shell Mercenaries. Their gaze sweeps across the tavern, measuring each patron as if calculating their potential threat."*
> 
> *"From behind the bar, a drow woman, her dark hair tied back in a loose braid, watches with sharp eyes. They catch her gaze for a moment; there's a flicker of recognition, but she quickly looks away, her lips curling into a knowing smile. And at the far end, in a dimly lit alcove, a gnome scribbles in a massive ledger, briefly catching eye contact with the drow the bar. The air is thick with the scent of spiced stew, mushroom ale, and the ever-present, earthy musk of the Underdark.”*
> 
> After an exhange of information and regcognition, Rizalia learns of the party’s true intentions; additionally, notes that timelines align with the last known contact with the miners. Additionally, she encourages them to speak more with Thurl, the head blacksmith of Murkhold, as that’s who Borin spoke with last. 
> 
> During this time, Cannonball was telling the tale of the Bear Clan to the Dragonborn who would drunkenly listen; additionally, the party learns that Rizalia runs Murkhold, runs the militia, has a strict 11:00 PM curfew but has given them leave to speak to Thurl and come back. The party has also paid for 2 rooms. 
> 

Upon leaving, they are confronted by 2 Hobgoblin’s who look eagerly at Morrighans familiar with a large desire to obtain it. Ibilith tells Cannonball that these Hobgoblins stole his beer, which enrages the Halfling - the Hobgoblin then makes the mistake for calling out Cannonballs size and calls him short. Battle commences. The party sees the Fae Dragonling familiar dissapear, while Ibilith ends up knocking out the first Hobgoblin. Multiple spells and actions are exchanged. Eventually one Hobgoblin calls for Roughneck, who is a LARGE Hobgoblin that runs from around the corner to engage, wherein Cannonball stands toe to..thigh with him. Morrighan flies up and hits one with Eldritch Blast, Cannonball disarms his foe and picks up his sword. Ibilith and Morrighan almost knock down the instigating Hobgoblin when a Duergar comes around the corner, slams Roughneck on the back of the legs with an oversized, enormous battlemaul - knocking it to it’s knees and follows up with an easy cross swing, splattering the brains of the large Roughneck on the wall. In rage, the final Hobgoblin shouts ROUGHNECK and runs over to confront the Duergar only to meet the flat end of the war maul, adding to the brain matter already on the wall.  

After combat subsides, Ibilith interrogates the knocked out Hobgoblin and learns they are filthy poachers, collecting exotic animals. The party sees Morrighans beautiful familiar land on the corpse of the intigation Hobgoblin, chitter out some strange noises, and then the body decomposes and in it’s place Fae Plants bloom. 

The Duergar (Thurl) invites the party to talk with them in the Workshop and session 2 ended.
