# The Shadowed Leaf Tea House

### **Exterior Description**

Nestled in the southern stretch of the Old Market, **The Shadowed Leaf** tea house stands apart from the surrounding hustle and bustle. Its architecture is a seamless blend of surface elegance and Underdark practicality. The tea house is crafted from dark-stained oak and polished stone, with delicate carvings of vines and flowers etched into its wooden beams. Softly glowing fungi hang in lantern-like enclosures, bathing the entrance in a warm, amber light. The carved sign above the door, reading "The Shadowed Leaf," is adorned with faintly shimmering runes—subtle evidence of magical protections.

The entrance is framed by a low, curved awning that shields patrons from the constant drizzle of condensation that drips from the cavern ceiling. Potted plants, a rarity in the Underdark, flank the doorway, their leaves unnaturally vibrant due to magical cultivation. The faint scent of herbs, spiced tea, and damp earth wafts through the air, inviting weary travelers and merchants alike.

---

### **Interior Description**

The moment one steps inside **The Shadowed Leaf**, the noise and chaos of the Old Market fade into a comforting hush. The air is warm and fragrant, filled with the mingling aromas of steeping tea leaves, fresh herbs, and exotic spices. A faint hum of magical wards lingers in the background, reassuring patrons that this is a safe space where secrets are kept and unwanted ears are deterred.

**Layout and Design**:

- The tea house is a single spacious chamber, divided into semi-private alcoves by carved wooden partitions adorned with surface-style floral motifs. These alcoves offer privacy for small groups or individuals, making the tea house a favored spot for discreet conversations.
- The main floor features polished stone tiles inlaid with faintly glowing geometric patterns, creating a sense of warmth and sophistication.
- A long, low counter at the back of the room serves as the tea preparation area. Behind it, shelves are lined with jars of dried leaves, crushed herbs, and small vials of infused oils. Each jar is meticulously labeled in both Common and Elvish, reflecting the tea house’s blend of surface and Underdark clientele.

**Lighting**:
The room is lit by softly glowing orbs that hover near the ceiling, their light dimmed by enchantments to create a soothing atmosphere. The glow alternates between soft amber and pale green, imbuing the space with a calming, otherworldly quality.

**Seating**:

- Seating consists of low wooden tables surrounded by cushioned chairs and plush floor mats. Each table has a small, ornate teapot resting on a heated stone centerpiece, with steam gently curling from its spout.
- Some alcoves feature curved benches with draping silk curtains for extra privacy, perfect for more secretive dealings.

---

### **Staff and Service**

- The tea house is run by **Syla Andure**, a half-elf with a serene demeanor and a sharp mind for business. Her flowing robes of deep green and gold give her an almost priestly air, though her sharp gaze betrays a mind attuned to the nuances of intrigue. Syla operates the tea house under a careful neutrality, ensuring that all customers feel safe within its walls.
- The servers are a mix of quiet, graceful attendants who glide between tables with precision. They wear simple black uniforms accented with silver embroidery, blending into the subdued elegance of the space.

---

### **Menu and Tea Selection**

The tea house boasts a selection of rare teas and beverages sourced from both surface and Underdark regions. The menu is written in flowing calligraphy on enchanted parchment that shifts between Common and Elvish.

**Popular Teas**:

1. **Moonlit Bloom**: A delicate surface tea with floral notes, infused with glowing feywild blossoms.
2. **Deepspire Brew**: A robust Underdark tea with earthy undertones, brewed from cave moss and spiced with crushed fire beetle shells.
3. **Shadowed Ember**: A smoky tea with a hint of spice, brewed from surface cinnamon bark and Underdark ember berries.
4. **Fungal Infusion**: A mildly sweet tea brewed from bioluminescent mushrooms, favored for its calming effects.
5. **Spider’s Grace**: A sharp, invigorating tea blended with Underdark herbs, rumored to sharpen focus and dull fatigue.

---

### **Ambience and Sounds**

- The tea house is always filled with a low murmur of conversation, never rising above a gentle hum. The sound of clinking porcelain and bubbling tea pots adds to the comforting atmosphere.
- In the corner, an enchanted harp plays soft, melancholic tunes, its strings plucked by unseen hands.

---

### **Unique Features**

1. **Privacy Wards**: The alcoves are enchanted with minor wards to suppress sound, ensuring conversations remain private. This makes The Shadowed Leaf a favorite spot for clandestine meetings.
2. **Message Services**: For a price, Syla offers secure delivery of messages via enchanted birds or shadowed servitors.
3. **Secret Passage**: Rumors suggest that a hidden passage beneath the tea house leads to a secure network of tunnels—ideal for a quick escape. Syla neither confirms nor denies this.
