These aren’t junky scrap piles — these are high-grade black-market warforged operatives. Their cores are worth more than half the criminals in Karnuk.

**on the body**

• Precision Arc-Bolt (1d4 shots left; deals +1 lightning)

• Ocular Targeting Lens (advantage once per day on a ranged attack)

• 14 gp worth of salvageable arc conduits

• Metal shard engraved with a timing rune (“23:17”)

• A *folded note*:

“Meet Crownjaw. No survivors. Especially the drow.”

**salvage (Int/Tools)**

• 1 Warforged Neurospine (valuable, 35 gp)

• 1 Broken Burst-Capacitor (unstable; alchemists pay 10 gp)

## **Tock (Sharpshooter #2)**

**on the body**

• A small brass clicker used for communication (click-codes)

• 9 gp mixed coin

• A chip of Embersteel Trigger assembly

**salvage (Int/Tools)**

• Memory Coil Fragment — clicking, stuttering, stores one corrupted message:

“Crownjaw… is waiting…”

## **Knifey Stabby (Knife-class Assassin)**

**on the body**

• Shadow-Blade Arm Shards (turn into daggers with 1 hour of work)

• Garrote Wire (counts as +1 for grapples requiring a tool)

• 6 gp in Syndicate markers

• Blackened matte-steel plate with a spider-thin crack glowing red

• A *hidden compartment* in his thigh containing:

• 1 vial of shadow-oil (gives +2 to a Stealth check once)

• 1 small torn scrap of parchment:

“The Parliament wants a show. Crownjaw delivers.”

**salvage (Int/Tools)**

• Shadowstep Core (can teleport 5 ft once/day when crafted into gear)

• Vocal Resonance Box (perfect mimicry of any voice heard 24 hours prior)
