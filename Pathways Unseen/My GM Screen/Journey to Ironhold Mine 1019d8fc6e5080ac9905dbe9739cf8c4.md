# Journey to Ironhold Mine

### **Six-Day Journey to the Ironhold Mine:**

### **Day 1:**

- **Travel through dark tunnels with strange echoes.** A small tremor shakes loose debris, and the ground briefly rumbles beneath their feet.
- **Minor Encounter:** A swarm of small, glowing insects that emit faint, disorienting light. They’re harmless but unnerving.

### **Day 2:**

- **Passage through a cavern filled with luminescent fungi.** Some of the fungi release spores that cause mild hallucinations. A successful **Nature** check reveals they’re non-lethal, but caution is advised.
- **Rest Spot:** The party finds a small underground stream with fresh water.

### **Day 3:**

- **Encounter with a group of deep gnomes** scavenging for rare minerals. They seem friendly but guarded, offering some information in exchange for food or small trinkets.
- **Rumor Opportunity:** The gnomes mention seeing a strange, small figure poking around—a halfling? No… smaller, with an odd confidence.

### **Day 4:**

- **Narrow tunnels force the party to move single file.** The sound of dripping water echoes endlessly, masking other noises.
- **Feral Dragonling Encounter:** The party spots the faint signs of a feral dragonling. Scales scattered on the ground and faint acid burns suggest they’re being watched.

### **Day 5:**

- **Campfire Discussions:** The party feels a sense of being watched, and they hear distant growls or snarls in the darkness.

### **Day 6:**

- **Arrival at the Ironhold Mine:** They finally reach the location, only to find it seemingly deserted or strangely quiet, with hints of recent disturbances—overturned carts, discarded tools, and signs of a skirmish.
