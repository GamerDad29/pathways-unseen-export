# 🕯️ Shadow Plague Hooks – Unified Encounter Design

## **Dinner Toll Encounter – Shadow Plague Tie-In**

### **Narrative Hook: Shallow Roots, Deep Shadows**

As the players settle into the disturbing civility of the dinner hall, faint resonances pulse through the area. Each host—**Balthier, Theryna, Maelis, and the Cook**—exhibits **subtle signs of Shadow Plague corruption**. These are not overt mutations, but **frayed mental tethers**, **borrowed behaviors**, and **unsettling echoes** that hint at something watching... or remembering. They do not know they are infected. But something in them *remembers* more than it should.

---

## **🧠 Shadow Plague Behavioral Symptoms (Dinner Host Edition)**

| **d20** | **Manifestation** |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Host finishes the PC’s sentence before they say it—then acts surprised. |
| 2 | One host pauses mid-sentence and stares at a wall, whispering, “The tether pulls…” |
| 3 | A shadow passes across the room. Only one PC sees it. It mimics their movements. |
| 4 | Theryna idly sketches a **glyph** in spilled wine—those who’ve seen plague runes recognize it. |
| 5 | Maelis hums a melody the party has only ever heard in a past dream. |
| 6 | A mirror in the personal quarters reflects the party with **slightly altered eyes**—black-veined, unknowingly foreshadowing infection. |
| 7 | The Cook mutters in a language no one speaks—but a PC *understands anyway*, as if remembering. |
| 8 | Balthier stares at a PC and says, “You wore red last time.” |
| 9 | Someone swears they hear their *own voice* arguing in the next room. No one else reacts. |
| 10 | The food seems warm even before it's served—as if it expected them. |

---

## **💀 Deeper Signs for the Observant**

- **Arcana or Medicine DC 17+**: A PC might recognize the effects as **non-magical psionic imprinting**, especially if they've studied the Shadow Plague.
- **Detect Magic**: Reveals faint aberrant auras near the base of Balthier’s cane, Theryna’s veil, and the back of Maelis’s neck.
- **Insight DC 15**: Realize their personalities seem **partially overwritten**—as if playing roles they *almost* remember being real.

---

## **📚 Lore Integration – Tying Back to Ioun and Ao**

- The **journal in the library** contains one page that *rewrites itself* in the player’s handwriting. It speaks of the "thread pulled too far" and "a god of secrets who forgot silence."
- The **haunted buckler** whispers: “They watched us dine. They watched us rot.”
- The **memory sap vial** grants a brief flash of the *Cook’s past*: he once stood beneath an alien sky, served something not quite a god, and was *told what flavor time has*.

---

## **🌒 Optional Table: Increasing Influence Triggers (Narrative Layer)**

If the players *spend too long* or **consume any food or drink**, roll once per PC:

| **d4** | **Effect** |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | A sudden memory of a place they’ve never been—and yet *long for*. |
| 2 | Their voice echoes with a second, delayed version. |
| 3 | The room tilts briefly, and a shadow crosses their vision—upside down. |
| 4 | They feel their thoughts *watched*, like a diary page being flipped. |

---

## **🧬 DM Notes: Shadow Plague Progression Marker**

If a PC fails **two or more Wisdom saves** in this lair or consumes food freely:

- Apply the **first symptom** from the Shadow Plague track:
    - *Paranoia and the feeling of being watched.*
- Use whispered cues: “The curtain is not meant to move like that.” or “You blinked, but someone else saw it.”

## **🧝‍♀️ Morrighan’s Fey Dragon Insight**

When Morrighan’s Fey Dragon lands upon one of the hosts, a wave of shimmering threads of light and illusion spreads around the contact point. The PC sees a rapid sequence of foreign memories:

### **Fey Dragon Vision: Flashbacks to the Dark Sun Planet**

**1.** A red, dying sun scorches a bone-white desert where screaming winds whisper names not spoken in centuries.

**2.** A humanoid kneels before a ziggurat carved into obsidian, surrounded by psionic flares that twist the sand around it like flame.

**3.** A banquet where none eat—only watch. Their eyes leak salt, and their mouths move in silent prayers to an unseen star.

**4.** The Cook, younger, bares a third eye stitched shut upon his forehead, preparing a dish while a voice from nowhere dictates flavor combinations that echo with truth.

**5.** A city fallen to memory—its streets walked by echoes. A woman who could be Maelis dances in the plaza, turning slowly in the shadow of something *alive* and *immobile*.

## **🗣️ Dialogue Triggers – Revealing Shadow Plague Threads**

Use these responses if players press with Insight, Persuasion, or Arcana:

- **Balthier** (pressed about his past):
    
    *"I was... a guest, once. At another table. Under a darker sky."*
    
- **Theryna** (when asked about her perfume):
    
    *"It's bottled silence. Caught from the breath of the last memory I owned."*
    
- **Maelis** (when shown a symbol from Athas):
    
    *"They marked me to remember... but I’ve forgotten what."*
    
- **The Cook** (if anyone enters the kitchen uninvited):
    
    *"You smell like freedom. You’ll taste like memory."*
    
- **Any Host, if questioned about the food**:
    
    *"It’s not meant to nourish. It’s meant to bind."*
