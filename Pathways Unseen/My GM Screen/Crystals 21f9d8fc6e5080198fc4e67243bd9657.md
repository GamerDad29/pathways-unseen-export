## **Resonant Heart Crystal (Large) – Advanced Study Outcomes**

1. **Crystal Echo Spell**
    
    *Once per long rest, you may copy a 1st–3rd level spell cast by another creature within 60 feet and store it. You may cast it once using your spellcasting ability, without components.*
    
2. **Ley Pulse Alignment**
    
    *While attuned and standing on a ley line nexus (DM-defined), you regain 1 expended spell slot of 3rd level or lower during each short rest.*
    
3. **Sentient Core Unveiled**
    
    *The crystal has a dormant sentience awakened through attunement. It communicates in flashes of color and intent, offering advantage on Arcana checks related to ancient magic.*
    
4. **Arcane Vein Amplifier**
    
    *Spells that deal force, radiant, or thunder damage deal +2 damage while the crystal is used as a focus. The crystal glows more intensely after each use (may attract attention).*
    
5. **Resonant Shield**
    
    *As a reaction to taking magical damage, you can activate the crystal to gain resistance to that damage type until the end of your next turn. Usable once per long rest.*

## **Flicker Shards (Medium) – Advanced Study Outcomes**

1. **Spellwarp Trigger**
    
    *Implant one shard in a magical trap or item to allow it to trigger under alternate conditions (e.g., based on alignment, time of day, or presence of a specific spell).*
    
2. **Phase Blinker**
    
    *Crushing the shard as a bonus action grants you the effect of blur or misty step (choose one on use), once per shard.*
    
3. **Fractured Memory Sync**
    
    *When meditated with during a long rest, the shard grants a single use of detect thoughts or mirror image the following day.*
    
4. **Crystal Grenade**
    
    *With successful alchemical treatment (Arcana or Alchemist’s Supplies DC 18), the shard can be converted into a thrown weapon that casts shatter (DC 13) once.*
    
5. **Whisper Echo Node**
    
    *Planting the shard allows it to transmit whispers magically up to 1 mile to another tuned shard. Requires synchronization ritual (Arcana DC 20).*

## **Spellgleam Dust (Small) – Advanced Study Outcomes**

1. **Flashcast Residue**
    
    *When sprinkled on a scroll or page of a spellbook and activated, the spell can be cast as a reaction once within 24 hours.*
    
2. **Spell Signature Distiller**
    
    *Proper infusion into arcane ink (Arcana DC 18) creates "Signature Ink" – reveals the school of magic, creator, or last user of a spell scroll or magical inscription.*
    
3. **Lens of Truth and Madness**
    
    *Inhaling a dust-mixed potion or tea grants true seeing for 1 minute and induces a vivid hallucination or vision from a crystal-aligned plane.*
    
4. **Chrono-Slip Spark**
    
    *Using the dust in a magical ritual (1 hour) allows a caster to delay the effect of a 1st–2nd level spell by up to 1 minute.*
    
5. **Ambient Flux Trigger**
    
    *Embedding dust in a weapon, it emits a burst of blinding arcane light the first time the weapon hits a creature—forces a DC 14 Con save or blinds for 1 round (once per day).*

### **Resonant Heart Crystal (Large)**

These are the miracles whispered only to those who listen longest:

- **Echo Spell Core**
    
    *The crystal remembers. It can mimic spells it has witnessed, once letting you cast one without words or components.*
    
- **Pulse-Bound Nexus**
    
    *Align it with a ley line. It will feed your strength with every moment of focused stillness.*
    
- **Awakened Sentience**
    
    *The crystal stirs. It knows you, offers its insight in color and hum. It remembers more than you.*
    
- **Arcane Vein Amplifier**
    
    *Magic courses cleaner through it. Certain energies strike with deeper force.*
    
- **Resonant Shield**
    
    *When danger strikes, the crystal thrums in warning, deflecting the blow with harmonic energy.*

Fragments of the song, still potent when properly tuned:

- **Phase Blink**

- **Whisper Node**
    
    *With care, link two shards across distance. They’ll whisper secrets carried by wind and weave.*

### **Crystalline Study Results – d20 Table**

Roll during a week of focused study. Multiple weeks may allow rerolls or advantage. Modify with Intelligence (Arcana) or relevant tools.

```
1–5    | The crystal’s hum is mesmerizing, but no effect is discerned.
6–9    | Gain minor insight: +1 to your next Arcana check related to the item.
10–14  | Partial resonance: You activate a weaker or once-only version of one benefit.
15–17  | You uncover one full effect, but it may be unstable or conditional.
18–19  | The crystal yields a true secret—choose one of the listed full effects.
20     | Critical Insight: The crystal grants one effect and reveals a hidden secondary benefit or prophecy.

```
