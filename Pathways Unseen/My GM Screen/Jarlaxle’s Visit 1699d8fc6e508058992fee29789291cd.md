# Jarlaxle’s Visit

### **Jarlaxle’s Appearance in Iblith’s Room**

---

**Scene Description:**

The flickering light of a single lantern casts long shadows in Iblith’s modest room. The faint hum of the Underdark’s ambient life is muffled here, leaving only the soft creak of the wooden bedframe and the occasional rustle of fabric. As Iblith settles in, a faint chill brushes the back of his neck—a presence that wasn’t there before.

**Jarlaxle’s Entrance:**

(*Leaning casually against the far wall, Jarlaxle Baenre stands, his wide-brimmed hat tilted just enough to cast a shadow over one eye. His grin is both charming and unreadable, the kind of expression that promises mischief but hides deeper intentions.*)

**Jarlaxle**:

“Well, my friend, it seems you’ve been quite the busy shadow. How fares my favorite monk of mystery?”

(*He steps forward, his polished boots making no sound against the floor. His gaze, sharp and calculating, softens just enough to convey genuine curiosity.*)

**Jarlaxle**:

“I don’t ask for reports, mind you. This isn’t business—well, not entirely. It’s rare that I find myself concerned about anyone beyond their usefulness, but you, Iblith... you’ve earned something more.”

(*He pauses, as if weighing his words.*)

“How are you holding up? Truly. You’ve been through more than most, and even the best of us need someone to lean on now and then. Think of this visit as a rare act of sentimentality from a man who rarely affords himself such luxuries.”

(*After allowing Iblith to respond, Jarlaxle’s grin widens, and he claps his hands together lightly.*)

**Jarlaxle**:

“Now, to the matter at hand. You’ll find a bounty on this fellow—one who calls himself the Smiling Knight.”

(*He produces a parchment from within his coat and hands it to Iblith. The image sketched upon it is of a sinister-looking man with a perpetual smirk etched into his face.*)

“A right unpleasant sort, this one. He’s caused more chaos than I care to recount, and I suspect he’s hiding somewhere in these parts. But that’s not all...”

(*Jarlaxle pulls out another slip of parchment, this one bearing the likeness of a rugged human man with piercing eyes and a stern expression.*)

“This is his associate, a ranger of no small skill. Goes by the name **Kellan Duskbane**. He’s clever, resourceful, and a thorn in the side of anyone who opposes the Knight. I suspect they’re traveling together, though Duskbane’s presence alone would be enough to warrant action.”

(*Jarlaxle’s tone shifts slightly, becoming more serious.*)

“Take this bounty, Iblith. But do it your way. No need for grand shows or unnecessary risks. Bring me their heads—or, at the very least, proof of their undoing—and you’ll find my appreciation more than generous.”

(*He adjusts his hat, his grin returning.*)

“Besides, I wouldn’t want you thinking I’ve forgotten how invaluable you are to me. Consider this not just a task, but an opportunity to remind these fools why the name Iblith carries weight in both the shadows and the light.”

(*With that, he tips his hat and vanishes into the shadows, leaving behind a faint scent of exotic cologne and the bounties in Iblith’s hand.*)

### **Details of the Bounties**

**Smiling Knight**

- **Class**: Fighter (Champion), Level 6
- **Description**: A cruel and cunning mercenary who earned his moniker through his unnerving smile, often plastered on his face even in the midst of battle. The Smiling Knight is known for ambushing targets and using overwhelming brute force to achieve his goals.
- **Location**: Suspected to be hiding in the ruins near Uttp, where he’s likely seeking allies for further disruption of the alliance.\

### **Kellan Duskbane**

- **Class**: Warrior (Level 4)
- **Description**: A human mercenary with sharp instincts and a disciplined approach to combat. Duskbane is a methodical fighter, excelling in small-unit tactics and close-quarters engagements. While fiercely loyal to the Smiling Knight, he is driven more by practicality than ideology and is not above abandoning the cause if he sees no path to victory.
- **Abilities and Combat Style**:
    - **Tactical Precision**: Duskbane employs strategic positioning and defensive maneuvers to outlast his opponents. His attacks are deliberate, aiming to exploit weaknesses.
    - **Unyielding Resolve**: Once per encounter, he can rally himself, gaining temporary hit points equal to his level (4).
    - **Shield Expertise**: Wields a shield with practiced efficiency, granting him superior defense while maintaining the ability to strike decisively with his longsword.
    - **Combat Maneuvers**: Uses well-honed techniques such as disarming strikes and shield bashes to disrupt his opponents’ tactics.
- **Equipment**:
    - **Longsword**: His weapon of choice, kept razor-sharp and polished.
    - **Steel Shield**: Engraved with the crest of a long-forgotten mercenary company, a relic of his early days.
    - **Reinforced Chainmail**: Provides solid protection without sacrificing mobility.
- **Location**:
    
    Duskbane is believed to be operating as the Smiling Knight’s advance scout, often establishing defensive positions or coordinating ambushes. His last known location places him near the ruins of a long-abandoned outpost on the outskirts of Uttp, where he is likely preparing for a confrontation.
