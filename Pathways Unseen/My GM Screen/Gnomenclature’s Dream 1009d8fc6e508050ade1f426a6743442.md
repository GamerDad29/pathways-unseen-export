# Gnomenclature’s Dream

*In your dream, you stand alone in a vast, dark expanse. A cold wind blows, whispering secrets you cannot quite hear. You look down and see a new tattoo etched across your chest, a black throne bound by chains that seem to pulse with a dim, malevolent light. The chains extend out into the darkness, tethering you to some unseen force.*

*The voice is faint at first, but it grows stronger, insistent:*

*"The Chain binds all… and it breaks none."*

*You try to move, but the chains tighten, pulling against your skin. The ground beneath you shudders, and a low growl rumbles through the air—a sound of something ancient waking. You see figures moving in the shadows, eyes gleaming like embers, watching you. The throne pulses, and you feel a pull towards it, a compulsion to sit upon it… to claim it… or to resist.*

*You awaken with a jolt, sweat beading on your forehead. The room feels colder, the darkness deeper. And there, on your sternum, the tattoo glows faintly in the dim light of the Shadowed Flask.*
