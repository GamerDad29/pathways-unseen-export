# Combined Encounter Table: Journey from Borin's Stash to Iron Hallow Mine

1. **Goblin Scavengers:** The party is ambushed by a small group of goblins scavenging the tunnels for supplies. (3 Goblins, AC 15, HP 7)
2. **Friendly Fungus Patch:** The party comes across a patch of bioluminescent fungi that light their path. Some of the mushrooms can be harvested and used as light sources or alchemical ingredients.
3. **Mysterious Traveler:** A cloaked figure passes the party without a word. If the party engages, the traveler reveals cryptic knowledge about the mine and the dangers within.
4. **Rockfall:** A minor earthquake shakes the tunnel, causing rocks to fall. Characters must make Dexterity saves to avoid taking damage.
5. **Hidden Shrine:** The party stumbles upon an ancient, forgotten shrine. Offering something to the shrine grants a minor boon (1d4 temporary HP).
6. **Underdark Wildlife:** The party encounters a herd of cave lizards, non-hostile but curious, watching the party pass.
7. **Strange Artifact:** Hidden in a crevice is an old dwarven artifact. Arcana checks (DC 12) reveal that it was used in ancient mining rituals.
8. **Drow Scouts:** A group of drow scouts passes the party. They are more interested in their own mission, but if approached aggressively, they will fight.
9. **Lost Dwarven Miner:** The party encounters a delirious dwarf who claims to have escaped the mine. He offers cryptic warnings about what lies ahead.
10. **Collapsed Tunnel:** A tunnel collapse blocks the way. The party can work together or use spells to clear the debris.
11. **Underground Lake:** The party comes upon an underground lake, serene and illuminated by glowing crystals. If the water is disturbed, creatures from the depths may emerge.
12. **Feral Dragonling:** The party finds evidence of a small feral dragonling in the area. Tracks and acid burns suggest it’s nearby.
13. **Echoing Voices:** Ghostly echoes of the lost miners can be heard, leading to eerie silence. Insight checks (DC 13) reveal they are illusions.
14. **Old Battle Remnants:** The party finds an old battleground with skeletal remains. Investigating provides clues to past conflicts between the miners and something far worse.
15. **Underground Nomads:** The party encounters a group of Underdark nomads. They offer information about nearby dangers in exchange for food or supplies.
16. **Ambush by Duergar Raiders:** A group of duergar raiders lie in wait. Combat is likely unless the party successfully negotiates.
17. **Runic Door:** The party finds an old, dwarven door sealed with a magical rune. A successful Arcana or History check (DC 15) will reveal its purpose and how to unlock it.
18. **Cursed Relic:** The party stumbles upon a cursed item from the mine that summons malevolent spirits. They must either break the curse or fight the spirits.
19. **Mysterious Fog:** A thick fog rolls in, disorienting the party. Inside the fog, shadowy figures move silently but do not engage unless provoked.
20. **The Zone:** The party crosses into an area connected to one of their prologue quests, finding clues or items directly tied to their personal stories.
