## ⚙️ Crafting Framework

### Requirements

- Proficiency with Alchemist’s Supplies (or similar tools)
- Access to a workshop or portable lab (even a makeshift one counts, but risky)
- Ingredients equal to the potion’s rarity or quality tier
- 1–4 hours of crafting time depending on complexity

### The Roll

Roll **1d20 + Intelligence modifier + proficiency bonus**.

Add **advantage** if working in ideal conditions, **disadvantage** if rushed or using unstable ingredients.

Cross-reference the result on the Alchemy Success Table below.

## 🧩 Ingredient Mechanics

### Basic Components

Every potion requires **3 categories of ingredients**:

- **Catalyst** – activates the reaction (minerals, powders, alchemical salts)
- **Essence** – defines the potion’s core (blood, venom, herbs, crystals)
- **Binder** – stabilizes the effect (fungal oils, resins, distilled spores)

The rarer or more exotic the source, the higher the potential DC and payout.

## 🧠 Crafting Difficulty

| Potion Type | DC | Base Time | Typical Cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Common | 10 | 1 hour | 25 gp |
| Uncommon | 13 | 2 hours | 50 gp |
| Rare | 16 | 4 hours | 200 gp |
| Very Rare | 18 | 8 hours | 500 gp |
| Legendary / Masterwork | 20+ | 1 day | 1,000 gp+ |

**Add +2 to the DC if:**

- Working without proper lab space.
- Substituting one or more ingredients.
- Attempting multiple potions simultaneously.

## 🧯 Optional: Mishap System

*(if you want the risk back)*

Even with success guaranteed, Iblith’s experiments are never without danger.

If a player rolls below 5 or fails a concentration check mid-craft, roll a **d6 Alchemical Mishap**:

| d6 | Mishap |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | The mixture combusts, dealing 1d6 fire damage in a 5-ft radius. |
| 2 | The fumes cause mild hallucinations — disadvantage on Insight for 1 hour. |
| 3 | The brew spoils but forms a 1d4 flask of weak acid instead. |
| 4 | Potion’s scent is addictive; DC 10 Wisdom save or drink it immediately. |
| 5 | Sudden pressure surge — flask explodes and destroys nearby notes. |
| 6 | The result mutates — color shifts, scent alluring; treat as a random potion effect for 24 hours. |

## 🧴 Examples in Play

**Example 1: Iblith crafts a Healing Potion (DC 13)**

Rolls 16 total → *Enhanced Mixture*

Potion heals 2d4 + 2 as normal, plus +1d4 bonus healing.

The liquid glows faint blue when held near wounds.

**Example 2: Crit Success – Masterwork (20+)**

Iblith crafts a Greater Healing Potion → rolls 21.

Result: *Purifying Draft.*

Heals 4d4 + 4 HP and cures one poison or disease. The liquid fizzes like silver foam.

## 🧬 Alchemy in the World of Karnuk

Alchemy is a gray-market science — part guild craft, part outlaw trade.

- **Deepstone Miners:** use alchemy for industrial solvents and explosives.
- **Steel Syndicate:** weaponizes Embersteel reagents for augmenting mechs.
- **Brotherhood of the Forge:** guards recipes for aesthetic potions (Feyglass Elixirs).
- **Bregan D’aerthe:** traffics antidotes, toxins, and nerve agents through the black market.
