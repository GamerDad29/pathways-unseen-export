# Quest Overview - Old City Investigation

**Primary Objective:**

- Discover what's happening in the abandoned sections beneath Karnuk
- Find out who killed Crownjaw and why (ritualistic murder)
- Stop whatever is causing the escalating chaos before it destabilizes the city

**What Vorgoth Knows/Suspects:**

- Fresh activity in sealed sections (tracks, blood trails)
- Spider silk strands leading deeper
- Ritualistic symbols at murder scene (unprecedented in modern Karnuk)
- Timing coincides with party's arrival (suspicious but maybe unrelated)

**What's Actually Happening (that he doesn't know):**

- Lolth worship has returned/reawakened
- Old City isn't empty—something is organizing down there
- Crownjaw's murder was ritual/religious, not gang violence
- Shadow Syndicate connections to the depths

**Success Conditions:**

- Return with concrete answers about what's in the Old City
- Identify the killers/cult/organization responsible
- Provide actionable intel to stop further attacks

**Entry Points (player choice):**

- Old merchant lift (warehouse district) - closest to Crownjaw's murder
- Collapsed temple entrance (Twilight Market)
- Maintenance tunnels (civic reservoir)

**Stakes:**

- City on edge of panic
- Steel Syndicate demanding blood
- Political pressure crushing Vorgoth
- Party gets cleared if they succeed, rot in prison if they fail
