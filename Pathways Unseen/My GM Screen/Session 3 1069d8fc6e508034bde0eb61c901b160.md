# Session 3

- *Session Recap: "A Clicking Dread…"**

- *Opening Scene: Bpop's Unplanned Acrobatics**

The session opened with Bpop awakening in a dark, dusty room, its door locked from the inside. Muffled shouts and the clang of steel echoed from the alley below. Pushing open the creaky window, Bpop spotted his companions battling in the streets. With a daring leap, he tried to descend but slipped—a natural one—tumbling down the awning. Cannonball caught him, but Bpop’s pet, following close behind, crashed into them both, knocking a point of damage into the kobold.

- *The Blacksmith's Workshop: Tensions Rise and Clues Uncovered**

After regrouping, the party ventured into the workshop of Thurl, the master blacksmith. Tense negotiations followed as the grizzled Duergar provided information about Borin's mining operations and his last sighting. A few well-placed persuasion rolls kept Thurl’s hand from gripping his war maul too tightly. The party learned of a hidden stash halfway between Iron Hollow Mine and Murkhold Crossing—a location worth investigating. Additionally, they learned about something called a Feral Dragonling... During and after the conversation, Thurl gave Cannonball some more liquor and Bpop a box of springs. When the group investigated the room where Bpop had awoken, they found layers of dust so thick it was evident the room hadn’t been used in years.

- *Gathering Supplies and Marketplace Encounters**

After a long rest, the next day saw the party at Drusk's Healing Hut, Iblith bartering gold and rare ingredients for potions and alchemical supplies. Bpop managed to secure a pearl amidst the dealings. They asked Drusk some questions about a human treated for wounds... the best info came about a clicking noise made before they sprang… From there, they wandered into the bustling marketplace, where they were swiftly ejected from a gnome crystal tent after Morrighan spotted a Fey Crystal and tensions spiked. They encountered an unsightly trader with goods and exorbitant prices before finally setting out on their journey.

- *The Journey to Borin's Stash**
- **Day One:** A minor tremor shook the ground, but they reached their campsite safely.
- **Day Two:** Passed uneventfully, a momentary calm before the storm.
- **Day Three:** They crossed paths with a group of deep gnomes, drunk and gossiping. One gnome shared rumors of a strange, small humanoid creature—*"Not a child, nor any race known to us. Something like nothing we’ve ever seen,"* they muttered.

Sensing they were near Borin's hidden stash, the party allowed the gnomes to move on, using the key to open a secret dwarven door, given to them by Thurl. The tunnel opened up, and they advanced cautiously.

- *Battle with the Feral Dragonlings**

During their careful investigation of the stash, Cannonball's sudden sneeze shattered the quiet, unknowingly alerting lurking threats. As they explored, Morrighan heard a clicking noise—a nat 20 perception. They prepared themselves, weapons drawn.

Three feral black dragonlings sprang from the shadows. A tense battle unfolded, with the creatures adapting and growing stronger at certain points, their scales darkening, hackles rising, and breaths acidic. Quick-thinking by Iblith, swift blows from Cannonball, a mental blast from Morrighan, and coordinated attacks from Bpop and his pet eventually brought down the foes. Among the highlights, Cannonball slapped a spit of acid back at the dragonling with the flat of his axe.

- *Discovering Borin’s Secrets**

Victorious, the party searched the stash and uncovered Borin’s hidden office and personal effects—a letter, manifests, and clues hinting at unrest among the surface races and clandestine meetings. They also discovered a special compass that seemed to point towards safe havens known only to Borin, a pristine dwarven mining pick etched with ancient runes, and several other intriguing artifacts.

- *Closing Thoughts**

The session ended with the party contemplating their next steps, the mysteries deepening around them.
