Info to Jared abotu Cogmind Collective 

The **Cogmind Collective** is a **neutral faction of master artisans**, known for creating **Warforged**—sentient constructs designed for combat, espionage, and protection. They are essentially the **arms dealers** of both the surface world and the Underdark, selling their creations to whoever can afford them, without allegiance to any side. They exist in a grey space, where **profit** and **craftsmanship** are their only true loyalties.

Though they remain **elusive**, their influence stretches far. Both surface rulers and Underdark factions seek their work, recognizing the unmatched quality of their Warforged soldiers and scouts. Each unit is a blend of **magic and mechanical ingenuity**, designed to adapt to any situation and, if necessary, to **self-destruct** before their technology can be studied by others.

The **Cogmind Collective** takes no sides in the conflict between the surface and the Underdark. They are **artisans and arms dealers**, playing both sides, with their ultimate goal being the perfection of their craft. They care little for politics or power—only in refining their creations and testing their limits in the field of battle.

The Warforged you faced bore their mark, the **sigil of the Cogmind Collective**, signifying their origin. And if these constructs are active this deep in the Underdark, it means someone, either on the surface or down below, is making moves—and they’re willing to pay a high price for the best soldiers money can buy.
