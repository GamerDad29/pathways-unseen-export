# Gorunn Stonebreaker

### **Gorunn Stonebreaker**

### **Description** (DM Version):

Gorunn Stonebreaker stands as a living embodiment of dwarven resilience and martial discipline. His stout frame, clad in battered but meticulously maintained armor, exudes an air of seasoned authority. A thick, gray-streaked beard falls to his chest, braided with small metal rings that gleam faintly in the light. His eyes, sharp and piercing as a whetted axe, seem to scrutinize everyone and everything with unwavering vigilance. Scars crisscross his weathered face, each a testament to a life spent in the unforgiving depths of the Underdark. At his side rests a finely crafted warhammer, the haft worn smooth from years of use, and a round shield adorned with the insignia of his clan.

### **Tone**:

- Gruff, pragmatic, and direct.
- No patience for pleasantries or long-winded speeches.
- Commands respect through sheer presence and gravitas.

---

### **Interaction Setup** (DM Version):

**Location:** Gorunn’s office is stark and utilitarian, reflecting his no-nonsense nature. The walls are lined with maps of the trading post and surrounding Underdark tunnels, dotted with strategic notations and troop placements. A large, stone table dominates the room, cluttered with reports, battle plans, and a well-worn dagger used as a paperweight. Torches set into iron sconces cast flickering light, creating deep shadows that play across the cold stone walls.

---

### **Interaction (DM to Player)**:

Gorunn doesn’t rise as the party enters. He leans over the stone table, studying a map with the intensity of a predator stalking its prey. Without looking up, he grunts, “Close the door behind you. Don’t need your chatter echoing through the halls.”

Once the door is shut, he looks up, his gaze like steel meeting flesh. “So, Elandra sent you, eh? Told me you’re here to lend a hand.” He straightens, crossing his arms over his chest. “Neutrality might be her dream, but it’s a damn fragile one. A single crack, and this whole place collapses into chaos. So tell me…” His voice lowers, a growl in the dark. “…what makes you think you’re cut out to help keep it standing?”

---

### **Potential Responses from Gorunn**:

1. **If the players show military or tactical knowledge**:“Good. At least you’ve got some sense for strategy. But words mean little here—show me you can act when it counts.”
2. **If the players appeal to unity or diplomacy**:“Diplomacy? Hmph. Fine words, but diplomacy won’t stop a blade in the dark. If you’re serious, you’ll have to prove you’re more than just talk.”
3. **If the players mention Elandra’s trust**:“Elandra’s got a good head on her shoulders, but trust doesn’t come cheap with me. You’ll earn it with actions, not words.”
4. **If the players hesitate or falter**:“Hesitation gets people killed. If you’re unsure now, you’re not ready for what’s coming.”

---

### **Gorunn’s Motives**:

- Protecting the Upper Tunnels Trading Post at all costs.
- Ensuring the party is trustworthy and capable of handling the threat of the Deep Market Syndicate.
- Supporting Elandra’s vision of neutrality, but only if it doesn’t compromise the trading post’s security.

### **Gorunn Stonebreaker’s Role in the Quest**

### **Ways Gorunn Can Help the Players**:

1. **Military Strategy and Tactical Support**:
    - Gorunn can provide the party with detailed maps of the Upper Tunnels Trading Post and nearby regions, marking known Syndicate hideouts, vulnerable chokepoints, and areas of heavy activity.
    - He can recommend tactics for infiltrating Syndicate-controlled areas, based on his extensive combat experience.
    - Gorunn may assign a small contingent of guards or mercenaries to aid the players during dangerous missions, but only if they prove their worth first.
2. **Resources and Equipment**:
    - Offers the party access to the trading post’s armory, including enchanted weapons or items confiscated from Syndicate operatives.
    - Can loan the party durable supplies like healing potions, anti-poison remedies, or tools for breaking through Syndicate defenses.
3. **Interrogation of Captured Syndicate Operatives**:
    - If the players capture Syndicate members, Gorunn can interrogate them personally. His blunt, no-nonsense demeanor makes him skilled at extracting information.
4. **Syndicate Weakness Knowledge**:
    - Gorunn shares intelligence on Syndicate tactics, such as their reliance on ambushes, misinformation, or bribes to maintain control.
    - Warns the party about the Syndicate’s use of human surface hunters and spies to sow distrust among the trading post’s residents.
5. **Direct Assistance**:
    - If the situation escalates, Gorunn might personally lead a strike team with the players, targeting a Syndicate stronghold.
    - His combat skills as a Battle Master (e.g., tactical maneuvers) can provide crucial advantages during a major fight.

### **Motivations for Helping**:

- **Loyalty to the Trading Post**: Gorunn will do anything to protect the Upper Tunnels Trading Post and its people from external and internal threats.
- **Proving the Syndicate’s True Threat**: Gorunn may initially be skeptical of the Syndicate's reach. If the party brings him hard evidence of their infiltration, he will take decisive action.
- **Alliance with Elandra**: While Gorunn respects Elandra’s leadership, he views her idealism as a weakness. Helping the party succeed allows him to ensure that her vision of neutrality is upheld without jeopardizing the trading post’s safety.

### Gorunn Stonebreaker’s Assistance Roll Table (1d20)

**1–5: Skeptical Assistance**

- Gorunn reluctantly provides basic assistance, still unsure of the party’s capabilities.
    - Aid:
        - A rough, outdated map of the trading post with Syndicate-controlled areas circled.
        - One healing potion from the guards’ reserve.
    - Quote:“Don’t think for a second this means I trust you. You’re on your own until you show me what you’re made of.”

---

**6–10: Cautious Help**

- Gorunn provides more practical support, though he remains wary.
    - Aid:
        - A detailed map of the Syndicate's suspected routes and hideouts.
        - 1 vial of anti-toxin and access to basic gear (simple weapons, torches, rations).
        - A list of potential Syndicate informants operating in the trading post.
    - Quote:“Here’s what I know. Use it wisely, and maybe we’ll talk about real support later.”

---

**11–15: Cooperative Support**

- Gorunn sees the party’s potential and begins to actively support their efforts.
    - Aid:
        - Assigns 2 guards (AC 16, HP 18) to assist in a mission.
        - Supplies 2 healing potions and 1 +1 weapon or shield.
        - Provides strategic advice on attacking a Syndicate stronghold, highlighting vulnerabilities.
    - Quote:“Alright, you’ve earned some trust. My men and I will back you up—for now.”

---

**16–19: Invested Partnership**

- Gorunn commits significant resources to the party’s efforts and takes a personal stake in their success.
    - Aid:
        - Gorunn personally joins the party on a mission as an NPC ally (Level 6 Battle Master).
        - Brings 2 elite guards (AC 17, HP 22, armed with +1 gear).
        - Grants access to high-quality armory equipment (e.g., +1 weapons, climbing gear).
    - Quote:“If we’re doing this, we’re doing it my way. Stay sharp, and don’t waste my time.”

---

**20: Full Commitment**

- Gorunn becomes fully invested in the party’s mission, dedicating the trading post’s military resources to their cause.
    - Aid:
        - Gorunn leads an elite strike team of 4 guards (AC 17, HP 30) into battle alongside the party.
        - Full access to the trading post’s advanced armory, including rare magical items.
        - Assigns additional scouts to track Syndicate movements in real-time.
    - Quote:“You’ve proven yourself more than capable. Now, let’s show these Syndicate bastards what real warriors look like.”
