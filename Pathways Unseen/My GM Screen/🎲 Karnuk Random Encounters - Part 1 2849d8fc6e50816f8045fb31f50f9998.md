# 🎲 Karnuk Random Encounters - Part 1

Street encounters organized by district, time of day, and escalation level. Roll d20 or pick based on situation.

---

## How to Use

**1. Determine District** (where is the party?)

**2. Check Time** (day or night?)

**3. Roll Encounter** (d20 on appropriate table)

**4. Add Complications** (optional, for spice)

**Escalation Scale:**

- 🟢 **Peaceful** - Flavor, information, shopping
- 🟡 **Tense** - Potential conflict, decisions matter
- 🔴 **Violent** - Combat likely or unavoidable
