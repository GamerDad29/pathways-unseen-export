# Footpath 2

### **Footpath 2: D8 Roll Table**

1. **Magical Phylactery Exposure:** The party finds a narrow tunnel branching off the main path. At the end of it, they discover a sealed chamber containing a **Magical Phylactery holding cell**. The room is ancient and the walls covered in intricate runes, pulsing faintly with magical energy.
2. **Stalagmites Ahead:** A forest of stalagmites blocks the path, forcing the party to carefully navigate around them.
3. **Underground River:** The party hears the distant rush of water. Upon investigation, they discover a small underground stream flowing alongside the path.
4. **Luminous Fungus:** Brightly glowing fungus lines the walls of the tunnel, lighting the way for the party.
5. **Crumbled Path:** A section of the path has crumbled away. The party must make a slight detour but avoids danger.
6. **Hidden Cache:** The party stumbles upon a hidden stash of mundane supplies, likely left by a previous traveler. Some food and water, a bedroll, and a simple dagger are found.
7. **Subterranean Wind:** A gust of wind rushes through the tunnel, cooling the air and giving the party an eerie feeling of being watched.
8. **Faint Runes:** The party notices faint dwarven runes etched into the walls. They tell of safe passage and warn of dangerous creatures deeper ahead.
