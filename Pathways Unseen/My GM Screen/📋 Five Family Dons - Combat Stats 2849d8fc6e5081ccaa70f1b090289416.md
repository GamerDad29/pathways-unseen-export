Complete combat stats for the Five Family leadership.

## Don Thorgar Ironhammer (Iron Hammer Clan)

**Level 12 Champion Fighter**

**AC:** 20 | **HP:** 168 | **Speed:** 25 ft. | **Initiative:** +0

**STR** 20 (+5) | **DEX** 10 (+0) | **CON** 20 (+5) | **INT** 12 (+1) | **WIS** 16 (+3) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saves:** STR +9, CON +9

**Weapon:** Oathkeeper (warhammer) - +10 to hit, 1d8+6 bludgeoning + 1d6 radiant vs. oath-breakers

**Multiattack:** 3 attacks

**Special:**

- Action Surge (1/rest) - 6 attacks total
- Second Wind (1/rest) - 1d10+12 HP
- Indomitable (2/rest) - Reroll save

**Tactics:** Defensive, methodical, targets oath-breakers. Never retreats.

**Personality:** Honorable • Traditional • Stubborn

**Voice:** Robert De Niro (The Irishman)

## Don Grommash Stonefist (Deepstone Miners)

**Level 10 Berserker Barbarian**

**AC:** 17 | **HP:** 155 | **Speed:** 40 ft. | **Initiative:** +2

**STR** 20 (+5) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 20 (+5) | **INT** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 16 (+3)

**Saves:** STR +9, CON +9

**Weapon:** Spiked Gauntlets - +9 to hit, 1d8+5 piercing each hand

**Multiattack:** 2 attacks (3 with Frenzy)

**Special:**

- Rage (4/day) - +3 damage, resistance to physical
- Reckless Attack - Advantage but enemies get advantage
- Frenzy - Bonus attack, 1 exhaustion after
- Intimidating Presence (DC 15 WIS)

**Tactics:** Charges strongest enemy, Reckless every turn, grapples casters.

**Personality:** Loud • Fair • Explosive

**Voice:** James Gandolfini (Tony Soprano)

## Don Azrael the Artisan (Brotherhood of the Forge)

**Level 11 Assassin Rogue / 3 Enchantment Wizard**

**AC:** 18 | **HP:** 95 | **Speed:** 30 ft. | **Initiative:** +5

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 20 (+5) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 18 (+4) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 16 (+3)

**Saves:** DEX +9, INT +8

**Weapon:** Jeweled Hammer - +10 to hit, 1d4+6 bludgeoning + 2d6 psychic

**Multiattack:** 2 attacks

**Special:**

- Sneak Attack - 6d6
- Assassinate - Crit on surprised
- Evasion, Uncanny Dodge

**Tactics:** Avoids combat, uses minions. If forced: *Hold Person* + poisoned attacks + Cunning Action. Flees at 30 HP.

**Personality:** Refined • Ruthless • Manipulative

**Voice:** Mads Mikkelsen (Hannibal)
