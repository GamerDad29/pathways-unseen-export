# DREAM for Bpop

### **Bpop’s Dream: The Writ of Chains**

Sleep takes you, but this is no gentle embrace.

The world around you shifts, the warmth of your resting place draining away into **cold, unyielding stone.** You stand alone in a vast **chamber of black marble**, veined with veins of molten gold, the walls towering high into a shadowed sky that has no end. The air is thick, humming with something **ancient**, something that watches.

And then, you hear it.

A **quill scratches against parchment**, slow and deliberate, the sound of ink sinking into paper like blood into dirt.

At the far end of the chamber, seated at an **impossibly long table**, **he** waits.

**The Chain.**

Bound yet unmoving, shackled yet sovereign, he **writes.** His throne of iron and root looms behind him, chains coiling around his form like living things. His **hand glides across the parchment**, filling page after page with a **calligraphy both elegant and cruel**.

You **cannot see the words**, but you **know what they are.**

**Writs.**

Each page carries **a name**. Each signature, a **death sentence**.

And as he writes, you feel them. **Thousands. Tens of thousands.** Names sealed in ink, lives forfeited with a stroke of his pen. Every word **presses down on your chest**, heavy as a tombstone.

*"Purge them,"* The Chain murmurs, his voice not rising, but still filling every inch of the room. The quill moves **faster now**, the ink pooling, the parchment stacking higher and higher.

*"They are stains upon the world. Unworthy of mercy. Unworthy of their breath. They scuttle in filth. They claw at the edges of civilization. They deserve nothing."*

A gust of wind **howls through the chamber**, but there are **no windows, no doors**—only the cold breath of **inevitable extinction**. The pages scatter, floating like falling leaves.

One drifts **toward you**.

A **single name** written in ink **as dark as iron chains**.

It is **your name.**

And beside it—**the mark of his signature.**

The Chain sets the quill down, his burning eyes locking onto you.

*"You are counted among them, little one."*

The chamber **begins to crumble**, the golden veins in the marble **shattering like glass**, revealing an abyss of **seething darkness** beneath.

Chains **erupt from the ground**, snaking toward your wrists, your throat, your ankles. **They are not meant to restrain. They are meant to drag.**

**Drag you down.**

*"Run."*

The voice is not The Chain’s.

It is your own.

And then you **wake.**

---

### **The Awakening**

You **gasp for breath**, your heart hammering, your body drenched in cold sweat.

Your fingers **ache**, as though they still clutched something **heavy**. You glance at your hands.

There, **pressed into your palm**, is the **faint outline of a signature**.

Not your own.

But his.

It fades within moments, but the weight of it remains.

Because you know, deep down, that somewhere in the world above, **another Writ is being signed.**

And more names are being **erased.**
