# Rumors

### **Rizalia’s Rumors:**

- **Nat 20 Response:**
    - "There’s talk of something odd—a small figure, quick and nimble, seen around the outskirts of the outpost. Not a child, nor any race known to us. It's like nothing we’ve ever seen… and it has the merchants uneasy. And those dragonlings, you ask? More sightings lately, feral and wild. Twisted by something dark, they’re getting bold, even near the roads."
- **Nat 1 Response:**
    - "Sounds like you’ve been listening to too many drunken tales. If it’s real, it’ll show itself. If not, it’s just shadows in the dark."

### **Bregan D'aerthe Agent’s Rumors:**

- **Nat 20 Response:**
    - "My sources mention a… peculiar creature—small, spry, with a way of disappearing like smoke in the wind. Doesn’t match any description of races from Toril. Might be useful… or dangerous. And those dragonlings? They've been seen more often, and their eyes… their eyes look almost mad. If you see one, I’d suggest keeping your distance."
- **Nat 1 Response:**
    - "Odd creatures, feral dragons? You've been in the dark too long. Focus on what’s real, or the shadows will eat you alive."

### **Drusk the Healer’s Rumors:**

- **Nat 20 Response:**
    - "I’ve felt an unusual presence… something small, with an energy unlike anything from the Underdark. Maybe a trick of the faerzress… or something else entirely. As for the dragonlings, they’re being driven out from their nests. Some dark magic is twisting them. Keep your wits about you."
- **Nat 1 Response:**
    - "Strange sightings, odd creatures… just stories to keep folks awake at night. Nothing to concern yourselves with."

### **Drunken Table Rumors:**

- **Nat 20 Response:**
    - "I saw it! A little thing, quick like a shadow, darting between stalls. Never seen its like before. And those blasted dragonlings—vicious and crazed like they’ve been touched by madness. The Underdark is getting stranger by the day, I tell ya."
- **Nat 1 Response:**
    - "Ah, who cares? Probably just a trick of the light, or some bugbear's bad joke. Now, drink up!"
