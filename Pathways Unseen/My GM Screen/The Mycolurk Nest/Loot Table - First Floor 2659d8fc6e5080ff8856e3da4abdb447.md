# 🕷️ Queen Spider & Brood Loot/Harvest Table

When the **Queen Spider** and her **three giant spider broodlings** are slain, the adventurers may attempt to harvest parts. Use **Survival or Nature checks** as indicated. On a failed check, the resource is ruined, or the harvester risks Shadow Plague exposure (DC 13 Con save vs. paranoia/whispers).

---

## Queen Spider (Boss)

| Component | DC | Yield | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Silken Spools (Mycolurk Silk)** | 15 | 1d4 bundles | Core of Frank’s quest. Can be crafted into stealth-weave rope, AC-boosting cloaks, or illusion-threaded couture. Frank offers powerful rewards for this silk. |
| **Venom Reservoir** | 16 | 1 vial | *Queen’s Toxin*: Injury poison, DC 15 Con save. Fail = 3d6 poison damage + hallucinations (disadvantage on Perception checks for 1 hour). Success = half damage. |
| **Ovipositor Sac** | 17 | 1 organ | Rare reagent. With alchemy, can brew a potion that summons a giant spider for 1 hour (*Conjure Animals*). |
| **Chitin Plates (Shadow-Tainted)** | 14 | 1d6 plates | Forged into necrotic-resistant armor/shields. Alternately ground to dust for enchanting. |
| **Eyes (Cluster)** | 12 | 2d4 | Brewed into potions of darkvision (30 min each). More than 4 eyes in a potion risks hallucinations. |
| **Spinneret Glands** | 16 | 1–2 | Single-use devices that cast *Web* (DC 14). Frank may trade these at premium value. |

## Giant Spiders ×3

Each broodling can yield the following:

| Component | DC | Yield | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Spider Silk (lesser)** | 11 | 1 bundle per spider | Strong rope (holds 1,000 lb). Glows faintly in fungal terrain. |
| **Venom Sac** | 13 | 1 vial | Injury poison, DC 12 Con save. Fail = 2d6 poison damage. |
| **Carapace Shards** | 12 | 1d4 shards | Craft into light armor giving advantage on Stealth in fungal terrain. |
| **Spider Eyes** | 11 | 1d2 | Darkvision potions (30 min each). |

---

## Special Treasure Drops (roll 1d4, Queen only)

1. **Wand of Webs** – 3 charges, cast *Web* (DC 13); fungal terrain increases range.
2. **Spore-Encrusted Ring** – Advantage vs poison; 1/day *Fog Cloud*; 1-in-4 chance of mushroom sprouting.
3. **Shadow-Tainted Ichor Vial** – Thrown (20 ft), explodes in 10-ft radius for 2d6 necrotic (DC 14 Con save half).
4. **Obsidian Coin (Parliament Mark)** – Parliament sigil, scratched with Bregan symbol. Important plot clue.

---

## Quest Tie-In (Frank’s Reward)

Delivering **Mycolurk Silk (from the Queen)** back to **Frank’s Phylactery** completes his flamboyant questline. Rewards include:

- **Custom Enchanted Item** (illusionwear)
- **Access to Frank’s Secret Chest** (weird rare loot)
- **Favor Token of Frankimus** (teleport to Phylactery, force reroll, or fabulous disarm effect)

---

👉 This makes the **Queen Spider fight + brood harvest** into a full adventure hook: not just combat XP, but tangible crafting materials, magical treasure, and progression on Frank’s silk quest.
