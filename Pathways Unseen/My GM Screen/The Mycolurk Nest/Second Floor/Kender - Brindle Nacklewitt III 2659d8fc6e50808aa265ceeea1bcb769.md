# Kender - Brindle Nacklewitt III

# 🎭 Kender NPC: Description & Conversation

### Appearance

- Small, bright-eyed, wiry figure with a satchel overflowing with mismatched junk: coins from other realms, bits of silk, feathers, glowing mushrooms, and one or two of the party’s own belongings (without realizing they’d been taken).
- Hair wild and unkempt, eyes constantly darting with wonder. Every item they touch seems to have a story — most made up on the spot.

### Personality

- Insatiably curious, endlessly talkative, prone to “borrowing” things and forgetting to return them.
- Speaks as if *none of this is strange*: being in a Shadow Plague cavern is just another Tuesday.
- Treats the party like old friends within minutes.

### Example Conversation Beats

- **On Meeting:**
    
    > “Oh! Finally, people! I was beginning to think the mushrooms were going to start talking back. They listen, you know, but they’re terrible conversationalists. By the way, do you always carry your crystals in that pocket? Because I’ve got one just like it!”
    > 
- **On the Silk:**
    
    > “This thread… oh, I’ve seen it before. Not here, no no no. Somewhere else. Different stars, different sky. It shouldn’t be here, but isn’t it marvelous when things show up where they don’t belong?”
    > 
- **On the Shadow Plague:**
    
    > “Ugh, nasty business, that. Spores that whisper, crystals that hum wrong. Reminds me of when my uncle inhaled a glowmoth whole. Glowed for weeks! But this is… darker.”
    > 
- **On the Party:**
    
    > “You’ve got the look of people with important things to do. Dangerous things. Which is perfect, because I’m very good at following people doing dangerous things!”
    > 

### Role in Story

- The Kender acts as a **living symbol of planar bleed-through**. They shouldn’t be here, but they are.
- They can provide **hints about the silk’s extradimensional nature** (it connects realms) and become a quirky ally, nuisance, or wild card depending on how the party treats them.

## 🎭 Kender Origin in His Own Words

Here are ready-to-read snippets of how the Kender might answer if the players press him on where he’s from, why he’s here, and how he ended up in the middle of a Shadow-Plague cavern. Each line is flavored with Kender chaotic honesty, curiosity, and lack of fear:

---

**On Where He’s From:**

> “Oh! Me? I’m from Kendermore. Lovely place! Or, well, was lovely before the dragon thing, but it’s still got the nicest view of the sea if you climb the right rooftops. We don’t have mushrooms this big, though… ours are about as tall as your knee. I tried standing on one once but it squished. This one looks like it’d hold a whole tavern!”
> 

**On How He Got Here:**

> “How did I get here? Well, you see, there was this silken thread — shimmering, sparkling, dangling right between two trees where no thread had any business dangling! And I thought, that’s odd, so I gave it a tug… and wouldn’t you know it, the whole world tugged back! Next thing I knew I was falling, and then not-falling, and then here I was! It’s rather convenient, really.”
> 

**On Whether He Knows He’s in a Different Realm:**

> “Different realm? Oh no, don’t be silly — I’m exactly where I’m supposed to be. I always am. If I weren’t supposed to be here, how else would I have gotten here? Besides, this looks exactly like that cavern under Aunt Tika’s pantry. Only bigger. And with fewer pickles.”
> 

**On Why He’s Here / What He’s Doing:**

> “Well, at first I was following the shiny silk, but then I saw these crystals — ooooh, crystals! — and I thought, wouldn’t it be something if they hummed when you touched them? (They do, by the way. And if you lick them — don’t lick them, but if you did — they taste like lightning mixed with mushrooms.) So now I’m just waiting to see what’s next! Oh, and I’ve been keeping these eggs company — I don’t think they like me much, but I like them.”
> 

**On the Shadow Plague / Cavern:**

> “Everything’s a bit… shadowy, don’t you think? Mushrooms whisper sometimes. The big brute with the crystals told me I wasn’t supposed to be here, but he was very bad at saying please. I didn’t like him. Do you know him? You look like you know him.”
> 

## 🎭 Brindle Nacklewitt III — In His Own Words

**On His Family Lineage:**

> “Brindle Nacklewitt the Third, thank you kindly! My da’s da’s da was the first of us, founder of the noble Nacklewitt line, and he built our family name with the key to a house someone clearly meant for him to have — why else would he have found it under that goose, eh? It was a very fine house until it wasn’t his anymore. We Nacklewitts are famous for keeping what’s clearly already ours!”
> 

**On How He Got Here:**

> “Well, there was this silky string — you wouldn’t believe it, shimmered like starlight on pond water — just dangling there between two trees. Naturally, I gave it a tug. One doesn’t leave perfectly good string dangling about, do they? The whole world tugged back, next thing I knew I was falling, tumbling, rolling, and here I was! Marvelous, isn’t it? Very efficient way to travel, if you don’t mind the landing.”
> 

**On Whether He Knows He’s in a Different World:**

> “Different world? Hah! If I were in a different world, I’d have noticed. I’m always where I’m supposed to be. The sky’s still up, the ground’s still down, the mushrooms are just a bit taller, that’s all. This looks exactly like Aunt Tika’s cellar… if her pickles had sprouted legs and tried to eat people.”
> 

**On Why He’s Here Now:**

> “What am I doing here? Why, I’m waiting for you, of course! People like you always show up when something interesting is about to happen. Also, there’s a big brute covered in crystals that told me I wasn’t allowed here, and when someone tells me that, I simply must stay.”
> 

**On His World:**

> “Krynn! Lovely place, full of dragons, heroes, chaos, and very nice pastries. I miss the pastries most. Do you have pastries here? No? Then clearly this isn’t another world, because no world worth being in would go without pastries.”
>
