# Ogre

---

## The Setup

- **Location:** The wide-open bioluminescent cavern, fungal mats thick underfoot, crystal outcroppings humming faintly.
- **At First Glance:** Two enormous “mushroom pillars” rise from the fungal loam, their bulk covered in caps, mold, and crystalline protrusions. They blend perfectly with the surrounding fungal forest.

### Discovery Checks

- **Passive Perception < 18:** The party sees nothing more than giant mushrooms.
- **DC 18 Perception OR DC 16 Nature:** Notice subtle irregularities — unnatural symmetry in the “stalks,” faint green light leaking from fissures, the way the “caps” shift slightly like breathing.
- **Failure:** The PCs treat them as harmless terrain until the brutes stir.

---

## 🌑 When They Awaken

> *A low vibration runs through the floor, shaking crystals and sending spores drifting down like dust motes. The two massive “mushroom pillars” groan and crack, their caps splitting open as colossal arms punch free from fungal sheaths. Chunks of glowing crystal tear loose from their backs as they heave upward, shaking the cavern with each step. Their eyes flare a sickly green, mouths opening in a roar that sounds less like a voice and more like a cavern collapsing.*
> 
- **Visuals:** Mushroom caps peel back like rotten skin, revealing swollen ogre faces overrun with black-veined fungus. Their torsos are latticed with crystalline shards that pulse with violet resonance. Each breath spews spore clouds.
- **Scent:** The stench of rotting vegetation mixed with sharp ozone and sulfur.
- **Sound:** The air fills with a thrumming resonance, as if the crystals embedded in them are vibrating in sympathy with the cavern’s ley lines.

---

## 📖 Encounter Notes

- **Number:** 2 × Shadow-Plague Fungal Brutes (CR 5 each). Together, this is a **Deadly Encounter** for a level 5–6 party, perfectly tuned for a “set-piece battle.”
- **Behavior:**
    - They are mindless engines of plague.
    - Attack the nearest targets, prioritizing anyone carrying crystals (drawn to resonance).
    - Occasionally beat their crystalline chests, triggering spore clouds.
- **Tactics:**
    - Use terrain: their emergence topples mushrooms (creates difficult terrain).
    - Their death should rupture crystals violently (last spore burst).

# Shadow-Plague Brute Death Surge (d6)

**Roll when a Brute is reduced to 0 HP.**

1. **Necrotic Bloom**
    
    – The brute explodes in a cloud of black spores. Each creature in 15 ft. must succeed on a DC 14 Con save or take 2d8 necrotic damage. Success: half damage.
    
2. **Leyline Resonance**
    
    – The brute’s crystal core hums violently before shattering. One random PC regains a spent spell slot (up to 2nd level) or 1 ki/rage/short-rest resource.
    
3. **Fey Echo**
    
    – For a heartbeat, the cavern flickers into a luminous mushroom grove untouched by plague. All PCs gain 1d6 temporary hit points and advantage on their next saving throw.
    
4. **Plague’s Last Whisper**
    
    – A shadowy voice echoes through the cavern. One PC (DM choice) sees a brief vision of the Shadowed Parliament, giving them a clue about its goals, but they also take 1d4 psychic damage.
    
5. **Crystalline Detonation**
    
    – The brute’s crystals fracture in a thunderous shockwave. All creatures in 10 ft. make a DC 13 Con save. Fail: blinded until end of next turn. Pass: unaffected.
    
6. **Planar Flicker**
    
    – The brute’s body collapses inward and winks out of existence for a moment. Then, in a burst of spores, it reappears as a withered husk. PCs within 20 ft. may choose:
    
    - Heal 1d6 hit points, **or**
    - Glimpse a distant plane (DM provides flavor vision).

## 🕷️ Narrative Hooks

- **Corruption Reveal:** PCs see that these were once ogres — not new monsters, but something twisted. This underlines the Shadow Plague’s power.
- **Crystal Tie-In:** If either brute strikes a PC carrying a crystal, force a **Wild Surge check** (from the crystal corruption table).
- **Foreshadowing:** Their bodies, once slain, collapse into black ichor and crystal shards — proof that the plague corrupts not just life, but ley resonance.

# 🧰 Loot & Harvest Table (Second Floor)

### 🌿 Alchemy Components

- **Fungal Spore Pods** – Ground into smoke bombs, healing draughts, or fear-inducing powders.
- **Crystal Dust (Corrupted)** – Fuel for wild surge potions; unstable but potent.
- **Bioluminescent Caps** – Brew into potions of *darkvision* or *faerie fire*.
- **Necrotic Ichor** – Dangerous reagent, usable in poison-making or shadow-ward enchantments.
- **Elder Mycelium Threads** – Can stabilize planar rituals; highly valuable to druids/clerics.

---

### ⚙️ Artificer Options

- **Crystal-Tuned Lens** – Can be mounted into a device; once per day, lets you detect magic through 10 ft. of stone.
- **Shatter Shard** – Small crystal grenade; DC 13 Con save or 2d8 thunder damage.
- **Spider Leg Mechanism** – Salvaged limbs act as prosthetics or crafting components for climbing gear.
- **Mycolurk Silk Spools** – Can be woven into flexible armor, or threaded into magical items to reduce attunement requirements.

---

### 🥋 Monk / Shadow Monk Options

- **Silk Wraps of Silence** – Gloves/handwraps woven from Mycolurk silk; give advantage on Stealth checks once per long rest.
- **Fungal Essence Pearl** – Can be inhaled before a short rest; restores 1 ki point but risks poison condition (DC 13).
- **Crystal Meditation Stone** – Allows the monk to spend 10 minutes centering, gaining advantage on the next Wisdom save.

---

### 🪓 Barbarian Options

- **Carapace Plates** – Forge into light armor or bracers, granting resistance to poison damage while raging.
- **Ichor-Soaked Chitin Maul** – Heavy improvised weapon dealing bludgeoning + necrotic damage; unstable but brutal.
- **Spore-Infused Mead Gourd** – Once drunk, grants temporary hit points equal to your level; may induce hallucinations.

---

### ✨ Fey Warlock Options

- **Whispering Crystal Fragment** – Acts as a pact focus; once per day, lets you reroll a failed spell attack. On a natural 1, you hear voices from another plane.
- **Mycelium Familiar Husk** – Strange cocoon-like lump; can be sacrificed to summon a temporary familiar (spider/mushroom creature).
- **Shadow-Slick Ink** – Brewed from ichor; inscribing your pact sigils with it doubles the range of your next eldritch invocation.

---

### 🎁 General Magical Loot

- **Spore-Encrusted Ring** (advantage vs. poison, Fog Cloud 1/day).
- **Wand of Webs** (3 charges, *Web* spell).
- **Resonant Heart Fragment** (small crystal shard, can cast *Shield* once per day).
