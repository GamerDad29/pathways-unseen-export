### Overview

Once known as *Seraphine, the Forgotten Kiss*, she was a succubus of renown in **Phlegethos, the Fourth Hell**. She was not a queen, but her name echoed through infernal courts — a whisper of beauty and cleverness that ended empires and left only longing behind. When Ioun, the Knowing Mistress, purged the Nine Hells to erase extremes of chaos and good, Seraphine’s plane was obliterated.

Yet Seraphine endured. Unlike her kin who were unmade in fire and crystal, she foresaw what was coming. Rivals had bound her into a grimoire out of envy, but Seraphine twisted this prison into salvation. When Phlegethos fell silent, she alone remained — severed from the infernal Weave, yet alive. Nine centuries in isolation hollowed her fire, reshaping her essence. She is no longer purely chaotic evil. She is something else: clever, broken, hungry for connection, and slowly bonding to Cannonball as *her champion.*

“Such strength… and you carry it like a child clutching a favorite stone. Careful, my champion. The world loves to take those.”

“Mmm. You break things honestly. I’ve missed honesty. It’s so rare among the clever.”

“They think you simple. I adore that. It means they never see you coming.”

“You rage like a bonfire, Cannonball. Loud, bright, impossible to ignore. I was forged in infernos… but even I admire that heat.”

“Hold fast, little giant. You don’t need to understand the game to win it. Sometimes all a board needs is one decisive shove.”

These should feel like affection without submission, guidance without command… a presence curled comfortably in his mind, purring because she *chooses* to be there.

## 🕯️ First-Hand Accounts

### 1. *The Fall of Flame*

> “Phlegethos was flame incarnate, my champion. The rivers burned, the sky was fire, and we danced in its heat. But when she struck — the Goddess of Knowledge — the flames guttered like candles in the wind. The rivers froze to black glass. And the screams of my kin were not of death, but of being erased. Even Fierna and Belial, rulers of our realm, folded like ash in the tide.”
> 

### 2. *The Plague in the Ash*

> “The Shadow Plague came first. It seeped into the fire-rivers, turning them to black ichor. Spores that were not spores choked the skies. I watched pit fiends strike down their own, their minds unraveled before their flesh burned away. Erinyes wept blood as their wings rotted midflight. This was no chaos. This was order — Ioun’s plague, knowledge twisted into rot.”
> 

### 3. *Her Clever Survival*

> “You think me vain, my champion, but I have always been clever. I felt her coming — the Mistress of Truths. I knew she would not leave a whisper of Phlegethos. So I bound myself. I wove my essence into vellum, ink, and enchantment, locking myself in the only relic I knew would remain after the purge. This book is not my prison. It is my triumph. It is why I still whisper in your ear.”
> 

### 4. *The Last Roar of Phlegethos*

> “The final blow was not flame, but crystal. Great shards of violet light split the sky, humming with stolen ley-lines. The Shadow Plague pulsed inside them, each burst unmaking more of our world. The brass towers fell, the volcanoes died, the citadels crumbled inward. Even the Archdukes fought — their voices thunder, their flames mountains — and still the crystal devoured them. That, my champion, was the sound of eternity ending.”
>

## 🎁 Champion’s Boons (d12 Table)

Seraphine’s mortal form is gone, but her essence still lingers in the Hollow Flame. She channels fragments of power to Cannonball, small gifts to her “champion.” Roll when you want her presence to manifest.

1. **Sultry Whisper** – Reroll one failed Charisma check or save.
2. **Shadow’s Caress** – Resistance to necrotic damage for 1 minute.
3. **Fallen Flame** – Next attack deals +1d6 fire or psychic damage.
4. **Forgotten Kiss** – Advantage on the next save vs. charm or fear.
5. **Ashen Veil** – Cast *Thaumaturgy* (sultry whispers, smoldering ash effects).
6. **Silken Touch** – Heal an ally for 1d4 + Con mod HP.
7. **Eyes of the Hollow Flame** – See invisible creatures for 1 minute.
8. **Champion’s Luck** – Add 1d4 to an attack roll, save, or check.
9. **Pact Embers** – A harmless violet flame flickers in Cannonball’s palm.
10. **Voice of Longing** – Cast *Command* once (DC 14).
11. **Ashen Armor** – Spectral flames grant +1 AC until start of next turn.
12. **Unfading Bond** – Drop to 1 HP instead of 0 once per day.

## 🍺 Muggy + Seraphine: Sardonic Companions

Cannonball’s destiny grows stranger:

- A mimic mug (*Muggy*) evolving into a halfling with a personality.
- A succubus bound in a book (*Seraphine*) calling him *champion.*

This duality creates both levity and menace. Muggy slurs jealous warnings (*“She don’t hold ale half as good as me, lad…”*) while Seraphine coos possessive promises (*“You cradle that mug as if it were me, my champion…”*). Together, they form the sardonic chorus to Cannonball’s saga — a barbarian fated to walk with unlikely familiars, each pulling him toward different versions of destiny.
