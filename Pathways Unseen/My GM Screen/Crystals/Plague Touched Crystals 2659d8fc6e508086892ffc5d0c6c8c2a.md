# Crystals & the Shadow Plague

## 🪨 Crystals Touched by the Plague

- After being near the **Shadow-Plague Fungal Brute**, any crystal the PCs carried (Resonant Heart, Flicker Shards, Spellgleam Dust, etc.) is now **tainted**.
- PCs themselves don’t show symptoms or catch the Plague yet, but the **resonance in the crystals has shifted**:
    - Hairline cracks, faint violet pulses, strange whispers during meditation.
- **Mechanical Hooks:**
    1. **Flavor only (for now).** Just descriptive weirdness.
    2. **Occasional Surges.** On use of an advanced crystal effect, roll d20. On 1–2, a shadow-tainted backlash (psychic/necrotic damage, hallucination, or hostile surge).
    3. **Long Arc Hook.** Eventually the tainted crystals could act as beacons or infection seeds, tying into the Shadowed Parliament.

---

## 🌱 Cleansing the Crystals

- The **Mushroom Grove beneath the mine** (the one with the teleportation circles) holds a **sacred Fey anchor point.**
- Within it, a **purification pool inside a giant mushroom** can cleanse tainted crystals and purge Shadow Plague resonance.
- Not immediate: requires a rare reagent or passing a Fey guardian’s test.
- This anchors a **future quest arc**: players know their crystals are compromised, and they have a path to eventual cleansing.

## ⚡ Crystal Categories (from the Crystalline Hollows)

### Resonant Heart Crystal (Large) – Advanced Outcomes

- Echo Spell Core (copy a 1st–3rd spell).
- Pulse-Bound Nexus (recover a slot on ley nexus).
- Awakened Sentience (communicates in color/hum).
- Arcane Vein Amplifier (+2 dmg force, radiant, thunder).
- Resonant Shield (reactive resist once/long rest).

### Flicker Shards (Medium) – Advanced Outcomes

- Trigger Tinker (alter magical traps).
- Phase Blink (*blur* or *misty step* once).
- Dreambound Illusion (*detect thoughts* or *mirror image* once).
- Crystal Grenade (*shatter* once).
- Whisper Node (whispers up to 1 mile).

### Spellgleam Dust (Small) – Advanced Outcomes

- Flashcast Residue (cast scroll spell as reaction).
- Signature Ink (reveal spell origin/creator).
- Vision Dust (*true seeing* + hallucination 1 min).
- Chrono-Slip Ritual (delay a 1st–2nd level spell).
- Flux Spark (blinding strike once/day).

## 🎲 Optional Crystal Surge/Corruption Table (d8)

Roll when a tainted crystal is used or struck by Resonant Slam.

1. Whispers flood your mind. Take 1d6 psychic damage, disadvantage on Insight checks until next turn.
2. The crystal bleeds violet light: 1d6 necrotic damage to wielder.
3. Wild surge: random cantrip bursts uncontrollably.
4. Flicker: PC teleports 10 ft in random direction.
5. Surge of clarity: gain advantage on next Arcana check.
6. Light flare: blinds wielder until end of next turn.
7. Crystalline hum: restores 1d4 temp HP.
8. Planar echo: brief vision of another realm, DM provides clue or lore fragment.
