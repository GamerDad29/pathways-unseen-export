# Recap

- **Departure from UTTP**: The party, still recovering from recent events, used Lyra’s scroll to teleport to Drogon. There, they delivered news of UTTP’s turmoil, which Drogon met with resigned understanding. In a somber moment, Beepop revealed Gnomonclature’s body. Drogon, moved by the gesture, honored the fallen by arranging a ceremonial burial, though the players chose not to attend.
- **Onto Karnuk**: With forged seals and new provisions, the party began the long descent to Karnuk. Along the **Old Ember Road**, they encountered a **passing contingent from Karnuk**, hinting at shifting political or military movements.
- **Crystalline Hollows & Ley Line Discovery**: The party unearthed a ley line pulsing with arcane energy—manifested as crystalline growths along the path. They harvested shards, with Cannonball narrowly avoiding disaster when a crystal rebounded and nearly hurled him into the Deep Dark below.
- **Night Watch at a Known Campsite**: During third watch, Beepop’s automaton detected movement. A flamboyant elf—later introducing himself as **Cave Boy**—approached, whistling and shouting incomprehensibly. His dramatic speech was cut short as Iblith cast **Silence**, and Morrighan, furious from her sleep being interrupted, obliterated him with four **Eldritch Blasts**.
- **Ambush by a Bulette**: The death throes attracted a subterranean predator. The **bulette burst from the ground**, drawn by vibrations of combat. A quick but intense battle followed. The party emerged victorious, continuing their march deeper into the Underdark.

**📜 Session Recap: The Ember Road Unfolds**

The shadow of loss still clung to the party as they stood within the fractured sanctuary of UTTP. Their wounds, some visible and others carved deeper, were barely beginning to close when Lyra Shadowheart unfurled the scroll. With arcane runes pulsing in sympathetic rhythm to the path ahead, the party vanished in a whisper of light and emerged before **Drogon Stonebeard**, Viceroy of Ulfgar.

Drogon received them not with surprise, but with the grim understanding of one who reads the tides beneath the earth. The tale of UTTP’s turmoil was laid bare: corrupted trade routes, Syndicate whispers, and Gnomonclature’s fall. With solemn hands and a heavy heart, Beepop drew the cleric’s body from the bag, the Feywild ink still clutched in hopeful offering. Drogon did not take it. Instead, he placed his callused palm on the fallen gnome’s brow and whispered rites in the Old Tongue, promising preparation for a proper burial. The party declined to stay for the ceremony, and yet, something sacred echoed as they departed.

**🔥 The March to Karnuk**

Bearing Drogon’s **Forged Seals** and hardened by recent loss, the party set foot upon the **Old Ember Road**, a path carved through the Underdark by ancient flame and tireless labor. Days passed in restless silence until the first sign of other life: a **contingent from Karnuk**, armored in smoke-treated steel, passed by with tight formation and quiet resolve.

Not long after, the earth shifted. The party found themselves at the edge of **the Crystalline Hollows**.

Here, the ley line pulsed through jagged crystal growths, glowing softly in fractured twilight. They were arcane conduits, untouched by time. Cannonball, eager and strong-armed, pried off several shards with ease. But one crystal struck back, rebounding with volatile force and nearly hurling him into the yawning darkness. He survived, barely, laughter and tension mixing in the air as the road pressed on.

**🌒 A Camp, A Whistle, A Warning Too Late**

At a long-used campsite, third watch fell to Beepop. It was not his eyes, but his ever-watchful automaton that sensed the presence. Soft whistling echoed along the corridor, out of place and laced with cheer.

From the dark stepped a surface elf, flamboyant and radiant against the gloom. His name was **Cave Boy**, and his voice, theatrical and shrill, called out proclamations in a language Beepop did not understand. Iblith approached, but Cave Boy’s grin twisted into condescension. "Fear not, dwellers of gloom, for your days of darkness are numbered." He never finished the sentence.

Silence fell as Iblith’s spell blanketed the space. Moments later, Morrighan rose from slumber, irritation woven into her form. With a flick of her ring and no words spoken, she unleashed four **Eldritch Blasts**, vaporizing Cave Boy where he stood.

The tremor of battle did not go unnoticed.

A **bulette**, drawn by vibration and blood, erupted from the stone below. The fight was fierce, its claws and hunger unmatched, but the party stood victorious, shaken yet resolute.
