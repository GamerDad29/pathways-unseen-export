# Steel Syndicate Intelligence - Shop Network

# 

## **The Chrome Salvage Yard - Vorgash "Scraps"**

**Steel Syndicate Connection Level:** Medium-High

**What Vorgash Knows:**

- Vorgash has direct dealings with Vorgoth's supply chain through "lost" shipments
- He pays protection money to the Steel Syndicate enforcers who control his district
- He knows the names and faces of 3-4 mid-level enforcers who come by monthly
- He's aware of recent crackdowns on illegal mech part sales (someone got executed last month)
- He knows where one of the Steel Syndicate's smaller armories is located (he "finds" parts nearby)
- He's heard rumors about internal power struggles but doesn't know details
- He knows the Steel Syndicate is hiring mercenaries for "something big" but doesn't know what

**What He'll Share (Based on Persuasion):**

**DC 10-12:** General information

- "Steel Syndicate runs this whole district. You pay, you work, you don't ask questions."
- Names of his regular enforcers who collect protection money
- Warning about not getting caught with illegal components

**DC 13-15:** Useful information

- Location of the small armory where he "acquires" parts
- Names of higher-ranking Steel Syndicate members (quartermasters, supply officers)
- Recent execution as a warning - "They're tightening up. Someone's been stealing too much."
- Best times to move illegal goods (when patrols change shifts)

**DC 16-18:** Valuable intelligence

- The Steel Syndicate is preparing for something - they've quadrupled weapon orders
- Internal conflict between Vorgoth's traditionalists and younger members wanting to expand territory
- A specific enforcer (name provided) who can be bribed
- Secret drop-off points for smuggled goods

**DC 19+:** Critical information

- Vorgash admits he's been skimming more than he should - he's scared
- There's a Steel Syndicate lieutenant who's unhappy with leadership and might be flippable
- The "something big" involves a planned assault on a rival faction's territory within the month
- If pressed and promised protection: He'll reveal he's been contacted by another faction looking to sabotage Steel Syndicate supply lines

**Vorgash's Personality Modifiers:**

- Respecting his time: +2 to roll (be direct, don't waste words)
- Buying expensive items first: +1 to roll (shows you're serious)
- Being a fellow craftsman/appreciating his work: +1 to roll
- Threatening him or being pushy: Disadvantage on roll
- Kobold artificer discussing technical details: +1 to roll (he respects the craft)

## **The Neon Piston - Rhythm**

**Steel Syndicate Connection Level:** High

**What Rhythm Knows:**

- Rhythm supplies combat drugs directly to Steel Syndicate arena fighters and elite enforcers
- He has a monthly meeting with a Steel Syndicate captain to discuss "performance enhancement" contracts
- He knows which Steel Syndicate members are addicted to his products (leverage)
- He monitors all conversations in his bar - he's a living surveillance system
- He knows about planned Steel Syndicate operations because warriors prep with his drugs before missions
- He has files on customer patterns, weaknesses, and dependencies
- He knows the Steel Syndicate has been experimenting with warforged soldiers (Rhythm is very interested in this)

**What He'll Share (Based on Persuasion):**

**DC 12-14:** Surface information

- "The Steel Syndicate keeps this district profitable. I keep their fighters... optimized."
- Confirms he supplies combat drugs to their forces
- General knowledge that they control the arena district
- Warning: "They don't like people asking questions about their business."

**DC 15-17:** Useful information

- Names of specific Steel Syndicate fighters and enforcers who are regular customers
- Which drugs are most popular among their forces (indicates combat style/strategy)
- A Steel Syndicate captain is a regular - comes every third day
- They've been buying more "Pilot's Edge" recently - suggests increased combat operations
- One high-ranking enforcer is severely addicted and desperate

**DC 18-20:** Valuable intelligence

- Exact timing of when the Steel Syndicate captain visits (opportunity for ambush/meeting)
- The addicted enforcer's name, rank, and weakness (potential to flip)
- Steel Syndicate has been asking about drugs that increase pain tolerance and fearlessness - "They're preparing soldiers for something unpleasant"
- Rhythm has overheard planning conversations - he can provide fragments about troop movements
- Several Steel Syndicate members are concerned about Vorgoth's leadership - names provided

**DC 21+:** Critical information

- Complete psychological profiles on 3-4 key Steel Syndicate members (Rhythm has been studying them)
- The Steel Syndicate is planning a major operation in 2-3 weeks - Rhythm knows approximate date
- Rhythm reveals he's been approached by rival factions to poison Steel Syndicate customers - he refused (so far)
- Rhythm has been recording conversations - he can provide specific quotes from Steel Syndicate planning sessions
- The warforged soldier experiments: Steel Syndicate is trying to create emotionless, drug-immune warriors

**Rhythm's Personality Modifiers:**

- Buying multiple items first: +2 to roll (investment in relationship)
- Discussing the "science" of his products: +1 to roll (he loves talking methodology)
- Showing interest in being a test subject: +2 to roll (he REALLY wants data)
- Being organic: +1 to roll (he finds organic beings "fascinating")
- Showing moral judgment about his work: Disadvantage on roll
- Approaching as fellow craftsman/scientist: +1 to roll
- Trying to intimidate a warforged: Automatic failure (he's amused by threats)

## **Gearworks Emporium - Tikka Sparkfuse**

**Steel Syndicate Connection Level:** Medium (Protected Status)

**What Tikka Knows:**

- She's under Veysha Geargrind's protection, which means indirect Steel Syndicate protection
- She builds experimental weapons that are "tested" in Steel Syndicate operations
- She knows several Steel Syndicate engineers who consult on weapon designs
- She's aware of specific weapon malfunctions and successes in recent operations
- She knows the Steel Syndicate is struggling with a specific technical problem (malfunctioning siege equipment)
- She's overheard Veysha discussing Steel Syndicate politics but doesn't understand/care about implications
- She has schematics for some Steel Syndicate weapons and fortifications

**What She'll Share (Based on Persuasion):**

**DC 10-12:** Basic information (she overshares naturally)

- "Oh yeah! I build stuff for the Steel Syndicate! Well, for Veysha who works with them! It's so cool!"
- Enthusiastic descriptions of weapons she's designed
- Names of Steel Syndicate engineers she's met
- "They have this BIG problem with one of their siege machines - keeps jamming!"

**DC 13-15:** Detailed technical information

- Complete descriptions of experimental weapons currently in Steel Syndicate use
- Weaknesses in their weapon designs (she designed them, she knows the flaws)
- "Oh! There was this big meeting last week about needing more firepower for something!"
- Names and locations of Steel Syndicate weapon testing sites
- Specifications on Steel Syndicate fortification designs

**DC 16-18:** Strategic intelligence (accidental)

- Tikka mentions what weapons are being prioritized - indicates their strategic focus
- "Veysha seemed really stressed about a timeline - something about three weeks?"
- Details about the malfunctioning siege equipment: where it is, what it's for, why it's important
- She has blueprints for a Steel Syndicate stronghold (she worked on the security systems)
- Information about weapon shipments and storage locations

**DC 19+:** Critical information (she doesn't realize the value)

- Complete schematics for Steel Syndicate defensive positions (she has them in her shop)
- "Oh yeah, Veysha was arguing with some scary dwarf about whether to attack someone? Didn't catch who."
- Test data showing which Steel Syndicate weapons are unreliable (could be sabotaged)
- Locations of weapon caches and armories
- "There's this secret project - something about a really really BIG bomb? I'm not supposed to talk about it!"
- If she really trusts Bpop: She'll share her entire design journal with notes on Steel Syndicate commissions

**Tikka's Personality Modifiers:**

- Being a fellow kobold: +3 to roll (PACK!)
- Being an artificer: +2 to roll (she loves talking shop)
- Showing genuine interest in her work: +2 to roll (she'll talk forever)
- Buying her experimental items: +1 to roll (supporting her craft)
- Complimenting her genius: +1 to roll (she's insecure despite her brilliance)
- Asking directly about secrets: -2 to roll (she suddenly remembers she's not supposed to tell)
- Showing concern for her "volunteers": -1 to roll (she gets defensive)
- Collaborating on a project together: Automatic success on next persuasion attempt (she trusts partners)

**Special Tikka Note:** She's the easiest to get information from, but she doesn't understand what's important. Players will need to ask the right questions or piece together implications she doesn't see.
