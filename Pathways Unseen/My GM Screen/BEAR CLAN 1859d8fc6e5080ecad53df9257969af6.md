# BEAR CLAN

---

### **The Bear Clan at the Pop-Up Pub**

**Setting the Scene**

In the dimly lit chaos of the **Barrel’s Rest**, a hastily assembled pop-up pub in the market, the **Bear Clan** goliaths stand out like mountains among hills. Their sheer size, booming voices, and confident presence draw both awe and caution from the surrounding patrons. The makeshift wooden walls creak under their weight as they lean casually on the tables, their mugs of ale comically small in their massive hands.

---

### **Bear Clan Members**

### **1. Torval Broadshoulder**

- **Role**: The leader of the group, tasked with ensuring Cannonball’s safety if he’s found.
- **Appearance**: Torval stands taller than most goliaths, his broad shoulders draped in a thick bear-hide cloak that still bears the scars of its former life. His pale, stone-like skin is marked with intricate clan tattoos, tracing down his arms and over his chest. His piercing gray eyes miss nothing, and the massive maul resting against the table beside him serves as a silent reminder of his power.
- **Personality**: Stern, disciplined, and deeply loyal to his clan. Torval speaks with measured authority, every word carrying the weight of a leader who’s seen countless battles. He keeps a steady eye on his companions, ensuring their antics don’t get out of hand.
- **Dialogue**:
    - *“If Cannonball’s out here, we’ll find him. And if he’s in trouble, he won’t face it alone.”*
    - *“The Bear Clan doesn’t leave its own behind. Not in the mountains, not in these cursed tunnels.”*
    - *“Keep drinking, Grell. Maybe you’ll finally grow into that mouth of yours.”*

---

### **2. Brona Ashhide**

- **Role**: Scout and tracker, skilled at navigating both the wilderness and the chaos of the Underdark. She has been tasked specifically to locate Cannonball.
- **Appearance**: Brona’s build is muscular yet lean, her stone-gray skin etched with claw-like markings that run across her shoulders and down her back. Her braided black hair is tied with strands of fur, and her attire is adorned with feathers and bones, a reflection of her deep connection to the clan’s spiritual traditions. She carries a massive spear with jagged edges, its shaft reinforced with iron bands.
- **Personality**: Witty, sharp-tongued, and fiercely protective of her clanmates. Brona is a natural at blending humor with wisdom, often teasing her companions while remaining deadly serious when it matters. She approaches challenges with calm confidence but never underestimates her enemies.
- **Dialogue**:
    - *“Cannonball’s not exactly subtle. If he’s down here, we’ll hear him before we see him.”*
    - *“Torval, relax. Cannonball could punch his way through a tunnel if he wanted. He’s fine—probably causing trouble, but fine.”*
    - *“Anyone else noticing how that guy over there hasn’t stopped staring? Keep your weapons close.”*

---

### **3. Grell Stonetooth**

- **Role**: The heart and soul of the group, a brash warrior whose presence inspires both laughter and occasional eye rolls. Though not officially tasked with finding Cannonball, Grell’s loyalty to his clan means he wouldn’t leave this mission for anything.
- **Appearance**: Grell is shorter than his companions, but his build is no less imposing. His bald head gleams in the light, his jaw framed by a wild, jet-black beard streaked with silver. His massive arms are bare, showcasing a patchwork of scars from countless brawls. A crude but effective battleaxe rests against the wall beside him, its edge chipped yet razor-sharp.
- **Personality**: Loud, boisterous, and always ready for a fight, Grell is the Bear Clan’s most animated member. While his mouth often gets him into trouble, his strength and heart make him a beloved companion. He’s quick to laugh, quicker to drink, and absolutely fearless in battle.
- **Dialogue**:
    - *“Cannonball? That walking disaster? If he’s out here, I’ll find him—and probably have to pull him out of whatever mess he’s made.”*
    - *“This ale tastes like a mountain troll’s bathwater, but I’ll be damned if I don’t drink it anyway!”*
    - *“Brona, stop glaring at people. You’ll scare off the fun before it starts.”*

---

### **Interaction Opportunities**

### **Torval’s Leadership**

- **Tone**: Serious and focused, Torval is keen to gather information about Cannonball and ensure the clan’s business remains discreet.
- If the party reveals they’ve seen Cannonball, Torval offers his gratitude but remains skeptical until proven true.
    - *“You’ve got my thanks, but words alone aren’t enough. Tell me where he is, and I’ll see for myself.”*
- If the party is evasive, Torval’s tone grows cold:
    - *“The Bear Clan doesn’t take kindly to games. If you know something, speak. If not, don’t waste my time.”*

---

### **Brona’s Wit**

- **Tone**: Brona is sharp and quick to tease but always alert, watching for potential threats in the room.
- She playfully prods the party for information about Cannonball, offering sarcastic remarks if they’re slow to respond.
    - *“C’mon, we’re not asking for your life story. Just tell us if you’ve seen a walking fortress swinging a greataxe around.”*
- If the party acts suspiciously, Brona narrows her eyes:
    - *“You’re hiding something. And let me tell you, you’re terrible at it.”*

---

### **Grell’s Boisterous Nature**

- **Tone**: Grell is loud, friendly, and eager to share tales of Cannonball’s exploits, regardless of whether they’re entirely true.
- He offers drinks and exaggerated stories of Cannonball’s past, trying to lighten the mood and earn the party’s trust.
    - *“There was this one time Cannonball leapt off a cliff to fight a roc. Beat the thing senseless before it hit the ground!”*
- If the party doesn’t respond to his humor, Grell shrugs:
    - *“Tough crowd. You’re missing out. The Bear Clan’s full of legends, and Cannonball’s one of the best.”*

---

### **Plot Hooks**

- **If the Party Shares Information**:
    - Torval offers to stay in touch and potentially aid the party.
        - *“The Bear Clan owes you for this. Let’s hope Cannonball doesn’t cause too much trouble before we reach him.”*
- **If the Party Holds Back**:
    - Brona grows impatient, her tone sharp:
        - *“We’re not here to waste time. If you know something, you’d better start talking.”*
- **If Tensions Escalate**:
    - Grell’s boisterous nature could lead to a scuffle, either with the party or the bar’s patrons.
        - Torval steps in, growling:
            - *“We didn’t come here to cause a scene. Grell, sit down before I nail you to that chair myself.”*

### **2. Bearhide Cloak**

- **Description**: A thick, fur-lined cloak made from the pelt of a massive bear, its fur a mix of black and silver. The hood is fashioned from the bear’s head, its gleaming teeth and fierce expression adding an intimidating flair. The inside is lined with stitched runes from the clan’s spiritual tradition.
- **Effect**:
    - Grants **resistance to cold damage**.
    - Once per long rest, Cannonball can pull the cloak tightly around himself to gain **temporary hit points equal to his level + Constitution modifier** as the spirit of the bear protects him.
- **Narrative Flavor**:
    - *“She said this was from a bear you helped bring down on your first hunt. She wanted it to protect you—said you’ve got the strength of a bear in you, but it doesn’t hurt to carry a little more.”*

---

### **3. Totem of the Bear Spirit**

- **Description**: A small wooden totem carved into the shape of a bear standing tall on its hind legs. The totem is polished smooth, and the bear’s eyes are inlaid with amber that glows faintly in low light. A leather cord allows it to be worn around the neck or tied to a weapon.
- **Effect**:
    - Grants **+1 to weapon attack rolls when tied to a melee weapon**.
    - Once per long rest, the totem can be used to cast *Shield of Faith* on Cannonball, granting +2 AC for 10 minutes.
- **Narrative Flavor**:
    - *“Your mother said this was carved from the wood of an ancient tree in our homeland. She wanted you to carry the spirit of the bear with you, no matter how far from home you wander.”*

---

### **4. Ancestral Axe**

- **Description**: A one-handed axe with a gleaming blade of dark steel, its haft wrapped in leather and fur. The head of the axe bears intricate carvings of bears and mountains, and the pommel is set with a bloodstone. Despite its age, the weapon feels perfectly balanced in Cannonball’s hand.
- **Effect**:
    - A **+1 weapon** that deals an additional **1d6 radiant damage against undead**.
    - The axe can also be thrown (range 20/60 feet), and it will return to Cannonball’s hand immediately after striking a target.
- **Narrative Flavor**:
    - *“She said this was your grandfather’s axe, passed down from generation to generation. It’s been reforged and blessed by the shaman. She wanted you to have it—said you’ve earned it.”*

---

### **5. Spirit-Bound Bracelet**

- **Description**: A thick leather bracelet adorned with polished stones and small metal bear-shaped charms. Each charm is inscribed with tiny runes, said to carry the prayers of the clan. When worn, the bracelet radiates a faint warmth, as if it holds the touch of home.
- **Effect**:
    - Grants **+1 to Wisdom saving throws**.
    - Once per long rest, the bracelet can be activated to cast *Enhance Ability (Bear’s Endurance)* on Cannonball, giving him advantage on Constitution checks and 2d6 temporary hit points for 1 hour.
- **Narrative Flavor**:
    - *“Your mother said this would remind you of the clan’s love. Each charm carries a blessing from someone back home—your mother, the shaman, and even the little ones. They’re with you, no matter where you go.”*

---

### **Delivery and Emotion**

The Bear Clan members would present the gift with great reverence, emphasizing its importance. Torval, as the leader, would likely say something heartfelt:

- *“Cannonball, your mother wanted you to have this. She said it wasn’t just a gift—it’s a part of home, something to carry with you through whatever lies ahead. She’s proud of you, even if she can’t be here to say it herself.”*

The gift not only adds to Cannonball’s arsenal but also ties deeply into his backstory and the ongoing presence of the Bear Clan in his life. Let me know which option resonates most, or if you’d like additional ideas!
