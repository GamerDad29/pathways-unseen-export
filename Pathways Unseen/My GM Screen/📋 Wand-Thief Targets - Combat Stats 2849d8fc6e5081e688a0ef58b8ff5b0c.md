# 📋 Wand-Thief Targets - Combat Stats

The three NPCs holding Thalnys's stolen wands.

---

## Krev Ironjaw (Serpent's Fang)

**Level 6 Berserker Barbarian - Steel Syndicate**

**AC:** 15 | **HP:** 78 | **Speed:** 40 ft. | **Initiative:** +2

**STR** 18 (+4) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 18 (+4) | **INT** 8 (-1) | **WIS** 10 (+0) | **CHA** 12 (+1)

**Saves:** STR +7, CON +7

**Weapon:** Greataxe - +7 to hit, 1d12+4 slashing

**Wand:** Serpent's Fang (doesn't know value) - *Poison Spray* DC 14, 2d12 poison, 5 charges

**Multiattack:** 2 attacks (3 with Frenzy)

**Special:**

- Rage (3/day) - +2 damage, resistance
- Reckless Attack
- Frenzy - Bonus attack, 1 exhaustion

**Tactics:**

- Round 1: Uses wand (*Poison Spray*)
- Round 2: Rage + Reckless + Frenzy
- Drops wand if grappled (doesn't know it's valuable)
- Fights to death for Syndicate

**Wand Location:** Belt pouch (Perception DC 12)

**Personality:** Brutal • Simple • Loyal

**Difficulty:** EASY - Doesn't value wand

---

## Zethrin Ashveil (Widow's Kiss)

**Level 7 Evocation Wizard - Brotherhood of the Forge**

**AC:** 15 (*Mage Armor*) | **HP:** 42 | **Speed:** 30 ft. | **Initiative:** +3

**STR** 8 (-1) | **DEX** 16 (+3) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 18 (+4) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 10 (+0)

**Saves:** INT +7, WIS +4

**Wand:** Widow's Kiss (thinks it's decorative art) - Powers unknown to him

**Special:**

- Sculpt Spells - Allies auto-succeed
- Potent Cantrip - Half damage on save
- Spells: *Fireball, Shield, Misty Step, Greater Invisibility, Counterspell*

**Tactics:**

- Pre-combat: *Mage Armor*
- Round 1: *Fireball* at cluster
- Uses *Shield* liberally
- *Greater Invisibility* + *Misty Step* to flee
- **Surrenders at 15 HP** - offers wand unknowingly as payment

**Wand Location:** Display case in workshop (thinks it's art)

**Personality:** Insecure • Ambitious • Cowardly

**Difficulty:** MEDIUM - Coward, will negotiate

---

## Silra Inkvein (Ghoststep)

**Level 8 Arcane Trickster Rogue - Independent Thief**

**AC:** 18 | **HP:** 65 | **Speed:** 30 ft. | **Initiative:** +4

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 18 (+4) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 16 (+3) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saves:** DEX +7, INT +6

**Skills:** Sleight of Hand +10, Stealth +10

**Wand:** Ghoststep (KNOWS its value) - *Misty Step* (1 charge), *Invisibility* (2 charges), **Etherealness (3 charges, 1 min)**

**Special:**

- Sneak Attack - 4d6
- Cunning Action, Evasion, Uncanny Dodge
- Spells: *Invisibility, Shadow Blade, Disguise Self*

**Tactics:**

- Pre-combat: *Invisibility* from wand
- Round 1: Sneak Attack from stealth
- Uses *Misty Step* every turn
- **ETHEREALNESS at 30 HP** - phases through walls, GONE

**Wand Location:** Hidden in boot (Perception DC 18) - **always on her**

**Personality:** Cunning • Greedy • Paranoid

**Difficulty:** HARD - Will use *Etherealness* to escape through walls

---

## Encounter Difficulty

**Krev** (Easy) - Brute who doesn't value wand, easy to trick/grapple

**Zethrin** (Medium) - Cowardly mage, will surrender and bargain

**Silra** (Hard) - **Knows wand's value**, uses *Etherealness* to escape

---

## Party Strategies

**For Krev:**

- Grapple him, take wand from pouch
- Convince him wand is worthless
- Wait until he's drunk at tavern

**For Zethrin:**

- Intimidate into surrender (he's a coward)
- Steal from display case while he's out
- Negotiate - he'll give it up if threatened

**For Silra:**

- **AMBUSH IS CRITICAL** - must prevent *Etherealness*
- *Counterspell* her escape
- *Hold Person* before she can react
- Steal boot while she sleeps
- **DO NOT** let her get below 30 HP conscious

---

## Wand Powers (For DM)

**Serpent's Fang:** 7 charges, *Poison Spray* (2d12, DC 15), regains 1d6+1 at dawn

**Widow's Kiss:** 7 charges, *Ray of Enfeeblement* (1), *Hold Person* (2), *Vampiric Touch* (3), regains 1d6+1 at dawn

**Ghoststep:** 7 charges, *Misty Step* (1), *Invisibility* (2), *Etherealness* (3 charges, 1 min), regains 1d6+1 at dawn
