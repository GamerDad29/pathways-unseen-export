# Iblith Training Session

---

### **Training Program: Iblith Under Grul Fang’s Guidance**

**Overview**:

Grul Fang, the legendary Level 20 Orc Chieftain Warlord, agrees to train Iblith, the Shadow Monk, imparting lessons in strength, balance, and tactical mastery. Over the course of 3-5 training sessions, Iblith must accumulate **15 Training Points** to unlock a permanent benefit, with additional rewards for exceptional performance.

---

### **Training Mechanics**

**Goal**: Iblith must accumulate **15 Training Points** through a series of intense exercises. Rolls during each session determine the points earned.

- **3 Sessions**: 15 points required.
- **5 Sessions**: 20 points for advanced benefits.

---

### **Training Exercises and Rolls**

1. **Shadow’s Embrace**
    - **Purpose**: Master combat in darkness.
    - **Roll**: Constitution saving throw or Wisdom (Insight).
    - **DC**: 16.
    - **Outcome**:
        - Success: +3 Training Points.
        - Failure: +1 Training Point (for persistence).
    - **Scenario**: Spar with Grul Fang in total darkness, relying on senses and intuition rather than sight.
    - **Lesson**: “In the dark, strength means nothin’ without clarity o’ mind.”

---

### **Progression and Results**

### **15-19 Points**:

- **Permanent Benefit: Shadow Strike Mastery**
    - Once per short rest, Iblith can reroll a missed attack roll with an unarmed strike or monk weapon.

### **20+ Points**:

- **Permanent Benefit: Grul Fang’s Endurance**
    - Iblith gains a permanent **+1 to Constitution** (up to 20).
    - Once per long rest, he can reduce damage from one attack by **10** as a reaction.

---

### **Optional Final Challenge**

At the end of the training, Grul Fang offers a final test of mastery.

- **Challenge**:
    - **Roll**: Dexterity (Acrobatics) or Wisdom (Insight).
    - **DC**: 18.
- **Reward**: If successful, Iblith gains **+1 AC** while unarmored (stacks with Unarmored Defense).

---

### **Grul Fang’s Final Words**

After the final session or challenge, Grul Fang addresses Iblith directly:

**Grul Fang**:

“Ye’ve done well, monk. Better than most would dare dream. But remember—this strength ain’t fer show. It’s a tool, a weapon tae protect those who cannae protect themselves. Balance is the key. Chaos an’ discipline—they ain’t opposites, lad. They’re two sides o’ the same blade. Learn tae wield both, an’ ye’ll become a force even the shadows fear.”

---

### **Narrative Integration**

This training program offers **mechanical growth** for Iblith while reinforcing the relationship between him and Grul Fang. Each exercise challenges Iblith’s core abilities as a monk, providing a meaningful progression both in-character and mechanically. Grul Fang’s philosophy on balance and strength is woven into every aspect, ensuring the training feels both personal and impactful.

### **Training Progression and Rewards**

### **Baseline Milestone – 15 Points**

**Reward: Shadow Strike Mastery**

- **Mechanic**: Once per short rest, Iblith can reroll one missed attack roll with an unarmed strike or monk weapon.
- **Narrative**: He has internalized Grul Fang’s philosophy of clarity in darkness and honed his reaction time in shadow combat.

### **Advanced Milestone – 20+ Points**

**Reward: Grul Fang’s Endurance**

- **Mechanics**:
    - Permanent **+1 to Constitution** (not exceeding 20).
    - Once per long rest, Iblith can reduce incoming damage from one attack by **10** as a reaction.
- **Narrative**: His body has been tempered through brutal orcish drills, representing an internal fusion of monk resilience and warrior endurance.

### **Optional Final Challenge**

- **Test**: Dexterity (Acrobatics) or Wisdom (Insight) vs DC 18.
- **Reward on Success**: **+1 AC** while unarmored (stacks with Monk’s Unarmored Defense).
- **Narrative**: This is Grul Fang’s final stamp of approval, acknowledging Iblith’s evolution as more than a monk — but a tactical master in body and spirit.
