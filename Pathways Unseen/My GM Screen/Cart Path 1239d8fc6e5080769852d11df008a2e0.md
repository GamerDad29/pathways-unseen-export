# Cart Path

### **Cart Path: D8 Roll Table**

1. **Magical Phylactery Exposure:** The party’s cart rattles over a slightly raised part of the track. Investigating the area reveals a hidden entrance leading to a small, sealed-off chamber that contains a **Magical Phylactery holding cell**. The air is heavy with arcane energy, and the ancient symbols seem to shift as they approach.
2. **Abandoned Cart:** The party finds an old mining cart tipped over on its side. The contents have long since rotted away, but the cart is still functional.
3. **Loose Track:** The cart path is mostly intact, but part of the track is loose. The party will need to stop and make minor repairs or risk a bumpy ride.
4. **Cave Lizards:** A group of small, harmless cave lizards scurries across the track, paying no attention to the party.
5. **Cave Echoes:** The sound of distant, heavy footsteps reverberates through the tunnel. They grow louder for a moment but then fade into nothing.
6. **Broken Lantern:** The party comes across an old, broken lantern. It seems like someone left it behind in haste.
7. **Old Rope Bridge:** The path leads to a rickety rope bridge over a narrow chasm. It sways in the wind but appears stable enough to cross.
8. **Footprints:** The party notices fresh footprints along the cart path. They’re not alone, but the owner of the tracks is nowhere to be seen.
