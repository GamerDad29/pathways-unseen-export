# Gorvan Emberforge

### Forge Dwarf NPC: **Gorvan Emberforge**

**Basic Info:**

- **Name:** Gorvan Emberforge
- **Race:** Forge Dwarf (Exclusive NPC race in the Underdark, altered by generations of forge work)
- **Age:** 370 years
- **Gender:** Male
- **Appearance:** Gorvan is stout, with soot-blackened skin that seems to shimmer faintly in the firelight, a unique trait of the Forge Dwarves. His beard, streaked with deep crimson strands, looks as though embers dance within its braids. His hands are broad and calloused, with veins of faintly glowing metallic lines—a signature of Forge Dwarves exposed to high elemental energies.

---

### **Backstory:**

Gorvan Emberforge is one of the most seasoned smiths from the legendary Forge Dwarf enclave of **Kurn Torahl**, a hidden settlement located within the molten core of the Underdark. Known for crafting tools and weapons infused with elemental fire, Gorvan’s creations are sought after by both the surface dwellers and the denizens of the Underdark.

For decades, Gorvan served as the chief forgemaster of Borin Stonebeard’s mining operation in Iron Hallow Mine. He was responsible for refining rare ores and crafting the specialized tools that allowed Borin’s miners to dig deeper than ever before. His masterpiece, the **Embersteel Pickaxe**, was said to cut through stone like butter, channeling the power of molten veins within the earth.

Gorvan is a pragmatist who values hard work and ingenuity, but his loyalty to Borin stemmed from a personal oath made years ago. Borin saved Gorvan’s life during an ancient skirmish with duergar raiders, a debt Gorvan vowed to repay by forging the finest tools and weapons for Borin’s operations.

---

### **What Happened to Gorvan?**

As the mining operation in Iron Hallow Mine expanded, whispers of strange occurrences began to surface. Tools would vanish, veins of ore seemed unnaturally depleted, and miners reported feeling an oppressive presence deep within the tunnels.

Gorvan was among the first to suspect that the mine had breached a dormant, ancient magic—something far older than the Dwarves’ mastery of the forge. When the tragedy struck and miners began disappearing, Gorvan was caught in a sabotage attempt aimed at shutting down the forge. He narrowly survived a confrontation with shadowy figures, only to be left for dead after an explosion that collapsed part of the mine.

---

### **Current State:**

The party finds Gorvan unconscious in a dark tunnel near a makeshift forge. His injuries are severe, and he is suffering from dehydration and exhaustion. His tools and protective gear have been stripped away, though a faint ember-like glow lingers on his body, a sign of his Forge Dwarf resilience.

---

### **Key Traits:**

- **Experienced Craftsman:** Gorvan’s skill with metalwork and forging is unparalleled. He can repair or enhance weapons, tools, and armor given the right materials and conditions.
- **Resilient Spirit:** Despite his age, Gorvan possesses a fiery determination. Once he recovers, he will eagerly assist the party in any effort to uncover the truth about the mine.
- **Keeper of Secrets:** Gorvan holds critical information about Borin’s operation, including the sabotage attempt and the possible involvement of external factions in the mine’s downfall.

---

### **How Gorvan Interacts with the Party:**

1. **Initial Reaction:** Once conscious, Gorvan will be wary but cooperative. He views the party as potential allies and will cautiously offer what information he can.
2. **Possible Quests:** Gorvan may request the party’s help to recover his stolen tools or investigate the collapse that left him for dead.
3. **Ongoing Role:** Gorvan could act as a forgemaster for the party, crafting unique weapons and items with materials they bring him.

---

### **Hooks for the Campaign:**

- **Lost Masterwork:** Gorvan reveals that his greatest creation, the **Embersteel Pickaxe**, was taken during the chaos. Retrieving it could unlock new paths in the mine.
- **Mine Saboteurs:** Gorvan’s testimony points to a faction—possibly tied to The Chain or the Shadowed Parliament—responsible for sabotaging Borin’s operation.
- **Forge Dwarf Legacy:** Gorvan’s connection to the reclusive Forge Dwarves could lead to discovering their enclave, which holds secrets and resources vital to the party’s quest.

This setup creates a compelling NPC with the potential to deepen the mystery of Borin’s Mine while offering practical and narrative benefits to the party.

### Roleplaying Tips for Gorvan's Accent

- **Compressed Words:** Gorvan often drops vowels and compresses phrases (e.g., "I'm" becomes "I'm," "you have" becomes "ye've").
- **Dwarven Colloquialisms:** He uses metaphors related to forging and mining (e.g., "forge's flame," "stone's not gettin' softer").
- **Deep Tone:** Speak with a gravelly, deep tone to convey his age and the weight of his experiences.
- **Expressive Gestures:** He punctuates his speech with hearty nods, chuckles, or by stroking his ember-like beard.
- **Limited but Meaningful Words:** Gorvan chooses his words carefully, often conveying complex thoughts succinctly.

### Incorporating Gorvan into Your Campaign

Gorvan's unique way of speaking adds flavor and authenticity to his character, making interactions memorable for the players. His gratitude towards the party can foster a strong alliance, and his skills as a Master Forgebearer offer practical benefits.

**Potential Developments:**

- **Quest Giver:** Gorvan might send the party on a quest to retrieve his stolen tools or investigate the mysterious forces that sabotaged the mine.
- **Crafting Ally:** With access to a proper forge, he can enhance the party's equipment, possibly unlocking new abilities or enchantments.
- **Cultural Insight:** Gorvan can provide valuable information about Forge Dwarf society, traditions, and possibly lead the party to Kurn Torahl.

Remember to balance his heavy accent with clarity during crucial plot points to ensure players understand important information.
