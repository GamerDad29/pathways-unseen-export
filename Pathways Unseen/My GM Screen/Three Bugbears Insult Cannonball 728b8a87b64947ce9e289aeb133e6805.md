# Three Bugbears Insult Cannonball

### **Encounter:**

---

**Location:** Central Market Square, Murkhold Crossing

**Setup:**

As Cannonball Kar-thul and the party make their way through the bustling marketplace, they pass by a trio of bugbears loitering near a vendor's stall. These bugbears, clad in mismatched armor and reeking of ale, glance over at Cannonball and snicker among themselves. One of them, the leader, a burly bugbear with a patch over one eye, steps forward, leans on his crude spear, and points at Cannonball with a toothy grin.

**Dialogue:**

- **Bugbear Leader:** "Well, well, look what we have here, boys! A little runt thinks he can strut around like a big shot, huh? Why don’t you get yourself a stool, shorty, so you can at least see over the crowd!"

The other two bugbears burst into mocking laughter, clearly trying to provoke Cannonball. Their taunts draw the attention of nearby vendors and travelers, forming a small crowd eager to see how Cannonball responds.

---

### **Possible Outcomes:**

1. **Confrontation:** If Cannonball takes the bait, the bugbears are eager to brawl. They taunt further, readying their weapons and preparing for a scuffle. The leader wields a crude spear, while the other two are armed with clubs. They are not heavily armored, but they are agile and quick to attack.
2. **Intimidation or Retort:** Cannonball could try to intimidate or outwit the bugbears. A successful intimidation check (DC 15) could send the bugbears packing, grumbling and swearing under their breath. A witty retort could earn Cannonball some admiration from the onlookers, potentially gaining favor with certain NPCs.
3. **Diplomacy:** The party could attempt to defuse the situation diplomatically, possibly by buying the bugbears a drink or finding a way to distract them. A successful persuasion check (DC 13) could prevent a fight and turn the encounter into an opportunity for information gathering or future favors.

### **Bugbear Stats:**

- **Leader Bugbear:** HP: 27, AC: 16 (Leather Armor, Shield), Spear (1d8+2 piercing)
- **Bugbear 1 and 2:** HP: 22 each, AC: 15 (Leather Armor), Clubs (1d6+2 bludgeoning)

---

### **Aftermath:**

Depending on the outcome, the bugbears might hold a grudge, becoming recurring nuisances, or grudgingly respect Cannonball, possibly sharing rumors or offering aid in exchange for a future favor.
