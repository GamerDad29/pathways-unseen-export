# 📋 Karnuk Quest Board - Part 1

Side quests, contracts, and opportunities available in Karnuk. Organized by patron and difficulty.

---

## How to Use

**Job Sources:**

- **Tavern Boards** - Public postings, usually legitimate
- **Family Contacts** - Direct offers from family leadership
- **Bregan Network** - Iblith's handler provides missions
- **Street Rumors** - Overheard opportunities
- **NPCs Approach Party** - Direct recruitment

**Difficulty Ratings:**

- ⭐ **Easy** (Level 5) - Low risk, quick completion
- ⭐⭐ **Medium** (Level 6) - Moderate danger, investigation required
- ⭐⭐⭐ **Hard** (Level 7) - High stakes, combat likely
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Deadly** (Level 8+) - Extremely dangerous, party could die

**Payment Scale:**

- ⭐ Easy: 50-100gp
- ⭐⭐ Medium: 100-250gp
- ⭐⭐⭐ Hard: 250-500gp
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Deadly: 500-1000gp+

## Iron Hammer Clan Contracts

*Honor-focused, traditional, protect legacy*

### ⭐ "Retrieve the Founder's Hammer"

**Patron:** Grimna Truehammer (The Ancestor's Forge)

**Payment:** 75gp + masterwork weapon discount

**Time Limit:** None

**Job:** Family heirloom hammer stolen by Steel Syndicate "collectors." Retrieve it intact.

**Location:** Syndicate warehouse in Innovation District

**Complications:**

- Hammer is display piece, well-guarded
- Syndicate won't sell it back
- Breaking in = making enemy of Kethar
- Alternative: Challenge for it in honor duel

**Success:** Iron Hammer reputation +1, masterwork weapon at cost

**Failure:** Syndicate keeps it, Iron Hammer loses face

---

### ⭐⭐ "Convince Borin to Return"

**Patron:** Thorgar Ironhammer (secretly through intermediary)

**Payment:** 200gp + future favor

**Time Limit:** 3 days before defection becomes public

**Job:** Thorgar's grandson Borin is defecting to Steel Syndicate. Convince him to stay or at least delay.

**Location:** The Neon Piston (Syndicate territory)

**Complications:**

- Borin truly believes Syndicate is future
- Kethar has offered him captain position
- Borin feels suffocated by tradition
- Syndicate guards won't let party talk alone
- Alternative: Kidnap him back (dishonorable)

**Success:** Borin delays defection, buys time for Thorgar

**Failure:** Borin publicly defects, Iron Hammer humiliated

---

### ⭐⭐⭐ "Sabotage the Syndicate Foundry"

**Patron:** Iron Hammer leadership (anonymous request)

**Payment:** 400gp

**Time Limit:** Before next mech demonstration (5 days)

**Job:** Syndicate's new mech prototype must not succeed. Sabotage without getting caught.

**Location:** Innovation District foundries

**Complications:**

- Heavy Syndicate security
- Veysha Geargrind working late nights
- Sabotage must look like accident
- Killing workers = dishonorable, Iron Hammer won't pay
- If caught, Iron Hammer denies involvement

**Success:** Mech demonstration fails, Syndicate embarrassed

**Failure:** Mech works, Syndicate gains legitimacy

---

### ⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Assassinate Kethar Steelheart" (TRAP)

**Patron:** Anonymous note, payment upfront

**Payment:** 1000gp (half upfront)

**Time Limit:** Within the week

**Job:** Kill Don Kethar Steelheart. Anonymous patron claims to be Iron Hammer loyalist.

**THE TWIST:** This is a **Brotherhood setup**. Azrael wants both families to go to war.

- Payment is real but traceable to Iron Hammer
- Kethar is actually well-protected
- If party succeeds, Iron Hammer gets blamed
- If party fails and is caught, they're executed
- If party investigates, can uncover Brotherhood plot

**Success (Accept):** Kethar dies, massive family war

**Success (Expose):** Reveal Brotherhood plot, gain Thorgar's trust

**Failure:** Party caught, executed or hunted
