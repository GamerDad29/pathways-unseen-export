# 🗡️ Yklwa of the Phantom Bloom

## **🗡️ Yklwa of the Phantom Bloom**

*Weapon (Yklwa), Very Rare, Requires Attunement (Warlock)*

> A weapon imbued with haunting beauty, its shaft entwined with ghostly vines that bloom ephemeral petals with every strike. Its presence is cold, elegant, and unsettling—like the memory of a flower pressed into a coffin’s lid.
> 

---

### **Magical Properties**:

- **Phantom Bloom**
    
    When you hit a creature with this weapon, ghostly petals scatter around them. The next attack against that target has **advantage** as spectral energy clings to them.
    
- **Wail of the Dead** *(1/5 days*
    
    When the spear is activated using the **Immovable Rod** function (as a bonus action), it emits a ghostly wail. Each creature of your choice within 10 feet must make a **DC 15 Wisdom saving throw** or become **Frightened** until the end of their next turn.
    
- **Spectral Return**
    
    As a **bonus action**, you can summon the Yklwa back to your hand from up to **120 feet away**, ignoring obstacles as it phases through the **Fae Realm**.
    
- **Void Thorns – Critical Effect**
    
    On a **critical hit**, roll a **d6** to determine the weapon’s phasing effect:
    
    - **1–2:** The weapon phases *through* the target, dealing **half damage** but ignoring all resistances.
    - **3–4:** The weapon deals an additional **3d6 psychic damage**, pulling at the target’s mind through the Veil.
    - **5–6:** The attack is automatically treated as a **critical hit**, even if it would normally just hit.
