# Quick GM Info: Next Step Contacts

## 1. **The Broker / "The Ironwright"**

**Contact Method:** He finds THEM. Party can leave word at "neutral ground" taverns (The Soot & Cinder, The Hollow Tap) or through fences/info brokers.

**Timing:** 1d4 hours after leaving word, or he shows up when dramatically convenient.

**What He Offers:** Kethar job details (infiltrate Parliament safehouse, steal ledgers), attunement ritual info (requires rare components from Syndicate vault), payment in gold + favors.

**Red Flag:** He's TOO helpful. Wants something long-term, not just this job.

## 2. **Yvess Caltren (Gnome Shopkeeper)**

**Location:** Caltren's Curios & Mechanisms (mid-tier district, near foundry quarter).

**Personality:** Nervous energy, talks fast, fidgets with gadgets while speaking.

**"Pressure" Explained:** Parliament agents visited, asking about "unusual purchases" (clockwork parts, alchemical triggers). Yvess didn't cooperate, but now he's scared.

**Hook:** If party helps, he'll discount gear OR share intel on who ELSE Parliament is investigating.

**Complication:** Syndicate also shook him down last week—he's caught between two fires.

---

## 3. **Iron Order Contact: Sergeant Durvin Ironshod**

**Location:** Iron Order precinct near the docks (fortified stone building, dwarven architecture).

**Personality:** Gruff, by-the-book, HATES the Broker but respects his usefulness. Loyal to Karnuk, not necessarily to Parliament.

**What He Knows:** Syndicate attacks are escalating. Parliament is "offering assistance" (read: seizing control). Broker is a wildcard—Order tolerates him because he keeps worse elements in check.

**Offer:** If party reports ambush officially, Iron Order will increase patrols (safer streets) BUT party goes on record as witnesses (Parliament might notice).

**Warning:** "Stay out of politics, or you'll end up like the last crew who got clever."

---

## 4. **Bregan D'aerthe Contact: "Whisper" (Drow Messenger)**

**Location:** Dead-drop in the Underdark tunnels beneath Karnuk (marked by three scratches on a stalactite).

**Personality:** Cold, efficient, speaks in questions. Tests Iblith's loyalty constantly.

**Message for Iblith:** Bregan is WATCHING the Broker alliance. Iblith's job: report Broker's moves, don't commit Bregan resources without approval. If the Broker burns them, Iblith is expendable.

**Side Offer:** Bregan has a separate job—sabotage a Parliament shipment (unrelated to Broker). Pays better, but splits party focus.

**Threat (unspoken):** If Iblith prioritizes party over Bregan, there will be consequences.

---

## 5. **Bear Clan Contact: Thrakka Stoneshoulder**

**Location:** The Iron Hearth (mercenary tavern, rough clientele, Bear Clan hangout spot).

**Personality:** Grizzled female Goliath, missing an eye, covered in ritual scars. Skeptical of Cannonball's "Goliath" claim but respects his SPIRIT.

**What She Offers:** Bear Clan has a contract in Karnuk—escort a caravan through hostile tunnels (Syndicate OR aberration threat). Pays in coin + clan favor tokens.

**Bone Charm:** Thrakka recognizes it—belongs to Karg the Unyielding, a Bear Clan warrior who went missing 2 years ago. "Where did you find this?"

**Hook:** If Cannonball investigates Karg's disappearance, it ties into EITHER Syndicate slave trade OR Parliament experiments. His choice.

## 6. **The Docks (Return Investigation)**

**What's Left:** Scorch marks, acid burns, blood stains. Dock workers are spooked—won't talk unless paid (5 sp each, or Intimidation DC 12).

**Worker Intel:** Syndicate has been "recruiting" aggressively (kidnapping?). Black dragonlings came from warehouse at pier 7 (abandoned, now locked down by Syndicate).

**Clue:** Partial trigger mechanism matches tech seen in warehouse 7. Someone is ARMING the Syndicate with advanced clockwork weapons.

**Ambush Risk:** If party lingers too long (>10 minutes), 1d4 Syndicate scouts spot them. Either another fight OR a tense standoff/chase.

**Environmental Detail:** The Broker's acid spray killed the 3rd dragonling—its corpse is GONE. Someone (Broker? Syndicate? Parliament?) collected it.
