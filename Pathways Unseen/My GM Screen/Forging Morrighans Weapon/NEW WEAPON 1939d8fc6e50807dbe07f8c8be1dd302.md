## **1. Yklwa of the Phantom Bloom**

*Weapon (Yklwa), Very Rare, Requires Attunement (Warlock)*

*A weapon imbued with haunting beauty, its shaft entwined with ghostly vines that bloom ephemeral petals with every strike.*

- **Phantom Bloom:** When you hit a creature with this weapon, ghostly petals scatter around them. The next attack against them has advantage.
- **Wail of the Dead:** Once per long rest, when the spear is activated with the Immovable Rod function, it lets out a ghostly wail. Each creature within 10 feet must make a **DC 15 Wisdom save** or be **frightened** until the end of their next turn.
- **Spectral Return:** As a bonus action, you can summon the yklwa back to your hand from up to **120 feet** away, ignoring obstacles as it passes in and out of the Fae Realm
- A critical hit wit hthe Yklwa of the Phantom Bloom causes the following
    - **Void Thorns:** The weapon constantly drifts between planes. roll a **d6**:
        - **1-2:** The weapon phases through the target, dealing half damage but ignoring resistances.
        - **3-4:** The weapon deals an additional **3d6 psychic damage** as the target's mind is pulled toward the Veil.
        - **5-6:** The weapon lands a **critical hit** if it would normally hit.
