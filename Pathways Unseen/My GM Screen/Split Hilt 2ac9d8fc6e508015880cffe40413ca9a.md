## Bramm Hilt’s Opening Line

Door’s open, forge’s hot, and I’ve no patience for browsers. If you’re here to buy, speak. If you’re here to waste time, leave it outside.

Split Hilt – Armor & Weaponsmith
Proprietor: Bramm Hilt, Master Forgewright
Species: Mountain Dwarf
Age: 132
Temperament: Dry, sardonic, unimpressed by anyone taller than him. Praise his work and he’ll tolerate you. Question it and he’ll throw you out. He’s the kind of dwarf who can identify a metal alloy by the smell of the sparks. He’s proud, stubborn, and secretly soft on adventurers who look like they’re about to make “a story worth a blade.”

Shop Aesthetic
Glowing forge light spilling into the street. Heavy anvils where most shops put chairs. Weapons dangling overhead by thick chains, creaking from the heat. The whole place smells of steel, burnt oil, and old grudges.

Reputation in Karnuk
Everyone knows Split Hilt. The Iron Order buys armor repairs there. Syndicate runners buy weapons there. And Bramm sells to both sides with the neutrality of a mountain. Nobody messes with him. You don’t start fights in a location where everything within arm’s reach is a weapon.
