### **Player-Facing Arrival Description**

As the iron-choked wind of the Underdark stirs the scent of rust and decay, a sudden glimmer breaks the gloom ahead. The darkness parts not with menace—but with *neon*. Pulsing lights in shades of scandalous pink and sultry indigo blink from behind fungal curtains. Then, rising before you like a monolithic jest against nature itself, is **the largest mushroom you’ve ever seen**. Its **gleaming cap** is unmistakably phallic, etched with glowing sigils and spinning enchantments. A glittering marquee of light spells out:

> WELCOME TO FRANK’S PHALLACTERY!
> 
> 
> *“Where Potency Meets Arcana!”*
> 

A disembodied chorus of magically amplified voices belts out a cabaret jingle from unseen horns and enchanted tambourines. The ground pulses faintly beneath your boots, as though the mushroom’s roots are tuned to the rhythm.

The path becomes plush red carpet, unfurling of its own accord. A velvet rope lifts lazily as if beckoning you inward. Above, a rotating magical sign displays vague silhouettes doing improbable things with wands and potions.

---

### **Player-Facing Interior Description**

The interior is a cavernous delight of bad taste and unapologetic confidence. **Glittering chandeliers made from crystal dildos** hang above, refracting magical light in pulsating bursts. Illusory fireworks pop in the corners. Arcane saxophone music croons through enchanted gramophones that hover lazily above the aisles.

Shelves sag under the weight of **potions, lotions, scrolls, salves, enchanted garments, toys, tools**, and “mystery kits” that promise both pleasure and danger. Every surface is lined in **deep red velvet**, and **gold-trimmed signage** advertises magical names like:

- *Turgid’s Tonic of Tenacity!*
- *Nymph’s Nuzzle Elixir™!*
- *Wand Wax™ — Shine Before You Cast!*

There’s even a private alcove labeled **“The Consultation Chamber”** behind shimmering pink curtains that whisper when passed.

### **Character Description: Frank the Proprietor**

Frank appears in a puff of glitter and citrus-scented fog. He’s a **lean, flamboyant human** (or elf? or tiefling?) with an opulent robe stitched from enchanted sequins, each one flashing a different lewd wink or heart when looked at. His **handlebar mustache curls like two question marks**, and his **eyebrows are animated**—literally. They emote on his behalf.

In one hand he twirls a wand shaped like a cane. In the other, a cocktail that glows and smokes of its own accord. His voice is rich, overly theatrical, and sprinkled with innuendo.

> “Darlings, you made it! Welcome to Frank’s Phallactery, where adventuring prowess isn’t just about swords and sorcery, mm?”
> 

He glides instead of walks, sometimes on glitter trails, sometimes via floating chaise lounge.

Frank is a **retired high-level adventurer**, once known as **Frankimus the Full-Staffed**, a bard-sorcerer who took one too many magical knockouts to the head and decided to pivot into a realm he felt was underserved: male-focused magical enhancement. His old fame echoes in occasional magical slip-ups—phantasmal army illusions, booming bardic voice echoes—and a small fleet of summoned furniture.

Despite his flamboyant nature, Frank is **genuinely powerful**. His shop is protected by enchantments that **disorient scrying, block teleportation**, and **repel Underdark threats** with pheromone-based illusory repulsion fields. To locals, he’s either **an arcane anomaly** or **too damn weird to mess with**.

He sees 1–2 caravans a month, all of which **pay toll in potions or novelty gear**. Frank also operates magically guided delivery chutes for discrete customers—including drow matriarchs and deep gnomes. He *has* seen people pass, and always knows *just enough* to tease but never enough to clarify.

> “Oh yes, darling—I did see a rather fetching party of gloom-stalkers three days ago. Looked like they needed my Stamina Stones. I do hope they made it!”
>
