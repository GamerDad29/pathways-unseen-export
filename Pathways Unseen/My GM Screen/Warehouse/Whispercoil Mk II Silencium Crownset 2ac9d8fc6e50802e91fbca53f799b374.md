# Whispercoil Mk II: Silencium Crownset

Whispercoil Mk II: Silencium Crownset
Weapon (hand crossbow), very rare (requires attunement by a monk or rogue)

This masterwork hand crossbow is constructed with the Silencium technique, an arcane-mechanical method that absorbs vibration and eliminates sound. The weapon is nearly weightless and fires with a breath-soft release. While holding the crossbow, you produce no noise when reloading or firing it.

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon.

Silent Release. A creature more than 5 feet from you cannot hear you fire this weapon. After you make an attack with the crossbow, you have advantage on the next Dexterity (Stealth) check you make before the end of your turn.

Shadow Dampening (1/Short Rest). As a bonus action, you can steady the weapon with a sheath of shadow. Until the end of your turn, attack rolls you make with this crossbow ignore disadvantage imposed by dim light, darkness, or half cover.

**Presentation**

The box is a matte obsidian case with beveled corners, lined in smoke-colored spider-silk. When opened, the interior exhales a faint chill like midnight air escaping from a crypt. The Whispercoil itself rests in a custom cradle of folded shadow-cloth, flanked by two glass vials of midnight-lacquer oil and a small etched plaque written in Undercommon:

“For those who must kill without a sound.”

**Weapon Identity**

This Whispercoil isn’t *a* Silencium Frame. It’s *the* Silencium Crownset prototype—the only one to ever leave the Greyhands’ vault before the project was shut down. A failed assassination attempt blamed on this weapon led the Iron Order to purge the entire engineering cell. Every Greyhand who knew the design died for it.

All that remains is this single piece.

**Magic Properties**

Silent Release. No shot can be heard beyond 5 feet. Even then, it resembles the exhale of someone dying quietly. After you fire, you gain advantage on your next Stealth check. If you disappear into dim light or darkness immediately afterward, creatures have disadvantage on Perception checks to locate you.

Shadow Dampening. Once per short rest, pull the shadows tight around your weapon. Until the end of your turn, all ranged attacks with this crossbow ignore disadvantage from dim light, darkness, or partial cover.

Mechanical Effects

Hand Crossbow +1

Range: 40/160

Reload: Free action (monk only)

Special: You can fire this crossbow as part of a Flurry of Blows, replacing one unarmed strike with a bolt attack.

Faction Origin

The Greyhands forged this as a tribute for Crownjaw himself—an “untraceable instrument of preemptive diplomacy,” as the note inside suggests. It was meant to be delivered through Syndicate channels, but the purge stopped it. When the party finds it in the hidden chambers beneath Crownjaw’s criminal estate, they aren’t just finding a weapon…

…they’re finding proof he was promised tools used only by assassins trusted at the highest levels of Syndicate command.

This box is a declaration of allegiance and intent.
