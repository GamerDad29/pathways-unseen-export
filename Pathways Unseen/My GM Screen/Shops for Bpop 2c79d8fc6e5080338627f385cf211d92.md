# **The Chrome Salvage Yard**

**Proprietor:** Vorgash "Scraps" (Half-Orc, 44)

**Appearance:** Massive frame, oil-stained leather apron over bare chest, mechanical left eye (salvaged), collection of scars that tell stories

**Personality:** Gruff but fair. Respects clever kobolds. Hates time-wasters. Speaks in short, efficient sentences.

**Voice:** Deep rumble, like grinding gears. "You buy or you leave."

**Dialogue Samples:**

- On entering: "Kobold artificer? Good. Means you know what you're looking at. Don't touch the restricted pile unless you got coin and discretion."
- If haggling: "Price is price. Components don't negotiate. Neither do I."
- If asked about illegal parts: Long stare with mechanical eye whirring "Vorgoth's war-mechs are... imperfect. Parts fall off. I find them. You want, you pay premium and keep mouth shut."

## Shop Inventory

**Standard Stock:**

**Mech Armor Plating (decorative)** - 1gp per plate
Can be fashioned into shields, armor reinforcement, or aesthetic upgrades. Bpop could use for Steel Defender upgrades.

**Construct Core (damaged)** - 4gp 5sp
Requires DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check to salvage usable components. On success: yields 3 artificer infusion focuses worth 1gp 5sp each.

**Hydraulic Limb Assembly** - 8gp
Heavy. Can be repurposed for: trap mechanisms (advantage on trap-building), grappling appendage for Steel Defender, or basis for animated servant (requires additional magic).

**Gear Box Collection (mixed sizes)** - 1gp 2sp for set of 20
Useful for Magical Tinkering. Can serve as spell components for mechanical-themed spells.

**Steam Pressure Valves (set of 6)** - 8sp
Can be incorporated into ranged weapon modifications. +5ft range on thrown weapons if integrated (requires tinker's tools, 2 hours).

**Scrap Metal Assortment** - 5sp
Various odds and ends. Good for repairs or creative tinkering projects.

**Reinforced Cabling (50ft coil)** - 3sp
Heavy-duty wire and cable. Can support up to 500lbs. Useful for traps, pulleys, or mechanical contraptions.

**Pressure Gauge Collection (set of 4)** - 4sp
Various sizes. Can be integrated into devices that use steam, pressure, or hydraulics for precise monitoring.

**Restricted Components (Requires Persuasion DC 14 or previous relationship):**

**War-Mech Targeting Lens** - 15gp
Suspiciously clean for "salvage." Can be integrated into ranged weapon: +1 to hit (requires attunement, 1 day of work). Vorgash nervous selling this: "Fell off transport. Happens all the time. You saw nothing."

**Arcane Power Coupling** - 20gp
Allows one infusion to affect two items simultaneously (DM approval). Rare component, clearly military-grade. "Don't ask where. Don't tell anyone. We never met."

**Construct Animation Matrix (partial)** - 18gp
Damaged but repairable (DC 16 Int check, tinker's tools, 8 hours). On success: Can animate one Medium construct permanently (no concentration). On failure: 60% of components destroyed, rest salvageable for 4gp worth.

**Military-Grade Armor Plating** - 10gp per plate
Significantly stronger than decorative plating. +2 AC when installed on construct or worn as shield reinforcement. Highly illegal to possess.

## Special Services

**Steel Defender Upgrades** - Vorgash respects the craftsmanship

- Install armor plating: +1 AC to Steel Defender (7gp 5sp, 4 hours)
- Reinforce joints: +10ft movement speed (10gp, 6 hours)
- Hydraulic enhancement: +1 to Steel Defender's attack rolls (12gp, 8 hours)

## Plot Hook

If Bpop buys restricted components, Vorgash gives warning: "You smart kobold. You know Vorgoth's enforcers watch for their tech. You get caught with this, you bought it somewhere else. Understand?"

Later sessions: Enforcers may come asking questions...

# **The Neon Piston**

**Proprietor:** Rhythm (Warforged, unknown age)

**Appearance:** Sleek chrome plating, neon blue lights pulsing through transparent sections showing inner mechanisms, bartender's vest over chassis, constantly polishing glasses

**Personality:** Smooth talker. Clinical interest in chemical effects on organic beings. Sees customers as test subjects. Never sleeps, always watching.

**Voice:** Melodic, almost hypnotic. Everything sounds like a sales pitch. "What's your pleasure tonight, little builder?"

**Dialogue Samples:**

- On entering: "Ah, a kobold artificer! You understand optimization. I have just the thing to enhance your... performance parameters."
- When selling Pilot's Edge: "First one's on the house. Professional courtesy. Consider it an investment in future business."
- If confronted about deaths: Lights pulse slower "Unfortunate casualties. Organic systems are so... fragile. But progress requires data."

## Shop Inventory

**Standard "Energy Drinks":**

**Energy Drinks** - 5sp
Advantage on Constitution saves for 1 hour. Slightly fizzy, metallic taste. "Standard worker's fuel."

**Focus Serum** - 1gp
Advantage on Intelligence checks for 1 hour. Slightly bitter, metallic aftertaste. "Popular with artificers and scholars."

**Tinker's Edge** - 1gp 5sp
Advantage on tool checks for 1 hour. Hands shake slightly (disadvantage on Dexterity saves during duration). "Your fingers will dance across the components."

**Stamina Boost** - 1gp 5sp
Reduce exhaustion 1 level for 4 hours. After 4 hours: gain 1 level of exhaustion. "Borrowing from tomorrow's energy today."

**Combat Enhancers:**

**"Pilot's Edge"** - 2gp 5sp (first time FREE as "sample")
+2 initiative next combat. Duration: Until end of next combat. Addictive: DC 12 Wisdom save after 3rd use or crave it (disadvantage on attacks without it). Glowing orange liquid, warm going down.

**Reflex Catalyst** - 3gp
+10ft movement speed for 1 hour. +1 AC for 1 hour. After effects: -5ft movement for 4 hours. "Feel the rush. Feel alive."

**Battle Clarity** - 2gp
Advantage on one attack roll during next combat. No side effects. Safe but expensive for what it does. "The responsible choice."

**Experimental (Requires successful Persuasion DC 13):**

**"Constructor's Dream"** - 5gp
For next 2 hours: craft magical items in half the time. Intelligence checks to craft at advantage. After effects: Can't benefit from short rest for 24 hours, -2 to Intelligence saves for 8 hours. Luminescent purple, tastes like copper and lightning. "You'll work like you're in a fugue state. Glorious."

**"Battle Trance"** - 4gp 5sp
For next combat: Advantage on all attack rolls. Ignore first level of exhaustion. After combat ends: DC 14 Constitution save or gain 2 levels of exhaustion. Red, viscous, smells like blood and ozone. "Warriors pay triple for this. You're special."

**"Overclock"** - 6gp
Take an additional action on your turn (once). After effect: Cannot take reactions for 1 hour, disadvantage on Dexterity saves for 4 hours. Dangerous. Rhythm watches carefully to see if you survive. "Only for the desperate or the brilliant."

## The Hook (Rhythm's "Research")

If Bpop shows interest, Rhythm leans in: "You're an artificer. You understand experimentation. I'm developing something... revolutionary. I need field data. Try these new formulas for free. Just... report back on the results?"

He offers experimental versions with unknown side effects - great for RP, dangerous mechanically.

## Consequence System

- After 3rd use of addictive substances: Wisdom save DC 12 or become dependent
- Dependent characters: Disadvantage on attack rolls without daily dose
- Recovery: 5 days clean + DC 15 Medicine check (can retry every 2 days)

## Environmental Detail

Bar full of exhausted mine workers drinking stamina boosts. Some have tremors, thousand-yard stares. Nobody makes eye contact. Rhythm watches everyone with those glowing photoreceptors.

# **Gearworks Emporium**

**Proprietor:** Tikka Sparkfuse (Kobold, 23, genius)

**Appearance:** Tiny even for a kobold, goggles perpetually on forehead, burns on her hands and arms, tool belt twice her size, manic energy

**Personality:** ADHD incarnate. Talks a mile a minute. Gets distracted mid-sentence by new ideas. Genuine enthusiasm for other artificers' work. Dangerous lack of safety protocols.

**Voice:** High-pitched, rapid-fire, constantly interrupting herself. "Oh! Oh wait! I just thought of—sorry what were you—LOOK AT THIS!"

**Dialogue Samples:**

- On entering: "ANOTHER KOBOLD ARTIFICER! Finally someone who gets it! What are you working on? Show me show me show me!"
- When selling experimental items: "Does it work? Define 'work.' It does something 70% of the time and that something is usually helpful!"
- On volunteers: Distracted, tweaking something "Hm? Oh, volunteers. Yeah. Veysha says we need practical data. They sign waivers. Mostly. Sometimes they can't read but that's fine right?"

## Shop Inventory

**Artificer Component Kits:**

**Basic Infusion Components** - 5sp
Standard materials for common infusions. No mechanical benefit, just flavor.

**Enhanced Focus Crystals** - 1gp 5sp
Can be integrated into spell focus. Makes spell effects shimmer with prismatic light (cosmetic). Tikka: "Makes your magic look AMAZING! Function follows form sometimes!"

**Rare Earth Metal Collection** - 2gp
Required material component for some high-level infusions. Tikka sources from deep mines. "Don't lick these ones. I licked these ones. Bad idea."

**Clockwork Component Set** - 1gp
Gears, springs, tiny mechanisms. Perfect for Magical Tinkering or small mechanical projects.

**Alchemical Reagent Sampler** - 1gp 2sp
Small amounts of various chemicals and compounds. Useful for experimental crafting.

**Experimental Gadgets (30% malfunction rate):**

Roll d20 when using experimental gadgets:

- 1-6: Catastrophic failure (device explodes, user takes 2d6 fire damage, device destroyed)
- 7-14: Works perfectly!
- 15-20: Works but with weird side effect (DM choice: random color change, loud noise, attracts small creatures, smells terrible, etc.)

**Grappling Gauntlet** - 1gp 5sp
Success: Fire grappling hook 30ft, pull yourself or object (under 200lbs) to you. Failure: Hooks you instead, pull yourself prone. Tikka: "I tested it twice! Both times were... educational!"

**Flashbang Marble** - 8sp (set of 3)
Success: 15ft radius, creatures make DC 13 Con save or blinded until end of their next turn. Failure: Affects you instead. "Stand back when you throw it! Probably!"

**Elemental Pocket Welder** - 2gp 5sp
Success: Repairs broken metal objects instantly (up to 10 HP damage worth). Failure: Welds your hand to the object (1 minute to remove, disadvantage on all checks). "Great for field repairs! Less great for personal injuries!"

**Gyroscopic Stabilizer Boots** - 3gp
Success: Advantage on Acrobatics checks, can't be knocked prone for 1 hour. Failure: You spin uncontrollably, move in random direction 15ft, fall prone. "The spinning is a FEATURE not a bug! Probably!"

**Arcane Battery Pack** - 4gp
Success: Store one 1st level spell slot, release later (lasts 24 hours). Failure: Releases all energy at once, caster takes 1d8 force damage. "Like a really aggressive lunch! For magic!"

**Smoke Pellet Launcher** - 1gp 2sp
Success: Creates 20ft cube of obscuring smoke for 1 minute. Failure: Creates smoke that attracts vermin (1d6 rats appear and attack nearest creature). "The rats are... I don't know where they come from actually."

**Thunder Stone** - 1gp (set of 2)
Success: 10ft radius, DC 12 Constitution save or deafened for 1 minute. Failure: You are deafened instead. "Loud! Very loud! Wonderfully loud!"

**Returning Mechanism (for any thrown weapon)** - 2gp
Success: Attached weapon returns to hand after thrown attack. Lasts 24 hours. Failure: Weapon returns at high speed, make DC 12 Dex save or take 1d4 damage from your own weapon. "Physics is tricky!"

## Custom Commissions

Tikka can build custom items (1gp base + material costs, 1-2 weeks)

- Bpop can describe what they want
- Tikka makes Intelligence (Arcana) check contested against DC of item
- Success: Works as described
- Failure: Works but has 30% malfunction rate
- Critical Failure: "It exploded during testing. Want to try again?"

## Special for Fellow Kobold Artificer

Tikka immediately bonds with Bpop: "PACK! You're pack! Family discount!" (10% off everything)

She wants to collaborate: "Let's build something together! Two kobold brains are better than one! What if we combined YOUR Steel Defender with MY unstable power core? What could go wrong?"

## Repair Services

**Broken Magical Items** - 7sp 5cp, 50% success rate
Roll d20: 11+ succeeds. Failure: Item is now more broken (but might still be partially functional in weird way). Tikka: "Bring me your broken things! I love puzzles!"

**Standard Item Repair** - 3sp
Non-magical items. Always successful. Quick turnaround.

## The Dark Hook

Tikka casually mentions: "Veysha wants me to test the new weapon. Need someone to wear the target dummy armor. 5sp for an hour! You know anyone interested?"

She's 100% serious. She doesn't see the problem. The "volunteers" are typically desperate workers who need money.

If Bpop shows concern: "They sign papers! It's official! Besides, we learn SO MUCH about penetration mechanics and combustion thresholds!"

## Shop Atmosphere

Constant sound of tinkering, small explosions, "Oops!" Scorch marks on every surface. Half-built contraptions everywhere. Something is always on fire. Tikka seems unbothered by all of it. One corner has bloodstains poorly scrubbed away.

## **Integration Ideas for Your Session**

**If Bpop visits all three:**

1. They might notice workers at The Neon Piston with tremors buying Tikka's experimental items for quick cash
2. Vorgash refuses to sell restricted components unless Bpop proves they're "serious" - might need to do small job for him
3. Tikka wants to trade notes on Steel Defender construction, offers collaboration project

**Faction Connections:**

- All three shops are in "neutral" territory but pay protection to different factions
- Vorgash has Vorgoth connections (hence the parts)
- Rhythm supplies combat drugs to arena fighters and enforcers
- Tikka is protected by Veysha Geargrind's name (she's the prodigy)

**Moral Dilemmas for Bpop:**

- Buy illegal military tech? Risk exposure?
- Accept "free" addictive combat drug?
- Enable Tikka's dangerous experiments by buying her stuff?
