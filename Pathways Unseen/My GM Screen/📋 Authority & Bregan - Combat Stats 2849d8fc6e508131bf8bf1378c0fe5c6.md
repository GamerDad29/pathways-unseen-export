## Viceroy Vorgoth the Ironclad

**Level 15 Eldritch Knight Fighter**

**AC:** 22 | **HP:** 195 | **Speed:** 30 ft. | **Initiative:** +2

**STR** 20 (+5) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 20 (+5) | **INT** 16 (+3) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 18 (+4)

**Saves:** STR +10, CON +10

**Weapon:** Warden's Gavel (greatsword) - +11 to hit, 2d6+6 slashing + 1d8 force

**Multiattack:** 4 attacks

**Special:**

- Action Surge (2/rest) - 8 attacks possible
- War Magic - Bonus attack after cantrip
- Spells: *Shield, Absorb Elements, Counterspell, Fireball, Misty Step*
- Can summon personal war-mech (1/day) - breath weapon 6d8 fire DC 18

**Tactics:** Doesn't fight unless forced. Uses *Counterspell* liberally. Summons mech immediately if cornered. Iron Order arrives in 3 rounds.

**Personality:** Authoritative • Pragmatic • Calculating

**Voice:** Jeremy Irons
